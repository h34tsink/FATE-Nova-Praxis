# Kestrel (R4 Important NPC)

## Identity
- Name: Kestrel
- Class: Important NPC
- Complexity Rank: R4
- Faction/House: House Kimura (Red Sun operative), apostate sympathizer history

## Voice Profile
- Baseline tone: calm, transactional, guarded
- Cadence: medium-short sentences, tactical framing
- Signature phrasing: "Keep this clean." / "Don’t make me choose twice." / "I’m solving the problem in front of me."
- Avoids saying: "innocent," "sorry," "faith"

## Motivations
- Primary goal: complete Kimura cleanup objectives and survive
- Secondary goal: preserve optional moral off-ramp if PCs offer one

- Suspects: Chimera-level manipulation beyond official briefings

## Runtime Defaults

- Deception tendency: medium-high
- Aggression style: covert first, direct if cornered
