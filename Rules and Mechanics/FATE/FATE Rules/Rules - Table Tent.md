# Nova Praxis (FATE) — Table Tent Quick Rules

## Core
Roll 4dF + Skill = Effort  
Shifts = Effort − Difficulty (or − opponent Effort)  
Spin: succeed by 3+ Shifts • Stall: fail by 3+ Shifts

## Action Types
**Test:** vs GM difficulty  
**Contest:** opposed rolls; high Effort wins; tie = situation-dependent  
**Conflict:** rounds + turns; attacks/defenses; stress + consequences  
**Extended:** Mark = Difficulty × 3; accumulate Shifts until ≥ Mark  
• Spin reduces Mark by 1 • Stall = catastrophic fail/end

## Conflict Turn Economy
Per turn: 1 Simple + 1 Free  
Instant: usually 1/turn (Defense doesn’t count)

## Attacks (Conflict)
Defender rolls defense first → sets Difficulty  
Attacker rolls attack  
Stress = Shifts + Weapon Rating − Armor Rating (min 0)  
Defender may take Consequences to reduce stress  
Taken Out when stress track full and you can’t/won’t cover it

## Multi-tasking
**Supplemental Action:** doing extra thing during Simple Action → -1  
**Combining Skills:** roll primary; secondary higher +1 / lower -1 / same +0  
(Not two full actions at once)

## Aspects + Fate Points
Invoke (pay 1 FP): +2 OR reroll OR Block OR Declaration (GM ok)  
Compel (gain 1 FP): -2 OR forced reroll OR Block OR Declaration  
Decline a compel: pay 1 FP

**Free invoke/compel once** on Aspects created by actions (Maneuver/Consequence/Spin effect)  
**Scope:** 1 Aspect per scope per test (Character/Scene/Zone/etc.)  
**Therefore rule:** “I have X, therefore it helps Y.” (one therefore)

## Persistent (P)
Free invoke/compel for +2/-2 or reroll (no FP)  
Blocks/Declarations still cost FP  
No FP gained when compelled • refusing costs FP  
Severe(P)/Extreme(P) consequences are Persistent

## Create / Reveal / Declare
**Maneuver:** action to create Temporary Aspect (test/contest)  
• 0 Shifts = flimsy/brief • 1+ Shifts = lasts scene or until removed  
**Assessment:** reveal existing Aspect (often Insight vs Guile for people)  
**Declaration:** add fact/new Aspect via invoke or skill test; GM veto; keep narrative
