Combat Actions

Ambushing

Target unaware of threat → no Skill on defense. Dice roll only.

Armor Rating still applies.

GM should still roll defense for mooks in this case.

Full Defense

Spend a Simple Action doing nothing but defending.

Gain +2 on all defense tests until your next turn.

If you haven't acted yet when first attacked, you may declare Full Defense and forfeit your Simple Action that turn.

Hold Your Action

Delay your turn to act after another character.

Hold through the entire round → go first next round.

Multiple held characters: Strategy contest to determine order.

Taking Aim

Simple Action · Perception (Diff: 2)

Success → place In My Sights Aspect on target.

Invoke free on your next attack only. Fragile — GM may require refresh if target moves erratically.

Taking Cover

Free Action to move behind cover in your zone.

TypeAspect Gained
Light CoverBehind Light Cover
Hard CoverBehind Hard Cover(P) — persistent; can declare "can't be hit" for 1 FP

Push / Pull / Throw

Contested Athletics + Size test.

Win → target moves to adjacent zone of your choice.

If larger: can push extra zones up to Size difference.

Can follow opponent as a Free Action.

Help from Others

Helper rolls same Skill vs. difficulty = primary's Skill rank.

Success+1 bonus to primary
Spin+2 bonus to primary
FailNo effect
Stall−1 penalty to primary
GM determines maximum number of helpers.

Block Actions

Declare what you block + Skill used → roll to set Block Potency.

Anyone attempting the blocked action must beat Potency (Simple Action). Success = overcome, but counts as Supplemental (−1 to the follow-up).

Sample BlockSkill
Suppression FireFirearms
RestrainMelee or Athletics
DistractionGuile or Diplomacy
Ignoring a Block may grant Blocker a free Instant Action response.

Hazards & Damage

Falling

Dice + bonus (no Skill) vs. Endurance.

Athletics 3+ → +1 defense bonus. Athletics 5+ → +2.

DistanceAttack Bonus
Up to 3m+2
Up to 6m+4
Up to 30m+8
Up to 150m+12
300m+ (terminal velocity)+18 (cap)
Armor does not help unless it has a Hydrostatic Gel Layer.

Fire & Environmental Hazards

Attacks everyone in zone each round. Dice + Intensity. Defend with Endurance. Most armor useless.

IntensityDescription
0Fire present but avoidable
3Waves of heat; most things burning
6Everything ablaze; direct flames
9Inferno
Direct ignition (flamethrower, etc.) = Intensity 6. Burning targets gain Aspect: On Fire!

Vacuum

Environmental hazard. Intensity 6. Defend with Endurance.

Vacuum Suits grant safe exposure. Cybersleeves are immune.

Zero Gravity

Zone gains Persistent Aspect: Zero-G(P).

Can be invoked or compelled for any character in the zone.

Contact → attack: Potency + dice (no Skill).

Poisons & Drugs

Both have Potency (resist with Endurance) and Subtlety (detect with Perception/Education).

Damaging Poisons: Attack at start of first turn after exposure; repeats each turn until end of scene or cured. Education (Diff: Potency +2) to treat. Spin on defense may stop it.

Drugs: Place Aspects on target (as Maneuver). Endurance vs. Potency.

LoseGain drug Aspects for listed duration (not Consequences)

Ego & Transhumanism

Resleeving / Becoming SIM

Mental attack, Edge 8. Defend with Cohesion. Mnemonics engineer may aid.

ConditionEdge Modifier
First resleeve ever+2
Remember dying before resleeve+2
Resleeving into clone / same model−2 (defense bonus)
SIM State(P) and Synthetic Biology(P) Aspects should be compelled during resleeving.

Unrecoverable: Severe Mental Consequence → mnemonics editing required. Extreme Consequence or mentally Taken Out → refusal to resleeve.

Forking

Illegal Humanity Preservation Act — punishable by death and erasure.

Learning you've been forked: mental attack, Edge +4.

Fork copy has all original's skills, memories, relationships. It is not a slave.

Forks may become hostile to the original — desire to preserve their own existence.

GM Options: Player runs all instances simultaneously, or pick one to play and treat others as GM-controlled NPCs. Consider a Lasting Aspect for a hanging copy.

Merging

Requires resurrection chamber. Combines multiple ego instances.

Mental attack: Edge = total instances merged + trauma bonus.

Favors

Favor Value = ½ item Cost (round up) for goods; GM-determined for services.

House Networking

SuccessFavor granted + Rep hit Value 1
FailFavor granted + Rep hit = Favor Value, OR withdraw (Rep hit 1)
StallNot granted + Rep hit = Favor Value + Lasting Aspect (~1 week)

Apostate Networking

SuccessFavor granted + Tapped My Network Aspect (~1 week)
FailFavor granted + Tapped Out My Network(P) (~1 month), OR withdraw (Tapped My Network, ~1 week)
StallTapped Out My Network(P) + Social Pariah (~1 month each)

Rep-Rating

Circular track of boxes. Fill wheel → Rep-Rating +1, reset. Max rating: 11.

Giving a Bump/Hit: You suffer Rep hit Value 1. Target gains bump/hit up to your Rep-Rating. Done anonymously.

ActionEffect on Track
BumpMark boxes clockwise, Value = bumper's Rep-Rating (max)
HitErase boxes counter-clockwise = hit Value. Empty wheel = Rep-Rating −1
Fame: gain via Fame Stunt or Agenda Celebrity Stunt. Infamy: reflected in Rep hits + Lasting Aspects.

Stealth & Infiltration

Hiding

Stealth vs. Perception. Active searcher with reason + time: +2 bonus.

Hiding from Monitor: Stealth (Diff: 3). Area's Mesh Aspect may modify.

Aspects (darkness, clutter, cover) are the primary source of bonuses and penalties.

Shadowing (Moving Unseen)

Stealth + Guile. Searchers gain bonus based on movement speed:

MovementDetection Bonus
Normal+4
Hustle+6
Running+8
Creeping: Maneuver (no test) → places Creeping Aspect. Requires Supplemental Action each turn to maintain.

Camouflage

Hardware Engineering + Stealth (Diff: 2). Shifts generated = bonus to hiding tests while camouflaged.

Takes ~1 hour to construct.

Software Engineering test. Describe the program → success places an Aspect (as Maneuver). Only creator benefits unless sent to others.

Hostile programs face CID AI security screening — very hard to deploy offensively.

Physical & Environment

Size & Lifting

Lift capacity = weight at your Size × Athletics. Athletics 0 → half capacity.

LoadTest Difficulty
Up to 150% of capacityAthletics + 2
Up to 200% of capacityAthletics + 4
Over 200%Cannot lift

Carrying counts as Supplemental Action:

≤ 50% of lift cap−1
> 50% of lift cap−2
> 100% (up to 200%)−3

Zones

Same zone = hand-to-hand range. Adjacent = thrown / short range. 5–10 zones = weapon range limits.

Zones represent relationships, not exact measurements. Use rough sketches — not grids — unless area is featureless.

Rooms, corridors, open areas = natural zone boundaries. Apply scene Aspects to each zone for flavor and mechanical hooks.
