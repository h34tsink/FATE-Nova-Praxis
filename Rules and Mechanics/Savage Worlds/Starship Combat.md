### 1. Chase Rules Adaptation
- **Spaceship Combats**: Follow Chase rules with modifications.
- **Piloting Rolls**: Encompass all maneuvers.
- **Bridge Crews**: For Large vessels and bigger, use Knowledge (Astrogation) for computer-managed controls.

### 2. Reaction Fire (Chase Rules Only)
- **New Ability**: For Chase Rules.
- **Firing**: Can fire without Advantage; targets must have Action Card ≤10.
- **Penalty**: Gunner takes a –2 penalty.

### 3. Boarding Procedures
- **Conditions**: Only possible on disabled ships.
- **Piloting Roll**: Required to match target's vector and speed; failure causes damage based on ship size.
- **Boarding Challenges**: Cut through hull, navigate internal obstacles, combat survivors.
- **Interior Toughness**: Walls/doors at 14 (4), secure areas at 27 (15).
- **Combat Options**: Use tactical combat or Mass Battle rules; defenders get +2 on Knowledge (Battle) rolls.

### 4. Firing Weapons
- **Ranges**: In tabletop inches; for combat, not maximums.
- **Size Adjustments**: +2 for larger targets, -2 for smaller.
- **Unstable Platform**: No penalty in space, applies in atmosphere or unstable conditions.
- **Weapon Arcs**: Full arsenal with Advantage and face card; half with lower card (excluding Fixed Weapons).
- **Linked Arms**: Count as one weapon choice.

### 5. Compartments & Critical Hits
- **Wound Limit**: Max two wounds for Large/Huge ships, one for Giant+ per attack.
- **Critical Hits**: Still applicable; Wrecked Critical Hit destroys the ship.
- **Critical Hits Table**: Use Starship Critical Hits Table for Large ships+.

### 6. Out of Control & Collisions
- **Out of Control**: In space, lose next action; no table roll.
- **Collision Risk**: Second Piloting roll in obstacles; failure leads to collision (max 20d6).
- **Disaster Result**: Causes a wound and automatic critical hit in Chase.
