**Suits:**
- **Spades:** Corporate Espionage and Sabotage
- **Hearts:** Rescue or Protection Missions
- **Diamonds:** Acquisition or Retrieval of Items or Information
- **Clubs:** Illegal or Morally Questionable Operations

**Face Value:**
- **2-10:** Indicate the level of difficulty and the scale of the operation. The higher the number, the higher the risk and reward.
- **Jack:** A mission with a twist or additional complication.
- **Queen:** A mission requiring sophisticated social manipulation or influence.
- **King:** A high-stakes mission involving powerful individuals or entities.
- **Ace:** A rare, potentially game-changing contract with enormous risks and rewards.

**Rewards:**
- **2-6:** Small rep bump or low-value gear template.
- **7-10:** Moderate rep bump or medium-value gear template.
- **Jack-Queen:** Significant rep bump, high-value gear template, or a minor favor.
- **King-Ace:** Major rep bump, cutting-edge gear template, asset bump, or a major favor.

### Example Contracts:

**Spades 10:** Infiltrate a top-security corporate facility to sabotage their newest prototype weapon. Reward: Significant rep bump, possibly some tech gleaned from the mission.

**Hearts Jack:** Protect a diplomat during a secret negotiation, but there's an unknown double agent in the mix. Reward: High-value gear template and a minor favor from the diplomat.

**Diamonds Queen:** Steal a set of rare data archives from a well-guarded private collection without alerting the owner. Reward: Significant rep bump and a high-value gear template.

**Clubs King:** Arrange and execute a black-market auction for a piece of illegal tech without attracting attention from the authorities. Reward: Major rep bump and a major favor from the underground.
