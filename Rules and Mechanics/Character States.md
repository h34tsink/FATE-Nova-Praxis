# Character States

A comprehensive reference for all character states in Nova Praxis.

---

## 🧑 Pure

> [!state]- Pure State Overview
> **Description:** Inhabits their original, un-Apotheosized body.
> 
> Pure characters still inhabit the body they were born with. They have not undergone Apotheosis, though they may still have any number of Augmentations, both bioware and cyberware.

> [!info]- Pure State Benefits
> **💪 Physical Skill Allocation:**
> - 8 ranks to distribute among Athletics, Endurance, and Perception
> - Maximum 5 ranks per skill
> 
> **🎯 Additional Aspects:**
> - Physical Trait: Describes some physical trait related to your body
> 
> **⚡ Refresh Rating Modifier:** +2
> 
> **⭐ Free Stunts:**
> - Carpe Diem
> 
> **📊 Skill Bonus:**
> - +1 to one non-physical skill (max rank 5)
> 
> **⚠️ Important Note:**
> Loses all State Benefits (including the Skill bonus) if Apotheosis Augmentation is taken later.

---

## 💻 SIM

> [!state]- SIM State Overview
> **Description:** A substrate-independent mind residing as pure software in a host computer.
> 
> The substrate-independent mind (SIM) has undergone Apotheosis, but has elected to reside in a host computer system purely as software instead of downloading into a sleeve.

> [!info]- SIM State Benefits
> **💪 Physical Skill Allocation:**
> - 7 ranks to distribute among Athletics, Endurance, and Perception
> - Maximum 5 ranks per skill
> - These skills allow interaction with virtual objects
> 
> **⭐ Free Stunts:**
> - SIM State (automatic)
> 
> **🎲 Choice Stunt (pick ONE from):**
> - Digitally Evolved
> - Digital Spider
> - Drone Jock
> - Infusion
> - Skilled (SINC)
> - System Slip
>
> > **House Rule:** +1 SINC and +1 Software Engineering are campaign additions, not in the core rulebook.
>
> **📊 Skill Bonuses (House Rule):**
> - +1 SINC (max rank 5)
> - +1 Software Engineering (max rank 5)

> [!warning]- SIM State Drawbacks
> **🔒 Interaction Limitation:**
> Can only view the world from one mesh at a time, taking about five minutes to transition. Can shift perspective within the same mesh at a pace equivalent to a running human.
> 
> **📡 Lag Penalty:**
> May suffer penalties due to lag on Skill tests when acting through machines located a great distance from the host:
> - Within 1000 km: no penalty
> - Same star system (>1000 km): -2 penalty
> - Outside star system: -4 penalty
>
> **⏱️ Hosting Transfer Times:**
>
> - Within 1000 km: ~10 minutes
> - Same star system (>1000 km): ~1 hour
> - Out-of-system host: ~4 hours
> 
> **🤖 Physical Interaction:**
> Requires drones for physical interaction.
> 
> **👻 Visibility:**
> Must appear as a semi-transparent ARO when ghosting in the physical world due to Humanity Preservation Act.
> 
> **🏠 Living Space:**
> Spends most time in a custom home virtual environment; SIMs have dominion over their home environment.

## 🧬 Sleeved

> [!state]- Sleeved State Overview
> **Description:** Has undergone Apotheosis and inhabits a sleeve.
> 
> You have undergone Apotheosis, and inhabit a sleeve. It may be the body you were born with, transformed via Apotheosis, or one you've purchased.

> [!info]- Sleeved State Benefits
> **🤖 Starting Sleeve:**
> You begin play with a biosleeve or cybersleeve with a Cost no greater than the higher of your Assets or Rep-Rating, +4.
> 
> **💰 Calculation:** MAX(Assets Skill Rank, Rep-Rating) + 4
> 
> **💪 Physical Skills:**
> Physical skills are determined by the chosen sleeve.
> 
> **📝 Note:**
> Later steps in the character creation process will guide you through determining your Assets and Rep-Rating.

---

## 🔄 Changing States During Play

> [!info]- State Transitions
> It is possible to change states during play. A Pure character can purchase the Apotheosis Augmentation. Once you have undergone Apotheosis, you can change between Sleeved and SIM states via a resurrection chamber.
>
> **However, you can never go back to being Pure.**
>
> Should you change states, you automatically lose the State Benefits of your previous state and gain the State Benefits of your new state.
