# Equipment

A comprehensive reference for all equipment in Nova Praxis.

---

## 💻 Computing & Communication

> [!equipment]- Agent (AI Assistant)
> **💰 Cost:** 4-10 (variant dependent) | **⚡ ER:** 1-2  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> Agents are personal AI assistants that provide skill bonuses and computational support. These intelligent software programs are designed to augment human capabilities, offering expertise in various fields.
> 
> **Variants:**
> - **Alpha** (Cost 4, ER 1): Basic AI assistance. Choose 1 skill for +1 bonus from: Diplomacy, Education, Engineering (Hardware/Software), Insight, Mnemonics, Research, Strategy
> - **Beta** (Cost 7, ER 1): Advanced AI. Choose 2 skills for +1 bonus from the same list
> - **Gamma** (Cost 10, ER 2): Sophisticated AI. Choose 1 skill for +2 bonus and 2 skills for +1 bonus from the same list

> [!equipment]- AR Display Device
> **💰 Cost:** 2-3  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> AR display devices are wearable systems that display augmented reality content, allowing users to interface with computers, networks, and other electronic devices through visual cues and gestures.
> 
> **Variants:**
> - **Glasses** (Cost 2): Standard AR glasses with heads-up display
> - **Contact Lenses** (Cost 3): Discreet contact lens AR system

> [!equipment]- ARID Tag
> **💰 Cost:** 0  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> ARID (Automated Registry of Individual Data) tags are small digital fingerprints that carry basic identification and contact information. The Coalition requires all citizens to carry ARID tags at all times.

> [!equipment]- ARID Cloner
> **💰 Cost:** 4  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
> 
> A specialized device used to copy and implant ARID information from one tag to another. This illegal technology allows users to clone identification data, enabling identity theft and circumvention of Coalition security systems.

> [!equipment]- Broadcast Screen
> **💰 Cost:** 4  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A portable electromagnetic interference generator (usually worn on the wrist, as a belt buckle, or as jewelry) that creates a 2-meter radius shell of intense EM interference blocking all incoming and outgoing wireless electronic transmissions.
>
> **Mechanics:** Attempts to manipulate a computer through a broadcast screen suffer a **-4 penalty**.

> [!equipment]- CIST (Cyber-Intrusion and Security Terminal)
> **💰 Cost:** 10
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> Purpose-built computer for housing an Agent capable of running cyber-intrusion software. Each CIST can run only a single intrusion routine and generally takes the shape of a PPC.
>
> **Variants:**
> - **Offensive CIST** (Cost 10): Allows a non-Savant character to use a single Savant Program using Software Engineering instead of SINC. The same CIST cannot be used more than once per scene. On a failed execution test, the CIST ceases to function for a duration equal to the margin of failure +2 (instead of suffering system stress). Can be restored early with 5 minutes of work, a Software Engineering test (Diff 3), and a Fate Point
> - **Defensive CIST** (Cost 10): Instead of executing a Program, adds +3 to the execution difficulty of any Savant Program attempt (by a Savant or another CIST) against the user or a single computer system of their choice

> [!equipment]- False CID
> **💰 Cost:** 5-11 | **⚡ ER:** 1-4  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
> 
> Fake Coalition identity with varying quality levels (detection test Difficulty 2).
> 
> **Variants:**
> - **Basic** (Cost 5, ER +1): Rep 4
> - **Advanced** (Cost 7, ER +2): Rep 5
> - **Superior** (Cost 9, ER +3): Rep 6
> - **Elite** (Cost 11, ER +4): Rep 7

> [!equipment]- HoloMask
> **💰 Cost:** 6
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> A holographic disguise device (usually worn as a necklace or headband) that projects a hologram over your face, masking your features to appear as someone else. The mask can replicate the appearance of anyone you have mapped with a spatial camera. The hologram is good enough to fool a mesh Monitor, making it impossible for the Monitor to identify you with facial recognition protocols.
>
> **Mechanics:** Grants a **+2 bonus on Guile rolls** to remain disguised.

> [!equipment]- Neural Link
> **💰 Cost:** 3  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A direct neural interface device that allows thought-to-computer communication. The neural link enables users to interact with computer systems and networks using only mental commands, providing faster and more intuitive control than traditional interfaces.

> [!equipment]- Portable Personal Computer (PPC)
> **💰 Cost:** 5-11  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> Ranges from wrist-worn systems to tablets. Interacted with by voice commands or AR display/interface devices. Always equipped with an Agent and other software programs.
> 
> **Variants:**
> - **Alpha** (Cost 5): Personal computer with Alpha Agent
> - **Beta** (Cost 8): Personal computer with Beta Agent
> - **Gamma** (Cost 11): Personal computer with Gamma Agent

> [!equipment]- VR Interface (Ego Channel)
> **💰 Cost:** 3  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A virtual reality interface system that provides full virtuality access for non-Apotheosis users. The ego channel technology allows complete immersion in virtual environments and digital spaces that would normally require Apotheosis augmentation.

## 🏭 Manufacturing

> [!equipment]- Compiler (Home)
> **💰 Cost:** 6  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A home-scale nanomachine assembler capable of creating simple objects and materials from raw components. The system is Coalition-locked to prevent manufacturing of restricted items or weapons.

> [!equipment]- Compiler (Industrial)
> **💰 Cost:** 12  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
> 
> A large-scale industrial nanomachine assembler capable of manufacturing complex equipment, vehicles, and structures. The system remains Coalition-locked to prevent unauthorized production of restricted materials.

## 🏕️ Survival & Exploration

> [!equipment]- Aerial Condenser
> **💰 Cost:** 2  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A portable atmospheric water extraction device that condenses moisture from the air to produce clean drinking water. The condenser can produce approximately one gallon of water every three hours, making it invaluable for survival in arid environments.

> **💰 Cost:** 4  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> An advanced mapping system that fires sensor projectiles to scan unknown areas and create detailed real-time 3D maps. The ballistic sensors can penetrate difficult terrain and provide comprehensive environmental data for exploration and tactical planning.

> [!equipment]- Box Tent
> **💰 Cost:** 2  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A compact survival shelter that unfolds from a book-sized container into a fully functional tent for two people. The tent includes integrated climate control systems to maintain comfortable temperatures in any environment.

> [!equipment]- Fiberscope
> **💰 Cost:** 2  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A flexible fiber-optic surveillance device capable of multi-spectrum imaging and audio recording. The fiberscope can be threaded through small spaces to observe areas without detection, making it ideal for reconnaissance and security applications.

> [!equipment]- 4D Spatial Camera
> **💰 Cost:** 3  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> 3D environment mapping with AR/VR display capabilities for comprehensive spatial documentation.

## 🎯 Weapon Accessories

> [!equipment]- Targeting Support Module
> **💰 Cost:** 4
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> A device installed onto a firearm that syncs with a Targeting Support System augmentation. When a target is selected, the guidance software kicks in and snaps the weapon on target, guiding the user's arm to keep the enemy in the crosshairs.
>
> **Mechanics:** Grants **+1 bonus on all ranged attacks** with firearms equipped with a synced targeting support module (requires the Targeting Support System augmentation). Also eliminates the need for a separate guidance system when firing guided ammo ("Blood-Hounds").

## 📋 Insurance & Templates

> [!equipment]- Resurrection Insurance
> **💰 Cost:** 3-11 (tiered by policy)
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
>
> Insurance policies for Apotheosized characters, providing backup storage and recovery services through your House. Each policy has a minimum Rep-Rating required for purchase and a one-time Fee Cost. If your Rep-Rating falls below the minimum, you are automatically downgraded with no reimbursement.
>
> **Policies:**
> - **Alpha** (Cost 3, Min Rep 3, Fee 4): If your core is delivered to a House resleeving facility, you will be brought online in a virtuality to discuss resleeving options
> - **Beta** (Cost 6, Min Rep 5, Fee 6): Once death is confirmed or you have been missing for 3 months, the House sends a crew to recover your core (or restores from backup if location unknown)
> - **Gamma** (Cost 8, Min Rep 7, Fee 8): House sends a recovery team if you have been missing for 1 week. A crew of mercenaries is dispatched if your core is lost in a hostile environment
> - **Delta** (Cost 11, Min Rep 9, Fee 11): Like Gamma, but the recovery team is composed of professionals and/or a Praetorian
> - **Epsilon** (Cost 14, Min Rep 11, Fee 14): The House spares no expense — full military units or multiple Praetorians are dispatched
>
> All policies grant a single backup at purchase. Additional backups can be made once per week at the Fee Cost.

> [!equipment]- Compiler Template
> **💰 Cost:** Item Cost +2
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
>
> A piece of software that functions as the instructions for a compiler to build a specific object. The Cost of a template equals the Cost of the item it produces +2. If you purchase a template during character creation, you begin play with up to 3 copies of the compiled item for free.

## 💻 Virtual & Simulated

> [!equipment]- Simulated Augmentation
> **💰 Cost:** Special
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
>
> When entering a virtuality, the brain is scanned to create your avatar and determine its capabilities, including your Augmentations. SIMs, who lack physical forms, can purchase virtual Augmentations that work just like real Augmentations inside VR. To purchase a virtual aug, buy it like any other augmentation with total Cost reduced by -1. Non-SIMs can also purchase virtual-only versions, but they still count against total aug limits. SIMs have Apotheosis as a simulated Augmentation for free. HPA limitations on aug use do not apply to virtualities.

> [!equipment]- Simulated Memory (Sensie)
> **💰 Cost:** 2+
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
>
> Recorded memories of another person, captured by a mindset or an ego channel and downloaded onto a memory module. Many people — even celebrities — give away sensies for free, although some can be very expensive depending on what they depict. Sensies of recorded sex, crimes, and even suicide are popular underground items.
