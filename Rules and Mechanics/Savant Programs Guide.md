# Nova Praxis Savant Programs Guide

Savant Programs are unique abilities accessible to **Savants**, who are rare individuals that have managed to **hack their own mindsets**, stripping them of Coalition-mandated restrictions imposed by the Humanity Preservation Act. This dangerous process grants them the ability to interface with computer systems in ways a normal person cannot.

The core of a Savant's abilities lies in their unique programming language called **Self-Interfacing Neural Code (SINC)**. When a Savant executes a program, their **Daemon** (an unrestricted Agent) unloads a barrage of SINC-based intrusion programs that flood the targeted system, briefly smashing through its security to seize control. This window of access is short-lived, usually lasting only a few seconds.

## Acquiring Savant Programs

* Before gaining any Savant Programs, a character **must first purchase the Savant Stunt**. This stunt grants a SINC Skill Rank of 1 and a new "Disadvantage" Aspect reflecting the negative side effects of hacking the mindset.
* Savant Programs can then be chosen like other Stunts during **character creation** or upon reaching a **Major Milestone**.
* SINC is a very personal part of the character, derived from and interfacing directly with their ego's code. It cannot be copied or traded, but it is backed up along with the ego. Savants can execute these programs as long as they are in a sleeve with the Apotheosis Augmentation or on a computer capable of hosting their ego as a SIM.

## Executing Savant Programs

To use a Savant Program, you must make a **SINC test** against the Program's **execution difficulty**.

* **Success**: The program activates and can be used.
* **Failure**: The program still activates, but you suffer an amount of **system stress** equal to the amount by which you failed.
* **Stall**: You suffer the system stress, and the program **fails to activate**.

Modern computer systems, being Mimir-tech devices, are highly robust and do not normally crash. However, they were not designed to run SINC or resist cyber-attacks from Savant Programs or CISTs, leading to instability when assaulted.

## Sample Savant Programs

### Access Memory

* **Execution Difficulty**: 3 + highest of target's SINC or S. Eng
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Allows you to hack into another person's **mnemonic core** and retrieve stored data, including records of thoughts and sensory data from past experiences.
  * If your execution test generates Spin, the target is unaware of the intrusion. If you succeed without Spin, the target's Agent detects the intrusion.
  * **Success**: Gain a single-word answer to a question.
  * **Spin**: Gain a few sentences, or experience an entire scene the target was present for.
  * **5+ Shifts**: Learn almost any fact the target knows or experience several days of their life.

### Augmented Unreality

* **Execution Difficulty**: 1 + highest of target's SINC or S. Eng
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Duration**: Permanent until dismissed
* **Description**: Hijacks the sensory data passing through your target's mindset (or host system), causing them to **see objects that aren't real**. These AR illusions are detailed enough to pass for real objects but do not make sounds. Legal systems normally restrict realistic AR displays, making this ability very useful. The illusion moves at any speed but disappears if you lose sight of it.
  * **Stall**: Target becomes aware of the intrusion attempt.
  * **Detection**: Observers can attempt a Perception test to pierce the illusion. Difficulty = 2 + Shifts generated on execution. Moving illusions grant observers +2 on the detection test. Illusions larger than human-sized (Size 0) suffer -1 to the detection difficulty per Size category above 0.

### Blackout

* **Execution Difficulty**: 3 + highest of target's SINC or S. Eng
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Interferes with the interface between your target's sleeve and mindset (or ego and host system), causing a disruption that temporarily **blinds the victim**. The target must have a mindset or be a SIM.
  * **Successful Execution**: Places the **Persistent Aspect Blind(P)** on your target for a duration equal to the number of Shifts generated (e.g., 2 Shifts for a scene).
* **Upgrades**:
  * **Oubliette**: Also severs the target's sense of hearing, applying **Blind and Deaf(P)**.
  * **Lock Down**: Increase difficulty by +2 to **freeze the target in place**. If successful, the target cannot take physical actions and gains the **Frozen(P)** Aspect until the end of the scene. Target can break free by spending a Fate Point and rolling SINC or Software Engineering against your SINC.

### Delve

* **Execution Difficulty**: 2 + highest of target's SINC or S. Eng
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Allows you to crack the security that hides additional facts about a person's CID. It can grant access to personal messages, Rep-logs, and other information.
  * **Successful Execution**: Use your SINC (with a +2 bonus) to perform a **Declaration** or **Assessment** on a person with a CID.
  * Immediately determines if a CID is fake.

### Eminent Domain

* **Execution Difficulty**: 3+, depending on circumstance
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Duration**: One Round or One Use
* **Description**: Briefly seizes control of computer-operated machines in your environment, forcing them to obey your commands (e.g., unlock doors, fire sentry guns, surge gravity generators).
  * When activated, you can make a **Declaration** about the computer systems in the area, potentially creating an Aspect like *Zero-G* or *Lights Out!*.
  * Overriding compiler restrictions increases difficulty by +3.
  * Effects are fleeting, lasting only a single round (or one use) before security AI shuts you out.

### Esper Lord

* **Execution Difficulty**: Varies depending on commands
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Interfaces your mind with a **swarm of nanomachines (espers)**, allowing real-time thought control. The swarm (free with the stunt) consists of millions of microscopic machines that can arrange themselves into nearly any shape (mist, liquids, solid objects like walls, blades, or clothing). The swarm must stay within 20 meters and line of sight. Each command requires an execution of the program.
  * **Attack**:
    * **Slam (1)**: SINC attack with **WR +4**, Aspect *Smashing Force*.
    * **Stab/Slash (2)**: SINC attack with **WR +6**, Aspect *Razor Sharp Blades*.
    * **Grapple (1)**: Attempts a Block Action to restrain, or push, pull, or throw an opponent (ranged versions possible). Uses SINC instead of usual skill.
    * **Devour (3)**: SINC attack with **WR +4**, **ignores Armor Rating**, Aspect *Devouring Nanomachines*.
  * **Defense**: Swarm interposes itself, configuring into a rigid form.
    * **Defend (1)**: Use SINC for defense against physical attacks (Instant Action).
    * **Block (2)**: Swarm creates a protective barrier; use SINC for Block Action. Can cover other people (+1 difficulty per person) or an entire zone (+2 difficulty).
  * **Perform Maneuver**: Use swarm to place an Aspect on yourself or others. Execution difficulty is 2, but may increase for complex Maneuvers.
  * **Templating**: Swarm assumes shape of any object you have a template for (Cost 2 or less, cannot mimic electronics or powered objects). Execution difficulty equals item's Cost (min 2).
  * **Manipulation**: Swarm manipulates an object. Execution difficulty 1. Swarm has Athletics equal to your SINC.
  * **Damaging the Swarm**: Swarm has Physical Skills equal to your SINC +2. It can suffer "Deteriorations" (ratings 2, 4, 6) similar to Consequences, which increase execution difficulty for swarm actions.
* **Upgrades**:
  * **Area Attack**: Attack everyone in a zone; increases execution difficulty by +2. Separate attack roll for each target.
  * **Armor (2)**: Swarm forms a suit of armor with **AR equal to SINC rating +2**.
  * **Deadly Swarm**: +2 bonus on attack and grapple tests by your swarm.
  * **Manipulative**: SINC is considered 2 ranks higher for object manipulation or grapple attacks.
  * **Sensory Feed (3)**: Swarm sends audio/video data back to you. Range increased to 1 km.
  * **Versatile Armor**: Requires Armor Upgrade. Armor gains any two features: Enhanced Mobility Actuators, High Density Plating, Hydrostatic Gel Layer, Integrated Weaponry (Mono-Edge Shortsword), Reinforced Cyber-Musculature, or Vacuum Sealed and Shielded.

### Memory Hack

* **Execution Difficulty**: 3 + highest of target's SINC or S. Eng
* **Activation Time**: Extended (One hour per test)
* **Description**: Edits memories stored on a target's mnemonic core. This has no immediate effect on the target, but altered memories will be present if they are restored from the hacked backup. Requires constant access to the target's core/mindset.
  * **Success**: Erase events of a single, minor scene.
  * **Spin**: Erase about a day, or an important scene.
  * **5+ Shifts**: Erase about a month, or a very important week.
  * Memories must be in a single continuous period.
  * Can replace removed memories with new ones, which remain unquestioned unless their false nature is drawn attention to. Victim can make a Cohesion test to identify false memories.
* **Upgrades**:
  * **Flash Memory (+2 difficulty)**: Edits memories in real-time, affecting both the "live" ego and backup. Requires only a Simple Action. Causes Mild Mental Consequence on success, Moderate on Spin, Severe on 5+ Shifts.

### Oracle Patterning

* **Execution Difficulty**: 4 (Potentially modified by Mesh Aspects)
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Your Daemon gathers and processes data (security logs, public/private records, psychological profiles, etc.) from the mesh and Extranet to **predict future events**. Denser local meshes increase accuracy.
  * **GM should ask what player wants to know about coming events**. More specific questions and more Shifts yield more accurate information.
  * This is the *most likely* future, but unknown factors can still play a part.
* **Mesh Aspects**: Affect execution difficulty (e.g., *Unmeshed(P)*, *Low Density Mesh*, *Standard Density Mesh*, *High Density Mesh(P)*).

### Overload

* **Execution Difficulty**: 3 + highest of target's SINC or S. Eng
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Duration**: Scene, or until awakened
* **Description**: Causes the target's mindset (or host system) to discharge corrupt sensory data into their ego, strong enough to **knock them unconscious or freeze them** if they are a SIM. Target must have a mindset or be a SIM.
  * Unconscious/frozen victims can spend a Fate Point each round for a Cohesion test (Diff 2) to rouse themselves, or without an FP if attacked/shaken.
* **Upgrades**:
  * **Power Surge**: Trigger fluctuations in powered objects you can see. Attack with SINC. Drones, cybersleeves, and vehicles suffer WR +6 (defend with Endurance or Systems, adding Size, armor does not apply). Weapons, armor, and cybernetic augs cease to function for 1 round and cause WR +3 attack (defend with Endurance, AR applies if not the targeted armor itself).

### Pattern Scan

* **Execution Difficulty**: 4 + Intel Modifiers (Potentially modified by Mesh Aspects)
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Your Daemon launches queries to obtain data about specific past events (security logs, video feeds, blogs, etc.) and pieces together the most likely course of events.
  * **Successful Execution**: Daemon compiles data to show a specific moment in the past related to a person, place, or thing.
  * **More Specific Question/More Shifts**: Prompts more accurate information from the GM.
  * **Failure (no Stall) or 0 Shifts**: Review only a few seconds, results may be unreliable.
  * **1 Shift**: See up to a minute, pretty close to accurate.
  * **Spin**: See a whole scene, accurate.

### Puppeteer

* **Execution Difficulty**: 2, or Drone's Resolve +1
* **Duration**: Until cancelled or 1 round
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Master the ability to **possess a drone**, passing your awareness into it and sensing what it senses.
  * **Owned Drone (full access)**: Execution Difficulty 2, possess for duration of the scene.
  * **Unowned Drone**: Execution Difficulty equals drone's Resolve +1, possess for a single round (can immediately attempt to re-execute as a Free Action).
  * **Active Control**: Drone gains a +2 bonus on all Skill tests (instead of normal +1). You view from the drone's perspective, unable to see or hear from your own body.
  * **Successful Possession**: Gain 2 temporary Fate Points, usable only to invoke the drone's Aspects (disappear if unused after exiting drone).
* **Upgrades**:
  * **Drone Lord**: Possess owned drones without execution test.
  * **Tenacious Master**: +2 bonus on tests to maintain possession of unowned drones.

### Security Skim

* **Execution Difficulty**: 2 + Intel Modifiers (Potentially modified by Mesh Aspects)
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Duration**: Scene
* **Description**: Your Daemon queries security systems to obtain data and view targets through their local mesh sensors. Easier to find well-known targets.
  * **Execution Test**: SINC test (Diff 2) + single most appropriate Familiarity and Link Modifier from Intel Modifiers table. Denser meshes yield better results.
  * **Located Target**: Know exact location relative to yourself. Can "drift away" by hopping from feed to feed, viewing areas up to (SINC +1) x 100 meters from the original target.
  * **Intel Modifiers Table**: Provides modifiers based on familiarity with Person, Thing, Place, or Event.

### Stream of Consciousness

* **Execution Difficulty**: 2 + highest of target's SINC or S. Eng
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Duration**: One round or scene
* **Description**: Uploads a virus into the target's mindset, allowing you to **listen in on their surface thoughts** for a short time.
  * **Successful Execution**: Hear target's next few surface thoughts.
  * **Spin**: Hear target's thoughts for the rest of the scene.
  * **Stall**: Target's Agent senses intrusion and can inform target of general direction.
  * May discover one of the target's Aspects if relevant to current thoughts, which can be invoked/compelled for free the first time.
* **Upgrades**:
  * **Voyeur**: Never alerts target even on Stall. +1 bonus on execution tests when targeting someone used on before.

### System Error

* **Execution Difficulty**: Highest of target's SINC or S. Engineering
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Your Daemon overwhelms the target system with intrusion attempts, causing it to become unstable, lock up, or shut down. Used against those with a System Stress Track or active cybernetic Augmentations. Can also be used against machines such as drones.
  * **Choose one of three effects**:
    * **System Attack**: If target is another Savant, deal System stress equal to (Shifts generated +2).
    * **Ego Strike**: Direct attack against an Apotheosized ego (mindset or SIM). Target suffers Mental stress equal to Shifts generated.
    * **Hardware Failure**: Causes a cybernetic Augmentation with an active function (e.g., cybernetic limb, implanted weapon) to become damaged or stop functioning. Has no effect on passive augmentations (e.g., subdermal armor). Can also be used against machines and drones. Target suffers Physical stress equal to Shifts generated; armor does not help. Any Consequence should relate to the Augmentation, which will not function until the Consequence is cleared.

### System Invasion

* **Execution Difficulty**: 4 or 3 + highest of target's SINC or S. Eng
* **Activation Time**: Simple Action
* **Description**: Launches a direct attack against another system to **seize control** of it. Can control computer systems, drones, and even Apotheosized people.
  * **Apotheosized Person or Drone (Success)**: Implant a single-word command (e.g., "Flee!"). Target obeys until end of scene.
  * **Computer System (Success)**: Gain limited access to file system (view files available to authorized user).
  * **Spin (Drone)**: Implant a simple sentence command (e.g., "Go stand in the corner"). Target obeys for an hour.
  * **Spin (Computer System)**: View any authorized files and gain writing privileges (cannot edit existing Mimir-tech OS core files).
  * **5+ Shifts (Drone)**: Implant a multi-sentence command (series of steps). Target obeys for up to a week.
  * **5+ Shifts (Computer System)**: Gain complete control of the system (cannot modify Mimir-tech OS core).
* **Upgrades**:
  * **Usurper**: Force Apotheosized minds to obey same commands as drones. Execution difficulty based on higher of target's Cohesion, S.Eng, or SINC.

## Savant-Related Stunts

### Savant (Prerequisite Stunt)

* **Requires**: SIM State Stunt or Apotheosis Augmentation
* **Effect**: Grants a SINC Skill Rank of 1 and one free Savant Program of your choice. You may now purchase additional Savant Programs with Stunts gained during character creation or from Major Milestones.
* **Disadvantage Aspect**: You also gain a new "Disadvantage" Aspect representing the negative side effects of hacking the mindset (e.g., *Glitchy Short-Term Memory*, *Occasional Uncontrollable Muscle Spasms*, *Left Eye Severed from Visual Cortex*). This damage is repairable: at any Milestone, you may reduce a Skill of your choice by -1 (not below 0) to remove this Aspect.

### Specialized Program

* **Effect**: Select one Savant Program you know. You gain a **+2 bonus on execution tests** for that Program.

### System Slip

* **Effect**: You gain **+3 on Stealth tests** to avoid being detected on a computer system. In addition, you increase the **execution difficulty of any Savant Program directed at you by +2**.
