# Augmentations

A comprehensive reference for all augmentations in Nova Praxis.

---

## ⭐ Special Augmentations

> [!aug]- Apotheosis
> **💎 AV:** 3 | **🎁 Free With:** Sleeve purchase | **🔒 Only For:** Sleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
>
> Required for sleeves; grants mindset and mnemonic core. Your sleeve has undergone Apotheosis and features a mindset and mnemonic core, granting the Apotheosized Aspect.

> [!aug]- Fully Synthetic
> **💎 AV:** N/A | **🔒 Only For:** Cybersleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
>
> Sleeve is completely synthetic with no biological components. Gains Synthetic Biology(P) Aspect.

> [!aug]- Cosmetic Customization
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
>
> Custom features like crystalline canines or glowing eyes, primarily cosmetic. Gains an Aspect describing the modification(s). Purchasing more than once for a human body or biosleeve violates the HPA.

> [!aug]- Striking Looks
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
>
> Sleeve is exceptionally attractive. Gains Striking Looks Aspect. A cybersleeve must have Bio-Skin Sheath with the Seemingly Real upgrade to take this Augmentation.

> [!aug]- Bio-Skin Sheath
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Cybersleeve, Sleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
>
> Natural skin-like covering for cybernetic limbs or sleeves. When used to fully sheath a cybersleeve, the Synthetic Biology Aspect is no longer Persistent.

> [!aug]- Optimized Power System
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Cybersleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No | **⬆️ Increases Max Aug Count By:** +1
>
> Top-of-the-line power management for cyberware efficiency. Once per scene, you may invoke an Aspect gained from a cyberware Augmentation without spending a Fate Point.

> [!aug]- Exceptional Genegineering
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Biosleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No | **⬆️ Increases Max Aug Count By:** +1
>
> Enhanced biological design providing superior performance. Once per scene, you may invoke an Aspect gained from a bioware Augmentation without spending a Fate Point.

## 💪 Physical Enhancement

> [!aug]- Adrenal Boost Module
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
>
> Trigger adrenaline surge for enhanced athletics and combat. Activate as Free Action: +1 Athletics and Melee Skills, +1 stress absorbed by Physical Consequences. Lasts a number of rounds equal to your Endurance, after which you gain the Got the Shakes Aspect until end of scene.

> [!aug]- Fibro-Muscle Weave
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
>
> Synthetic fibers woven into muscles for superhuman strength. Grants Fibro-Muscle Weave Aspect. Can be invoked on Athletics tests and on attack tests with melee/thrown weapons (only after a hit is confirmed).

> [!aug]- Reinforced Skeleton
> **💎 AV:** 2
>
> Ceramics and metals reinforce bones. Severe/Extreme Physical Consequences absorb +3 stress.

> [!aug]- Skinweave Armor
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> Spider silk/graphene mesh woven into skin. +1 Armor Rating (stacks with other armor).

> [!aug]- Subdermal Plating
> **💎 AV:** 2
>
> Thin flexible armor beneath skin. Mild/Moderate Physical Consequences absorb +2 stress.

> [!aug]- Synthesis
> **💎 AV:** 3 | **🔒 Only For:** Biosleeve | **⚡ Requires Apotheosis:** Yes
>
> Nanocybernetic fibers woven through tissue; pinnacle of evolution. Gains the Synthesized Biological Perfection Aspect. Immune to poisons and disease. No longer adversely affected by prolonged exposure to microgravity. As long as you are within range of a broadcast power field, you only need to eat, drink, and sleep half as much as a normal person.

> [!aug]- Neura-Surge
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> Overclocks nerves/muscles for instant action. Activating is an Instant Action that may interrupt another character's turn; once activated, you may immediately perform a Simple Action (max once per turn). Requires an Endurance test (Difficulty 4) to activate. On failure, the neura-surge still activates but you suffer physical stress equal to the margin of failure. Activation difficulty increases by +1 for each subsequent use in the same scene.

> [!aug]- Performance Enhanced
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Sleeve
>
> Sleeve quality boost: +1 rank in chosen Physical Skill (may exceed the normal max of 5). Can be purchased up to three times, each time enhancing a different Skill.

> [!aug]- Alternative Mobility System
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **⚠️ HPA Violation on Biosleeve**
>
> Legs replaced or augmented with non-humanoid limbs. Gains a Persistent Aspect describing your movement type (e.g. Wheeled(P), Cyberspider Legs(P), Snake-Like Lower Body(P), Tracked Mobility(P)).

> [!aug]- Extra Limb
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **⚠️ Restricted on Biosleeves (HPA Violation)**
>
> One or more additional prehensile limbs via extreme genetic tampering or grafted cybernetics. Gains a Persistent Aspect describing the limb (e.g. Prehensile Tail(P), Cybernetic Tentacle(P)). Can be invoked for grappling, climbing, running, or tasks where an extra hand is useful.

> [!aug]- Gyroscopic Stabilizers
> **💎 AV:** 2
>
> Wrists and elbows outfitted with small gyroscopes for hand stabilization. Gains the Gyro-Stabilized Aspect. Can be invoked for delicate tasks, full-auto attack tests, or when placing the In My Sights Aspect when aiming.

> [!aug]- Massive Size
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **🔒 Only For:** Sleeve | **⚠️ Restricted on Biosleeves (HPA Violation)**
>
> Sleeve towers 3–4 meters in height. Size 1; gains +1 bonus to WR of all melee weapon attacks. Gains the Massive Aspect.

> [!aug]- Nano-Cyber Limb
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
>
> A limb replaced with a nanocybernetic prosthetic (non-magnetic metal skeleton, artificial muscles). Limited tactile sensation but stronger and more versatile. Gains an Aspect (e.g. Cyber-Arm, Shiny New Leg). Nano-cyber limbs can support other augs such as Fibro-Muscle Weaves or Esper Vents.

> [!aug]- Tiny Size
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **⚠️ Restricted on Biosleeves (HPA Violation)**
>
> Sleeve is very small, less than a meter in height. Size -1; suffers -1 penalty to WR of all unarmed/melee weapon attacks. Gains +1 bonus on all Stealth tests and Athletics-based defense tests. Gains the Tiny Aspect.

## 👁️ Sensory Enhancement

> [!aug]- Auditory Amp and Filter Module
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> Enhanced hearing and background noise filtering. Gains the Auditory Amplifier and Filtering Module(P) Persistent Aspect.

> [!aug]- Beholder Sensory Suite
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **⚡ Requires Apotheosis:** Yes
>
> Distributed sensors provide 360° vision. Gains the Beholder Sensory Suite(P) Persistent Aspect.

> [!aug]- Particle Detection and Analysis Suite
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> Heightened sense of smell so acute you can track by scent, plus chemical analysis output to PPC or mindset to determine gas composition. Gains the Particle Detection and Analysis Module Aspect.

> [!aug]- Pheromone Control & Sensitivity
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
>
> Emit desired pheromones to subtly influence others' emotions; actively sense others' pheromones. Once per scene, gain +2 bonus on any test to convince a biological being or determine another person's emotional state.

> [!aug]- Retinal Implant
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> Nanocybernetic eye with zoom and enhanced low-light detail. Gains the Retinal Implant Aspect. Can pair with other computers to function as an entoptic display and AR interface.

> [!aug]- SMR Transceiver and Translation Suite
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> Sub-millimeter radiation sensors allow seeing through materials (clothing, paper, wood, masonry, plastic, ceramics). Cannot penetrate skin, water, or metal. Poorer resolution than normal sight.

> [!aug]- Sonar Reception Array
> **💎 AV:** 2
>
> Audio-based echolocation feeds visual cortex for real-time sonar. Allows seeing in the dark and aids perception of light-screened objects. Gains the Sonar Reception Array(P) Persistent Aspect.

## 🥷 Stealth & Combat

> [!aug]- Hidden Weapon System
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> Built-in hidden weapon mount (blade or firearm). Weapon must be acquired separately. May be purchased multiple times if GM agrees there is room.

> [!aug]- Light Screen Camouflage
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> Magnetic field generators bend light for near-invisibility. +3 Stealth when not moving, +2 when moving.

> [!aug]- Poison Gland
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> Produces toxin (commonly in saliva). Basic: two uses/day, Potency 4.

> [!aug]- Retractable Claws
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> Razor-sharp catlike claws. Weapon Rating of 2. If you also have the Poison Gland Augmentation, you can administer poison with a claw attack instead of a bite or kiss.

> [!aug]- Targeting Support System
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> Sensors in hand, arm, and mindset sync with a targeting support module (Restricted, Cost 4 — a separate hardware item installed on the firearm) to enhance aim. +1 on all ranged attacks with firearms equipped with a synced targeting support module. Also eliminates the need for a blood-hound guidance system on weapons firing guided ammo.

> [!aug]- Stillness Body Lock System
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> Nanocybernetically modified joints can be selectively locked at will; includes an implant that stores oxygen for extended breath-holding. Popular with precision shooters and the poser scene. Gains the Stillness Body Lock System Aspect.

## 🔧 Technology Integration

> [!aug]- Cogware Partition
> **💎 AV:** 3 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes | **⚡ Requires Apotheosis:** Yes
>
> Mindset partition for streaming temporary skills and knowledge via cogware. Loading cogware is a Simple Action (Difficulty 4 Cohesion test); on failure the cogware loads but you suffer mental stress equal to the margin of failure; on a Stall you suffer the stress and it fails to load. Duration: Scene. Loading provides an Aspect representative of the cogware (e.g. Kung-fu Master, Shuttle Pilot, Brain Surgeon). Standard cogware provides a standard Aspect (Cost 4); high-quality cogware provides a Persistent Aspect (Cost 8). Both types are restricted. You receive one piece of standard cogware free with purchase. Only one piece of cogware may be loaded at a time; cogware must be purchased separately.

> [!aug]- Crypto-Laser Link
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> IR laser transceiver somewhere on your sleeve. When aimed at another person's crypto-laser transceiver, enables encrypted, invisible (to the naked eye), and silent wireless communication. With a mindset, send/receive via mental commands; otherwise route through a PPC.

> [!aug]- Custom Built
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Sleeve | **⚡ Requires Apotheosis:** Yes
>
> Sleeve custom-built for a specific task or environment (e.g. high-gravity worlds, harsh climates, stealth, intimidation). Gains an Aspect describing the specialization.

> [!aug]- Data Jack and Storage
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🎁 Free With:** Apotheosis
>
> Wired interface to external systems and storage. Port connects to internal data storage; with a mindset, can allow read/write access to a partition.

> [!aug]- Esper Vent
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> Parts of sleeve anatomy replaced with hollow synthetic replacements to store an esper swarm. Vents allow the swarm to emerge from the body. Commonly placed in arms with muscles replaced by nanocybernetic fiber bundles.

> [!aug]- Smuggler's Compartment
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
>
> Hidden compartment in your body (thigh, torso, etc.) capable of storing items about the size of a fist or small pistol. Disguised and insulated; will not set off metal detectors. Contents still visible to X-Ray scanners unless outfitted with a Charlatan upgrade.

> [!aug]- SIM-Cell Module
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **⚡ Requires Apotheosis:** Yes
>
> Mindset partition and second mnemonic core to host another ego. The SIM can dwell in its home environment or view the real world through the host's senses.

> [!aug]- Physiological Behavior Mods
> **💎 AV:** 1
>
> Genetic modifications triggering specific emotional responses (desire to bond, urge to hunt, overactive libido, etc.). Gains an Aspect reflecting the behavior (e.g. Pack Mentality, Insatiable Curiosity, Solitary Predator).

## 🏥 Medical Enhancement

> [!aug]- Arterial Toxin Filter
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
>
> Heart filter cleanses toxins/bacteria, aids resistance and healing. Gains the Arterial Toxin Filter(P) Persistent Aspect.

> [!aug]- ELS System
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
>
> Emergency life support auto-activates under lethal threat. Provides up to 3 hours without oxygen, hydration, vitamins/minerals, and painkillers (12 hours — Severe Physical Consequence loses Persistent during this time). Prevents poison attacks for 12 hours. Cannot be used again for 48 hours. AV reduced to 1 if you have Synthesis.

> [!aug]- Gills Gene-Graft
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
>
> Fish-like gills allow oxygen extraction from water. Technically violates HPA but was granted an exception.

> [!aug]- Medichine Factory
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
>
> Nanomachine factory aids natural healing. Reduces time for Physical Consequences to degrade by 1 time increment.

## Upgrade Paths

Upgrade stunts that enhance base augmentations (each adds to the base AV):

> [!upgrade]- Fibro-Muscle Weave Upgrades
> - **Titan Weave (+2):** Muscles more synthetic than biological. Fibro-Muscle Weave Aspect becomes Persistent.

> [!upgrade]- Subdermal Plating Upgrades
> - **Composite Plating (+1):** Reinforced with lightweight composite materials. Mild/Moderate Consequences absorb +3 stress instead of +2.

> [!upgrade]- Poison Gland Upgrades
> - **Lethal Dose (+1):** Poison Potency increases to 6.
> - **Increased Production (+1):** Produce four uses per day instead of two.

> [!upgrade]- Beholder Sensory Suite Upgrades
> - **Hyper-Aware (+1):** Enhanced interface for noticing small details. +3 bonus on initiative tests in physical conflicts (as long as you can see the threat).

> [!upgrade]- Bio-Skin Sheath Upgrades
> - **Seemingly Real (+1) (Restricted):** Bio-skin appears as real human skin. Sleeve loses the Inhuman Appearance Aspect; indistinguishable from a living human to naked eye and SMR transceiver. X-rays can still penetrate.
> - **Charlatan (+2) (Restricted):** See Charlatan entry.

> [!upgrade]- Striking Looks Upgrades
> - **Absolutely Stunning (+1):** Almost unnaturally attractive. Gains the Persistent Aspect Absolutely Stunning(P) instead of the standard Aspect.

> [!upgrade]- Performance Enhanced Upgrades
> - **Top of the Line (+2):** Sleeve gains an additional +1 bonus rank (to a max of 7) in a Skill that already has a bonus from Performance Enhanced.

> [!upgrade]- Alternative Mobility System Upgrades
> - **Flight Capable (+2):** Thrusters, wings, or similar addition renders you capable of flight at about twice average human running speed. Gains a Persistent Aspect describing the flight method. +3 bonus to Hustle, Run, or related tests.

> [!upgrade]- Nano-Cyber Limb Upgrades
> - **Bioskinned (+1):** Limb covered in bioskin for greater tactile sensitivity. May appear natural or obviously synthetic.
> - **Multiple Limbs (+1):** Two limbs of the same type replaced. Gains a Persistent Aspect (e.g. Nanocybernetic Arms(P)) instead of a standard Aspect.

> [!upgrade]- Cogware Partition Upgrades
> - **Multi-Partition Cog-Drive (+2):** Mindset can load two pieces of cogware at once (Execution Difficulty 6).

> [!upgrade]- Pheromone Control & Sensitivity Upgrades
> - **Aura (+1):** Emit high levels of pheromones affecting everyone around you. Spend a Fate Point to gain +1 on all Diplomacy or Guile tests/contests with biological beings for the scene. If you have Exceptional Genegineering, you may use it to activate the aura for free.

> [!upgrade]- Particle Detection and Analysis Suite Upgrades
> - **Integrated Filter (+1):** Filters out harmful gasses, making you immune to hazardous gas exposure. Also sterilizes air to kill airborne diseases.

> [!upgrade]- Smuggler's Compartment Upgrades
> - **Charlatan (+1) (Restricted):** Light screen tech fools X-ray, SMR, and radiation-based sensors, showing a hologram of what should be there. Does not fool the naked eye.

> [!upgrade]- Esper Vent Upgrades
> - **Charlatan (+1) (Restricted):** See Charlatan entry.

> [!upgrade]- Hidden Weapon System Upgrades
> - **Charlatan (+1) (Restricted):** See Charlatan entry.

> [!upgrade]- Retinal Implant Upgrades
> - **Drone (+1):** Eye detaches and flies as a small drone (Athletics 2, Perception 2). While active, you can see through both the drone's eye and your own (gaining the Distracted(P) Aspect while doing so).
> - **X-Ray Sight (+2):** Emits X-Rays to see through most objects. Spend a Fate Point to see through walls, clothing, doors, etc. (~1 ft of material, varies by density). Gains X-Ray Vision(P) Persistent Aspect when activated. Activation: Simple Action, Duration: Scene.

> [!upgrade]- Fully Synthetic Upgrades
> - **Reinforced Substrate (+1 to Sleeve Cost):** Reinforced structural members and robust joints. Endurance +2, but gains Slow and Heavy(P) Persistent Aspect.
> - **High Tensile Muscular Fibers (+2 to Sleeve Cost):** Synthetic muscles much stronger than normal; gains same benefits as Fibro-Muscle Weave.
