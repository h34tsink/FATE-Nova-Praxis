# General Aesthetics

- **♂ Masculine**: Traditional masculine appearance or traits.
- **⚨, ⚬ Androgynous**: A blend or ambiguity of masculine and feminine characteristics.
- **♀ Feminine**: Traditional feminine appearance or traits.

## Specific Aesthetic Options

- **☍ Other Gender**: Represents genders outside the traditional binary.
- **☿ Intersex / Hermaphrodite**: Characteristics of both male and female biology.
- **⚧ Transgender / Non-Binary**: Identifying as a different gender from birth or outside the gender binary.
- **⚲ Neutrois / No Gender**: No association with any gender.
