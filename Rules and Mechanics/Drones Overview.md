# Nova Praxis Drones Overview

Drones are a ubiquitous part of life in the **Coalition** and many **apostate enclaves** of _Nova Praxis_. These versatile machines are operated by sophisticated narrow AIs, but can also be remotely piloted by human users.

---

## Common Uses of Drones

- **Hazardous Jobs:**  
    Mining, search & rescue, exploration—if a job would kill a human, there’s a drone for it.
    
- **Security:**  
    Law enforcement models like the _Lonestar_ often work alongside Centurion forces.
    
- **Military Operations:**  
    Most military operations are conducted primarily by drones—sometimes exclusively.
    

> **Note:** While this overview focuses on human-sized drones, almost any vehicle (starship, warframe, etc.) can be fitted with an AI or remotely piloted. Distance introduces _lag_ (same penalties as SIM lag), which can impact performance at great distances.
>
> **System Attacks:** Savant Programs can target drone controllers, not just the drone itself. A Savant who successfully attacks a drone operator through the control link can disrupt the operator's connection or cause feedback stress.

---

## Controlling a Drone

- **Passive Control:**  
    Most drones run on narrow AI and respond to simple commands as a **Free Action**.
    
- **Active Control:**  
    Direct remote control (neural link, mindset, etc.) is a **Simple Action** and gives the drone a **+1 Skill bonus**.
    
- **Puppeteer Savant Program:**  
    When a Savant uses this program to _possess_ a drone:
    
    - **+2 Skill bonus**
        
    - User perceives only through the drone’s senses (no meatspace awareness)
        
    - Gain **2 temporary Fate Points** (usable only for the drone’s Aspects, lost when you exit the drone)
        

---

## Creating a Drone

Drones are “built” almost like cybersleeves, but with these crucial differences:

- **Extra Aspects:**  
    Drones get two additional Aspects (beyond _Synthetic Biology_ and _Inhuman Appearance_) to define purpose/function.
    
- **No Hostware:**  
    Drones cannot support a _hostware_ suite.
    
- **Reduced Cost:**  
    Cost is **1 rank less** than a comparable cybersleeve.
    
- **Legal Restrictions:**  
    Drones with restricted Augmentations require a license.
    
- **Skill Ranks:**
    
    - Drones get **2×** the Physical Skill Ranks of a comparable cybersleeve.
        
    - Allowed Skills: _Athletics, Endurance, Perception, Demolitions, Insight, Firearms, Melee, Pilot, Resolve, Stealth, Strategy_
        
    - **Max 5 ranks** in any Skill
        
- **Weapons & Armor:**
    
    - May be equipped with weapons up to **Strategy** rating (max weapon Cost = _Strategy +2_)
        
    - Armor Bonus: **Endurance +1**
        
- **Drone Jock Stunt:**
    
    - Cost reduced by -1
        
    - Two Skills (chosen by player) +1 rank each
        
    - +1 Augmentation Point
        
- **Fate Points:**
    
    - Drones don’t normally have Fate Points
        
    - User can spend their own Fate Points to invoke the drone’s Aspects
        
    - _Optimized Power System_ Augmentation may enable extra invocations
        

---

## Drone-Specific Augmentations

- **Very Tiny Size (AV 0):**
    
    - Size: -2
        
    - +2 bonus to Athletics-based defense
        
    - -1 Endurance
        
    - +1 Stealth
        
- **Armored (AV 2):**

    - +3 to natural Armor Rating

    - Cannot be taken more than once

    - Cannot be stacked or combined with Skinweave
        
- **Armed (AV 2):**
    
    - Carry 1 weapon (Cost ≤ drone’s Cost) or 2 additional weapons
        
    - Cannot be taken more than once
