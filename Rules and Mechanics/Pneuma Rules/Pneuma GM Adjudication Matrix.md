# Pneuma GM Adjudication Matrix

**Summary:** Fast ruling matrix for Pneuma actions in Nova Praxis FATE.

## Lore
Pneuma should feel precise, costly, and traceable. The matrix is for speed and consistency, not to replace fiction-first adjudication.

## Mechanics
### Action × Tag Matrix (default opposition)

| Tag | Overcome | Create Advantage | Attack | Defend |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Warp | H.Eng counter-lattice or environment obstacle | Place spatial aspect (*Sheared Lane*, *Local Grav Drift*) | Direct crush/shear vs Athletics/H.Eng | Cohesion or Athletics to slip vector |
| Kinesis | Athletics/H.Eng anchors | Place movement-control aspect (*Pinned Vector*) | Impact/throw vs Athletics/Melee | Athletics/Melee with positional fiction |
| Sensory | Perception/Insight to pierce | Place occlusion/emotion aspect | Usually indirect (setups) unless severe sensory shock | Perception/Insight/Resolve |
| Discharge | H.Eng/S.Eng hardening obstacle | Place arcing or EM-denial aspect | Direct energy attack vs H.Eng/S.Eng | H.Eng/S.Eng/Cohesion if artifact-mediated |
| Temporal | Insight/Strategy timing obstacle | Place tempo aspect (*Out of Phase*) | Rare direct attack; normally setup then strike | Insight/Strategy/Cohesion |
| Schema | S.Eng/Mnemonics gate obstacle | Place mesh/control aspect | Programmatic strike vs S.Eng/Mnemonics | S.Eng/Mnemonics/Resolve |

### Cost & Risk Defaults
- Use canonical PL ladder: PL2/4/6/8.
- Stress cost by PL step above 2.
- Overchannel check: Cohesion or Resolve vs PL+2.
- On tie/fail overchannel, assign Glitch/feedback consequence immediately.

### Consequence Tier by Outcome
- **Minor miss/tie:** temporary glitch aspect
- **Moderate fail:** mild or moderate feedback consequence
- **Severe fail at PL8/public:** severe feedback + HPA clock tick

### Signature & Attention
- Private/subtle use: usually no campaign tick unless repeated.
- Public/high-profile use: tick HPA attention.
- Repeated same signature: watchers gain +2 on trace attempts.

### Speed Rule (table flow)
If uncertain between two skills:
1. Pick the skill that matches declared fiction.
2. If still tied, default to **Cohesion**.
3. Keep ruling and move; log edge case after scene.

## Continuity
- **Assumptions:** This matrix follows canonical Pneuma chapter and Nova Praxis skill names.
- **Drift Guard:** If a compendium uses different skill labels, canonical file overrides.
