# Pneuma Player Handout

**Summary:** Player-safe rules for using Pneuma at the table in Nova Praxis FATE.

## Lore
Pneuma is rare, dangerous access to reality’s interface. It is not free-form magic: each use is local, brief, and leaves a trace.

What players reliably know:
- Pneuma usually requires an attuned artifact or a rare Pneumatic savant.
- Cohesion matters most.
- Overuse causes backlash and attracts institutional attention.

## Mechanics
### 1) What skill do I roll?
- Usually **Cohesion**.
- You may use **S.Eng** or **H.Eng** when the action is explicitly schema/hardware-facing.

### 2) Choose your Fate action
- **Overcome** to bypass barriers.
- **Create Advantage** to create or lock scene aspects.
- **Attack** to directly apply harmful effect.
- **Defend** to resist incoming force/effects.

### 3) Choose Power Level
- **PL2** minor
- **PL4** significant
- **PL6** major
- **PL8** extreme

### 4) Pay cost
Mark stress on your Pneuma track (or artifact Stability) by PL step:
- PL2 = 0
- PL4 = 1
- PL6 = 2
- PL8 = 3

### 5) Overchannel risk
If you exceed your normal ceiling, make backlash check:
- Roll **Cohesion** (or **Resolve** to brace) vs **PL + 2**.
- Failure means consequence-level feedback.

### 6) Signature traces
Each use leaves a signature. If used publicly/often, HPA attention rises.

## Quick Actions by Tag
- **Warp:** gravity/space edits
- **Kinesis:** pushes, pulls, vectors
- **Sensory:** illusions/occlusion
- **Discharge:** energy/EMP
- **Temporal:** brief tempo shift
- **Schema:** software/mesh effects
