## Stunts

- **Savant** - Grants SINC rating of 1, allows selection of Savant Programs, imposes a "Disadvantage" Aspect
- **Scion of House Kimura** - +1 bonus to Notice or Knowledge (Software) tests; reduces cost of Agents, Computers & Software, Cyber Sleeves, Cyber Augmentations, SIM Augmentations, VR Software, and Lite Combat Frames by 1
- **Drone Bond** - Can spend Bennies (Fate Points) for any drones Sara directs or controls
- **Drone Jock** - Reduces cost of drones by 1, increases two drone skills by one step, grants drones 1 additional Augmentation Point
- **Eminent Domain** (Savant Program) - Temporary control over computer-controlled environment parts
- **System Slip** - +3 Stealth to avoid detection on computer systems; increases Savant Program execution difficulty against Sara by +2
