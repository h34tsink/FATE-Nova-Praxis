### Exoplanet Sanctuary Overview
- **Gravity:** 1.07G
- **Distance from Sol:** 70 Light Years
- **Established:** 78 PS (Post-Singularity)
- **Estimated Population:** 9.4 million

#### Notable Settlements/Locations

##### New Montreal (“The Fold”)
- **Nature:** The original settlement, a cluster of interconnected ships buried under sand for concealment.
- **Lifestyle:** Basic life support functions are maintained, with air supplied through disguised vents and food grown hydroponically.

##### Valhalla
- **Vision:** Planned as the first true [[Groups/Apostate]] city on the planet's surface.
- **Current State:** Consists of a cleared area with a few prefabricated shelters, representing the aspirations for a free and independent community.

##### Sanctuary’s Hydroponics Farms
- **Function:** A network of caves adapted to grow food, meeting the colony’s needs and generating surplus for trade.
- **Security:** These facilities are heavily guarded, with a shoot-first policy against intruders.

#### Sanctuary's Unique Characteristics
- **[[Groups/Apostate]] Colony:** Founded by apostates as a haven from Coalition control, not officially recognized by the Coalition.
- **Harsh Environment:** Covered in sand and sandstone, with extreme heat and frequent sandstorms, yet hospitable enough for human life without terraforming.
- **Strategic Concealment:** Initial settlement designed for stealth, with ships buried to avoid detection from orbit.

#### Sociopolitical Dynamics
- **Independence and Expansion:** The colony’s growth from a secretive underground settlement to proposed surface expansion reflects a desire for autonomy and growth.
- **Population Pressures:** Increasing population and limited space are causing tension between those favoring expansion and those prioritizing secrecy.

#### Current Issues and Activities
- **Vulcan Swarm Acquisition:** The colony's need for a Vulcan swarm to expand their living space has become a top priority, with both underground and surface factions vying for it.
- **Supply Challenges:** The ongoing need for supplies not producible locally has created a demand for better black market channels.
- **Security Concerns:** The near-fatal assault on the only savant capable of operating the colony's compiler raises fears of possible Coalition discovery or internal strife.
