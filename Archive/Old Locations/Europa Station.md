# Europa Station

Europa Station is a major Coalition installation in the Jovian sphere, used as a transport, logistics, and operations hub.

## Notes for Play

- **Function:** Transit + supply + security chokepoint.
- **Tension:** A perfect place for credentialed infiltration, “routine” maintenance cover, and quiet asset moves.
- **Fallout:** The station becomes politically reactive after a public incident—security posture hardens and blame seeks a target.
