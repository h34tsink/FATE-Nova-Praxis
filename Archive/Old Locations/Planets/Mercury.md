### Mercury Overview
- **Gravity:** 0.37G
- **Day Length:** 58.65 [[Earth]] days
- **Population:** 52,000

#### Notable Locations
- **Specular Spread:** Solar panel field and production ([[House Cipriani]])
- **Base 10:** Antimatter production ([[House Cipriani]])
- **Tellerus Fields:** Site of major battle between [[Earth]]'s governments and Coalition
- **Mercurial Gateway:** One of two jump rings in Sol system
- **Mercurian Tunnel System:** Connects habitats and facilities
- **Balor:** Surface habitat, vacation spot for workers

#### Notable Locations

##### Specular Spread
- **Description:** A vast solar panel field and production facility.
- **Ownership:** Operated by [[House Cipriani]], this location is pivotal for solar energy collection and processing.

##### Base 10
- **Nature:** A key antimatter production facility.
- **Ownership:** Also under the control of [[House Cipriani]], contributing significantly to the house's influence in energy production.

##### Tellerus Fields
- **Historical Significance:** The site of a major battle between [[Earth]]'s government remnants and the Coalition, marking a turning point in the Coalition's establishment.
- **Current State:** Now a landmark remembering the Coalition's rise to power.

##### Mercurial Gateway
- **Function:** One of two jump rings in the Sol system, crucial for interstellar travel and trade.
- **Strategic Importance:** Facilitates the movement of antimatter and other resources throughout the Coalition's territories.

##### Mercurian Tunnel System
- **Description:** An extensive network of tunnels connecting habitats and facilities on Mercury.
- **Utility:** Essential for transportation and logistics on the planet, also serves as a potential hideout for remnant supporters.

##### Balor
- **Nature:** A large surface habitat.
- **Purpose:** Serves as a recreational and vacation destination for workers at Mercury's various facilities.

#### Mercury's Role in the Coalition
- **Economic Powerhouse:** Primarily known for its antimatter production, which is central to the galaxy's energy needs. [[House Cipriani]]'s dominance here cements its role as a major power player.
- **Historical Importance:** The battles fought on Mercury, especially at Tellerus Fields, are key to understanding the Coalition's rise and the shifting dynamics of power.
- **Strategic Location:** The Mercurial Gateway's position makes Mercury a crucial node for interstellar transportation, impacting trade and military strategies within the Coalition.

#### Sociopolitical Dynamics
- **[[House Cipriani]]'s Influence:** The house's control over energy production and strategic facilities like Specular Spread and Base 10 underlines its significant influence in Coalition politics.
- **Remnant Forces and Secret Supporters:** Ongoing concerns about hidden remnant forces in the Mercurian Tunnel System reflect the underlying tensions and unresolved conflicts within the Coalition.

#### History
The Coalition, facing opposition from [[Earth]]'s governments, secured its position with the battle at Tellerus Fields. Mercury, crucial for antimatter production, was a key battleground. Despite resistance, the Coalition prevailed, and remnants of [[Earth]]'s forces retreated into the Mercurian tunnels.

#### Current Affairs
[[House Cipriani]] now controls most antimatter facilities, making Mercury pivotal in Coalition space. The vast solar panel fields, managed by Specular Spread, fuel numerous antimatter plants under Base 10. Antimatter is shipped via Icarus Hubs to the Mercurial Gateway, one of two jump rings in the system.

**Security Concerns:** Increasing piracy and potential remnant forces attacks on shipments have raised suspicions about organized activities in the Mercurian tunnels.
