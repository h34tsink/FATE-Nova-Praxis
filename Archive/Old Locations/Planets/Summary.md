### [[Mercury]]
- **Visual Description:** A barren, rocky world with extreme temperature variations and a sun-scorched landscape.
- **Notable Features:** Known for solar panel fields, antimatter production, strategic jump ring (Mercurial Gateway), and historical battle sites.

### [[Venus]]
- **Visual Description:** Characterized by a thick, toxic atmosphere, volcanic landscapes, and sulfuric acid clouds.
- **Notable Features:** Hosts Gondolin ([[Groups/Apostate]] enclave) and The Venusian Maiden (research station).

### [[Earth]]
- **Visual Description:** A quarantined, post-apocalyptic world with ruins of cities and technophage-infested landscapes.
- **Notable Features:** [[Locations/Plot Locations]] like Brasilia (technophage ground zero), Marianas Trench, and various Lagrange Point stations.

### [[Luna]] (The Moon)
- **Visual Description:** A barren, crater-filled landscape with a stark, gray surface.
- **Notable Features:** Cities like Serro and Asola, entertainment hub Rho, and significant research stations.

### [[Mars]]
- **Visual Description:** A red, desert-like world with rocky terrain and dust storms.
- **Notable Features:** Olympus city, diverse population, and mining operations.

### Asteroid Belt
- **Visual Description:** A vast expanse of space dotted with rocky and metallic asteroids.
- **Notable Features:** Pirate bases, Tartarus prison, and mining operations.

### [[Jupiter]]
- **Visual Description:** A massive gas giant with swirling cloud bands and the Great Red Spot.
- **Notable Features:** Europa's IT Alpha station studying extraterrestrial marine life.

### [[Saturn]]
- **Visual Description:** Known for its stunning rings and gas giant appearance with pale gold color.
- **Notable Features:** Titan City, mining operations in The Rings.

### [[Uranus]]
- **Visual Description:** An ice giant with a pale blue color, featureless except for faint cloud bands.
- **Notable Features:** The Colony, an [[Groups/Apostate]] community in interconnected spacecraft.

### [[Neptune]]
- **Visual Description:** Similar to [[Uranus]] but with a deeper blue color and more pronounced cloud features.
- **Notable Features:** Naesock Processing Hub and mining operations in the planet’s arcs.

### [[Pluto]]
- **Visual Description:** A small, icy, and rocky world, primarily gray with some brownish-red terrain.
- **Notable Features:** Persephone Station for deep space research and Charon Crossing mining settlement.
