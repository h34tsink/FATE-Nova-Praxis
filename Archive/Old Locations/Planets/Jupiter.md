### Jupiter Overview
- **Gravity:** 2.36G
- **Day Length:** 9.8 hours
- **Estimated Population:** 2.8 million (including moons and orbital settlements)

#### Notable Settlements/Locations

##### Europa
- **Significance:** Europa's sub-ice oceans are a site of major scientific interest, harboring the first discovered extraterrestrial life forms.
- **IT Alpha:** A subterranean research station jointly operated by Houses Cipriani and Silva, dedicated to studying Europa's unique marine life.
- **Research Focus:** Investigating the rapid evolutionary cycles of Europa's aquatic life, which evolved more complexly and quickly compared to [[Earth]].
- **Current Challenges:** IT Alpha faces mysterious issues like power outages, an unidentifiable mold, and an unusual spike in cancer cases among its crew.

#### Jupiter's Role in the Coalition
- **Hostile Environment:** Jupiter itself is largely ignored due to its extreme and inhospitable environment, offering little in terms of resources.
- **Europa's Importance:** The moon Europa stands out as a significant site for scientific discovery and research, especially in the study of extraterrestrial life and evolutionary biology.

#### Sociopolitical and Scientific Dynamics
- **Joint Venture on IT Alpha:** The collaboration between Houses Cipriani and Silva on IT Alpha represents a significant investment in scientific research, underlining the Coalition's commitment to understanding alien ecosystems.
- **Unsolved Mysteries:** The recent anomalies at IT Alpha, including technological malfunctions and health concerns, pose new challenges and raise questions about the potential risks involved in deep-sea extraterrestrial research.
