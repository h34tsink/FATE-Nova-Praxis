Astralists adhere to the concept that human beings possess a superior state of consciousness known as the soul, existing on an elevated plane, termed the astral, beyond our normal dimensional perception. 

In their belief, the brain serves as a medium allowing the high consciousness (the soul) to interact with the low consciousness (the ego), and thereby influence the physical world. 

They propose that the mnemonic core alters the soul-ego linkage to a new physical form and a replicated lower consciousness, akin to a mag-rail switching tracks. 

Consequently, in the process of resleeving, the soul is thought to reconnect with the new physical form and the newly copied ego.
