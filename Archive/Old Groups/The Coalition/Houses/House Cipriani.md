# Cipriani (SIP-REE-AH-NEE)
**Traits:** Purist, Elegance and Style, Power Systems and CIST (Cyber-Intrusion and Security Terminal) Technology

**Discounts:** Cost of purchasing any of the following types of goods is reduced by 1: 
- Atmospheric Flying Vehicles 
- Energy (Broadcast Power Generators, etc.)
- CISTs
- Transportation

**Skill Bonus:** In addition, choose Persuasion or Intimidation. You gain a +1 bonus on rolls with your chosen Skill.
