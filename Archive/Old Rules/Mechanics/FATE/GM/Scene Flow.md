# 🎬 Nova Praxis Scene Flow Cheat Sheet
*Fate Core Narrative Structure & Transitions*

---

## 🧠 SESSION AS A 3-ACT CYBER-NOIR FILM

> **Setup ➜ Escalation ➜ Consequence ➜ Fallout ➜ Transition ➜ Next Hook**

Each “act” is about revealing a **truth**, not just resolving a conflict.

| Act | Focus | Typical Scene Count | Emotional Pulse |
|-----|--------|--------------------|----------------|
| **I – Setup** | Establish characters, loyalties, and secrets. | 2-3 | Curiosity → Tension |
| **II – Escalation** | Conflicts collide; secrets bite back. | 3-4 | Anxiety → Defiance |
| **III – Resolution** | Stakes crystallize; choices define identity. | 2-3 | Catharsis → Uneasy Calm |

---

## 🧩 STANDARD SCENE STRUCTURE

**1️⃣ Establish Context**  
• Where are we, what’s the tone, which Aspects shape it?  
> _Set Scene Aspects: “Neon Rain,” “Omnipresent Surveillance,” “Synthetic Charm.”_  

**2️⃣ Inciting Spark**  
• What changes right now? (A compel, clue, betrayal, or emotional shift.)  

**3️⃣ Complications**  
• Players act, create advantages, or face obstacles.  
• Push Aspects until something *breaks or bends.*  

**4️⃣ Resolution / Shift**  
• What changed?  
– Did a goal advance, an Aspect flip, or a secret surface?  

**5️⃣ Transition Line → Next Scene**  
• End on a strong image, echo, or emotional beat.  
> “Her reflection dissolves into code… cut to the next scene.”  

---

## ⏱️ SCENE RHYTHM & TIMING

| Type | Real-Time Length | Purpose | Typical Rolls |
|------|------------------|----------|---------------|
| **Micro / Transition** | 5-10 min | Link scenes, show downtime or travel | 0–1 |
| **Standard / Core** | 20-40 min | Main conflict, clue, or decision | 2–5 |
| **Extended / Climax** | 45-90 min | Showdown, revelation, multi-step conflict | 4–10 |

💡 *If nothing new is revealed or decided within ~10 minutes, fade to the next beat.*

---

## 🎭 TRANSITION TYPES

| Transition | Cue Example | Story Effect |
|-------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Time Shift** | “Three days later, the clone arrives on Orpheus Station.” | Keeps pacing tight. |
| **Location Shift** | “Fade from sterile white lab to smoky Apostate bar.” | Resets tone & power. |
| **Emotional Shift** | “Even as she smiles, the echo in her head won’t quiet.” | Carries feeling forward. |
| **Aspect Shift** | Scene Aspect ‘False Calm’ → ‘Security Alert!’ | Signals state change; time to cut. |

---

## ⚙️ GM CUES FOR CUTTING OR CONTINUING

Ask yourself:
1. Has any Aspect (scene or character) changed meaningfully?  
2. Did someone spend or earn a Fate Point?  
3. Is there a fresh question hanging in the air?

✅ If **yes** → Keep rolling.  
❌ If **no** → End on a transition line and move to the next hook.

## 🎞️ EXAMPLE FLOW

**Scene 1 – The Heist**  
> Goal: Steal the Pneuma shard.  
> Outcome: Shard secured, surveillance tripwire triggered.  
→ **Transition:** “Alarm klaxons bleed into music at the next scene.”

**Scene 2 – The Confession**  
> Goal: Convince Dr. Imani to decode the shard.  
> Outcome: Gain ally + new problem: Imani’s memories infect Lira.  
→ **Transition:** “Her eyes blink in sync with mine… fade to black.”

**Scene 3 – The Echo**  
> Goal: Cleanse the infection or merge with it.  
> Outcome: Philosophical choice defining Lira’s identity.  
→ Scene ends on **Fallout + Next Hook.**

## ❤️ GM MANTRA

> *Every scene changes someone or something.*  
> *Every cut leaves a question.*  
> *Every hook invites a choice.*
