# Pneuma Backlash Consequences (Flavor Pack)
*Use these when Pneuma’s “pressure” converts into harm. All are written as Consequence Aspects you can invoke/compel.*

> **GM note:** These are best when they **change choices**, not just add color—pull the character toward relevance, exposure, or obsession.

---

## Mild Consequences (quick sting, annoying, story-driving)

### Physical (Mild)
- **Threadburn Migraine** — pulsing pain whenever you ignore “the thread.”
- **Static in the Veins** — tremors, microspasms, shaky fine motor control.
- **Adrenal Overclock** — jittery, fast heartbeat; you can’t fully downshift.
- **Nosebleed of Certainty** — minor bleeding + lightheaded “click” moments.
- **Vertigo of Alignment** — the world feels slightly misregistered; balance suffers.

### Mental (Mild)
- **Determinism Itch** — you *must* follow leads; resisting feels physically wrong.
- **Pattern Hunger** — you keep seeing links; can’t stop connecting dots.
- **Synaptic Ringing** — thoughts “echo,” making quick decisions noisy.
- **Compulsion to Verify** — you double-check everything, even when time matters.
- **Crowded Causality** — too many relevant thoughts at once; attention fragments.

---

## Moderate Consequences (noticeable impairment, strong compels)

### Physical (Moderate)
- **Causality Sickness** — nausea and cold sweats when probability “shifts.”
- **Motor Phase Lag** — reactions stutter; you’re half a beat behind your intent.
- **Pressure Bruising** — mysterious bruises/burst capillaries from “forced” alignment.
- **Reality Rash** — skin irritation like contact allergy to certain environments/events.
- **Bone-Deep Fatigue** — your body feels like it ran a marathon inside your skull.

### Mental (Moderate)
- **Convergence Fixation** — one goal becomes a gravitational well; you neglect others.
- **Paranoid Patterning** — you assume every coincidence is hostile until proven otherwise.
- **Intrusive Foreshadowing** — flashes of “what must happen” that distract and scare.
- **Narrative Lock** — you can’t accept alternate plans; flexibility becomes painful.
- **Signal-to-Noise Collapse** — the important detail is always there… but buried.

---

## Severe Consequences (P) (Persistent)
*These should feel like Pneuma leaving a scar. Severe and Extreme are typically Persistent (P) in your rules, which makes them mechanically louder.*

### Physical (Severe) (P)
- **Threadscar Neuropathy (P)** — chronic nerve pain; the body remembers convergence.
- **Autonomic Desync (P)** — breathing/heartbeat drift; stress spikes unpredictably.
- **Sensory Recalibration Failure (P)** — light/sound sensitivity; you flinch at “omens.”
- **Tremor Cascade (P)** — fine control is unreliable; precision work becomes risky.
- **Zero-Point Exhaustion (P)** — deep depletion; you’re running on fumes for days.

### Mental (Severe) (P)
- **Determinism Vertigo (P)** — free will feels like a lie; you hesitate at choices.
- **Causal Overidentification (P)** — you start believing you *are* the thread.
- **Reality-Reference Drift (P)** — memory and meaning wobble; “what was true?” becomes a fight.
- **Convergence Dependency (P)** — you feel powerless without Pneuma running.
- **Omnirelevance Disorder (P)** — everything feels important; priorities implode.

---

## Extreme Consequences (P) (Persistent, identity-changing)
*Use sparingly; these are campaign-level scars and should rewrite how the character moves through the world.*

### Physical (Extreme) (P)
- **Permanent Phase Misalignment (P)** — you’re never fully “in sync” with the moment.
- **Systemic Threadburn (P)** — chronic pain + fatigue; Pneuma now always costs.
- **Embodied Pressure Conduit (P)** — your body becomes the release valve; bad luck clusters around you.

### Mental (Extreme) (P)
- **The Thread Owns Me (P)** — the universe’s agenda always shows up first.
- **Irreversibly Pattern-Bound (P)** — you cannot ignore convergence without severe fallout.
- **Fate-Lattice Imprint (P)** — your presence alters scenes; coincidence follows you like a shadow.

## Quick Pairings (fast picks at the table)
- **Resonance backlash:** Synaptic Ringing / Signal-to-Noise Collapse / Omnirelevance Disorder (P)
- **Convergence backlash:** Motor Phase Lag / Pressure Bruising / Threadscar Neuropathy (P)
- **Pressure backlash:** Paranoid Patterning / Narrative Lock / The Thread Owns Me (P)

## GM Compel Seeds (use these to “make it bite”)
- “Your **Determinism Itch** won’t let you leave this alley until you check the *one* door.”
- “With **Motor Phase Lag**, you’re a beat late—your shot’s fine, but the recoil knocks you into the open.”
- “Because of **Convergence Fixation**, you ignore the secondary objective… and it becomes the next crisis.”
- “Your **Reality-Reference Drift (P)** makes two witnesses contradict each other in a way you can’t reconcile—yet.”
