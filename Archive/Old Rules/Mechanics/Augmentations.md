# Augmentations

A comprehensive reference for all augmentations in Nova Praxis.

---

## ⭐ Special Augmentations

> [!aug]- Apotheosis
> **💎 AV:** 3 | **🎁 Free With:** Sleeve purchase | **🔒 Only For:** Sleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
> 
> Required for sleeves; grants mindset and mnemonic core. Your sleeve has undergone Apotheosis and features a mindset and mnemonic core, granting the Apotheosized Aspect.

> [!aug]- Fully Synthetic
> **💎 AV:** N/A | **🔒 Only For:** Cybersleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
> 
> Sleeve is completely synthetic with no biological components. Gains Synthetic Biology(P) Aspect.

> [!aug]- Cosmetic Customization
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
> 
> Custom features like crystalline canines or glowing eyes, primarily cosmetic.

> [!aug]- Striking Looks
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
> 
> Sleeve is exceptionally attractive. Gains Striking Looks Aspect.

> [!aug]- Bio-Skin Sheath
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Cybersleeve, Sleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No
> 
> Natural skin-like covering for cybernetic limbs or sleeves.

> [!aug]- Optimized Power System
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Cybersleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No | **⬆️ Increases Max Aug Count By:** +1
> 
> Top-of-the-line power management for cyberware efficiency.

> [!aug]- Exceptional Genegineering
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Biosleeve | **📊 Counts Against Limit:** No | **⬆️ Increases Max Aug Count By:** +1
> 
> Enhanced biological design providing superior performance.

## 💪 Physical Enhancement

> [!aug]- Adrenal Boost Module
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve | **🎁 Free With:** Sleeve purchase
> 
> Trigger adrenaline surge for enhanced athletics and combat. +1 Athletics and Melee Skills, +1 stress absorbed by Physical Consequences.

> [!aug]- Fibro-Muscle Weave
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **🔒 Only For:** Human, Biosleeve
> 
> Synthetic fibers woven into muscles for superhuman strength. Grants Fibro-Muscle Weave Aspect.

> [!aug]- Reinforced Skeleton
> **💎 AV:** 2
> 
> Ceramics and metals reinforce bones. Severe/Extreme Consequences absorb +3 stress.

> [!aug]- Skinweave Armor
> **💎 AV:** 1
> 
> Spider silk/graphene mesh woven into skin. +1 Armor Rating (stacks with other armor).

> [!aug]- Subdermal Plating
> **💎 AV:** 2
> 
> Thin flexible armor beneath skin. Mild/Moderate Consequences absorb +2 stress.

> [!aug]- Synthesis
> **💎 AV:** 3 | **🔒 Only For:** Biosleeve | **⚡ Requires Apotheosis:** Yes
> 
> Nanocybernetic fibers woven through tissue; pinnacle of evolution. Immune to poisons and disease.

> [!aug]- Neura-Surge
> **💎 AV:** 2 | **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
> 
> Overclocks nerves/muscles for instant action. Can interrupt another's turn for immediate Simple Action.

> [!aug]- Performance Enhanced
> **💎 AV:** 1 | **🔒 Only For:** Sleeve
> 
> Sleeve quality boost: +1 rank in chosen Physical Skill. Can be purchased up to three times.
