### Exoplanet Sagitta Overview
- **Gravity:** 1.22G
- **Distance from Sol:** 57.6 Light Years
- **Established:** 62 PS (Post-Singularity)
- **Estimated Population:** 6.2 million

#### Notable Settlements/Locations

##### Gruber Station
- **Purpose:** The primary mining hub in Sagitta's resource-rich debris ring.
- **Function:** Facilitates the extraction of materials from the ring, contributing to Sagitta's self-sufficiency.
- **Structure:** Composed of large tethered rocks, deploying drones for mining operations.

##### Sagitta’s Great Rift
- **Formation:** A massive chasm formed by an asteroid impact on an ancient tectonic fault.
- **Significance:** Hosts all of Sagitta’s inhabitants, stretching a third of the way around the planet along the equator.

##### Site XS-306
- **Discovery:** A recent magnetic anomaly detected north of the Great Rift.
- **Activity:** [[House Dalianis]] has established a secretive research station at the site, refusing to disclose details.

#### Sagitta's Unique Characteristics
- **Contingency Plan:** Originally colonized by Keppler Shipyards as a backup for humanity during the Consolidation Wars.
- **Asteroid Impact:** The chasm created by the asteroid brought life and a proto-atmosphere, making the planet habitable within the impact zone.
- **House Dynamics:** Majority controlled by [[House Dalianis]], with other Houses like Kimura, Tsarya, and Jinzhan having a presence.

#### Sociopolitical Dynamics
- **Expansionist Motive:** [[House Dalianis]]'s colonization of Sagitta reflects its ambition to expand humanity's reach.
- **Shared Governance:** Despite Dalianis's majority control, the presence of other Houses creates a diverse political landscape.

#### Current Issues and Activities
- **Praetorian Investigation:** A Praetorian agent is covertly investigating Dalianis’s activities, seeking assistance for a clandestine operation.
- **Probe Disappearances:** The mystery of missing probes carrying rocks for processing, hinting at unauthorized jump gate usage.
- **Missing Vulcan Swarm:** Jinzhan’s lost Vulcan swarm has triggered a desperate search, with suspicions of [[apostate]] involvement or inter-House intrigue.
