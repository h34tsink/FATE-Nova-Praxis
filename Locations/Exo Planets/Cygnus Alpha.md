### Exoplanet Cygnus Alpha Overview
- **Gravity:** 1.04G
- **Distance from Sol:** 52 Light Years
- **Established:** 54 PS (Post-Singularity)
- **Estimated Population:** 6.5 million

#### Notable Settlements/Locations

##### The Warehouse
- **Function:** A clandestine storage facility for House Dalianis, holding weapons, antimatter, and war supplies.
- **Security:** Highly secretive, the exact location is unknown, but it's a target for local criminals seeking to acquire valuable resources.

##### The Warrens
- **Character:** An extensive network of tunnels known as the Ratways, serving as a haven for apostates and criminal elements.
- **Activity:** A hotbed for illicit trade and black market dealings, where the wrong inquiries can be fatal.

##### APEX Test Site
- **Purpose:** Used by House Cipriani for testing new APEX technology, taking advantage of Cygnus Alpha's isolation.
- **Security Concerns:** The site is heavily guarded due to the valuable antimatter and the risk of piracy.

#### Cygnus Alpha's Unique Characteristics
- **Iron-Rich Crust:** Initially colonized for its abundance of iron, essential for the construction of starships.
- **Harsh Environment:** Notably inhospitable, with extreme cold, violent winds, and a surface littered with sharp metallic debris.
- **Subterranean Living:** Major cities are built in deep canyons for protection from the winds, with a vast underground network connecting different areas.

#### Sociopolitical Dynamics
- **House Rivalries:** Tensions between Keppler Shipyards and Giordino Automotive escalated during the Consolidation Wars, leading to physical conflicts over mining claims.
- **Post-War Changes:** The end of the war brought a population influx and a brief period of prosperity, followed by a decline as mining demand decreased.
- **Criminal Underworld:** The extensive tunnel network now harbors apostates, smugglers, and pirates, creating a complex underground society.

#### Current Issues and Activities
- **Weaponized Satellite:** Discovery of a weaponized survey satellite has sparked tensions between Houses Dalianis and Cipriani.
- **Emerging Groups:** The arrival of the Talons of Freedom in the Warrens is causing unrest among the local criminal and [[Groups/Apostate]] communities.
- **Stolen Combat Frame:** A missing heavy combat frame from the Warehouse has put House Dalianis on a frantic search, with suspicions pointing towards the Warrens.
