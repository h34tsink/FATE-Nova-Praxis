### Exoplanet Proch (PRO-CH) Overview
- **Gravity:** 0.87G
- **Distance from Sol:** 8.5 Light Years
- **Established:** 53 PS (Post-Singularity)
- **Estimated Population:** 11.7 million

#### Notable Settlements/Locations

##### Samara
- **Character:** Largest city, cut from equatorial jungles, with a beanstalk for interplanetary traffic.
- **Attractions:** Famous for a massive zoo showcasing Proch's wildlife and offering guided hunts for adventurous tourists.

##### Akim Ranch
- **Operation:** A venture by [[House Tsarya]] into high-quality cattle ranching, adapting to the presence of large predators.
- **Product:** Offers premium beef, competing with [[House Jinzhan]]'s food market despite higher costs due to security measures.

##### Newcastle
- **History:** Originally a fortress city, still recovering from a nuclear attack during the Federation’s guerrilla campaign.
- **Demographics:** Populated mostly by apostates seeking citizenship in [[House Tsarya]], working in heavy armor and war frame factories.

#### Proch's Unique Characteristics
- **First Human Colony Outside Sol:** Proch's historical significance as the first extraterrestrial human colony sets it apart.
- **Diverse Ecosystem:** Echoes [[Earth]]’s Mesozoic period with large reptiles and ferocious mammals, making it geographically and biologically diverse.
- **Hostile Environment:** Challenges include extreme cold, lack of ground water, and violent winds, resulting in unique adaptations by both wildlife and human inhabitants.

#### Sociopolitical Dynamics
- **[[House Tsarya]]'s Control:** Post-Consolidation Wars, Tsarya consolidated power, impacting local governance and industry.
- **Federation and Alliance History:** The initial colonization competition between the Federation and Alliance was a catalyst for the Consolidation Wars.

#### Current Issues and Activities
- **Hunting and Wildlife Conservation:** Proch is a hotspot for hunters seeking glory, while scientists study its unique fauna.
- **Underground Fights:** An emerging trend of animal fights has caught Tsarya's attention due to its unregulated nature and potential profits.
- **Mysterious Disappearances:** Reports of missing people and livestock have sparked rumors of dangerous new wildlife or possible insurgent activities.
- **Freedom Fighter Rumors:** [[Apostate]] rumors about a group of freedom fighters challenge Tsarya’s authority and control on Proch.
