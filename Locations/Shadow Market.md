# Shadow Market

A hidden network of traders, information brokers, and smugglers operating outside Coalition law. The Shadow Market is a prime location for gathering intel, making deals, or finding rare goods.

## Notable Features
- Black market goods
- Information brokers
- Talon and apostate contacts

## GM Notes
- Use for scenes involving negotiation, trade, or clandestine meetings.
- Possible Aspects: *Web of Favors*, *Dangerous Deals*, *Hidden Connections*.
