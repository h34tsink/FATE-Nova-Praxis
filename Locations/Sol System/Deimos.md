## Overview

Deimos contains two major settlements in its craters: **Kratos** and **Voltair**. Together they form a key Martian military training and support complex.

## Settlements

- **Kratos:** Purpose-built military training city (weapons, tactics, pilot programs, specialist instruction)
- **Voltair:** Main family/support settlement for non-Kratos troops; culturally distinct and more dispersed in layout

## Strategic Role

- Pipeline for Coalition combat readiness
- Rotational staging for high-volume military personnel
- Cultural and doctrinal shaping point for future officers and specialists

## Campaign Hooks

- Training exercise that becomes a real hostile incursion
- Recruitment scandal tied to elite-track selection at Kratos
- Domestic-political arc involving Voltair’s religious/social power blocs
