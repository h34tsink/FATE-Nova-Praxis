# Sol System Overview

The Sol System is humanity's birthplace and the heart of the Coalition. Despite Earth's fall to the Technophage, the system remains the political, economic, and cultural center of human civilization.

## Planets

### Inner System
- [[Mercury]] - 260,000 population, House Cipriani antimatter monopoly
- [[Venus]] - 435,000 population, Gondolin apostate enclave
- [[Earth]] - Quarantined homeworld, Lagrange station network
- [[Luna]] - 8.5M population, three major cities (Serro, Asola, Rho)
- [[Mars]] - 21.2M population, Coalition capital at Olympus

### Outer System
- [[Jupiter]] - Europa Research Station, alien life discovery
- [[Saturn]] - Titan City fuel monopoly
- [[Uranus]] - The Colony apostate haven
- [[Neptune]] - Naesock Processing Hub weapons facility  
- [[Pluto]] - 12,000 frontier population, Kuiper Belt research

## Strategic Significance

The Sol System contains:
- **Political Capital**: Olympus on Mars
- **Economic Centers**: Mercury (energy), Titan (fuel), Luna (manufacturing)
- **Research Hubs**: Europa (xenobiology), Earth Lagrange (technology)
- **Military Strongholds**: Sentinel Station, Deimos bases
- **Shadow War Fronts**: Rho, Olympus, multiple hidden agendas
