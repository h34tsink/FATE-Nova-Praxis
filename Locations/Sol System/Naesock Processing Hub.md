# Naesock Processing Hub

The **Naesock Processing Hub** is a massive space station orbiting **Triton**, Neptune's largest moon. Originally designed as a primary processing and storage facility for resources extracted from Neptune's arcs, the station has undergone significant changes in recent years.

---

## Overview

- **Initial Purpose:**  
    The station was constructed to handle vast quantities of materials harvested from Neptune's arcs. Unlike Saturn’s rings, these arcs are irregularly shaped and contain rare elements, making them a valuable resource for the Coalition.
    
- **Decline and Transformation:**  
    Over the past decade, mining operations in Neptune’s arcs have dwindled, leading to the closure of more than half of the Naesock Processing Hub.
    
    - The decline resulted in a significant reduction in the station’s workforce, leaving it largely understaffed and underutilized.
    
- **Rise of the Apostate Enclave:**  
    The lower half of the station, separated from the operational upper levels by thirteen abandoned decks, has become a haven for apostates and a thriving black market hub.
    
    - This section operates outside the Coalition’s control, providing a refuge for those living beyond the reach of the Houses.
    
- **House Silva's Involvement:**  
    Officially, the lower decks are designated as a storage facility for House Silva’s outdated weapons technology. Unofficially, House Silva tolerates and even utilizes the apostate enclave.
    
    - **House Silva** leverages this area to maintain contact with their apostate allies and monitor black market activities, allowing them to exert influence over this otherwise independent community.

---

## Summary

The **Naesock Processing Hub** stands as a symbol of the changing dynamics between the Coalition’s reach and the resilience of those seeking alternatives to its control.

- What was once a symbol of industrial might has become a refuge for those living outside the Coalition’s rigid social structures.
- **House Silva’s covert involvement** highlights the power plays and strategic maneuvering within the **Nova Praxis** setting.

---

## Key Points to Consider

- The **Naesock Processing Hub** could serve as a valuable location for the PCs to gather information, acquire illicit goods, or establish contact with [[Factions/Ideologies/Apostates]] factions.

- **House Silva’s influence** within the station might present both opportunities and challenges for the PCs, depending on their objectives and allegiances.

- The presence of a **thriving black market** suggests the existence of various criminal elements and potentially dangerous individuals operating within the station’s lower levels.
