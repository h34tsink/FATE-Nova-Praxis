## Overview

Phobos hosts a single city of the same name and serves as a major House Dalianis power center. It combines large-scale manufacturing with high-Rep resort infrastructure.

## Infrastructure

- Three primary domes with dense vertical hive-like urban structures
- Massive manufacturing belts between hive zones where super-heavy ships are assembled
- Leased Cipriani facilities supporting APEX reactor assembly and prep

## Strategic Value

- Industrial cornerstone for Martian military and fleet expansion
- Cross-house cooperation node (Dalianis, Cipriani, Jinzhan support networks)
- Access to advanced resleeving via Thetis Revival Labs presence

## Campaign Hooks

- Industrial sabotage in mixed-house reactor assembly lines
- Contract sleeve work gone wrong in Thetis-linked logistics teams
- Social arc splitting high-Rep tower life and labor-level production zones
