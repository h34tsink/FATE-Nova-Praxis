## Profile of the Lunar City of Asola

Asola is the second largest city on the Moon. [1] Like the largest lunar city, Serro, Asola features domes constructed by Vulcan swarms that offer protection and atmosphere to its inhabitants. [2, 3] However, Asola is considered lower class compared to Serro. [2, 3]

Here are some key characteristics of Asola:

- **Oppressive Atmosphere:** The Asola lifestyle is generally oppressive, which is reflected in the city's condition. [3] Despite efforts to maintain it, the city appears dirty, with poorly filtered air and a constant state of disrepair. [3] Malfunctioning gravity generators and excess carbon dioxide are not uncommon. [3]
- **[[House Tsarya]]'s Influence:** Asola is home to [[House Tsarya]]'s largest manufacturing facility in the solar system. [1] Life in Asola revolves around working for [[House Tsarya]] or providing goods and services to those who do. [4]
- **Purist Tradition:** Asola has a strong Purist tradition, which [[House Tsarya]] supports. [1] This preference for unaltered human bodies likely contributes to the lower-class status of Asola, as transhuman advancements are often associated with wealth and privilege in Nova Praxis.
- **Seedy and Violent Entertainment:** Asola's entertainment caters to the downtrodden [[Apostate]] workers who seek distractions from their difficult lives. [4] They spend their Tsaryan credits on alcohol, drugs, prostitution, gambling, cage fighting, and other vices. [4]
- **Macro-factories:** Asola's landscape is dotted with numerous smaller domes that house sprawling manufacturing facilities. [4] These "macro-factories" compile and assemble products for [[House Tsarya]], highlighting the city's role as a major industrial center. [4]
- **Ground Vehicles Over Skiffs:** Ground vehicles are more common than skiffs in Asola. [4] While skiffs offer greater mobility and are often associated with a higher social standing, their lower prevalence in Asola suggests a more practical, grounded lifestyle for its inhabitants.
