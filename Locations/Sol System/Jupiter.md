## Basic Data

**Classification:** Gas Giant
**Moons:** 79+ known moons
**Habitable zones:** Upper atmosphere and orbiting stations
**Primary Settlement:** Europa Research Station (100,000+)
**Strategic Importance:** High; biological research priority

## Overview

Jupiter dominates the outer solar system as the largest planet in human space. While the gas giant itself cannot support human settlement, its extensive moon system—particularly Europa—has become critical to Coalition research and operations. The discovery of indigenous life forms beneath Europa's ice has elevated the Jovian system to strategic importance, with significant military and scientific presence.

## Physical Characteristics

### Atmosphere
- **Composition:** Hydrogen and helium dominant
- **Zones:** Distinct band structure with varying conditions
- **Storms:** Great Red Spot and other persistent weather systems
- **Radiation:** Intense magnetic field and radiation belts
- **Hazard:** Lethal to unprotected humans at all accessible altitudes

### Moons
- **Major moons:** Io, Europa, Ganymede, Callisto
- **Minor moons:** 75+ smaller satellites
- **Orbits:** Complex orbital mechanics; some moons tidally locked
- **Resources:** Various mineral deposits on rocky moons
- **Population:** Minimal except on Europa

## Europa Research Station

### Facility Overview
- **Location:** Orbiting moon Europa
- **Population:** Estimated 100,000+ researchers and support staff
- **Operator:** House Silva (official); House Jinzhan involvement (controversial)
- **Status:** High security; classified research
- **Access:** Restricted; minimal civilian presence

### Research Focus
- **Primary objective:** Understand/study extraterrestrial life forms
- **Subject:** Organisms discovered in subsurface ocean beneath ice
- **Methods:** Robotic probes; water sample analysis; containment studies
- **Scope:** Advanced biological research at Coalition cutting edge
- **Classification:** Heavily classified; public information minimal

### Critical Issue: Disease Outbreak
- **Status:** Unusual illnesses affecting research personnel
- **Pattern:** Inexplicable diseases; no identified pathogen
- **Casualties:** Deaths increasing; official reports say "contamination"
- **Cause:** Unknown; theories range from alien biology to weapons
- **Response:** Cover-up; minimal information released to public
- **Concern:** Coalition worried about biological threat escaping containment

### Mysterious Discoveries
- **Organisms:** Strange life forms adapted to extreme conditions
- **Behavior:** Unusual properties not matching known biology
- **Origins:** Indigenous Jovian life vs. contamination theories
- **Purpose:** Unknown why House Silva prioritizing this research
- **Speculation:** Possible bioweapon development or genetic study

## Io & Other Moons

### Io
- **Characteristics:** Volcanically active; extreme conditions
- **Settlement:** Research stations only; no permanent population
- **Resources:** Sulfur compounds; useful for fuel and materials
- **Operations:** Automated mining and processing facilities
- **Access:** Difficult; hazardous environment

### Ganymede & Callisto
- **Status:** Research and mining stations
- **Population:** Scattered small settlements
- **Resources:** Ice and rock deposits
- **Purpose:** Resource extraction and scientific study
- **Development:** Limited; low priority

## Strategic Significance

### Scientific Importance
- **Life research:** Critical to understanding extraterrestrial biology
- **Expansion:** Discoveries driving new research directions
- **Technology:** Europa station at cutting edge of biotechnology
- **Knowledge:** Potential to revolutionize understanding of life itself

### Military Implications
- **Bioweapons:** Possible development of biological weapons
- **Resource control:** Struggle for dominance over research access
- **Intelligence:** Critical information for House advantage
- **Threat:** Unknown dangers possibly escaping containment

### Coalition Priority
- **Funding:** Significant resources devoted to Europa research
- **Personnel:** Best researchers recruited for station
- **Security:** Heavy military presence and oversight
- **Classification:** Among most secret Coalition operations
