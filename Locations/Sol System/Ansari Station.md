# Ansari Station

The largest space-based shipyard in the galaxy, operated by [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis|House Dalianis]] at Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 3 near [[Locations/Sol System/Luna|Luna]]. Ansari is where Dalianis builds its starship fleet — from civilian transports to military dreadnoughts.

## Capabilities

- **Shipbuilding:** Full-spectrum construction from frigates to capital ships
- **Drydocks:** Multiple simultaneous builds; largest drydock can house a Delta-type dreadnought
- **Refit and repair:** Coalition-wide contract for military vessel maintenance
- **Jump ring assembly:** Components for the jump ring network are fabricated here

## Security

Ansari is one of the most heavily defended installations in Coalition space. Dalianis treats any approach without clearance as hostile. The [[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Scholae Palatinae|Scholae Palatinae]] maintain a permanent garrison.

## Strategic Significance

Control of Ansari gives Dalianis leverage over every other House — they build the ships everyone needs. This is the foundation of Dalianis political power and their expansionist agenda.
