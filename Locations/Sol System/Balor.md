## Overview

Balor is a major Mercury habitat referenced as both a practical operations base and an occasional respite zone for crews cycling through extreme thermal/industrial assignments.

## Strategic Role

- Supports maintenance and crew turnover for Mercurial installations
- Functions as a logistics touchpoint between heavy industry and transport corridors

## Campaign Hooks

- Contract dispute over habitat access during emergency thermal surge
- Black-market deal hidden within a legitimate worker-rotation transfer
