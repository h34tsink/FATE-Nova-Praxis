# Europa Research Station

Coalition research outpost orbiting [[Locations/Sol System/Jupiter|Jupiter]]'s moon Europa. Originally established to study the alien life discovered on Europa by NASA's Poseidon probe in 1 PS, the station has grown into a significant scientific facility.

## Research Focus

- **Xenobiology:** Studying Europa's rapidly evolving alien ecosystem beneath the ice
- **Containment:** Monitoring and preventing cross-contamination between human and alien biology
- **Deep ocean probes:** Robotic exploration of Europa's subsurface ocean

## Campaign Relevance

The crew's early missions (Sessions 1-3) centered on Europa Station during a crisis involving stolen tech and an apostate operation. The station was left damaged and paranoid after the crew's intervention.
