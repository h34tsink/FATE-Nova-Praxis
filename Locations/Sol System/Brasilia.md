## Overview

Brasilia was the Alliance capital and the initial deployment site of the Gray Plague/technophage during the Consolidation Wars. The strike spiraled beyond intended control and marked the irreversible loss of Earth.

## Current State

- Region largely consumed and reworked by machine activity
- Persistent orbital bombardment zones target emergent technophage structures
- Considered one of the most dangerous and symbolically significant sites in human history

## Campaign Hooks

- Recover pre-collapse archival data from a sealed diplomatic bunker
- Verify rumors of an intact command vault beneath bombardment ruins
