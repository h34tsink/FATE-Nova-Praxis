## Basic Data

**Classification:** Ice Giant
**Moons:** 27+ known moons
**Orbital characteristics:** Rotates on side; unusual angle
**Primary Settlement:** The Colony (500,000+ apostates)
**Strategic Status:** De facto apostate territory

## Overview

Uranus orbits at the edge of Coalition space, a distant ice giant orbited by a massive mobile apostate settlement. The Coalition officially acknowledges The Colony's existence but doesn't enforce law there, effectively recognizing Uranus as apostate-controlled territory. The planet's distance from central Coalition authority makes it an ideal refuge for stateless individuals and freedom-seeking communities.

## Physical Characteristics

### Atmosphere & Conditions
- **Composition:** Water ice, methane ice, ammonia ice ("ices")
- **Temperature:** Extremely cold; lethal to unprotected humans
- **Winds:** Supersonic winds in upper atmosphere
- **Color:** Blue-green appearance from methane
- **Accessibility:** No accessible habitable zones in atmosphere

### Unusual Rotation
- **Angle:** Rotates on its side (98-degree axial tilt)
- **Result:** Unique seasonal patterns and lighting
- **Navigation:** Creates challenging orbital mechanics
- **Appearance:** Distinctive orientation visible from space
- **Effects:** Complex moon orbital patterns

### Moons
- **Quantity:** 27+ known moons
- **Characteristics:** Mix of ice and rock
- **Population:** Minimal; mostly empty
- **Resources:** Unexploited; limited strategic value
- **Development:** Minimal except for orbital space

## The Colony: Apostate Haven

### Overview
- **Population:** 500,000+ apostates (hiding from Coalition)
- **Structure:** Mobile; community of connected large vessels
- **Organization:** Self-governing; consensus-based leadership
- **Location:** High orbit near Uranus; capable of relocating
- **Status:** De facto independent; Coalition doesn't enforce law

### Physical Configuration

**Vessel Network:**
- **Primary ships:** Several dozen large transport/habitat vessels
- **Interconnection:** Connected by tunnels and docking systems
- **Modularity:** Can separate and relocate if threatened
- **Defensive:** Limited but mobile defensive systems
- **Stealth:** Designed to avoid Coalition detection

**Habitat Details:**
- **Prefabs:** Portable habitat modules for rapid deployment
- **Movement:** Entire colony capable of relocating to different orbital position
- **Sustainability:** Self-sufficient agricultural and manufacturing systems
- **Transportation:** Internal transit systems between vessel sections
- **Efficiency:** Compact design maximizes space utilization

### Population & Organization

**Demographic Composition:**
- **Apostates:** Stateless individuals; those fleeing Coalition
- **Refugees:** Political dissidents and persecuted groups
- **Criminals:** Some wanted individuals hiding from justice
- **Idealists:** Those believing in apostate philosophy
- **Families:** Multi-generational apostate communities

**Governance Structure:**
- **Council:** Leadership by elder apostates; consensus-based decisions
- **Philosophy:** Minimize centralized authority; maximize individual freedom
- **Values:** Personal autonomy; mutual aid; no forced augmentation
- **Debates:** Constant internal discussions about governance and rules
- **Flexibility:** Structure adapting as population grows

### Internal Tensions

**Philosophical Debates:**
- **Authority:** How much central governance needed for survival?
- **Growth:** Expansion bringing integration challenges
- **Defense:** How to defend without becoming militaristic?
- **Resources:** Fair distribution of limited supplies
- **Secrecy:** Maintaining invisibility while expanding

**Practical Challenges:**
- **Supplies:** Dependent on trade with fringe settlements
- **Manufacturing:** Limited industrial capacity
- **Sustainability:** Difficult to maintain permanent population
- **Recruitment:** Drawing new members while avoiding detection
- **Medical:** Limited healthcare; serious illness poses problems

## Threats to The Colony

### Coalition Pressure
- **Periodic raids:** Occasional Coalition attempts to raid or destroy colony
- **Ultimatums:** Pressure to relocate or submit to Coalition authority
- **Surveillance:** Increasing intelligence focus on colony location
- **Response:** Colony developed mobility to avoid capture
- **Escalation:** Risk of major Coalition military action

### Children of Dionysus
- **Threat level:** Significant; apocalyptic faction spreading chaos
- **Attacks:** Direct assaults on colony; ideology spreading
- **Chaos:** Colony trying to maintain stability against external chaos
- **Resources:** Diverting resources to defense and security
- **Existential:** Threat poses potential extinction risk

### Resource Scarcity
- **Supplies:** Dependent on unreliable external trade
- **Manufacturing:** Cannot produce everything internally
- **Expansion:** Limited capacity for population growth
- **Trade:** Relationships with trading partners fragile
- **Sustainability:** Questionable long-term viability

## Strategic Significance

### For Coalition
- **Threat:** Large organized apostate community
- **Vulnerability:** Independence undermines Coalition authority
- **Logistics:** Distance and mobility make colony difficult to attack
- **Concern:** Growing population and capabilities
- **Tolerance:** Coalition doesn't want to drive apostates to violence

### For Apostates
- **Sanctuary:** Safest known apostate location
- **Independence:** Closest thing to free apostate society
- **Refuge:** Destination for escaping Coalition
- **Community:** Large established apostate population
- **Future:** Hope for apostate autonomy

## Campaign Hooks

- **Colony contact:** Making contact with apostate leadership
- **Refugee transport:** Helping apostates travel to The Colony
- **Supply runs:** Smuggling supplies to/from The Colony
- **Defense mission:** Helping defend colony against threats
- **Internal politics:** Navigating colony governance debates
- **Chaos threat:** Fighting against Children of Dionysus
- **Information gathering:** Learning about colony capabilities
- **Sabotage:** Working for Coalition to undermine colony
