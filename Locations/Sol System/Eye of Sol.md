## Overview

Located at Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 2, Eye of Sol is one of the two public jump gates in the Sol system. The original installation was destroyed during the Consolidation Wars and later rebuilt as a critical transit hub.

## Civilian & Strategic Function

- **Transit:** Primary public gate access for non-military traffic
- **Facilities:** Traveler hotel and museum on the Consolidation Wars and Coalition founding
- **Military flow:** Key routing point for materiel before onward processing at [[Locations/Sol System/Sentinel Station]]

## Campaign Hooks

- Intercepting a target during legal gate transit
- Recovering records tied to the destroyed original Eye
- Managing civilian panic during a gate interruption event
