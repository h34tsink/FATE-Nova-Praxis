## Overview

Odysseus Station is one of the largest lunar research stations and a major House Dalianis jump-technology site.

## Research Focus

- Advanced jump-drive architecture and high-risk modifications
- Rumored work on gravity-well-capable jump concepts (often referenced as a "space bridge")

## Known Incident

- A large station section exploded, causing major casualties
- Official cause cited as industrial fire reaching oxygen infrastructure
- Unofficial narratives point to covert theft and internal sabotage

## Campaign Hooks

- Recover project fragments from sealed damaged decks
- Track a stolen mnemonic core tied to the incident
- Run a political fallout arc between Dalianis and rival House operatives
