## Overview

Enceladus is a Saturn-system science node with persistent interest in subsurface water dynamics and extreme-environment biology.

## Strategic Relevance

- Scientific counterpoint to Titan’s industrial dominance
- Lower-traffic, high-value site for discreet research programs

## Campaign Hooks

- Quarantine scenario from a contaminated ice-core mission
- Diplomatic friction over data ownership between competing research teams
