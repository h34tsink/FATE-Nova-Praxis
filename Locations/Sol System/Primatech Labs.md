## Overview

Primatech Labs sits at Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 4 and functions as a House Jinzhan R&D/manufacturing complex. Its primary mission is armament and ordnance support for ships and kill-sats enforcing Earth quarantine.

## Classified Work

- **Technophage program:** Reverse-engineering remains of the modified drone that attempted an attack on [[Locations/Sol System/Vantage]]
- **Access control:** Study compartment sealed to non-Jinzhan personnel
- **Political fallout:** Growing friction between Houses Jinzhan and Dalianis over data access and control

## Current Tensions

- Multiple assigned scientists have disappeared under officially benign explanations
- Other Houses are pressuring for inspection rights and technical disclosure

## Campaign Hooks

- Heist for black-box data from the drone-analysis team
- Counterintelligence operation around missing researchers
- Negotiation scene between House envoys on shared weapons protocols
