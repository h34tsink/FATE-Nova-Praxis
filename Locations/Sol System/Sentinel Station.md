## Overview

Located at Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 5, Sentinel Station is the largest Coalition military compound in known space. It serves as both an operations center and a distribution nexus for military assets moving through the Earth theater.

## Operations & Control

- **Primary operators:** House Silva (Valkyrie subsidiary) and House Tsarya
- **Role:** Military staging, command-and-control, and fleet logistics
- **Supply chain:** Major equipment traffic passes through Sentinel after transfer via [[Locations/Sol System/Eye of Sol]]

## Strategic Assets

- **Compiler banks:** Hundreds of compilers for restricted military materiel
- **Resurrection capacity:** Large decant/resleeving throughput for combat casualties
- **Apotheosis path:** One of the key military sites where soldiers undergo [[Glossary/Apotheosis]]

## Campaign Hooks

- Convoy interdiction or escort mission involving restricted military cargo
- Extraction of a soldier before compulsory apotheosis processing
- House rivalry pressure between Silva and Tsarya over command authority
