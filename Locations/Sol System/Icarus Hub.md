# Icarus Hub

Orbital station around [[Locations/Exoplanets/Proch|Proch]] in the Sirius system. A major logistics and transit hub operated by [[Factions/Subsidiaries/Icarus Interplanetary|Icarus Interplanetary]], a [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani|House Cipriani]] subsidiary that manages all interplanetary import and export.

## Layout

A large cubic-section station with multiple docking bays, commercial districts, residential quarters, and administrative sectors. The station serves as a waypoint for cargo and personnel moving between Sol and the outer colonies.

## Key NPCs

- **[[Isabella Nowak]]** — [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya|Tsarya]] representative, Executive Administrator of Planetary Logistics
- **Lighthouse security forces** — [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis|Dalianis]] security subsidiary maintaining order

## Campaign Relevance (Session 9, Part 3)

Icarus Hub is the site of the apostate uprising orchestrated by [[Chimera]] in coordination with the [[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Talons of Freedom|Talons of Freedom]]. The crew must navigate the chaos to reach [[Isabella Nowak|Nowak]] before [[Kestrel]] eliminates her.
