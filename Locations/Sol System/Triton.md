## Overview

Triton anchors the Neptune theater as the nearest major body to the Naesock Processing Hub and related arc-mining logistics.

## Strategic Role

- Staging reference for traffic into/out of Neptune’s arc extraction corridors
- Peripheral node for classified operations benefiting from system remoteness

## Campaign Hooks

- Supply or personnel transfer disrupted by unexplained station-sector lockdowns
- Covert investigation into hidden lower-hub programs tied to Naesock
