## Overview

Io is Jupiter’s most volatile major moon and functions as a high-risk operations site rather than a civilian settlement center.

## Operational Profile

- Extreme volcanic activity and unstable terrain
- Limited-duration technical deployments favored over permanent habitation
- Strategic value in niche materials, field testing, and hazard-adapted systems

## Campaign Hooks

- Emergency extraction from a failing volcanic observation platform
- Retrieval of a prototype tested in hostile environmental conditions
