# Vantage Station

Earth-Moon Lagrange Point station serving as quarantine command and observation platform for the [[Locations/Sol System/Earth|Earth]] blockade. One of the Coalition's most strategically important facilities — it coordinates the kill-sat network and monitors the [[Glossary/Technophage|technophage]] on Earth's surface.

## Function

- **Quarantine command:** Central coordination for the Earth quarantine fleet
- **Observation:** Monitoring technophage evolution and surface conditions
- **Military staging:** Deployment point for [[Factions/The Coalition/Protectorate|Protectorate]] forces
- **Intelligence:** Long-range sensors tracking anything approaching Earth

## House Presence

Jointly operated by [[Factions/Houses/House Jinzhan|Jinzhan]] (security, ordinance) and [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis|Dalianis]] (fleet assets). [[Primatech Labs]] operates nearby at E-M Lagrange Point 4, manufacturing armaments for quarantine enforcement ships.

## Campaign Relevance

The crew escaped Vantage in Session 7 with [[Lester Lucas]] aboard the *Chickenhawk*. [[Valare]]'s biosleeve was killed here during a grenade exchange — her mnemonic core survived and was hidden aboard the ship.
