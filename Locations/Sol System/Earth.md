## Basic Data

**Gravity:** 1.0G
**Length of Day:** 24 hours
**Estimated Population:** 0 (officially)
**Status:** Under permanent quarantine

## Overview

Humanity's homeworld, once the cradle of civilization, now lies dead beneath a blanket of technophage machines. The attempt to deploy the Gray Plague as a weapon during the Consolidation Wars backfired catastrophically, resulting in the extinction of all human life on Earth and the creation of a self-replicating technological threat that continues to evolve and adapt.

## The Technophage Catastrophe

### Origins
- **Weapon creation:** Federation created technophage to destroy Alliance capital Brasilia
- **Intended effect:** Destroy city, force Alliance surrender
- **Actual result:** Technophage gained sentience; became independent threat
- **Uncontrollable spread:** Gray Plague spread globally like infection
- **Outcome:** All Earth governments forced to evacuate; planet lost to machines

### Current Threat Status
- **No human survivors:** Presumed 0 humans remain alive on Earth
- **Autonomous drones:** Thousands of self-directed war machines patrol surface
- **Adaptive defenses:** Technophage continues evolving and improving systems
- **Active threat:** Continually developing new capabilities and weapons
- **Exodus consequence:** Required abandonment of humanity's birthworld

## Quarantine Enforcement

### Military Apparatus
- **Constant bombardment:** Earth orbit maintained with military assets
- **Kill-sat network:** Space-based weapons continuously target technophage
- **Fleet presence:** Coalition warships maintain permanent patrol
- **Defensive stations:** Lagrange Point facilities support quarantine

### Enforcement Effectiveness
- **Orbital superiority:** Can destroy most terrestrial threats before launch
- **Limitation:** Cannot eliminate technophage swarms in ocean depths
- **Incomplete containment:** Technophage evolving faster than countermeasures
- **Unknown ceiling:** Unclear what threat level technophage can reach

## Notable Locations on Earth

### Brasilia
- **Status:** Complete destruction; ground zero of technophage deployment
- **Destruction:** Entire region (300+ sq. km.) consumed by machines
- **Current state:** Gray powder remains; nanofactories continuously rebuild
- **Orbital bombardment:** Kill-sats destroy new construction every few days
- **Strategic significance:** Original outbreak site; maximum technophage concentration

### Marianas Trench
- **Technophage concentration:** Primary gathering point for swarm forms
- **Depth advantage:** Deep ocean location difficult to attack from orbit
- **Recent activity:** Increasing drone emergence in more locations
- **Intelligence concern:** Escalating drone activity suggests strategic shift
- **Unknown depth operations:** Technophage activities at trench bottom unknown

### Denver Interplanetary Spaceport
- **Mysterious activity:** Technophage engaged in large-scale construction
- **Shield creation:** Built protective dome/shield over former spaceport
- **Drone gathering:** Majority of drones in Denver region concentrated here
- **Purpose unknown:** What is technophage building beneath shield?
- **Speculation:** Possible new weapon or manufacturing center

### Vantage (E-M Lagrange Point 1)
- **Location:** Between Earth and Moon
- **Function:** Quarantine headquarters and tourist attraction
- **Museum:** Exhibits dedicated to Earth's history
- **Observation:** Visitor decks provide views of dead homeworld
- **Strategic value:** Primary command center for quarantine enforcement
- **Security note:** Staff rumored to have remnant sympathies

### Eye of Sol (E-M Lagrange Point 2)
- **Function:** Rebuilt jump gate facility
- **Original:** Gate destroyed during Consolidation Wars
- **Reconstruction:** Now serves as primary gateway to Earth system
- **Hotel:** Features accommodation for travelers and historians
- **Museum:** Consolidation Wars and Coalition founding exhibits
- **Traffic:** Handles all non-military traffic to Earth system

### Ansari Station (E-M Lagrange Point 3)
- **Operator:** House Dalianis
- **Function:** Massive starship manufacturing facility
- **Scale:** Largest space-based shipyard in known galaxy
- **Population:** Home to thousands of workers and families
- **Architecture:** Multi-layered city of stacked disks
- **Strategic importance:** Produces largest warships in fleet

### Primatech Labs (E-M Lagrange Point 4)
- **Operator:** House Jinzhan
- **Function:** R&D and manufacturing facility
- **Specialization:** Drones and ordinance for quarantine enforcement
- **Current project:** Reverse-engineering modified technophage drone
- **Security:** Highly classified; restricted access
- **Conflict:** Jinzhan refusing House access to captured drone data

### Sentinel Station (E-M Lagrange Point 5)
- **Designation:** Largest Coalition military installation anywhere
- **Operators:** House Silva (Valkyrie subsidiary), House Tsarya
- **Functions:** Command center, asset distribution hub, military training
- **Equipment:** Hundreds of compilers for military production
- **Resurrection:** Large resleeving facility for fallen soldiers
- **Apotheosis:** Primary location for military consciousness uploading

## Critical Events

### Drone Attack on Vantage
- **Incident:** Technophage-modified drone attempted attack on quarantine HQ
- **Drone status:** Upgraded with APEX bomb and new weapons
- **Proof of sentience:** Demonstrated technophage intelligence and planning
- **Response:** Drone destroyed by Kamarov before reaching weapons range
- **Evidence:** Analysis confirmed technophage reprogramming and enhancement
- **Concern:** Showed technophage can modify and improve military hardware

### Denver Spaceport Anomaly
- **Timeline:** Recent event causing Coalition concern
- **Construction:** Technophage cannibalized drones to build shield
- **Scale:** Majority of regional drones gathered for project
- **Mystery:** Purpose and contents of shield construction unknown
- **Bombardment:** Nearly continuous orbital attacks on shield (ineffective)
- **Speculation:** Possible weapon manufacturing or new machine type creation
