## Overview

Tartarus is the most notorious high-security incarceration site in Coalition space, used for dangerous political, criminal, and black-program detainees.

## Strategic Role

- Long-term detention and interrogation hub
- Symbol of Coalition coercive power and legal finality
- Frequent pressure point in House politics and prisoner-exchange schemes

## Campaign Hooks

- Prison transfer ambush across belt traffic lanes
- Covert extraction of a witness before sealed-court sentencing
