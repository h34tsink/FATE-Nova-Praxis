## Basic Data

**Gravity:** 0.16G
**Length of Day:** 27.3 Earth Days
**Estimated Population:** 8.5 million
**Primary Control:** Multiple Houses (Silva, Tsarya, Cipriani, Kimura)

## Overview

Luna represents humanity's first true off-world colony and remains crucial to Coalition operations. Three major cities—Serro, Asola, and Rho—showcase the diversity and class structure of Coalition civilization. The moon also hosts dozens of smaller settlements and high-altitude research stations.

## Major Cities

### Serro: House Silva's Seat of Power
- **Population:** Largest lunar city
- **Control:** Primarily House Silva
- **Development:** Grown from original colonies through Vulcan swarm expansion
- **Architecture:** Sprawling series of domes containing multi-story "hives"
- **Connection:** Mag-rail and skiff transportation network between hives
- **Gravity:** Upper hives use gravitic compensation; surface unmodified

**Notable Features:**
- **Hives:** 2-3 square miles each; 100-150+ story buildings
- **Labs:** Hundreds devoted to House Silva biotech research
- **Surface attractions:** Crash fields (low-gravity football variant)
- **Population:** Mostly House Silva members and lab workers
- **Economy:** Biotech research and production

**Surface Level:**
- **Gravity:** Weak lunar gravity (1/6 Earth)
- **Legality:** Illegal permanent residence (health concerns)
- **Reality:** Apostate cybersleeved individuals maintain hidden settlements
- **Activity:** Tourist attractions and shops despite weak gravity
- **Recreation:** Popular low-gravity recreation areas

### Asola: Industrial Working-Class City
- **Population:** Second largest; primarily lower-class workers
- **Control:** House Tsarya dominant
- **Development:** Vulcan swarm-built domes like Serro (smaller scale)
- **Architecture:** Smaller domes with sprawling manufacturing facilities
- **Character:** Oppressive, dirty, constantly in disrepair

**Manufacturing:**
- **Macro-factories:** Massive manufacturing complexes
- **Products:** Ground vehicles, warframe components, household items
- **Assembly:** Template writing and compilation facilities
- **Scale:** Multi-layered factory systems

**Working Conditions:**
- **Labor force:** Mostly apostates working to earn House membership
- **Compensation:** Paid in Tsaryan credits (not Rep or gold)
- **Membership process:** Extremely difficult; conditions essentially slavery
- **Incentive:** House membership provides status and better treatment
- **Culture:** Oppressive; constant environmental failures (gravity, air, pressure)

**Entertainment:**
- **Outlets:** Seedy and violent options reflecting desperation
- **Activities:** Alcohol, drugs, prostitution, gambling, cage fighting
- **Purpose:** Workers venting on underpaid wages
- **Class:** Not civilized; rough workers' entertainment

### Rho: The Jewel of Luna
- **Population:** Smallest city; most exclusive
- **Control:** Houses Silva, Cipriani (with significant apostate population)
- **Designation:** Entertainment capital of Sol system
- **Atmosphere:** Beautiful surface architecture; ugly shadow war underbelly

**Public Face:**
- **Architecture:** Stunning, beautiful cityscape
- **Population:** Apotheosized elites in high-end sleeves
- **Augmentation:** Even non-augmented sport high-end cosmetic enhancements
- **Appeal:** Extravagant caters to privileged whims
- **Water feature:** Luna Lake with Earth fauna (cloned by Vitae subsidiary)

**Dark Reality:**
- **Shadow War:** Major battleground for inter-House conflicts
- **Black market:** Significant illegal goods and services trade
- **Mercenary recruitment:** Primary location for hiring covert operatives
- **Soul selling:** Where influential people negotiate illegal operations

**Physical Layout:**
- **Dome:** Single large dome containing entire city
- **Center:** Artificial lake (Luna Lake) with parkland
- **Towers:** Four large gambling guild towers (Kimura, Tsarya, Silva subsidiaries)
- **Scale:** Most buildings 2-3 stories (opposite of Serro)
- **Gravity:** Distributed generators (some intentionally disabled for recreation)

**Facilities:**
- **Entertainment:** Theaters, arenas, virtuality kiosks, shopping
- **Social:** Hundreds of bars, nightclubs, and restaurants
- **Recreation:** Microgravity recreation areas
- **Culture:** Constant activity providing cover for illegal operations

**Talons of Freedom Presence:**
- **Location:** Base of operations for US military remnant
- **History:** Conducted devastating attacks on nascent Coalition
- **Current role:** Infiltrating Houses, recruiting disenfranchised
- **Economy:** Black market weapons and military hardware supplier
- **Operations:** Using Rho's underworld as cover for insurgency

## Research & Military Installations

### Orbital Stations
- **Quantity:** Several dozen research stations in lunar orbit
- **Function:** Classified and banned research
- **Operators:** Houses Silva and Jinzhan (controversial work)
- **Location:** High altitude to minimize surface impact
- **Security:** Heavily restricted and guarded

### Odysseus Station
- **Operator:** House Dalianis
- **Function:** Jump gate technology research and development
- **Specialization:** Jump drive modifications and new designs
- **Scale:** Largest of lunar research stations
- **Recent incident:** Large explosion three months ago; 300+ dead
- **Official story:** Oxygen line fire (implausible)

**Explosion Mystery:**
- **Alternative theory:** House Jinzhan mercenary extraction mission
- **Possible target:** Strategic technology or high-value individual
- **Evidence:** Rumored space bridge design theft attempt
- **Alternative:** Kill Dalianis target; steal mnemonic core
- **Current status:** Cover-up; investigation ongoing

## Demographics & Population Distribution

**Population Centers:**
- Serro: ~4 million (estimated)
- Asola: ~3 million (estimated)
- Rho: ~1 million (estimated)
- Smaller settlements: ~400,000 distributed

**Smaller Settlements:**
- Abandoned mining sites converted to temporary settlements
- Former dangerous research labs housing overflow population
- Many long since abandoned due to mining decline
