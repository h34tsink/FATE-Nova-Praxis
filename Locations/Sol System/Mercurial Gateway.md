## Basic Data

**Location:** Mercury orbital vicinity
**Type:** Jump gate facility — one of two in the Sol system
**Primary Function:** Interstellar transit for antimatter shipments; gateway to exoplanet systems
**Alternate Route:** Access to [[Locations/Sol System/Eye of Sol]] near Earth
**Operator:** [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani|House Cipriani]] (operational control)

## Overview

The Mercurial Gateway is one of two [[Glossary/Jump Gate|jump gates]] in the Sol system. Its position near Mercury is not incidental — it was sited to serve as the primary export route for Mercury's antimatter production. Nearly every shipment of processed antimatter leaving for the exoplanet systems passes through this gate.

[[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani|House Cipriani]] controls both the antimatter production at [[Locations/Sol System/Mercury|Base 10]] and the primary gate that ships it out. This double-lock on energy distribution is the foundation of Cipriani's political leverage across Coalition space.

## Function & Traffic

- **Primary traffic:** Antimatter shipments outbound to exoplanet colonies and outer system stations
- **Secondary traffic:** General interstellar transit for cargo, passengers, and military vessels
- **Alternate link:** Transit corridor to the [[Locations/Sol System/Eye of Sol]] near Earth provides inner-system routing flexibility
- **Gate infrastructure:** Coalition-maintained physical gate ring; Cipriani-controlled scheduling and security
- **Throughput:** Among the busiest transit points in Coalition space

## Strategic Significance

Control the Mercurial Gateway and you control when antimatter moves — and when it doesn't. Cipriani has never publicly weaponized this leverage, but every House and the Coalition Senate is aware of it.

The gate is also a natural chokepoint for intelligence: everything passing through is logged, scanned, and subject to Cipriani inspection. Smugglers and faction operatives use the Eye of Sol route specifically to avoid this scrutiny.

## Security Posture

- **Heavy Coalition naval presence** — protecting the gate is a Coalition military priority
- **Cipriani screening protocols** — cargo manifests and biometrics checked against House blacklists
- **Piracy interdiction** — ongoing threat from organized pirate groups targeting antimatter shipments
- **Insurgent concern** — Tellerus Fields survivors in Mercury's tunnel systems represent a potential threat to gate operations

## Campaign Hooks

- Transit through the gate while carrying something Cipriani would confiscate
- Disruption or seizure of the gate as a strategic move in House conflict
- Intelligence operation using gate traffic logs to track a target's movements
- Coordinating with the Eye of Sol route to move something without Cipriani's knowledge
