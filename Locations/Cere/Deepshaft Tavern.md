# Deepshaft Tavern

A notorious gathering place in Cere, frequented by miners, refugees, and information brokers. The Deepshaft Tavern is a hub for rumors, deals, and clandestine meetings.

## Notable Features
- Kal Paddock (information broker)
- Refugee patrons
- Shadow Market connections

## GM Notes
- Use for scenes involving social encounters, information gathering, or tense negotiations.
- Possible Aspects: *Web of Favors*, *Distrustful Locals*, *Hidden Connections*.
