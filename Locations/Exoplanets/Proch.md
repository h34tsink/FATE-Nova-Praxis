## Basic Data

**Location:** Exoplanet; significant distance from Sol
**Gravity:** 0.95G (near Earth-normal)
**Conditions:** Temperate; rich ecosystem; active dinosaur populations
**Estimated Population:** 600,000
**Status:** Independent; minimal Coalition control

## Overview

Proch holds special significance in human history as the first human colony established on an exoplanet. Now over a century old, the colony has developed into a thriving civilization despite—or perhaps because of—the presence of active dinosaur populations. The planet's diverse and dangerous ecosystem drives constant adaptation and innovation. Proch maintains relative independence from Coalition control, leveraging its distant location and harsh environment to preserve autonomy.

## Physical Environment

### Landscape & Ecology
- **Continents:** Multiple continents with varying ecosystems
- **Primary settlement:** Largest continent (temperate zone)
- **Forests:** Dense forests covering much of planet
- **Water:** Extensive river systems and oceans
- **Geology:** Geologically active; occasional seismic activity
- **Biodiversity:** Extremely high; thousands of unique species

### Dinosaur Populations

**Megafauna:**
- **Large herbivores:** Elephant-sized or larger plant-eaters
- **Large predators:** T-Rex equivalent creatures; apex predators
- **Flying reptiles:** Pterosaur-equivalent flying creatures
- **Marine reptiles:** Large creatures in aquatic environments
- **Small dinosaurs:** Smaller species (1-3 meters)
- **Unknowns:** Undiscovered species rumored

**Danger & Adaptation:**
- **Human threat:** Dinosaurs pose constant hazard to humans
- **Settlements:** Protected by fences, walls, and active defense systems
- **Hunting:** Dinosaur hunting common recreational and survival activity
- **Loss:** Regular casualties from wildlife encounters
- **Culture:** Dinosaur hunting drives much of colonial culture

### Flora
- **Vegetation:** Mix of Terran and alien-origin plant life
- **Forests:** Tall forests with dense understory
- **Agricultural zones:** Cleared areas for food production
- **Dangerous plants:** Some vegetation toxic to humans
- **Resources:** Various plants harvested for medicine and material

## Colonial History

### Initial Settlement
- **Timeline:** Over 100 years ago; first exoplanet colony
- **Purpose:** Scientific exploration and resource development
- **Population:** Initial 1000 colonists; rapidly grew
- **Challenges:** Dinosaur predation caused early casualties
- **Success:** Colony survived and thrived despite hazards

### Development Phases
- **Phase 1:** Survival and settlement establishment
- **Phase 2:** Population growth and infrastructure development
- **Phase 3:** Economic development and resource extraction
- **Phase 4:** Cultural divergence and independence movement

### Modern Status
- **Population:** 600,000+ spread across multiple settlements
- **Independence:** De facto independence; minimal Coalition oversight
- **Resistance:** Federation tunnels and conflicts somewhat contained
- **Development:** Thriving independent economy
- **Culture:** Unique culture evolved from dangerous environment

## Major Settlements

### Keppler: The Primary City
- **Population:** Estimated 250,000
- **Location:** Central plateau; defensible high ground
- **Architecture:** Combination of fortified structures and modern buildings
- **Defense:** Walls and active defense systems protect from dinosaur incursion
- **Character:** Center of government, commerce, and culture

**Notable Features:**
- **Arena:** Large dinosaur arena for hunting and sport
- **Market:** Major trading center; goods from across planet
- **Government:** Local council providing autonomous governance
- **Universities:** Research institutions studying native ecosystem
- **Industry:** Manufacturing and processing facilities

### Giordino: Secondary Settlement
- **Population:** Estimated 150,000
- **Location:** Coastal region; port facilities
- **Focus:** Fishing and maritime trade
- **Character:** Less formal than Keppler; frontier atmosphere
- **Trade:** Handles maritime commerce and resource export

### Smaller Settlements
- **Quantity:** 10-15 smaller communities
- **Combined population:** 200,000+
- **Distribution:** Spread across habitable regions
- **Character:** Mix of agricultural, industrial, and research communities
- **Autonomy:** Local self-governance common

## Federation Tunnels

### Historical Remnants
- **Origin:** Ancient Federation tunnels discovered beneath surface
- **Age:** Thousands of years old; well preserved
- **Purpose:** Unknown; possibly transportation or storage
- **Construction:** Advanced technology; materials still analyzed
- **Mystery:** No evidence of Federation presence on surface

### Exploration
- **Status:** Partially mapped; many sections unexplored
- **Depth:** Extensive; some tunnels very deep underground
- **Discovery:** New sections still being found
- **Contents:** Artifacts and technology inside tunnels
- **Danger:** Tunnel systems unstable; collapses occur

### Speculation
- **Purpose:** Ancient transportation network; possible weapon storage
- **Conflict:** Federation may have conducted operations on Proch
- **History:** Possibly site of ancient human-alien conflict
- **Evidence:** Weapons and fortifications found in some sections
- **Relevance:** May provide clues to human/Federation history

- **Status:** Technically Coalition member; actually independent

- **Taxes:** Minimal Coalition obligations; pays nominal tribute
- **Autonomy:** Maintains effective independence through distance and terrain
