## Basic Data

**Location:** Exoplanet; distance from Sol unknown
**Gravity:** 0.88G (slightly lower than Earth)
**Conditions:** Varied; featuring massive chasm systems; temperate regions
**Estimated Population:** 300,000
**Status:** Newer colony; rapid expansion

## Overview

Sagitta represents the Coalition's newest major colonial venture—an aggressive expansion into previously uncolonized territory. The planet's unusual geography, featuring massive chasm systems, has driven development of unique underground settlement patterns. However, the discovery of anomalous magnetic phenomena has made Sagitta a focus of intense scientific and military interest, transforming it from simple colony into strategic location.

## Physical Environment

### Chasm Systems
- **Primary feature:** Massive chasm/canyon network covering significant portion
- **Depth:** Some chasms extend kilometers underground
- **Width:** Varying from hundreds to thousands of meters
- **Length:** Individual systems extend for hundreds of kilometers
- **Geology:** Created by tectonic activity and water erosion

### Settlement Pattern
- **Chasm settlements:** Most population lives in settlements built into chasm walls
- **Advantages:** Natural protection from weather; abundant vertical building space
- **Disadvantages:** Difficult access; geological instability risks; isolation
- **Adaptation:** Architecture specially designed for chasm living
- **Expansion:** Settlements constantly expanding upward and downward

### Above-Chasm Regions
- **Surface:** Relatively barren; sparse vegetation
- **Climate:** Harsh; temperature extremes between chasm and surface
- **Settlements:** Few settlements on surface; mostly research installations
- **Resources:** Surface mining for minerals in some areas
- **Danger:** Extreme weather; difficult to travel safely

## Magnetic Anomaly

### Discovery
- **Timeline:** Identified within last 10 years
- **Location:** Northern continent; primary anomaly centered on region
- **Strength:** Extremely strong; unusual magnetic signature
- **Extent:** Regional; not planet-wide but significant area
- **Nature:** Unknown; unlike any known natural phenomenon

### Characteristics
- **Field strength:** Far exceeds normal planetary magnetic field
- **Pattern:** Irregular patterns; not typical magnetic pole configuration
- **Effects:** Interferes with equipment, navigation, communications
- **Variation:** Field strength fluctuates unpredictably
- **Expansion:** Evidence suggests anomaly is slowly growing

### Research Status
- **Investigation:** Multiple Coalition research teams stationed at anomaly
- **Priority:** High; Coalition considering anomaly strategically important
- **Findings:** Research ongoing; results mostly classified
- **Theories:** Origin unknown; Federation involvement speculated
- **Weapons speculation:** Theory that anomaly could be weaponizable technology

### Potential Origins
- **Natural phenomena:** Possibly unique geological process
- **Federation remnant:** Possible Federation technology or weapon
- **Alien installation:** Unknown alien technology buried underground
- **Colony accident:** Possible result of Coalition experimentation
- **Misidentification:** Small chance anomaly has mundane explanation

## Settlements & Development

### Primary Settlement: Chasm City
- **Population:** Estimated 150,000
- **Location:** Largest chasm system; primary population center
- **Architecture:** Multi-level city built into chasm walls
- **Vertical extent:** Extends 1000+ meters from chasm rim to bottom
- **Features:** Markets, residential areas, manufacturing, administration

**Development Status:**
- **Growth rate:** Rapid; population increasing quickly
- **Construction:** Continuous expansion and building
- **Organization:** Coalition administration with House participation
- **Problems:** Geological instability; occasional structural failures
- **Supplies:** Imported from other Coalition worlds; expensive

### Secondary Settlements
- **Quantity:** 10-15 smaller chasm settlements
- **Combined population:** 100,000+
- **Development:** Mix of agricultural and mining operations
- **Connection:** Connected by tunnel systems and surface transport
- **Character:** Each community developing distinct character

### Research Stations
- **Magnetic anomaly base:** Large military research installation
- **Population:** Estimated 50,000+ (military, scientists, support)
- **Location:** Northern continent; at anomaly center
- **Purpose:** Investigating magnetic phenomenon
- **Security:** Heavily guarded; restricted access
- **Speculation:** Possible weapons testing facility

## Economy & Resources

### Agriculture
- **Vertical farming:** Hydroponics and vertical gardens in chasm settlements
- **Limited scope:** Cannot feed colony entirely; requires imports
- **Crops:** Focus on nutritious crops providing calories
- **Supplement:** Hunting of native animals in some areas
- **Challenge:** Limited space constrains agricultural production

### Mining
- **Primary resources:** Minerals extracted from surrounding rock
- **Operations:** Open pit and underground mining in chasm walls
- **Minerals:** Iron, copper, and valuable minerals
- **Processing:** Facilities in chasm settlements
- **Export:** Ore shipped to Coalition worlds

### Manufacturing
- **Scale:** Limited; mostly supports local needs
- **Products:** Structural materials, tools, equipment for settlements
- **Labor:** Significant employment in manufacturing sector
- **Growth:** Expanding as colony develops
