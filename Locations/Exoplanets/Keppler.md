## Overview

Keppler is the principal city on Proch and a major industrial center tied to historic shipyard expansion and wartime production culture.

## Strategic Role

- Administrative and industrial anchor for Proch operations
- Key transit and staffing hub feeding Icarus logistics corridors

## Campaign Hooks

- Strike action in a critical shipyard district during escalating unrest
- Investigate legacy war-production contracts now tied to modern House leverage
