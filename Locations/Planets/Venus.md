### Venus Overview
- **Gravity:** 0.91G
- **Day Length:** 243 [[Earth]] days
- **Estimated Population:** 438,000 (413k in Gondolin, 25k on The Maiden)

#### Notable Locations

##### Gondolin
- **Nature:** A hidden [[Groups/Apostate]] enclave and the largest of its kind in the solar system.
- **Population:** Approximately 435,000, residing in a complex built by Vulcan swarms, now facing overpopulation issues.
- **Government and Society:** Governed by an elected mayor, currently a transhumanist SIM named Camber Roche. The city struggles with strict population control measures, including laws restricting families to one child.
- **Cultural Dynamics:** Inhabited by a diverse population including former Americans, Italians, Greeks, and Africans. Despite electing a transhumanist mayor, most residents are Purists, wary of Apotheosis.
- **Economy and Infrastructure:** Features resurrection chambers, Apotheosis facilities, and a secret working compiler crucial for survival and trade, operated by a Savant named Gulo.

##### The Venusian Maiden (The Maiden)
- **Function:** A research station set up to study Venus, contributing to understanding of planetary formation and [[Earth]]'s history.
- **Additional Activities:** Houses one of House Dalianis’ weapon labs and serves as a covert meeting point for dealings with Gondolin.
- **Population:** Around 25,000, involved in research and experimental work.

#### Venus in the Coalition's Perspective
- **Environmental Conditions:** Venus is characterized by its volcanic landscapes, rivers of lava, and a toxic atmosphere, making it largely undesirable for conventional Coalition activities.
- **Strategic Value:** Despite the harsh conditions, Venus serves as a refuge for apostates and a hub for clandestine research and development, underlining its unique position in Coalition politics and power play.

#### Gondolin's Significance
- **Survival Against Odds:** Thriving in an inhospitable environment, Gondolin stands as a testament to human resilience and ingenuity, especially for those seeking freedom outside Coalition control.
- **Technological Marvel:** The existence of a working compiler, a closely guarded secret, makes Gondolin a significant but vulnerable player in the broader Coalition landscape.

#### The Maiden's Role
- **Research Hub:** Its focus on planetary research places The Maiden at the forefront of scientific exploration, contributing to a deeper understanding of planetary phenomena.
- **Covert Operations:** The station's dual role in research and covert activities, including its connections with Gondolin, highlights the complex interplay of politics and science within the Coalition's realm.
