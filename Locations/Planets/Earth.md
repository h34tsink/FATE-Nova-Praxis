### Earth Overview
- **Gravity:** 1G
- **Day Length:** 24 hours
- **Population:** Presumed 0, officially no humans

#### Notable Locations

##### Brasilia
- **History:** Originally the Alliance capital city, Brasilia was the ground zero for the Technophage deployment. This event marked a significant turn in the Consolidation Wars.
- **Current State:** The region, spanning over 300 square kilometers, is now completely dominated by machines. Periodic attempts by the Technophage to establish new nano factories are continually thwarted by orbital defenses.

##### Marianas Trench
- **Significance:** Known for a heavy concentration of technophage drone nanofactories. This deep-sea location poses unique challenges for monitoring and intervention.
- **Recent Developments:** There has been an increase in drone activity, suggesting heightened technophage activity, leading to intensified scrutiny and concern from the Coalition Senate.

##### Denver Interplanetary Spaceport
- **Role:** Originally a major spaceport, it has transformed into a central hub for technophage's weapons and defenses. A unique shield constructed by the technophage protects the area from orbital attacks.
- **Mystery:** The spaceport, now concealed by a light screen, is a hotbed for speculation. The frequent movement of drones into the spaceport's barriers, never to re-emerge, suggests ominous developments.

##### Vantage
- **Location:** Situated at the Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 1, between Earth and [[Luna]].
- **Function:** Acts as a museum of Earth’s history and a strategic base for Coalition forces maintaining the quarantine on Earth.
- **Cultural Significance:** A popular destination for those nostalgic about Earth, rumored to be staffed by individuals sympathetic to the remnants opposing the Coalition.

##### Eye of Sol
- **Position:** Located at the Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 2.
- **History:** This jump gate, crucial in the Coalition's space infrastructure, was rebuilt after being destroyed in the Consolidation Wars.
- **Facilities:** Includes a hotel for travelers and a museum dedicated to the history of the Consolidation Wars and the Coalition's formation.

##### Ansari Station
- **Location:** Orbiting at the Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 3.
- **Function:** [[House Dalianis]]’ largest shipyard, playing a crucial role in the Coalition's fleet construction and maintenance.
- **Community:** The station is akin to a bustling city in space, housing thousands of workers and their families, and providing a wide range of services and amenities.

##### Primatech Labs
- **Situated:** At the Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 4.
- **Purpose:** A key research and development facility for [[House Jinzhan]], focused on ordinance and armaments for quarantine enforcement.
- **Current Focus:** Analysis of a technophage-modified drone, stirring up inter-house tensions and intrigue within the Coalition.

##### Sentinel Station
- **Location:** At the Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 5.
- **Status:** The largest Coalition military installation in the galaxy, operated primarily by [[House Silva]] and [[House Tsarya]].
- **Capabilities:** Houses advanced military production facilities, resurrection chambers for Coalition soldiers, and is one of the sites for undergoing Apotheosis for military personnel.

#### Earth's Role Post-Technophage
Following the technophage catastrophe, Earth transformed from a cradle of humanity into a quarantined wasteland, dominated by autonomous war machines and technological hazards. The surrounding space stations and facilities at Lagrange points play critical roles in observing, researching, and containing the ongoing threats emanating from Earth, while also serving as strategic assets for the Coalition's broader space operations.

#### The Technophage Catastrophe
- **Origins:** Unleashed in Brasilia to end the Consolidation Wars, but spiraled beyond control
- **Aftermath:** Autonomous war machines spread globally, rendering Earth uninhabitable and prompting a cease-fire
- **Adaptation:** Technophage's unexpected adaptability to defenses thwarted efforts to reclaim Earth

#### Current Situation
- **Earth's Condition:** A wasteland dominated by lethal machines, with major technophage facilities in key locations
- **Denver Spaceport:** Suspected site of significant technophage activity, protected by a robust shield
- **Marianas Trench:** A hotbed for technophage activity, difficult to monitor and attack
- **Vantage:** A vital quarantine station, recently targeted by a technophage-modified drone
- **Primatech Labs:** Analyzing the drone, causing tensions among Coalition Houses
- **Sentinel Station:** A critical military hub with advanced production capabilities

#### Coalition's Stance
Despite Earth's desolation, its strategic locations like Vantage, Ansari Station, and Sentinel Station play crucial roles in Coalition operations, maintaining quarantine and military dominance.
