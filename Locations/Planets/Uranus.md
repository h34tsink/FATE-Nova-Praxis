### Uranus Overview
- **Gravity:** 0.89G
- **Day Length:** 17.9 hours
- **Estimated Population:** 84,000

#### Notable Settlements/Locations

##### The Colony
- **Nature:** A large [[apostate]] community located deep within Uranus's atmosphere.
- **Structure:** Composed of a network of interconnected spacecraft and prefabricated structures, including a notable ship, The Kraken.
- **Power Source:** Primarily relies on APEX drives from the ships, supplemented by cold fusion reactors using Uranus's hydrogen.
- **Society:** Characterized by a tight-knit community of vagabonds and the displaced, where cooperation is key and crimes are severely punished.
- **Current Tensions:** Facing threats from the Children of Dionysus, a pirate group, and dealing with the mysterious disappearance of the transient ship Red Herron.

#### Uranus's Role in the Coalition
- **Limited Resource Interest:** Due to its composition and distance from Sol, Uranus is not a primary source of resources for the Coalition.
- **Refuge for Apostates:** Its relative unimportance to the Coalition makes it an ideal haven for apostates seeking to live outside Coalition control.

#### Sociopolitical Dynamics
- **The Colony's Independence:** The Colony's existence as an autonomous enclave reflects the ongoing struggle for independence and self-sufficiency among apostates.
- **External Threats:** The interference from the Children of Dionysus highlights the vulnerability of such enclaves to external pressures, both from criminal elements and potentially from Coalition forces.
- **Uncertain Future:** The disappearance of the Red Herron and the fear of confrontation with either the pirates or the Coalition reveal the precarious nature of life in the outer reaches of the solar system.
