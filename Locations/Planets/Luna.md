### Luna Overview
- **Gravity:** 0.16G
- **Day Length:** 27.3 [[Earth]] days
- **Estimated Population:** 8.5 million

#### Notable Locations

##### Serro
- **Description:** Serro, the largest lunar city, has evolved from the original colonies into a vast array of domes and towering complexes known as hives. These hives, some reaching up to 150 stories, are interconnected by bridges and mag-rail trains, with skiffs available for private transportation.
- **Attractions:** The city's surface level is a bustling tourist attraction, featuring shops, low-gravity play areas, and unique sports like Crash fields, a variant of American football adapted to low gravity.
- **Demographics:** Predominantly inhabited by House Silva members, Serro is a hub for biotech research, with numerous labs and residential areas for scientists and workers.

##### Asola
- **Characteristics:** As the second-largest city on Luna, Asola contrasts with Serro by housing smaller domes and expansive manufacturing facilities. These macro-factories, crucial for House Tsarya, produce ground vehicles and military hardware components.
- **Social Structure:** Asola is home to a large population of poor workers, many of whom are apostates striving for House membership. The city reflects the harsh realities of their lives, with suboptimal living conditions and a prevalence of seedy entertainment to distract from the daily grind.
- **Environmental Concerns:** The city grapples with environmental issues, including sporadic gravity fluctuations and subpar air quality, creating a challenging living environment.

##### Rho
- **Identity:** Known as the "Jewel of Luna," Rho stands out as a city dedicated to entertainment and relaxation. It is a melting pot of diverse cultures, hosting a significant [[Groups/Apostate]] population and the Alliance remnant force, the Talons of Freedom.
- **Backing:** Supported primarily by Houses Silva and Cipriani, Rho offers a variety of leisure activities and serves as a cultural and recreational haven on Luna.

##### Odysseus Station
- **Purpose:** Positioned in lunar orbit, Odysseus Station is a significant research facility operated by House Dalianis. The station is at the forefront of jump gate technology, spearheading advancements and innovations in space travel.
- **Significance:** As a hub for cutting-edge research, the station plays a vital role in the Coalition's efforts to explore and expand into new territories, making it a key strategic asset.

#### Significance of Luna in Coalition Space
Luna holds a unique place in human history as the first celestial body to be stepped on by humans. Its transformation from the site of the first manned missions to a thriving colony with sprawling cities and advanced research stations reflects humanity's technological progress and ambition. The diverse array of cities and facilities on Luna showcases the intricate balance of power, culture, and technology within the Coalition's spacefaring civilization.
