# Opportunity Four

A remote [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura|House Kimura]] research outpost in an unregistered system with no jump ring connection. Operated by [[Dr Haruto Nakamura|Dr. Nakamura]]'s Mindscapes subsidiary team. This is where [[Chimera]] was created through illegal ego-merging experiments on the crew.

## Layout

- **Outer hatch:** Locked to Nakamura's CID — requires his authorization or bypass
- **Lab section:** Where the ego-merging experiments were conducted; Nakamura's primary workspace
- **Digital systems:** Chimera has embedded himself in the outpost's network
- **Escape pod:** Chimera has one prepped for evacuation to the planet surface
- **Comm-buoy:** System-wide communications relay — Chimera will destroy it when the PCs arrive, cutting all Extranet access

## Defenses

- Security drones controlled by Chimera through the outpost systems
- Nakamura's research staff (non-combatants, but may impede PCs)
- Digital warfare — Chimera controls environmental systems (locks, atmosphere, power)
- **Isolation:** No Extranet after comm-buoy destruction; no reinforcements, no backup

## Strategic Details

| Detail | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Location | Remote system, no registered settlements |
| Access | Jump coordinates from Nowak or Kestrel's comms |
| Chimera's position | Inside outpost digital systems; escape pod prepped |
| Nakamura's position | Lab section; will negotiate if cornered |
| Kestrel's ETA | ~30 minutes after PCs arrive |
| Victory conditions | Capture Chimera's core + survive Kestrel's assault |

## Planet Surface

Below the outpost, on the planet surface, Chimera has stashed the original crew ship from their first mission. If he reaches the escape pod and makes it planetside, he can jump out of the system.

## Campaign Relevance

The crew learned about Opportunity Four in Session 6 when they were captured and experimented on. Returning here is the climax of the [[The Chimera Conspiracy - Arc Tracker|Chimera arc]] (Session 10 / Part 4). Coordinates are obtained from [[Isabella Nowak|Nowak]] (if reached alive) or from [[Kestrel]]'s intercepted communications.
