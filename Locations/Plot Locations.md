## **Celestial Locations**

### **1. House Cipriani's Holo-Masquerade Ball**

- **Location:** **Festus Station**
  - A grandiose orbital space station positioned near **Jupiter's** moon, **Io**. Known for its lavish events and opulent architecture, the station has panoramic views of the massive gas giant and its volcanic moon.

### **2. Data Center (Elysian Enigma Project)**

- **Location:** **Lunar Base Delta**
  - Nestled in the Sea of Tranquility on the **Moon**, this highly secure data center is shielded from solar radiation and largely inaccessible, requiring specific clearance codes to even approach.

### **3. Victor Belmont & Xander Kress Rendezvous**

- **Location:** **Neo-Venice**
  - A sprawling floating city on **Titan**, Saturn's moon, which features a unique blend of cryovolcanic structures and methane lakes. Known for its black-market activities and concealed meetings.

### **4. Ariadne Vega's Cybernetic Puzzle Hub**

- **Location:** **Helian Hub**
  - An enigmatic space station positioned near the exoplanet **Helios Prime**. The hub is known for its advanced VR and simulation sectors, where cyber experts come to solve the universe's greatest puzzles.

### **5. House Cipriani's Research Facility**

- **Location:** **Phaeton Labs**
  - Located on the dwarf planet **Eris**, it's isolated and away from the prying eyes of rival houses. The facility boasts state-of-the-art labs and research centers.

### **6. Rafael Cortez's Safehouse**

- **Location:** **Deimos Haven**
  - A secretive underground facility on Mars' moon, **Deimos**. With tunnels carved deep into the moon, it's a safe hideout for those wanting to disappear for a while.

### **7. Marissa "Speed Daemon" Delgado's Street Race**

- **Location:** **Prometheus Ringway**
  - A famous racing track built on the giant ring structure orbiting **Umbra-4**, an exoplanet known for its neon-rich atmosphere and adrenaline junkies.

### **8. The Forbidden Earth**

- **Location:** **The Silent Cradle**
  - The remnants of what once was the bustling metropolis of **Shanghai** on **Earth**. Now, it stands as a silent testament to humanity's past mistakes, where the gray-goo event is said to have started. While it's forbidden to approach, it is rumored that the deepest secrets of the universe, and possibly the key to understanding the Elysian Enigma, might still be buried here.
