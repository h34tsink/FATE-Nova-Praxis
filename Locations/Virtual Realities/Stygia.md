## Stygia: The Premier Virtuality

---

### Overview

- **Position:** Number one virtuality in use.

- **Setting:**
  - Massive urban metropolis inside a small Dyson sphere.
  - Mimics real-world physics and architecture, offering a grounded, less fantastical experience.

- **Purpose:** Mainly used as a social platform.
  - Business and casual meetings across interstellar distances.
  - Popular venue for business deals.

---

### Demographics

- **Visitors:** Attracts members from all Houses and thousands of SIMs.

---

### Importance

- **Status:** Holds a crucial position in the virtual world.
  - High popularity and Kimura's controlling interest make it a frequent cyber attack target.
  - Despite challenges, remains one of the most important and popular persistent virtualities.

---

### Glitches in Stygia

- **Black Spheres:** Reports of mysterious black spheres appearing and disappearing in the city.
  - Resulting in missing elements that are hardcoded into the city.

---

### Issues with AI

- **Unusual Behavior:** Alpha and Beta AIs are acting out of character.
  - Some exceed their limits, while others shut down entirely.

- **Kimura's Response:** Maintenance crews attribute it to faulty code or AIs needing pruning. However, not everyone is convinced.
