## The Crossroads: Fantasy Virtuality

---

### Overview

- **Comparison:** In many ways, the polar opposite of [[Stygia]].
  
- **Setting:** 
  - Sprawling city and rural landscape.
  - Fantasy version of Europe during the Middle Ages.

- **Demographics:** Attracts serious escapists who find the real world too complex, stressful, or mundane.

---

### Features

- **Random Event Algorithms:** Designed to maintain user interest indefinitely.

- **Community Events:** Weekly and monthly activities such as invasions or monster hunts.
  
- **Resident SIMs:** Prefer to stay away from "tourist" events, residing in small communities where these events don't intrude.

---

### Popularity

- **Audience:** Immensely popular among escapists.
  
- **Learning Curve:** New visitors might find that their usual techniques don't apply in this fantasy setting.

---

### Issues in The Crossroads

- **It’s Dangerous to go Alone:** 
  - New players are offered seemingly powerful items for minimal rep.
  - Items have a homing algorithm attracting player killers after a set time.

- **Peace and Quiet Intruded:** 
  - Random events now affect the quiet towns where SIMs live.
  - Events are now causing real pain to the SIMs when attacked, indicating a likely malicious change in the code.

---

### Current Crisis

- **Modulator Investigation:** Modulators are quickly probing the anomaly, especially the induced pain in SIMs due to the events.
