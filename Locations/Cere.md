# Cere

**Type:** Mining Colony  
**Environment:** Harsh, failing infrastructure, dangerous  
**Notable NPCs:** [[Kal Paddock]] (Deepshaft Tavern)  
**Factions:** Shadow Market presence, [[Talons of Freedom]] sympathizers  

## Overview

Cere is a brutal mining colony at the edge of viable habitability. Infrastructure fails regularly, resources are scarce, and the locals are hard-edged from years of survival. It's the kind of place where questions asked too loudly get you tracked, and where the right information can be bought—if you know whom to ask and what to pay.

## Key Locations

- **[[Cere/Deepshaft Tavern]]** — Hub for miners, information brokers, and refuge seekers
- **Mining Shafts** — Dangerous tunnels, unstable gravity, industrial hazards
- **Refugee Quarters** — Where the displaced hide and wait
- **Abandoned Mining Access** — Old, unmapped passages leading deeper into the colony

## Session 9 Relevance

Cere is where [[Valare|Valare's fork]] has been hiding. The harsh environment and patchwork social networks make it ideal for someone trying to disappear while still monitoring threats through digital means.

## GM Notes

**Use Cere for:**

- Environmental hazards that force choices (speed vs. care)
- Social encounters with distrustful locals
- Digital countermeasures and network monitoring
- Escalating pressure as factions converge

**Possible Complications:**

- *Hostile Environment* — Bad air, unstable gravity, power fluctuations
- *Patchwork Systems* — Outdated networks, unreliable comm
- *Distrustful Locals* — Refugees and miners don't trust outsiders
- *Shadow Market Presence* — Rivals closing in
