# Cere

A mining colony known for its harsh environment, patchwork systems, and distrustful locals. Cere is a prime location for investigation, exploration, and social encounters in the search for Valare's fork.

## Notable Features
- Deepshaft Tavern
- Mining shafts and tunnels
- Refugee quarters

## GM Notes
- Use Cere for scenes involving exploration, hazards, and negotiation with locals.
- Possible Aspects: *Hostile Environment*, *Patchwork Systems*, *Distrustful Locals*.
