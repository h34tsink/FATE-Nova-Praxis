## Overview

In the Nova Praxis setting, virtual realities are persistent shared simulated environments accessible via [[Glossary/Extranet|Extranet]] and [[Glossary/Mesh Web|Mesh Web]] interfaces. They range from commercial entertainment spaces to high-security business environments to Apostate-run off-grid domains. Full-immersion virtuality is common for the Apotheosized class; limited access is available to most citizens.

## Major Virtual Realities

- **[[Locations/Virtual Realities/Stygia]]** — The premier commercial virtuality; built as a Dyson sphere city simulation. Primary venue for high-stakes business, social networking, and entertainment for the elite.
- **[[Locations/Virtual Realities/The Crossroads]]** — Fantasy-themed escapist virtuality with a large active user base. Currently experiencing an in-fiction crisis that has spilled into real-world consequences.

## Mechanics Note

Access, infiltration, and operations within virtual realities use the **SINC** skill. Persistent virtual environments have their own Aspects and threat profiles. See [[Rules and Mechanics/Rules Quick Reference - Unified]] for active rules.
