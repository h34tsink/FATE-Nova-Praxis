# Galaxy Map

**Summary:** A map-of-content for known human space — every star system, world, station, and significant location currently catalogued in the vault. Use this as the navigation hub when you need to know where something is or what's near it. Travel between systems requires a [[Glossary/Jump Gates|Jump Gate]] connection unless otherwise noted.

## Travel Notes

- **Jump Gates** are required for interstellar travel. The Coalition maintains a network of fixed jump rings; the [[Locations/Santa Maria|Santa Maria]] flotilla uniquely carries its own deployable jump apparatus aboard the *Gatekeeper*.
- **Sol jump rings:** The [[Locations/Sol System/Mercurial Gateway]] near Mercury and the [[Locations/Sol System/Eye of Sol]] near Earth are Sol's two jump rings. Most outbound traffic flows through one or the other.
- **Sub-light travel** within a system is routine but slow on a planet-to-planet scale. Plot accordingly.
