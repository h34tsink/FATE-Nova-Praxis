Born of an experiment in cutting-edge mnemonics technology, **[[Chimera]]** is a gestalt entity, a being created from the **PCs' merged egos**. Every **Apotheosized** PC who had gone on the mission to prospect the outpost has been merged into [[Chimera]], all inhabiting a single sleeve. [[Chimera]] remembers everything they did, knows everything about them, and **fears them**.

Forking an ego is illegal and a severe breach of the **Humanity Preservation Act**. The experiments that led to the creation of [[Chimera]] go far beyond simple forking—they could **kill and erase** everyone and everything involved.

**Note:** [[Chimera]] can identify as male, female, or neither, depending on its composition. If mostly composed of male egos, it generally considers itself male, and the same goes for female egos. To maintain consistency, we will assume [[Chimera]] identifies as male in this writing.

---

### What Does Chimera Know?

- **[[Chimera]]** has the complete memories of every Apotheosized PC, meaning he knows almost everything going on.
- He has been covering his tracks and distancing himself from the PCs by **wiping data** from their personal data logs and social networks.
- [[Chimera]] **blames Nowak** for his creation and the mess they’re in now. Being somewhat insane, he has targeted her for revenge by fanning the flames of rebellion on the **Icarus Hub** and aiding the **[[Talons of Freedom]]**.
- **[Secret: 3]** The [[Apostate]] uprising is backed by the **[[Talons of Freedom]]**.
- **[Secret: 5]** The **[[Talons of Freedom]]** were aided and encouraged to act now by [[Chimera]].
