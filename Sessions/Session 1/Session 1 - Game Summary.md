
## Session 1 Summary

**Date:** Saturday, July 9th, 104 Post-Scarcity (2146 AD)

### Overview
The session began with our characters scattered across the System, each drawn into a plot involving a mysterious new faction of Apostates. Their individual stories converged around the theft of advanced technology and a looming threat to Europa Station.

---

### Doc (Pilot & Engineer)
- Diagnosed a ship malfunction and intercepted a distress call from an APEX reactor explosion survivor.
- Rescued the survivor, who revealed a stolen high-tech mech and a plan to attack Europa Station.

### Kallius (Mnemonic Expert)
- Helped a high-reputation individual recover from severe fragmentation.
- Learned his custom walker was stolen by Alaric "The Broker" Baines, implicating the Apostates and Parallax Station.

### Grace (MMO Specialist)
- Contacted by a former guildmate about an Apostate plot using a stolen walker and CIST laptops for a real-world operation.
- Discovered Crossroads MMO was being used for secret meetings.

### Dustin "Grift" Halloway (Data Retrieval Specialist)
- Visited by Briar Blackwood, then contracted for a security simulation program.
- Noted similarities between the simulation and Europa Station's security protocols.

### Aeddarius Crucial (Newly Minted Lord)
- Assigned to infiltrate Europa Station, suspected as the Apostates' next target.
- Took on a new identity as a Sensi Group financier and recruited Azaria Dawson.

### Azaria Dawson (Actress & Spy)
- Posed as an art historian at an auction, was captured by art thieves, and escaped by foiling their forgery plot.
- Joined Aeddarius to investigate the situation on Europa Station, already contracted for the Sensi project.

---

### Key Events & Convergence
- Doc, Kallius, Grace, and Grift tracked the stolen mech to Parallax Station and overcame its guards, with Kallius showing unexpected strength.
- On Europa Station, Aeddarius and Azaria discovered the maintenance team had been replaced by Apostate operatives.
- Azaria's social skills revealed critical information about the faction's intentions.

---

### Session Outcome
- The party uncovered the Apostates' plot and began to understand the scope of the threat to Europa Station.
- Their actions set the stage for future collaboration and deeper investigation into the new faction.
