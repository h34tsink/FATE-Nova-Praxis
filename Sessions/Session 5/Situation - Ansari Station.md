# Session 5: Situation – Ansari Station

## Location Overview

**Ansari Station** is House Dalianis’ largest shipyard, located at E-M Lagrange Point 3, directly opposite Luna on the far side of Earth.

This massive space station is the largest shipyard in the known galaxy, home to thousands of workers and their families. The station is structured as a multi-layered city of stacked disks, primarily populated by House Dalianis citizens working in manufacturing, but also hosting people from other Houses who provide supporting services, entertainment, and more.

## Current Situation

The freighter previously owned by Icarus Interplanetary, a subsidiary of House Cipriani, has been in dry dock for repairs ever since you salvaged it.
