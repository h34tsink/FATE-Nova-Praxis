### Mission Framework for "The Compiler Heist"

#### Goal

- **Primary Goal:** Successfully steal the unique compiler template from the high-security office.

#### Time Between Segments

- Each Segment represents approximately 30 minutes of in-game time.

#### Framework Characteristics

1. **High-Security Environment:** The skyscraper is equipped with advanced security measures like biometric scanners and surveillance cameras.
2. **Corporate Activity:** The office is bustling during the day, offering cover but also increasing the risk of exposure.
3. **Advanced Tech Presence:** The presence of cybernetically-enhanced guards and security drones adds a layer of challenge.

#### Framework Complexity

- Complexity Rating: 6 (representing the intricate steps and coordination required for the heist).

#### Framework Complications

1. **Infiltration (Complexity 6):** Bypassing or deceiving the security system to gain entry.
2. **Navigation (Complexity 6):** Moving undetected inside the office, avoiding cameras and guards.
3. **Retrieval (Complexity 8):** Accessing the vault and decrypting the compiler template.
4. **Exfiltration (Complexity 6):** Exiting with the template, evading or confronting any pursuit.
5. **Intel Gathering (Complexity 4):** Collecting information on the House's projects discreetly.
6. **Planting False Data (Complexity 4):** Uploading misleading information without detection.

#### Success or Failure

- Players must strategically use their Skills to reduce the Framework’s Complexity to 0.
- Failure can lead to increased security response, capture, or loss of the template.

#### Running the Framework

- Players decide which Traits to apply for each Complication, with rolls determining success.
- Creating an Advantage might involve hacking a system to disable cameras temporarily or gaining an ally within the office.
- Using Advantages could involve applying Bennies to crucial rolls, like cracking the vault's safe.
- Dealing with Disadvantages might involve increased security measures if an alarm is triggered.
- Framework Failure occurs if the Complexity is not reduced to 0, leading to possible capture or mission abortion.

#### Special Elements

- **Savants and SINC Skills:** Can be used to bypass digital security measures or gain crucial information.
- **Favors:** Players can call in favors to assist in overcoming specific challenges.
- **Combat:** If combat occurs, it's resolved as larger-scale outcomes rather than individual skirmishes.

#### Making it Your Own

- Customize NPCs and environmental details to fit the group’s story and characters.
- Introduce unexpected twists, like a rival group appearing or a betrayal within the team.
