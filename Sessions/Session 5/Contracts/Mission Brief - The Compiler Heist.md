# Session 5: The Compiler Heist

## Overview

The team is tasked with infiltrating a high-security corporate office to steal a rare compiler template. The mission involves careful planning, stealth, and negotiation with potential allies and adversaries. Success could mean significant rewards, but failure may have lasting consequences.

---

### Mission Brief: "The Compiler Heist"

#### Objective

- **Primary Goal:** Infiltrate a well-guarded subsidiary office of a powerful House to steal a unique compiler template. This template is critical for developing advanced tech that your client desires.

- **Secondary Goals:** Gather intel on the House's upcoming projects, and if possible, plant false data to mislead them.

#### Location

- **Setting:** A high-tech urban environment, with the office located in a skyscraper bustling with corporate activity during the day and tight security at night.

- **Security Features:** Biometric scanners, surveillance cameras, patrolling security drones, and cybernetically-enhanced guards.

#### Challenges

1. **Infiltration:** Finding a way into the building without raising alarms. Options include hacking the security system, creating a distraction, or using social engineering.

2. **Navigation:** Avoiding detection while inside. This involves stealth, disabling or evading security measures, and potentially dealing with employees.

3. **Retrieval:** Accessing the secure vault where the compiler template is stored. This requires bypassing advanced security measures like multi-factor authentication systems or cracking a high-end safe.

4. **Exfiltration:** Exiting the building with the template without being caught. This could involve a pre-planned escape route, a high-speed chase, or a stealthy exit.

#### NPCs

- **Target:** A mid-level executive who has access to the vault.

- **Security Chief:** A former military officer with a keen eye for detail and no tolerance for breaches.

- **IT Specialist:** A potential ally who disagrees with the House's ethics and might be willing to help for the right price or persuasion.

#### Complications

- The House may have insider information about the heist, making the mission more dangerous.

- The compiler template is encrypted and needs a specific key or expertise to be useful.

- Rival groups might also be after the template, leading to unexpected confrontations.

#### Rewards

- Substantial monetary payment from the client.

- Gaining a rare and valuable piece of technology.

- Potential allies or enemies based on how the mission is handled.

#### Ethical Considerations

- The impact of stealing the compiler template on innocent employees and the broader implications in the world of Nova Praxis.

- Decisions on how to deal with guards and employees – lethal or non-lethal approaches.

### Detailed Specifics for Silvermist's Interaction with the Players

#### The Initial Meeting with Grift

- **Location:** "The Nebula Lounge," a luxurious yet discreet bar known for its privacy and clientele from the higher echelons of society. The lounge is dimly lit, with holographic projections of distant galaxies adorning the walls, creating an atmosphere of otherworldly elegance.

- **Scene Setup:** Grift receives a cryptic message on a secure channel, directing him to meet at the Nebula Lounge at a specific time. The message includes a passphrase for identification.

- **Encounter Details:** Silvermist awaits in a private booth, shrouded in shadows. She greets Grift with a nod, offering a seat. She wastes no time, swiftly moving to discuss the mission, laying out holographic blueprints and data files as she talks.

#### Mission Briefing

- **Objective Explanation:** Silvermist details the importance of the compiler template, emphasizing its rarity and the security measures protecting it.

- **Intel Provided:** She shares intelligence on the building's layout, security shifts, potential weak points, and the location of the vault. She also provides information about key personnel, including the security chief and the IT specialist.

- **Resources Offered:** Advanced hacking tools, a blueprint of the security system, fake IDs, and a small device capable of temporarily disabling electronic locks.

- **[[Sessions/Session 7/Timeline]]:** She stresses the urgency of the mission, indicating a narrow window of opportunity due to an upcoming security system upgrade.

#### Communication Style and Follow-Ups

- **Secure Communications:** Post-meeting, Silvermist uses an encrypted messaging system for updates, coded language, and emergency contacts. She expects brief, clear updates and dislikes unnecessary chatter.

- **Check-Ins:** Scheduled check-ins before and after critical mission phases. She insists on being updated immediately if anything goes awry.

#### Handling Conflict and Failure

- **Scenario Planning:** Silvermist expects the players to plan for contingencies and to adapt as needed. She cautions against underestimating the security team.

- **Response to Failure:** If the mission fails or the team is compromised, Silvermist becomes distant and calculating. She may offer a secondary mission to salvage the Situation or cut ties if she deems the team a liability.

- **Redemption Opportunities:** Depending on the nature of the failure, Silvermist might offer a chance for redemption through another, possibly more dangerous mission, or require the team to fix their mistake without additional support.
