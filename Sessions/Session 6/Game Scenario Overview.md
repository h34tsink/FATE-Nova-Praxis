# Session 6: Game Scenario Overview

## Part 1: Awakening in Uncertainty
- **Location:** The PCs awaken on [[Asola]], with fragmented memories.
- **Situation:** They remember being backed up by Second Chances, a [[House Tsarya]] division, before meeting [[Kestrel]].
- **Discovery:** Their records are scrubbed of the last two months' events, except for their meeting with [[Kestrel]].
- **Challenge:** Convince [[Kestrel]] to help by linking their situation to a similar event on Vantage.

## Part 2: Uncovering Truths on Vantage
- **Objective:** Investigate erased records on Vantage.
- **Antagonist:** The erasure is traced to [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]], a Savant and [[Talons of Freedom]] member, now deceased.
- **Revelation:** Valare was hired by Tsaryan official Nowak for the erasure, related to the PCs' mission.

## Part 3: Chaos on Icarus Hub
- **Incident:** Arrival at Icarus Hub is met with an explosion, marking an [[Apostate]] uprising led by The [[Talons of Freedom]] and [[Chimera]].
- **Motivation:** Chimera uses the uprising for revenge against Nowak and complicates the PCs' quest.
- **Complication:** Nowak mistakenly identifies the PCs as uprising instigators, leading to a manhunt by Lighthouse forces.
- **Resolution Path:** The PCs need to reach Nowak for a deal or face elimination by [[Kestrel]] and the Red Sun enforcers.

## Part 4: Showdown at Opportunity Four
- **Escape:** Departure from Icarus Hub to the outpost, now as outlaws or apostates.
- **Confrontation:** Battling Chimera and outpost defenses, with intersystem communications cut.
- **Discovery:** Unraveling the origins of Chimera and the mutiny on Icarus Hub.
- **Aftermath:** Dealing with [[Kestrel]]'s arrival and his mission to erase all evidence.

## Key Decisions and Consequences
- **If Nowak is saved:** She reveals the outpost's location and promises to clear the PCs' names.
- **If [[Kestrel]] kills Nowak:** The PCs are given the outpost's location but remain branded as traitors.
- **Defeating Chimera:** Reveals evidence to clear the PCs' names and implicates House [[Kimura]].
- **Chimera's Escape:** The PCs must capture him before Kimurans do, to prove their innocence and expose the illegal research.

## Game Dynamics
- **Navigating Alliances:** Players must decide whom to trust and how to leverage information.
- **Combat vs. Diplomacy:** Choices between direct confrontation or strategic negotiation will shape the narrative.
- **Reputation Management:** Actions will affect how factions view and interact with the PCs.
- **Moral Dilemmas:** Decisions on handling Chimera, Nowak, and Kestrel have far-reaching consequences.
