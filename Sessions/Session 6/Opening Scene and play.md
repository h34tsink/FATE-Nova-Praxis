# Framework: Operation Freedom's Claw

## Goal
The PCs must infiltrate, take control of, and navigate the "Liberty's Claw" spaceship from The [[Talons of Freedom]] to a secure location without being detected or engaging in direct conflict with Talon forces.
## Complexity: 8 
## Segment Time: 10 min

## Characteristics
- The "Liberty's Claw" is currently docked at a hidden asteroid base within a nebula, making it difficult to approach undetected.
- The [[Talons of Freedom]] have recently increased their security measures due to intelligence leaks.
- The spaceship is equipped with state-of-the-art AI defense systems that control most of the ship's operations.
- The crew aboard "Liberty's Claw" are loyal to the Talons and highly trained in combat and security protocols.
## Complications
1. **Unknown Security Protocols** (Mission Critical, Diff: 6)
   - The ship's new security measures are unknown and must be bypassed to gain access.
     - AI Defense Systems (Diff: 8)
       - Advanced AI defenses need to be hacked or deceived to take control of the ship's systems.
       
1. **Secure the Bridge** (Mission Critical, Diff: 6)
   - The bridge is the control hub of the ship, heavily guarded and locked down.
     - Elite Guard Presence (Diff: 8)
       - A squad of elite guards protects the bridge, requiring stealth or non-lethal measures to bypass.

2. **Disable Communication Arrays** (Diff: 4)
   - Prevent the ship from sending distress signals to avoid drawing attention from Talon reinforcements.

3. **Access to Engine Control** (Diff: 6)
   - Gain control over the ship's engines to navigate it out of the nebula without alerting nearby Talon patrols.

4. **Evacuation Protocols** (Diff: 4)
   - Ensure the ship's evacuation protocols are disabled to prevent the crew from escaping and raising the alarm.

5. **Neutralize Ship's Drones** (Diff: 6)
   - The ship is equipped with defensive drones that need to be neutralized or reprogrammed.

## Disadvantages
- **Enhanced Security Measures** - Recent upgrades to the ship's security protocols provide a -2 penalty on all hacking attempts or actions against the ship's systems.
- **Loyal Crew** - The crew's loyalty to the Talons gives them a morale boost, making them more resistant to intimidation or persuasion (-2 on social interaction rolls).
## Success or Failure
- The operation is considered a success if the PCs can navigate the "Liberty's Claw" out of the nebula without alerting Talon forces, ensuring the crew is dealt with in a manner that aligns with the mission's ethical guidelines.
- Failure occurs if the ship is unable to leave the nebula without detection, or if the PCs are forced into a direct conflict with arriving Talon reinforcements, potentially leading to a high-stakes escape or combat scenario.
