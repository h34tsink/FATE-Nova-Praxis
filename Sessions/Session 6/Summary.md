# Session 6: Summary

## Scenario Overview

The Apotheosized crew is sent on a salvage contract by Kestrel (Kimuran Astralist, Apostate sympathizer) for Expanse World Systems (House Tsarya). Their mission: investigate an abandoned outpost orbiting RS-412 (Opportunity Four). Upon arrival, they are attacked by automated defenses, captured, and discover illegal mnemonics research by Mindscapes (House Kimura). Surviving PCs’ egos are merged into Chimera, their digital identities erased, and they are restored by insurance after being declared dead.

## Key Events

- Salvage contract briefing by Kestrel.
- Outpost encounter, capture, and illegal research discovery.
- Creation of Chimera and erasure of evidence.
- Restoration and new mission: uncover erased records, clear names, and expose illegal research.

## Framework: Operation Freedom's Claw

- **Goal:** Infiltrate and take control of the "Liberty's Claw" spaceship from The Talons of Freedom.
- **Complexity:** 8; Segment Time: 10 min.
- **Characteristics:** Hidden asteroid base, advanced AI defenses, loyal crew, elite guards.
- **Complications:** Unknown security protocols, bridge lockdown, disable comms, engine control, evacuation protocols, neutralize drones.
- **Disadvantages:** Enhanced security (-2 hacking), loyal crew (-2 social rolls).
- **Success:** Navigate ship out undetected, deal with crew ethically.

## NPCs & Dynamics

- **Kestrel:** Connector, naive, entry point to larger conflict.
- **Lester Lucas:** Relatable subplot, espionage/negotiation.
- **Valare:** Digital espionage, moral implications, resurrection plot.
- **Isabella Nowak:** Ambition, central narrative drive, diplomacy/conflict.
- **Chimera:** Ethics of technology and identity, complex ally/antagonist.

## Game Dynamics

- Navigating alliances, reputation management, moral dilemmas.
- Combat vs. diplomacy, leveraging technology and magic-like abilities.
