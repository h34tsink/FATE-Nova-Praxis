# Session 7: Timeline of Events

## Several Months Ago

- **The Incident on Vantage:** A mesh blackout occurs on Vantage Station, hiding a deal gone wrong between smuggler [[Lester Lucas]] and the Talons of Freedom. Several people die, including Savant Valare. The Talons take possession of Valare's mnemonic core and her ship, the Chickenhawk.

## Recent Past

- **The PCs' Mission:** Kestrel, a Red Sun operative, hires the PCs for a seemingly routine salvage mission to a supposedly abandoned House Kimura outpost on Opportunity Four.
- **Betrayal and Merger:** The outpost is not abandoned. The PCs are captured by Mindscapes, a Kimura subsidiary conducting illegal mnemonics research. Their egos are merged into a single entity, Chimera.
- **Resurrection and Blackout:** The PCs are resurrected by Second Chances, a backup service. They discover a gap in their memory and that their online presence has been erased.

## Present

- **Seeking Answers:** The PCs, guided by Kestrel, travel to Vantage Station to investigate the recent mesh blackout, hoping for clues about their own Situation.
- **Meeting Lester:** The PCs find [[Lester Lucas]], who provides information about the blackout and the Talons' involvement. He reveals Valare's ability to black out the mesh and the location of her core on the Chickenhawk.
- **Retrieving the Core:** The PCs brave station security and the Talons to reach the Chickenhawk and acquire Valare's core. They learn about the Talons' planned attack on the Proch Icarus Hub.
- **The Mutiny:** Arriving at the Icarus Hub, the PCs witness the beginning of the Talon's attack. They encounter Isabella Nowak, who reveals the truth about their mission and Chimera's creation.
- **Confrontation on Opportunity Four:** Returning to the outpost, the PCs confront Chimera and Dr. Nakamura. Depending on their choices, they may kill Chimera, strike a deal, or allow his escape.
- **Cleanup Crew:** Kestrel arrives with a Red Sun hit squad to eliminate all evidence of the illegal research and silence everyone involved, including the PCs.

## Cast of Characters

### PCs

- **Chimera:** A gestalt entity formed from the merged egos of the PCs. He possesses their memories, skills, and fears, and is driven by self-preservation.

### Allies

- **Kestrel:** A Red Sun operative and [[Groups/Apostate]] sympathizer who initially hires the PCs. He provides information and resources but prioritizes his loyalty to House Kimura.
- **[[Lester Lucas]]:** A down-on-his-luck smuggler who provides crucial information about the Vantage blackout and the Talons' activities.
- **Valare (Deceased):** A Savant smuggler working for House Tsarya, capable of creating mesh blackouts. Her core holds crucial information about the PCs' Situation.

### Antagonists

- **Dr. Haruto Nakamura:** The lead scientist at the Mindscapes research outpost. He is responsible for creating Chimera and seeks to protect his research at all costs.
- **Isabella Nowak:** A high-ranking member of House Tsarya involved in the Shadow War. She orchestrated the PCs' original mission and seeks to cover up the illegal research.
- **The Talons of Freedom:** A radical [[Groups/Apostate]] group planning a major attack on the Proch Icarus Hub. They possess Valare's core and unwittingly benefit from Chimera's actions.

### Organizations

- **House Kimura:** A powerful corporation involved in the Shadow War. Mindscapes, a subsidiary, conducted illegal mnemonics research.
- **House Tsarya:** Another powerful House engaged in the Shadow War, employing Valare and orchestrating the PCs' mission.
- **Red Suns:** A clandestine group within House Kimura responsible for covert operations and eliminating threats.
