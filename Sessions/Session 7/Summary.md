# Session 7: Ghosts in the Darkness – Summary

## Overview

This scenario is divided into four parts: **In the Dark**, **A Different Vantage Point**, **Civil Unrest**, and **Opportunity Four**. At least two PCs should have undergone Apotheosis. The session explores memory loss, investigation, and a web of intrigue involving Kestrel, Isabella Nowak, Lester Lucas, the Talons of Freedom, and Chimera.

## Part 1: In the Dark

- PCs awaken at Second Chances facility on [[Asola]], dominated by [[House Tsarya]].
- Memories of the past two months are wiped; last memory is a meeting with [[Kestrel]].
- Security footage and intelligence from their last assignment are missing.
- PCs investigate Second Chances, using Networking skills.
- Secret: Second Chances discreetly handles sensitive situations.
- PCs seek out [[Kestrel]], a secretive information broker.
- Kestrel reveals he was hired to arrange a meeting with [[Isabella Nowak]] on Vantage Station.
- Mission involved a security breach at a [[House Tsarya]] research outpost.
- Red Sun mercenaries were involved in the breach.

## Part 2: A Different Vantage Point

- PCs travel to Vantage Station, orbiting [[Proch]].
- Investigation leads to Lester Lucas, Talons of Freedom, and Valare.
- PCs uncover clues about the mesh blackout and the Talons' activities.
- Valare’s mnemonic core holds vital information.

## Part 3: Civil Unrest

- PCs arrive at Icarus Hub during a Talons of Freedom attack.
- Encounter Isabella Nowak, who reveals the truth about their mission and Chimera’s creation.
- PCs must navigate the chaos and decide their next steps.

## Part 4: Opportunity Four

- PCs return to the outpost, confront Chimera and Dr. Nakamura.
- Choices: kill Chimera, strike a deal, or allow his escape.
- Kestrel arrives with Red Sun hit squad to eliminate evidence and silence witnesses.

## Session Outcome

- PCs’ actions determine if their names are cleared, if illegal research is exposed, and the fate of Chimera.
- Reputation and alliances shift based on decisions made during the session.
