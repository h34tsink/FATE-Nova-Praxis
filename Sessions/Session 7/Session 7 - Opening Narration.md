# Session 7 — Opening Narration

## **Arrival at Vantage from Asola**

---

#### **Leaving [[Asola]]**

You leave behind the industrial sprawl of **Asola**, a city dominated by **House Tsarya** and their strict Purist traditions. The landscape of massive fabrication plants and worker dormitories, framed by the desolate lunar surface, is a constant reminder of the harsh life on the moon—a place shaped by the demands of the Coalition quarantine.

Your journey begins at the [[Asola]] spaceport, a hub of steel and glass that offers a rare glimpse of the wider universe. After boarding your ship, the **Topaz Shadow**, the familiar grey terrain of Asola slowly fades, replaced by the endless blackness of space. The pale blue sphere of Earth, beautiful yet unreachable, hangs in the distance like a forbidden paradise.

#### **The Journey to Vantage**

As your ship ascends, the inky black of space is broken only by the cold light of distant stars. Your destination, **Vantage Station**, looms ahead—a mix of old-world history and modern practicality, set against the celestial backdrop at the Earth-Moon Lagrange Point 1. The station stands out, a contrast to the utilitarian structures of Asola, a beacon of activity and life.

#### **Approaching Vantage**

The silhouette of Vantage grows larger, revealing its intricate structure, a blend of historical exhibits and bustling spaceport. Unlike the stark industriality of Asola, Vantage is vibrant—a living connection to Earth's past, a bridge between the quarantined planet and the stars. As you approach, a message crackles over the comms:

> **Vantage Traffic Control:** _"Topaz Shadow, this is Vantage Traffic Control. You are cleared for approach on vector seven. Prepare for final docking sequence at Bay 342."_

You feel the ship hum as you confirm the approach vector and prepare for docking. The station’s lights twinkle ahead, and your senses sharpen as you draw closer to the hub of activity.

> **[Player, as pilot]:** _"Vantage Control, Topaz Shadow confirming. Approaching on vector seven, preparing for final docking sequence."_

The ship vibrates slightly as it aligns with the docking vector. You feel the magnetic pull as the docking bay grips the **Topaz Shadow**, and with a series of metallic clunks, the ship is secured.

> **Vantage Traffic Control:** _"Docking complete, Topaz Shadow. Welcome to Vantage. Power down your drive systems and engage safety protocols. A docking crew will be with you shortly."_

With a final sigh, the ship settles into place. You power down the systems, feeling the slight weight shift as Vantage’s artificial gravity takes hold. The air is charged with a sense of anticipation—the journey is over, but the adventure has just begun.

---

#### **Disembarking at Vantage**

The airlock hisses open, and you step from the familiar confines of your ship onto the polished floors of Vantage. A group of uniformed docking crew waits to secure the ship, while a **Coalition Security Officer** approaches, eyes sharp and professional.

> **Security Officer:** _"Hold it right there. Standard security check. Please present your CIDs and step through the scanner."_

The security checkpoint feels sterile, almost clinical, with advanced scanners humming quietly as they search for contraband. A nearby screen displays a bold message:

> **Coalition Weapons Policy:**  
>
> - **Coalition Security Carry Only**: Only authorized personnel may carry weapons on Vantage Station.  
> - **No Explosives**: The possession or transport of explosives is strictly prohibited.  

The atmosphere is tense, a stark reminder of the Coalition’s tight grip on the station. Compliance here isn’t just expected—it’s demanded.

---

#### **Exploring Vantage**

As you pass through the security check, the ambiance shifts. You’re greeted by a bustling scene—crowds of tourists marvel at the view of Earth through massive observation windows, Coalition officers march with determined precision, and merchants hawk their goods to eager travelers. Vantage is alive, a crossroads for those who still yearn for a glimpse of Earth.

Everywhere you look, there are traces of history—statues, artifacts, and displays paying homage to Earth's past. But the view of Earth itself, hanging just beyond the glass, is a constant reminder of what’s been lost. It’s a haunting sight—a paradise just out of reach.

---

### **Seedy Underbelly of Vantage**

But there’s another side to Vantage, one that’s less polished and far more dangerous. You’ve heard rumors—whispers of hidden markets and forbidden transactions.

- **Hidden Markets** are said to operate in abandoned maintenance areas, selling restricted goods and black market weapons to those who know where to look.
- In the depths of the station lies **The Gutter**, a place of shadows and whispered deals, where smugglers, fences, and information brokers meet to do business.
- There’s talk of a place called **[[Whisper]]’s Den**, a secretive speakeasy where the real power brokers of Vantage gather—mercenaries, operatives, and apostates alike.
- Rumors suggest that deep in the lower levels are **Illicit Augmentation Clinics**, where rogue techs perform high-risk installations for the desperate and well-funded.
- For those who know the way, **Smuggler Routes** wind through the station’s hidden tunnels, allowing contraband to flow beneath the Coalition’s watchful gaze.

Vantage is a place of contrasts—a shining hub of civilization with a shadowy core, where opportunity and danger are always in balance. Whatever you seek, you know it can be found here… if you’re willing to pay the price.
