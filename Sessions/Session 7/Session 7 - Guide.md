### **Overview**

In this adventure, the players investigate a mesh blackout on **Vantage Station** that mirrors a similar incident affecting them. Their investigation leads them through a web of intrigue involving **[[Lester Lucas|Lester Lucas]]**, the **Talons of Freedom**, and ultimately points them toward **Isabella Nowak** of **House Tsarya** on the **Icarus Hub** orbiting **Proch**. Along the way, they encounter **Chimera**, a gestalt entity intent on self-preservation at any cost.

---

## **Part 2 - A Different Vantage Point**

### **Preparation**

- **Review Key NPCs:**
  - **[[Lester Lucas]]**: Smuggler entangled with the Talons, desperate to escape.
  - **Valare**: Savant whose mnemonic core holds vital information.
  - **Talons of Freedom Members**: Antagonists with their own agenda.
  - **Additional NPCs**: Dockworkers, security officers, merchants, and informants on Vantage.

- **Familiarize with Locations:**
  - **Vantage Station**: Public areas, hidden markets, the Gutter, observation decks.
  - **Docking Bay 117**: Site of the blackout and explosion.
  - **The Chickenhawk**: Lester's damaged ship attached to a secure arm of Vantage.

- **Prepare Materials:**
  - Maps of Vantage Station, including public areas and hidden routes.
  - Stat blocks for NPCs and enemies.
  - Clue handouts or descriptions.

---

### **Step 1: Arrival at Vantage Station**

**Scene Setting:**

- Describe the vibrant atmosphere of Vantage Station, contrasting it with Asola's industrial environment. [[Sessions/Session 7/Notes/Notes for Arrival at Vantage|Arrival at Vantage]]
- Emphasize the strict security measures, especially regarding weapons.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Security Officer:** Conducts CID checks and reminds them of the weapon policies.
- **Merchant or Tour Guide:** Provides general information and hints about the station's underbelly.
- **Whisper's Den Lead:** A subtle invitation to a hidden speakeasy for those seeking illicit goods or information.

**Potential Player Actions:**

- **Exploration:** Players might explore the station, leading to side quests or additional clues.
- **Gathering Rumors:** Use **Persuasion** or **Streetwise** to learn about recent events, including the blackout.
- **Attempting to Smuggle Weapons:** If they try, introduce challenges and consequences, such as potential fines or encounters with black market dealers.

---

### **Step 2: Initiating the Investigation**

**Objective:**

- Begin gathering information about the mesh blackout that parallels their own experience.

**Methods:**

- **Networking and Social Skills:**
  - Use **Persuasion**, **Streetwise**, or **Intimidation** to ask around.
  - **Research** can be used at a -1 penalty to search public records or forums.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Friendly Dockworker:**
  - Shares that a blackout occurred on **Level 11**.
  - [**Secret 2**] The blackout happened in Docking Bay 117.

- **Coalition Security Officer:**
  - Provides official statements, hints at increased security due to the incident.

- **Information Broker (perhaps at Whisper's Den):**
  - Offers detailed information for a price or favor.
  - [**Secret 3**] The [[Vehicles/Ships/The Chickenhawk - CHKN738921|Chickenhawk]] was involved in the blackout.

**Alternative Clues if Players Deviate:**

- **Eavesdropping in Bars:**
  - Overhear conversations about strange activities or missing people.
- **Data Terminals:**
  - Access public notices or maintenance logs indicating disruptions on Level 11.

---

### **Step 3: Exploring Docking Bay 117**

**Objective:**

- Investigate the site of the blackout for clues.

**Possible Actions:**

- **Visiting the Docking Bay:**
  - Might be restricted; players need to find ways in (stealth, bribery, or forged credentials).
- **Analyzing the Scene:**
  - Use **Notice** or **Investigation** to find remnants of the explosion or security camera blind spots.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Maintenance Worker:**
  - Reluctant to talk but can be persuaded to mention bodies being carried out.
  - [**Secret 4**] An explosion occurred during a transfer of goods.

- **Talon Sympathizer:**
  - If approached carefully, may reveal that multiple bodies were removed and that the captain of the Chickenhawk is missing.
  - [**Secret 5**] Lester was smuggling weapons to the Talons.

**Clues to Provide:**

- Scorch marks, debris from the explosion.
- A discarded item bearing the Chickenhawk's insignia.

**Alternative Paths:**

- **Hacking Security Feeds:**
  - Allows viewing of tampered or missing footage, indicating a cover-up.
- **Finding a Witness:**
  - A nervous individual who saw something but fears repercussions.

---

### **Step 4: Locating Lester Lucas**

**Objective:**

- Find Lester, who can provide critical information.

**Methods:**

- **Following Leads:**
  - Learn that Lester has been seen at the **Waypoint Bar**.
  - [**Secret 6**] Lester is hiding on Vantage, fearing for his life.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Bartender at Waypoint Bar:**
  - Knows Lester but is protective; may require a favor or payment to talk.
- **Suspicious Patron:**
  - Warns the PCs to stay away from Lester, hinting at danger.

**Alternative Clues:**

- **Anonymous Message:**
  - Lester contacts the PCs after hearing they're looking for him.
- **Interception by Talons:**
  - The Talons attempt to mislead the PCs or threaten them to stop their investigation.

---

### **Step 5: Meeting with Lester**

**Scene Setting:**

- A clandestine meeting on an observation deck overlooking Earth.
- Atmosphere filled with tension and urgency.

**NPC Interaction:**

- **Lester Lucas:**
  - Paranoid, desperate, but willing to make a deal.
  - Explains his situation and offers information in exchange for help.

**Information Lester Provides:**

- Details about the blackout, the explosion, and Valare's core on the Chickenhawk.
- The Talons are after him; he needs to escape Vantage.

**Possible Player Reactions:**

- **Agreeing to Help:**
  - Lester provides routes to avoid security and warns of Talon interference.
- **Refusing or Betraying Lester:**
  - He becomes defensive; may flee or alert the Talons about the PCs.
- **Protecting Lester:**
  - Ensures he isn't captured, preventing the Talons from learning the PCs' plans.

---

### **Step 6: Planning the Recovery of Valare's Core**

**Objective:**

- Formulate a plan to reach the Chickenhawk and retrieve Valare's core.

**Challenges:**

- **Security Inside the Arm:**
  - Heavily secured passageways with Centurions and drones.
- **External Approach:**
  - Requires EVA suits, navigating mesh sensors, and potential ambushes.

**Possible Plans:**

- **External Route (Lester's Recommendation):**
  - Avoids internal security but risks exposure to Talons and environmental hazards.
- **Internal Route:**
  - Requires stealth or deception to bypass security checkpoints.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Equipment Supplier:**
  - Provides EVA suits and mag boots; may require favors or extra payment.
- **Station Insider:**
  - Offers access codes or distractions in exchange for assistance.

**Adding Clues and Assistance:**

- If players struggle, introduce an NPC who offers critical information or equipment.
- Discover a hidden maintenance hatch leading closer to the Chickenhawk.

---

### **Step 7: Navigating Complications**

**Lester's Fate:**

- **If Unprotected:**
  - The Talons capture and interrogate Lester, learning of the PCs' plans.
  - PCs may find his body as a grim warning during their EVA.
- **If Protected:**
  - Lester remains safe, but the Talons may still attempt to ambush the PCs based on other intel.

**Ambush Scenario:**

- **Talons Ambush:**
  - Equal in number to the PCs plus one.
  - One has the CID needed to access the Chickenhawk.
- **Environmental Hazards:**
  - Debris from previous incidents; risk of suit punctures or equipment failure.

**Adjustments if Players Deviate:**

- If they choose a different route, introduce challenges appropriate to their path.
- If they attempt to negotiate with the Talons, create a tense standoff scene.

---

### **Step 8: Boarding the Chickenhawk**

**Scene Setting:**

- The ship is damaged, eerily quiet, with signs of the explosion evident.
- Zero gravity and low lighting add to the tension.

**Challenges:**

- **Locked Entrance:**
  - Requires the CID from a Talon or hacking skills to bypass.
- **Potential Traps:**
  - The Talons may have booby-trapped the entrance or interior.

**NPC Interaction:**

- **Valare's Sleeve:**
  - Found in the engine room; her body shows signs of explosive decompression.

**Clues to Provide:**

- Data logs indicating recent activity.
- Personal effects that hint at Valare's connection to Nowak.

---

### **Step 9: Interacting with Valare**

**Objective:**

- Access Valare's mnemonic core and extract information.

**Methods:**

- **Virtual Reality Interface:**
  - Engage with Valare in a controlled VR environment.
- **Psychological Support:**
  - Use **Persuasion**, **Therapy**, or **Empathy** to help her cope with trauma.

**Information Valare Provides:**

- She was hired by **Isabella Nowak** to scrub the PCs' net presence.
- Details about Nowak's role on the **Icarus Hub** and involvement with the Talons.
- Insights into the Talons' upcoming mutiny on the Icarus Hub.

**Valare's Request:**

- Asks the PCs to needlecast her ego to her insurance provider on Rho.
- Offers to provide additional intel or assist remotely if they comply.

**Possible Complications:**

- **Trauma Response:**
  - If mishandled, Valare may become unresponsive or provide fragmented information.
- **Data Integrity Issues:**
  - Parts of her memory may be corrupted, requiring technical skills to repair.

## **Part 3 – Civil Unrest**

### **Step 10: Traveling to the Icarus Hub**

**Objective:**

- Reach the Icarus Hub to confront **Isabella Nowak**.

**Possible Actions:**

- Use the Chickenhawk or another ship to make the journey.
- Prepare for potential dangers, given the Talons' involvement.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Ship Crew or Passengers:**
  - Share rumors of unrest at the Icarus Hub.
- **Coalition Broadcasts:**
  - Receive alerts about increased security or incidents near Proch.

---

### **Step 11: Arriving at the Icarus Hub During the Mutiny**

**Scene Setting:**

- As they approach, the PCs witness an explosion; a corner of one of the hub's cubes breaks away.
- Chaos unfolds as ships scramble, and security forces mobilize.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Docking Control:** Communications are chaotic; docking may be restricted.
- **Panicked Civilians:** Seek help or offer information in exchange for assistance.
- **Talons of Freedom Operatives:** May attempt to prevent the PCs from docking or attack on sight.

**Possible Player Reactions:**

- **Attempt to Dock:** Requires skillful piloting and possibly deception.
- **Offer Assistance:** Gain favor with Coalition forces or civilians.
- **Stealth Entry:** Find alternative ways to board the station.

---

### **Step 12: Navigating the Chaos**

**Objective:**

- Find and confront **Isabella Nowak** amidst the mutiny.

**Challenges:**

- **Combat Zones:** Areas controlled by the Talons or under attack.
- **Security Measures:** Lockdowns, blast doors, and surveillance systems.
- **Chimera's Interference:** Evidence planted to implicate the PCs in the mutiny.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Coalition Security Forces:**
  - May be suspicious of the PCs due to Chimera's tampering.
  - Could be allies if convinced of the PCs' innocence.

- **Apostate Workers:**
  - Some may assist the PCs, others might be hostile.

- **Chimera's Digital Presence:**
  - Hints of his manipulation; strange glitches or messages.

**Alternative Paths:**

- **Following Clues:**
  - Discover logs or messages indicating Nowak's location.
- **Rescuing Key Personnel:**
  - Gain assistance by helping trapped security officers or technicians.

---

### **Step 13: Confronting Nowak**

**Outcome 1: Success**

- **Finding Nowak Alive:**

  - Located in a security post, guarded by wounded Lighthouse operatives.
  - She is desperate and may initially deny knowledge.
  
- **Interrogation:**

  - Use **Persuasion**, **Intimidation**, or **Evidence** to get her to talk.
  - She reveals:
    - She hired the PCs to investigate the outpost.
    - She ordered the data scrub when things went wrong.
    - She had no idea about Chimera's creation or actions.

**Outcome 2: Failure**

- **Nowak is Dead:**

  - Killed by the Talons, apostates, or Kestrel.
  - The PCs find her body, and shortly after, receive a message from **Kestrel** containing the outpost's coordinates.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Nowak (if alive):**
  - May attempt to strike a deal or plead for protection.
- **Kestrel:**
  - Could appear in person, offering information in exchange for assistance.
- **Chimera:**
  - Leaves taunting messages or misleads the PCs further.

---

### **Step 14: Dealing with Aftermath**

**Objective:**

- Decide on next steps after confronting Nowak.

**Possible Actions:**

- **Pursue Chimera:**
  - Use clues to track him down, possibly leading to the outpost.
- **Expose the Truth:**
  - Present evidence to the Coalition to clear their names.
- **Escape the Hub:**
  - Navigate through the chaos to safely depart.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Coalition Officials:**
  - May arrest or assist the PCs based on their actions and evidence.
- **Talons of Freedom:**
  - Continue to pose a threat; might attempt to eliminate the PCs.
- **Survivors:**
  - Could become allies or offer rewards for assistance.

---

### **Step 15: Planning the Next Move**

**Clues to Bring Players Back on Track:**

- **Recovered Data:**
  - Information pointing to the outpost's location.
- **Message from Kestrel:**
  - Provides coordinates, urging them to uncover the truth.
- **Chimera's Slip-ups:**
  - Digital traces that reveal his next move or weaknesses.

**Encouraging Player Engagement:**

- **Highlight Stakes:**
  - Emphasize the broader implications of Chimera's actions.
- **Personal Motivations:**
  - Remind players of their lost memories and the need to uncover the truth.
- **Allies' Assistance:**
  - NPCs offer support or resources for the next phase.

---

### **General Tips for Running the Scenario**

- **Adapt to Player Choices:**
  - Be prepared for unexpected actions; adjust the narrative to keep them engaged.
- **Use Descriptive Narration:**
  - Bring environments and scenes to life with vivid descriptions.
- **Balance Action and Investigation:**
  - Alternate between combat, exploration, and social interactions.
- **Provide Moral Dilemmas:**
  - Challenge players with tough choices that impact the story.
- **Keep Clues Accessible:**
  - Ensure essential information is available through multiple avenues.

---

**By integrating these steps and being responsive to your players' decisions, you'll create an immersive and dynamic adventure that keeps them invested in unraveling the mystery of Chimera and their own forgotten past.**
