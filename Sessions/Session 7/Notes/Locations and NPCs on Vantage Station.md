## Locations and NPCs on Vantage Station

Here are some locations and NPCs you could use on Vantage Station, drawing inspiration from the sources and our conversation:

### The Waypoint Bar

A dimly lit establishment popular with low-Rep civilians and apostates. Known for its cheap drinks and a relaxed atmosphere where people can temporarily forget about the Coalition's watchful eyes.

- **Bartender:** **Kira "Six" Savalas** ([[Groups/Apostate]], Sleeved). A gruff but fair bartender who's seen it all. Wears a battered Courier sleeve with numerous patches and modifications. Keeps a stash of illegal stims behind the bar for trusted customers.
- **Patron:** **Jax "Lucky" Teller** ([[Groups/Apostate]], Pure). A young information broker with a knack for finding things others would rather keep hidden. Always looking for an angle and willing to trade favors for valuable intel.
- **Patron:** **Dr. Elias Vance** (House Silva, Sleeved). A disgraced mnemonics engineer who lost his Rep after a botched resleeving procedure. Now spends his days drowning his sorrows in cheap synthohol, occasionally offering his expertise to those in need.

### Level 11 Docking Bay

A bustling area with constant activity as ships arrive and depart. Dockworkers, technicians, and security personnel go about their business, amidst the noise and fumes of spacecraft engines.

- **Dockworker:** **Zeke "Gears" O'Reilly** ([[Groups/Apostate]], Pure). A veteran dockworker with a reputation for efficiency and reliability. Augmented with cybernetic limbs for increased strength and dexterity. Has a network of contacts throughout the station and can arrange discreet cargo transfers for the right price.
- **Security Officer:** **Ava "Stonewall" Chen** (House Tsarya, Sleeved). A no-nonsense Centurion tasked with maintaining order in the docking bay. Wears a standard Justicar sleeve and carries a non-lethal shock baton. Suspicious of anyone who looks out of place.
- **Technician:** **Raj "Sparks" Patel** (House Cipriani, Sleeved). A skilled technician specializing in starship repairs and modifications. Wears a custom-designed Ingot sleeve optimized for working in tight spaces and handling delicate electronics. Eager to earn Rep and climb the ranks within House Cipriani.

### Observation Deck

One of several observation decks offering a breathtaking view of Earth. Popular with tourists and those seeking a moment of solitude.

- **[[Characters/NPCs/Named NPCs/Lester Lucas]]** (House Silva, Sleeved). Hiding out on the observation deck after a deal with the Talons of Freedom went sour. Desperate to escape their clutches and willing to hire the PCs for help.

### Black Market Dealer's Stall (Not in Sources)

Tucked away in a dimly lit corner of the station's commercial district, this stall offers a variety of illicit goods and services, from fake CIDs to smuggled weapons.

- **Dealer:** **"Whisper"** ([[Groups/Apostate]], Sleeved). A shadowy figure shrouded in a hooded cloak, their true identity unknown. Their face is obscured by a mask, and their voice is digitally modulated. Deals only in gold and favors.

**Note:** The existence of black market dealers on Vantage Station is not explicitly mentioned in the sources. However, given the presence of apostates and the station's role as a transit hub, it seems plausible that such activities would occur. The GM can use this location to introduce elements of intrigue and danger to the station.
