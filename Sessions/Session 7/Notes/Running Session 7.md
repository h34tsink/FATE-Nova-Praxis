**Step-by-Step Guide for Running "Part 2 - A Different Vantage Point" Scenario**

---
### **Overview**

In this scenario, the player characters (PCs) are investigating a mesh blackout on **Vantage Station** that mirrors a similar event affecting their own mesh presence. Their investigation leads them to **Lester Lucas**, a smuggler entangled with the **Talons of Freedom**, and ultimately points them toward **Isabella Nowak** of **House Tsarya**.

---

### **Preparation**

- **Review Key NPCs:**
  - **Lester Lucas**: Smuggler fearful for his life.
  - **Valare**: A Savant whose mnemonic core holds vital information.
  - **Isabella Nowak**: Executive Administrator involved in shady dealings.
  - **Talons of Freedom Members**: Potential antagonists.

- **Familiarize with Locations:**
  - **Vantage Station**: Public areas, security checkpoints, observation decks.
  - **Docking Bay 117**: Site of the previous blackout.
  - **The Chickenhawk**: Lester's ship attached to the station's arm.

- **Prepare Materials:**
  - Maps of Vantage Station and the Chickenhawk.
  - Clue handouts or descriptions.
  - Stat blocks for NPCs and enemies.

---

### **Step 1: Arrival at Vantage Station**

**Scene Setting:**

- Describe the bustling corridors of Vantage Station filled with tourists, officers, and merchants.
- Emphasize the contrast between Vantage's vibrant atmosphere and Asola's industrial environment.
- Highlight the strict security measures and weapon policies upon docking.

**Potential Player Actions:**

- **Exploration:** PCs may wish to explore shops, historical exhibits, or observation decks.
- **Gathering Information:** They might start asking about the blackout immediately.
- **Avoiding Security:** Attempts to smuggle weapons or contraband past security.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Customs Officer:** Checks their CIDs and reminds them of station policies.
- **Merchant or Tour Guide:** Provides general information about the station.
- **Coalition Security:** Enforces rules, may become suspicious of illicit activities.

---

### **Step 2: Initiating the Investigation**

**Objective:**

- Begin gathering information about the recent mesh blackout that parallels their own experience.

**Methods:**

- **Networking and Social Skills:**
  - Use skills like **Persuasion**, **Streetwise**, or **Intimidation** to ask around.
  - **Research** can be used at a -1 penalty to search public records or forums.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Friendly Dockworker:**
  - Shares that a blackout happened on **Level 11**.
  - [**Secret 2**] The blackout occurred in a docking bay on Level 11.

- **Suspicious Centurion:**
  - May require convincing but can reveal more details.
  - [**Secret 3**] The blackout happened in Docking Bay 117 while the **Chickenhawk** was docked there.

- **Talon of Freedom Member:**
  - If intimidated or persuaded, reveals:
    - [**Secret 4**] The blackout hid a transfer of goods; an explosion was heard.

**Alternative Clues if PCs Deviate:**

- **Eavesdropping:**
  - Overhear conversations in bars or public areas about strange occurrences.
- **Public Terminals:**
  - Access news archives mentioning the blackout and Docking Bay 117.
- **Rumors from Merchants:**
  - Hear about increased security or strange activities on Level 11.

---

### **Step 3: Investigating Docking Bay 117**

**Objective:**

- Gather deeper insights into the blackout incident and the Chickenhawk's involvement.

**Possible Actions:**

- **Visiting the Docking Bay:**
  - Attempt to access Docking Bay 117, possibly encountering restricted areas.
- **Analyzing the Scene:**
  - Use **Notice** or **Investigation** skills to find clues.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Maintenance Worker:**
  - Mentions multiple bodies were carried out after the explosion.
  - [**Secret 5**] The captain of the Chickenhawk was smuggling weapons to the Talons in exchange for food.

- **Freighter Captain:**
  - Knows about smuggling operations and shares insights.

**Clues to Provide:**

- Evidence of damage from the explosion.
- Discreet markings or graffiti indicating Talon activity.

**Additional Clues if PCs Struggle:**

- **Surveillance Footage:**
  - Hack into security systems to view footage of the incident.
- **Discarded Items:**
  - Find a piece of equipment or personal item linked to Lester or the Talons.

---

### **Step 4: Locating Lester Lucas**

**Objective:**

- Find Lester, who can provide critical information.

**Methods:**

- **Following Leads:**
  - Use information gathered to deduce that Lester frequents the **Waypoint Bar**.
  - [**Secret 6**] Lester is usually found at the Waypoint Bar.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Bartender at Waypoint Bar:**
  - Provides information on Lester's habits or current whereabouts.
- **Bar Patrons:**
  - Share rumors about Lester being in hiding or looking for help.

**Alternative Paths if PCs Deviate:**

- **Message from Lester:**
  - He contacts the PCs after hearing they're looking for him.
- **Intercepted Communication:**
  - PCs stumble upon a message indicating Lester's location.

---

### **Step 5: Meeting with Lester**

**Scene Setting:**

- Arrange a clandestine meeting on an observation deck or a secluded corner of the bar.
- Atmosphere should be tense; Lester is paranoid and jumpy.

**NPC Interaction:**

- **Lester Lucas:**
  - Desperate and eager to escape the station.
  - Willing to trade information if the PCs help him get his ship back.

**Information Lester Provides:**

- Details about the blackout, the explosion, and his involvement with the Talons.
- Explains that Valare's sleeve and mnemonic core are on the Chickenhawk.
- Describes the challenges in accessing the ship.

**Possible Player Reactions:**

- **Agreeing to Help:**
  - Lester provides all the information and assists in planning.
- **Refusing to Help:**
  - Lester becomes guarded; may withhold information.
- **Threatening Lester:**
  - May result in a confrontation or him fleeing.

---

### **Step 6: Planning the Recovery of Valare's Core**

**Objective:**

- Formulate a plan to reach the Chickenhawk and retrieve Valare's core.

**Challenges:**

- **Security Inside the Arm:**
  - Three sealed blast doors and a checkpoint with Centurions and drones.
- **External Approach:**
  - Requires EVA suits and equipment to traverse the station's exterior.
- **Locked Ship Entrance:**
  - Requires a Talon's CID to open.

**Possible Plans:**

- **External Route (Recommended by Lester):**
  - Avoids heavy security but risks exposure to space and potential ambush.
- **Internal Route:**
  - Face security checkpoints; may require stealth, deception, or combat.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Equipment Dealers:**
  - Acquire necessary gear for EVA.
- **Station Contacts:**
  - Potential allies who can provide access codes or distractions.

**Clues and Assistance:**

- Lester provides safe routes to avoid mesh sensors.
- Warns about the Talons' potential interference.

---

### **Step 7: Navigating Complications**

**Lester's Fate:**

- If PCs leave Lester unprotected, the Talons capture and interrogate him.
- **Outcome:**
  - Talons learn of the PCs' plans and set up an ambush.
  - PCs may find Lester's body as a warning.

**Adjusting the Scenario:**

- **If Lester is Captured:**
  - Increase security or Talon presence along the route.
  - Add an ambush scene where Talons attack the PCs during their EVA.

- **If Lester is Safe:**
  - PCs can proceed with minimal interference.
  - May still encounter environmental hazards or minor Talon patrols.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Talon Operatives:**
  - Ambush teams equal to the number of PCs plus one.
  - One operative has the CID needed to access the Chickenhawk.

---

### **Step 8: Boarding the Chickenhawk**

**Scene Setting:**

- The ship shows signs of damage; the umbilical is destroyed.
- Interior is dark, with emergency lighting casting eerie shadows.

**Challenges:**

- **Environmental Hazards:**
  - Zero gravity areas, compromised hull sections.
- **Potential Combat:**
  - Remaining Talons might be on board, setting traps or defending the ship.

**NPC Interaction:**

- **Valare's Sleeve:**
  - Found in the engine room, body shows signs of explosive decompression.
- **Accessing the Mnemonic Core:**
  - May require technical skills to safely retrieve.

---

### **Step 9: Interacting with Valare**

**Objective:**

- Extract information from Valare's mnemonic core.

**Methods:**

- **Virtual Reality Interface:**
  - PCs enter VR to communicate with Valare.
- **Calming Valare:**
  - Use **Persuasion** or **Therapy** skills to help her overcome trauma.

**Information Valare Provides:**

- She was hired by **Isabella Nowak** to scrub the PCs' net presence.
- Details about Nowak's position on the **Icarus Hub** orbiting Proch.
- Insights into the Talons' plans and her role in aiding them.

**Valare's Request:**

- Asks the PCs to needlecast her ego to her insurance provider on Rho.
- May offer additional help or information if they agree.

**Possible Complications:**

- **Valare's Trauma:**
  - If not handled gently, she may withhold information or become unresponsive.
- **Data Corruption:**
  - Parts of her memory may be damaged, requiring technical skills to repair.

---

### **Step 10: Resolving the Situation**

**Objective:**

- Decide on the next steps based on the information gathered.

**Possible Actions:**

- **Escape Vantage:**
  - Use the Chickenhawk or their own ship.
  - Navigate any remaining security challenges.

- **Pursue Leads:**
  - Head to the **Icarus Hub** to confront Nowak.
  - Investigate the Talons' activities further.

**NPC Interactions:**

- **Lester (if alive):**
  - May join the PCs or part ways.
- **Station Authorities:**
  - If alerted, may attempt to detain the PCs.
- **Allies:**
  - Potential help from contacts on Vantage or elsewhere.

**Additional Hooks:**

- **Talons' Retaliation:**
  - Talons may pursue the PCs for interfering.
- **Nowak's Response:**
  - She might send agents to deal with the PCs or offer a deceptive truce.

---

### **Adding Extra Clues and NPC Interactions**

**If Players Deviate:**

- **Lost or Uncertain:**
  - Introduce an NPC who provides a hint or a piece of crucial information.
  - **Example:** An informant approaches them with news about Lester.

- **Side Quests:**
  - Offer optional tasks that lead back to the main plot.
  - **Example:** Help a dockworker with a problem in exchange for info.

**Clues to Bring Them Back on Track:**

- **Mysterious Messages:**
  - Receive encrypted communications pointing them toward key locations.
- **Evidence Drops:**
  - Find discarded items or data pads containing valuable clues.

**Enhancing NPC Interactions:**

- **Complex Characters:**
  - Flesh out NPCs with unique personalities, motives, and backgrounds.
- **Moral Dilemmas:**
  - Present choices that test the PCs' ethics, such as saving Lester or pursuing their own goals.

---

### **Concluding the Scenario**

**Ensuring Player Engagement:**

- **Recap Achievements:**
  - Highlight the information they've uncovered and the progress made.
- **Set Up Next Session:**
  - Tease the challenges awaiting them on the Icarus Hub.
- **Encourage Player Input:**
  - Ask for their plans moving forward to tailor the upcoming adventure.

---

### **General Tips**

- **Be Flexible:**
  - Adapt the scenario based on player decisions, keeping the core narrative intact.
- **Maintain Pacing:**
  - Balance investigative scenes with action to keep players engaged.
- **Use Atmosphere:**
  - Leverage Vantage's unique environment to enhance immersion.
- **Reward Creativity:**
  - Encourage and reward inventive solutions or role-playing.

---

By following this guide and being responsive to your players' actions, you'll create an engaging and dynamic experience that keeps them invested in the unfolding story.
