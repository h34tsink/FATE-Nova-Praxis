# Guide for Running the Scenario: Sessions 7–9 (Original Backup)

## Overview

This adventure arc follows the PCs as they investigate a mesh blackout on Vantage Station, uncover a web of intrigue involving Lester Lucas, the Talons of Freedom, Isabella Nowak, and Chimera, and ultimately confront the consequences of their lost memories and the Shadow War. The guide is organized for quick reference and session-to-session continuity.

---

## Session Progression

### Session 7: Ghosts in the Darkness
- Arrival at Vantage Station from Asola; strict security and vibrant atmosphere.
- Investigation into the blackout, meeting Lester Lucas, and uncovering Valare’s fate.
- Recovery of Valare’s mnemonic core from the Chickenhawk.
- Key NPCs: Lester Lucas, Valare, Talons of Freedom, Coalition Security, Information Brokers.
- Major Locations: Vantage Station, Docking Bay 117, Waypoint Bar, Chickenhawk.
- Complications: Security measures, Talons interference, environmental hazards.
- Outcomes: PCs decide what to do with Valare’s core and how to proceed.

### Session 8: Aftermath and Transition
- Recap: PCs narrowly escape Vantage with Lester’s help, recover Valare’s core, and must decide next steps.
- Transition: The guide should reference the recap in the Session 8 folder and help the GM set up the next phase—travel to Icarus Hub, dealing with the fallout, and planning the confrontation with Nowak and Chimera.

### Session 9: Civil Unrest and Opportunity Four
- Travel to Icarus Hub; witness the mutiny and chaos.
- Confrontation with Isabella Nowak; uncover the truth about the mission and Chimera’s creation.
- Final showdown at Opportunity Four; choices about Chimera, Dr. Nakamura, and the Red Sun cleanup crew.
- Outcomes: PCs’ reputations, alliances, and the fate of illegal research.

---

## Running Each Phase

### Arrival and Investigation
- Set the scene with vivid descriptions of Vantage Station and Asola’s contrast.
- Use NPCs to provide clues, rumors, and side quests.
- Emphasize security, weapon policies, and the need for stealth or persuasion.

### Key NPCs and Interactions
- Lester Lucas: Desperate smuggler, source of information, needs protection.
- Valare: Savant, her core holds vital clues, trauma and requests for help.
- Talons of Freedom: Antagonists, may ambush or mislead PCs.
- Isabella Nowak: Coalition official, orchestrator of the mission, possible ally or adversary.
- Kestrel: Information broker, Red Sun operative, provides coordinates and support.

### Major Locations
- Vantage Station: Public areas, hidden markets, Docking Bay 117, Waypoint Bar.
- Chickenhawk: Damaged ship, site of Valare’s death and core recovery.
- Icarus Hub: Site of mutiny, chaos, and final confrontation.
- Opportunity Four: Outpost for the final showdown.

### Complications and Challenges
- Security measures, environmental hazards, Talons interference.
- Moral dilemmas: Protecting or betraying NPCs, handling Valare’s trauma, exposing illegal research.
- Multiple paths: Stealth, negotiation, combat, hacking.

### Outcomes and Next Steps
- PCs’ choices affect reputation, alliances, and future missions.
- Use clues and NPCs to guide players toward the next session’s objectives.
- Reference the Session 7 Recap in Session 8 folder for continuity.

---

## GM Tips
- Adapt to player choices; be flexible with narrative and challenges.
- Use descriptive narration to bring scenes to life.
- Alternate between action, investigation, and social interaction.
- Provide clues through multiple avenues to avoid dead ends.
- Highlight stakes and personal motivations to keep players engaged.

---

By following this guide, you’ll ensure smooth session transitions, clear objectives, and an engaging experience for your players as they unravel the mystery of Chimera and their own forgotten past.
