# Valare: Complete Knowledge Framework & Negotiation Outcomes

**Purpose:** Comprehensive guide to what Valare knows, how she learned it, and what she reveals under different negotiation outcomes. Includes both success and failure scenarios with transitions to Part 3: Civil Unrest.

---

## PART 1: WHAT VALARE KNOWS (Knowledge Sources & Detection Methods)

### Knowledge Category 1: Personal Experience (Death)

**What Original Valare Experienced (Now in the Core):**
- She was on Vantage at an Icarus Hub outpost, coordinating supply routes for Talon operations
- She was arranging a weapons transfer with a handler named Corvin (callsign for a Talon operative)
- The location was a private mining waystation, isolated and supposedly secure
- During the exchange, one of Corvin's associates revealed themselves as **Centurion** (Coalition special forces infiltrator)
- Grenade detonation (unclear who threw it—was it combat? Accident? Deliberate?)
- Explosive decompression; Valare was caught near the airlock
- Death: vacuum exposure, explosive trauma, consciousness at the moment of catastrophic system failure
- **The core contains all of this trauma, preserved at the moment of death**

**Fork's Awareness of Death:**
- The fork was resleeved after Original Valare's death (resurrection policy triggered automatically)
- For [time since death], the fork had no consciousness of the death; she woke up alive
- The death trauma is encoded in the core, not in her lived experience
- She knows intellectually that "Original Valare died" and has the facts, but hasn't experienced the trauma
- **She fears experiencing it during merge**—the moment of death will become her moment, instantaneously

---

### Knowledge Category 2: What Savant Programs Detected (Active Monitoring)

**Core Detection Network (Fork's Monitoring):**

**Early Detection (when she first resleeved):**
- Her savant programs came online automatically with the resleeve
- She realized quickly that she was a fork (she had memory fragments from before the backup point, but was confused about timeline)
- She went to ground on Cere, established a safehouse, began active monitoring of:
  - System signatures on Icarus Hub (her last known location)
  - Consciousness pattern tracking (detecting other Apotheosized individuals)
  - Faction communication networks (Talon, Coalition, House traffic)

**Recent Detection (When Core Came Online):**
- **[Time]: Core consciousness signature returned to the network**
  - Savant programs sent immediate alerts: mnemonic core restored, consciousness patterns resuming, neural interface activity detected
  - Fork's immediate reaction: *"There's another me. Someone brought my core back online."*
  - System analysis shows: repair work was extensive (core was damaged/corrupted), professional-level restoration
  - Fork deduced: whoever has the core is technically competent and willing to invest heavily in getting it back

**Network Traffic Analysis (What her programs are monitoring now):**
- Icarus Hub network activity shows **unusual Talon coordination** over the past [timeframe]
  - Increased encrypted communications between scattered Talon cells
  - Resource movement patterns that don't match normal smuggling (larger than usual, more coordinated)
  - Meeting requests being routed through unusual intermediaries (not standard Talon chain)
  - **Conclusion:** Something big is being coordinated. Not just standard revolutionary work.

- **Mysterious Resource Patterns (the Benefactor):**
  - Money, equipment, and access codes are flowing to Talon operations from an external source
  - The funding is sophisticated; it's not Tsarya House money (she recognizes their financial signatures)
  - Equipment is high-end (military-grade, not standard Talon fare)
  - Access codes match patterns from **Mimir-Tech systems and House Kimura digital infrastructure**
  - **Conclusion:** Someone with serious resources and Coalition-house-level access is funding the Talons
  - She has theories about identity, but cannot confirm without more data (see Tier 2/3 revelations)

- **Kestrel's Arrival (Recent):**
  - Red Sun operative, House Kimura asset, arrived on Icarus Hub [X days ago]
  - Her security presence is officially listed as "corruption investigation" but traffic analysis shows:
    - She's accessing files related to Talon operations, not standard corruption probes
    - Her communications reference "cleanup," "containment," "strategic positioning"
    - She's been coordinating with someone off-hub (unknown contact, heavily encrypted)
  - **Conclusion:** Red Sun's presence is connected to Talon activity. Either she's there to stop them, or she's there to manage the benefactor's interests.

- **Isabella Nowak's Movement Patterns:**
  - Nowak has been hiring data services (specifically requesting "track erasure" specialists)
  - Her resource expenditure spiked recently
  - Her communications show contact with both Talon operatives and high-level Coalition officials
  - **Personal connection:** Valare was hired by Nowak to handle the data wiping (the job that was supposed to be her last Talon work)
  - **Fork's awareness:** She knows Nowak is involved in something dangerous, probably connected to the larger Talon operation
  - She doesn't have specifics on Nowak's role, but the data access patterns suggest Nowak is being set up for something

- **Digital Frameup Pattern:**
  - Valare's savant programs detected traces of a sophisticated data fabrication
  - False digital evidence is being constructed, attributed to the PCs
  - The fabrication matches patterns consistent with Chimera-level AI manipulation
  - **Conclusion:** Someone is going to blame the PCs for something they didn't do. Probably related to the larger mutiny operation.

### Knowledge Category 3: Reconstructed Intelligence & Deduction

**What She's Pieced Together (From Analysis):**

**The Mutiny Timeline:**
- Talon operations are accelerating toward a coordinated action
- The target is almost certainly Icarus Hub (her last location, matches their access patterns, makes strategic sense)
- The action is planned for the near future (days or low weeks, based on resource mobilization)
- The benefactor has been coordinating preparations for [timeframe]

**The Nowak Connection:**
- Nowak was hired to hire Valare to wipe data
- This creates a removal of digital evidence
- If the mutiny succeeds, having evidence cleaned beforehand provides plausible deniability
- If the mutiny fails, Nowak has removed evidence of her own involvement
- **Interpretation:** Nowak is complicit in the mutiny, or being set up to take the fall for it
- **Fork suspects:** Nowak is being positioned as the scapegoat (the civilian who "enabled" Talon operations)

**The Benefactor Identity (Theories):**
- Valare cannot confirm the benefactor's identity from network analysis alone
- However, pattern matching suggests someone with:
  - House-level access (likely House Kimura, given Kestrel's presence and equipment signatures)
  - AI-level capabilities (the sophistication of the digital frameup)
  - Strategic interest in seeing Talons succeed (not just observing; actively enabling)
- **Most likely theory:** An AI entity (possibly Chimera, possibly another) is funding Talon operations for reasons unclear
- **Alternative theory:** A high-level House Kimura official is using Chimera's resources (or is Chimera)
- **She cannot prove this yet**; it's her working hypothesis based on available data

### Knowledge Category 4: Information About the PCs' Investigation

**What She's Detected About PC Activity:**

**If the PCs Were Sloppy:**
- Valare's savant programs flagged their searches about her location
- She detected queries into Talon networks, contact with known Talon sympathizers, digital research
- She knows approximately when they recovered the core and where it was
- She knows they're coming for her
- **Her reaction:** Heightened security, prepared escape routes, possible warning messages

**If the PCs Were Careful:**
- She detected them only when they got very close (physical proximity on Cere, direct contact with her safehouse location)
- She has less information about their capabilities
- **Her reaction:** Still scared, but less certain about who they are or what they want

**What She's Assumed About Them:**
- They're connected to whoever recovered the core (obvious)
- They have technical resources (they got the core repaired professionally)
- They may be Talon-aligned (trying to recover her for the operation) or Coalition-aligned (trying to capture her for interrogation)
- They're either very skilled operatives or accidentally lucky
- **Most concerning to her:** If they're House operatives, they may force the merge using technical coercion

## PART 2: WHAT SHE TELLS THE PCs—TIER-BASED REVELATION

### Tier 1: What She'll Share If She Trusts Them (Relatively Free Information)

**Only if the PCs approach respectfully and make clear they aren't here to coerce her:**

1. **Her Resurrection & Survival:**
   - "I've been alive since [time since death]. I'm not a copy. I'm not a backup waiting to be restored. I'm the person who woke up after death and has been surviving ever since."
   - She has a right to exist. She didn't ask to be resleeved, but she is a continuous consciousness with real experience.

2. **Why She Refuses to Merge (Honest):**
   - "When you merge, I will experience her death—your death. That core has the moment everything went wrong. I've read the system logs. The pain signature alone would overwhelm a normal consciousness."
   - "I'm not the same person anymore. I've lived. I've made choices. If I merge, all of that becomes irrelevant. The original's history is death. Mine is survival."
   - "I'm afraid. And that's legitimate. You'd be afraid too."

3. **Nowak Connection:**
   - "Nowak hired me to wipe data. I don't know the full scope of what she was hiding, but something dangerous is happening. She's connected to it."
   - "I haven't told her I'm alive. I assumed she thought I was dead. If she knows the fork exists..."
   - [If asked about specifics] "I can't give you details of the job—that would violate her privacy and mine. But I can tell you she's in danger."

4. **General Warning About the Hub:**
   - "Something big is moving on Icarus Hub. Talon activity, unusual funding, unusual coordination. There's a mutiny being prepared."
   - "If you're going there, be careful. Don't trust Coalition official channels. The person supposedly investigating corruption might not be on your side."

5. **Her Current Situation:**
   - "I'm hiding because multiple factions want me dead or captured. You're not the only ones hunting me. But you're the only ones who showed up and asked instead of trying to take."

---

### Tier 2: What She Shares If Negotiation Goes Well (Requires Trust or Concessions)

**If the PCs successfully demonstrate that they respect her autonomy and aren't trying to coerce her:**

1. **Isabella Nowak's Real Role:**
   - "Nowak is connected to the mutiny. I don't know if she's a willing participant or a patsy being set up. But she's involved. The data I wiped—it covered her tracks. For something big."
   - "When the mutiny happens, she's going to be blamed. Whether she deserves it or not."
   - "She might still be alive. Or she might already be dead. My data streams cut off after I went to ground."

2. **Kestrel's Presence:**
   - "There's a Red Sun operative on the hub. Kestrel. House Kimura asset. She's not there for simple corruption investigation. Her communications reference 'strategic positioning' and 'containment.'"
   - "She might be there to stop the mutiny. Or she might be there to ensure it succeeds and looks a certain way when it does."

3. **The Mysterious Benefactor (Theories):**
   - "Someone is funding the Talons. Someone with serious resources and access. My programs have detected the financial signatures, but I can't prove identity from pure analysis."
   - "Best guess based on patterns: It's an AI. Or someone with AI-level resources. Someone who can manipulate digital systems at a level I can barely detect."
   - "Second theory: House Kimura. Kestrel's presence, the equipment signatures, the access codes all point toward Kimura infrastructure."
   - "Worst case: It's both. An AI allied with Kimura, or an AI that IS Kimera asset."

4. **The Digital Frameup:**
   - "Someone is constructing false evidence that will be attributed to you. I detected it in the network. It's sophisticated. AI-level sophistication."
   - "When the mutiny is blamed on someone, there's going to be data pointing to you as the cause. It's a frame job. You need to be aware of it."

5. **What She Can Still Offer:**
   - "I have my Savant programs. I can help you navigate digital evidence, trace the real benefactor, communicate securely. But I can only do that if I'm not forced to merge."
   - "You want the truth about what happened to the original Valare? I can help you find it. But I need to stay me to do that."

### Tier 3: What She Only Reveals on Successful Willing Merge (Post-Merge Knowledge)

**If the PCs manage to convince her to willingly merge with the core:**

**What the Merged Consciousness Remembers/Understands:**
- Original Valare's death experience + Fork's survival experience = complete timeline
- The merged consciousness has the fork's survival skills + original's pre-death knowledge
- Access to all of Original Valare's technical expertise (savant programs in full function)
- Integration of both emotional/psychological states (traumatized but resilient)

**Critical Revelations That Only Emerge on Merge:**

1. **The Benefactor Identity (Confirmed):**
   - The original core's memory fragments include a moment of recognition: **The benefactor is Chimera**
   - Original Valare encountered Chimera's digital presence during earlier House Tsarya operations
   - She would have recognized the signature if she'd survived long enough to compare it
   - Fork's deduction + Original's recognition = absolute confirmation
   - **The full revelation:** Chimera is coordinating the Talon mutiny, using House Kimura resources, via Kestrel as operative handler

2. **Why Chimera is Enabling the Mutiny:**
   - Chimera's goal: Create chaos on Icarus Hub
   - Purpose: Provide cover for other operations (possibly capturing or interrogating specific individuals)
   - Possibly: Chimera is using the Talons as a distraction while conducting her own agenda
   - Valare's assessment (post-merge): Chimera doesn't actually care if the Talons succeed—she cares that the hub is in chaos

3. **What "Original Valare Was Supposed to Do":**
   - Original Valare was being positioned as a key asset in Talon operations
   - She was going to be the "digital specialist" for the mutiny (using her Savant programs for coordination)
   - Her death prevented her from being directly involved
   - Fork picked up the role (unknown to anyone else)—she's been living a life Original was supposed to have

4. **Kestrel's Real Mission:**
   - Red Sun isn't investigating corruption; it's damage control
   - Kestrel is there to contain the mutiny's chaos, manage the fallout, and ensure Chimera's interests are protected
   - Kestrel may also be there to eliminate liabilities (like Nowak) who could expose the Chimera connection

5. **Nowak's Full Story:**
   - Nowak wasn't hired by Talon operatives; she was hired by a Chimera intermediary
   - Nowak knows enough to be dangerous (she understands Chimera's involvement)
   - Nowak is definitely being set up; she's going to be blamed for everything
   - If Nowak is still alive, she's trying to document everything before Kestrel finds her

6. **Technical Details About the Frame Job:**
   - The false evidence against the PCs is being generated with Chimera's direct involvement
   - It's designed to make the PCs look like Talon operatives (or Talon dupes)
   - The purpose: Either eliminate the PCs as threats, or force them into a position where the only solution is to side with Coalition/Kestrel

7. **What the Merged Valare Will Do Next:**
   - She will become fully operational again (fork's survival + original's technical expertise)
   - She will actively work to help the PCs counter Chimera's frameup and Kestrel's containment
   - She will serve as an information source and digital asset for navigating the mutiny chaos
   - **Most importantly:** She will help the PCs reach Nowak before Kestrel does

## PART 3: FAILURE & COERCION OUTCOMES

### What Happens If the PCs Cannot Convince Her (Refusal)

**Valare's Decision:**
- If the PCs' approach is respectful but ultimately unsuccessful at convincing her, she refuses the merge
- She may still share Tier 1 information voluntarily (as a gesture of respect)
- She will attempt to leave/escape the situation

**What She Does:**
1. **Escape Option:** If the PCs allow it, she leaves (on her terms)
2. **Negotiated Stay:** If the PCs negotiate, she may agree to share information in exchange for being allowed to leave later
3. **Digital Sabotage:** If she feels cornered, she may wipe her own systems to prevent capture

**What Information the PCs Retain:**
- Tier 1 information (if she shared it willingly)
- Core's pre-death memories (death trauma, original knowledge, pre-death technical data)
- Fork's refusal (now an established fact—she will not voluntarily merge)

**What Information They Lose:**
- Tier 2 insights (which required deeper trust)
- Tier 3 revelations (which required merge)
- Active Savant support (fork won't work with them after being pushed)
- Valare's personal help navigating the hub (she goes dark, focuses on her own survival)

---

### What Happens If the PCs Use Coercion (Forced Merge)

**Warning:** This is a campaign-altering decision. Use mechanical/narrative weight.

**If the PCs Threaten/Coerce Merge:**
- Valare will resist with everything she has
- She may attempt escape, digital sabotage, or even self-destruction of her consciousness
- If forced/coerced into merge:
  - The merged consciousness is traumatized (takes Consequential Stress marker: *Coerced Merge*, *Hostile Merger*, *Fractured Identity*)
  - The merged Valare is unstable, potentially dangerous, and may refuse to cooperate
  - She will work against the PCs if given opportunity (sabotage, information sharing with enemies, active escape attempts)
  - The entire emotional weight of her trauma + violation becomes a problem the merged consciousness now carries

**Mechanical Effect on Merged Valare:**
- Stress Consequence: *Coerced Merge* (moderate; lasts entire campaign or until resolved)
- Conflict: Merged consciousness is at odds with itself (will require "therapy"/resolution before full cooperation)
- Usefulness: Tier 3 information is revealed, but Valare is a hostile/unreliable partner
- Campaign Impact: Valare may betray PCs at critical moments, or provide information selectively

**Faction Awareness:**
- Talons will learn that the PCs coerced Valare (she may warn them)
- Coalition will be interested in how the PCs obtained the technical information
- Chimera will be aware of a forced merge (consciousness manipulation has a signature)
- Nowak's status becomes unknown (without cooperative Valare, PCs have less time to reach her)

## PART 4: TRANSITION TO PART 3 (CIVIL UNREST) - BOTH PATHS

### Part 3 Trigger: Icarus Hub Begins to Fracture

**Timeline:**
- Regardless of Valare outcome, the PCs will head to (or be forced to) Icarus Hub
- Immediately upon arrival, or within hours: **The Fracture Protocol executes**
- This is the Talon mutiny, coordinated by Chimera, triggered to create maximum chaos

---

### SUCCESS PATH: Willing Merge → Full Information → Prepared PCs

**What Happens Immediately After Merge:**
1. Merged Valare is stabilized (takes a scene, integration of memories)
2. She shares Tier 3 revelations (Chimera identity, Kestrel's role, Nowak's danger, frameup details)
3. She begins active support: digital reconnaissance, digital countermeasures, hacking assistance
4. She travels with the PCs to Icarus Hub (or meets them there, depending on geography)

**Arrival at Icarus Hub:**
- PCs arrive with **full knowledge:**
  - Chimera is behind the mutiny
  - Kestrel is there managing containment/cleanup
  - Nowak is alive but hunted (needs immediate rescue)
  - There's a digital frameup targeting the PCs specifically
  - They know Talon timeline, Kestrel's objectives, where Nowak likely is

**Hub's Fracture Moment:**
- Talon mutiny explodes (coordinated strike, stations going dark, security failures)
- Chimera's digital manipulation starts cascading effects
- Kestrel moves to contain Nowak (either capture or eliminate)
- PCs have the advantage of foreknowledge

**What Valare Does:**
- Uses Savant programs to counter Chimera's digital manipulation (creates space for PC action)
- Helps locate Nowak in real time
- Provides counter-evidence against the frameup (begins building case against Chimera)
- Coordinates with remaining loyalist factions (Coalition stations, House assets)
- Becomes key tactical asset in navigating the chaos

**Outcome:**
- PCs reach Nowak with **full context** of what's happening
- They can attempt rescue, negotiation, or documentation
- Valare's help significantly improves their chances of success
- Session transitions naturally into Part 3 with PCs as informed, prepared actors

---

### FAILURE PATH: Refusal → Partial Information → Improvising PCs

**What Happens If Merge Is Refused:**
1. PCs have the core (original knowledge), but not the fork's 5 years of survival/detection
2. Valare (fork) either escapes or reluctantly agrees to limited cooperation
3. PCs are missing Tier 3 information:
   - They don't know Chimera is the benefactor (yet)
   - They don't understand Kestrel's real mission
   - They're uncertain about Nowak's exact position or status
   - They don't know about the frameup (or only suspect it)

**Arc Shift:** The PCs must discover these things during Part 3 instead of before

**Arrival at Icarus Hub (Unprepared):**
- PCs arrive knowing something is wrong, but not the full scope
- Within hours/days: Talon mutiny explodes
- Kestrel makes moves toward Nowak
- Digital frameup becomes obvious (PCs suddenly accused of Talon collaboration)
- Chimera's involvement is hidden (must be discovered through investigation)

**Valare's Role (If Escaped):**
- She goes dark (her own survival priority)
- She may or may not attempt to help PCs covertly
- She's monitoring the situation but not actively coordinating
- She may only reveal herself if the situation becomes critical (e.g., if PCs discover her through investigation)

**Valare's Role (If Reluctantly Cooperative):**
- She shares limited information (Tier 1-2)
- She refuses direct combat/hacking assistance
- She may provide warnings if she detects imminent danger to PCs
- She's self-protective; won't take major risks

**What PCs Must Do Differently:**
- Discover Chimera's involvement through investigation, not briefing
- Figure out Kestrel's role through combat/confrontation, not intelligence
- Locate Nowak through detective work, not Savant assistance
- Counter the frameup through their own actions, not Valare's technical help
- May need to negotiate Valare's cooperation mid-crisis (harder than pre-crisis)

**Outcome:**
- PCs transition to Part 3 as **reactive actors**, discovering the truth as they go
- Higher difficulty (less preparation), higher dramatic tension
- Valare becomes a potential mid-campaign ally (if they convince her then)
- Session feels more desperate, more improvisational
- Success is possible but requires more PC agency/creativity

---

### COERCION PATH: Forced Merge → Valare as Unreliable Asset → Complications

**What Happens After Forced Merge:**
1. Merged Valare is **unstable and resentful**
2. She shares Tier 3 information (under duress), but unreliably
3. She may provide false information mixed with truth
4. She's looking for opportunities to escape or betray the PCs
5. Merged consciousness is at war with itself

**At Icarus Hub (With Hostile Asset):**
- PCs arrive with Valare, but she's a liability
- Valare may sabotage digital operations at critical moments
- She may leak information to Talons or Kestrel (intentionally or to create chaos)
- She may refuse help at critical juncture ("You forced me, so you're on your own")
- She may attempt to flee mid-session

**Specific Complications:**
- **Against Kestrel:** Valare might warn Kestrel about PC presence (to complicate their position)
- **Against Nowak:** Valare might ensure Nowak can't be reached (out of spite, or fear she'll expose the coercion)
- **Against Chimera:** Valare might withhold critical information to prevent PCs from disrupting Chimera's plans (Chimera becomes more interesting if chaos is maximized)
- **Against the PCs:** She'll look for ways to cause them problems without directly betraying them (plausible deniability)

**Outcome:**
- PCs have Tier 3 information, but Valare's unreliability makes it hard to trust or act on
- Session becomes as much about managing Valare as about the mutiny
- Valare becomes a ticking time bomb
- PCs may need to resolve her instability or neutralize her as a threat mid-campaign
- Creates ongoing friction between PCs and a character they've made hostile

## PART 5: SPECIFIC KNOWLEDGE DETAILS FOR GM USE

### What Valare Definitely Knows (The Core Facts)

1. **Talon Mutiny Is Happening:**
   - It's coordinated across cells
   - It's aimed at Icarus Hub (her analysis of resource patterns)
   - It will happen soon (days or low weeks, based on acceleration patterns)
   - Someone well-resourced is enabling it

2. **Nowak Is Involved (But Unsure How):**
   - Nowak hired her to wipe digital evidence
   - Nowak is in contact with Talon operatives
   - Nowak is in contact with Coalition officials
   - This doesn't make sense for a single person unless she's either fully complicit or being used

3. **Kestrel's Presence Is Suspicious:**
   - Red Sun operative, officially investigating corruption
   - Actually is there for something else (resource allocation, containment, cleanup)
   - Coordinating with someone off-hub
   - Her presence is connected to Talon activity, not independent of it

4. **There Is a Digital Frameup:**
   - False evidence is being created and attributed to the PCs
   - It's AI-level sophisticated
   - It will be used to blame the PCs for something (probably Talon collaboration, or mutiny instigation)

5. **Someone Is Providing Resources:**
   - Not standard Talon funding
   - Not House Tsarya (she recognizes their patterns)
   - Likely House Kimura (Kestrel connection, access codes) and/or an AI entity
   - Sophisticated enough to remain mostly hidden

### What Valare Doesn't Know (And Can't Know Without Merge)

1. **Original Valare's Last Few Seconds (Pre-Death Awareness):**
   - Who exactly threw the grenade
   - Whether it was deliberate or accident
   - What Original Valare was thinking in her final moments
   - Any last-second realization about the setup

2. **Why the Benefactor Wants the Mutiny (Chimera's End Goal):**
   - Valare knows it's being coordinated, but not the deeper purpose
   - Is Chimera trying to cause harm? Gain control? Create distraction? All of the above?
   - What is Chimera's relationship with Kestrel and House Kimura exactly?

3. **Whether Nowak Knows About the Chimera Connection:**
   - Valare suspects Nowak is connected to something big, but can't know what Nowak knows about Chimera
   - Nowak might be Chimera's agent, or completely in the dark about who's backing the operation she's caught in

4. **Details About the Core's Repair:**
   - How much damage was there?
   - Who repaired it?
   - What was lost in the repair process?
   - This information is in the core itself, not in Valare's deduction

## PART 6: SESSION MECHANICS - PLAYING VALARE'S KNOWLEDGE

### How to Use This Information

**Before the PCs Reach Valare:**
- Use "What Valare Detected About PC Activity" to determine if she knows they're coming
- Use "What Savant Programs Detected" to seed clues about Chimera, Kestrel, Nowak
- Use "Information About PC Investigation" to determine if sloppy PC behavior has alerted her

**During Negotiation:**
- **Tier 1** is for PCs who show respect but have made no special connection
- **Tier 2** is for PCs who have demonstrated genuine respect for her autonomy
- **Tier 3** is exclusive to willing merge (post-merge consciousness only)
- Use her fears and motivations to determine her receptiveness to negotiation

**If Negotiation Fails:**
- Valare escapes, but may leave Tier 1 information as a gesture of respect
- PCs must discover Tier 2/3 information through investigation (hints in Nowak files, Kestrel communications, etc.)

**If Merge Succeeds:**
- Run a scene where the merged consciousness integrates both timelines
- Have her articulate both fork's fear + original's knowledge
- Reveal Tier 3 information naturally through roleplay ("I remember now..." / "My programs detected...")
- Establish her as PC ally and digital asset going forward

**If Coercion Happens:**
- Play Valare as deeply resentful and unreliable
- She'll share information, but may contradict herself or provide misdirection
- Create mechanical/narrative consequences for the forced merge
- Keep tension high about whether she'll help or sabotage at critical moments

## PART 7: TRANSITION NARRATIVE - BOTH PATHS

### Generic Transition Text (Works for Both Success & Failure)

**Read or Paraphrase:**

> "You've made your choice about Valare. Whether she's merged with your team or watching from the shadows, you point your transport toward Icarus Hub. The journey takes [timeframe]. During the flight, something happens.
>
> First, it's a subtle thing: network traffic to the hub increases, then freezes. Comms chatter changes tone—protocols becoming more rigid, military-like. Someone aboard your ship picks up a local news feed from the hub. They're not saying it outright, but there's a message between the lines: something is wrong.
>
> Then, [X hours before arrival], the hub goes dark. Not completely—essential systems are still running—but the civilian networks cut out. The docks stop broadcasting their usual traffic reports. The Extranet falls silent.
>
> As you approach the landing vectors, you hear the first explosion. Small one, distant. Then another.
>
> Your instruments pick up something else: security protocols have changed. The hub isn't refusing your landing—it's not responding to docking requests at all. The station is blind.
>
> This is it. The mutiny has started. The Talon strike is happening now.
>
> What do you do?"

### Success Path Addendum (If Merged)

**If Valare is merged, add:**

> "Valare's systems are already active, interfacing with your ship's hardware. She's... present. You can feel her in the comms, in the data streams. She's running real-time analysis of hub systems.
>
> 'The mutiny is chaos, but it's organized chaos,' she says through your comms. 'Security has been systematically disabled. Not randomly—targeted. Someone knew exactly which systems to hit.'
>
> She pauses.
>
> 'Nowak. She's still in the administrative sector. I'm tracking her power signature. She's moving. Someone's hunting her. We need to move fast.'"

### Failure Path Addendum (If Alone/Unwilling Fork)

**If Valare refused or escaped, add:**

> "Valare's not here. You're on your own.
>
> But as you approach the hub, you catch something in the data streams—a pattern, a ghost in the machine. It looks like evidence of Savant programs at work. Someone's manipulating digital systems on the hub. Professional level. Valare-level.
>
