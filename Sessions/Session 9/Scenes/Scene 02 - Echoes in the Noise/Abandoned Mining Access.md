# Abandoned Mining Access

**Zone Type:** Physical — derelict tunnels, environmental hazards, off-grid territory

## Aspects

- *The Rock Remembers*
- *Unstable Footing, No Witnesses*

## Description

Old mining shafts that Cere's operators sealed off and forgot about — or pretend they forgot about. The tunnels are dark, the air is thin, and the footing shifts between compacted regolith and loose rubble. No surveillance, no mesh coverage, no help if something goes wrong. The kind of place someone goes when they don't want to be found by anyone with access to a network.

## Clue

Fresh handprints on a support strut, heat residue on a wall panel, and a dropped med injector — Valare's. She came through here recently. Available through Notice / Investigate (physical) rolls.

## Complication

Environmental hazards (unstable sections, bad air pockets) or territorial scavengers force a speed-versus-care choice. Pushing through fast risks stress or injury aspects. Going careful costs time.

## Compel Target

- *The Rock Remembers* → The tunnels shift; a section collapses behind the PCs, forcing them forward
- *The Habit Behind the Composure* (Aeddarius) → He pushes through a hazard rather than admitting caution, revealing urgency
