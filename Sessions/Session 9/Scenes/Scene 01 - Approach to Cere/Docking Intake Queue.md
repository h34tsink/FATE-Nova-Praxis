# Docking Intake Queue

**Zone Type:** Transitional — ship-to-station boundary

## Aspects

- *Everyone's Watching the New Arrival*
- *Fees Are Flexible If You Know Who to Pay*

## Description

The intake queue is a bottleneck of ships jockeying for berth assignments. Dock runners and fee collectors circle like scavengers. Every new arrival gets sized up — cargo, crew complement, how much they're willing to pay to skip the line. [[GM AI/Entity Cards/R2/Kal Paddock (R2 Minor Contact)|Kal Paddock]] operates here, smoothing entry for the right price.

## Mechanics

- **Kal Paddock (social):** He's seen Valare. Will confirm she was here — for a price, and only if the PCs don't set off his instincts
- **Compel:** *Everyone's Watching the New Arrival* → A local power broker notices the crew's ship and sends someone to assess them before they dock — costs time and cover

## NPC Introduction — Kal Paddock

Kal is part of this zone's infrastructure. He doesn't approach — the PCs have to pass through him.

> *"The dock intake clears you through, but there's a second check. Not official — just a guy in coveralls at a secondary console, scanning manifests and watching the foot traffic off the ramp. He hasn't looked at you directly, but he hasn't waved you through either. Everyone else who walked past got a nod. You didn't."*

**Why now:** The PCs are new and unfamiliar. Kal assesses every new arrival to decide if they're profit, trouble, or both. He's not curious — he's doing his job.

**If PCs ignore him:** He lets them pass, but they miss the easiest lead in the scene. He doesn't chase.

**If PCs engage:** He opens transactional: *"You can ask once cheap, twice expensive."*
