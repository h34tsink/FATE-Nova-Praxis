# Outer Approach

**Zone Type:** Ship sensors / comms range — pre-docking

## Aspects

- *Cluttered Traffic, Sloppy Transponders*
- *This Place Doesn't Want to Be Found Easily*

## Description

The outer approach to [[Locations/Cere's Asteroid Habitats|Cere]] is a disorganized queue of battered haulers and rock-runners circling a habitat cluster that looks bolted together by someone who ran out of time and material. No beacon, no welcome signal — just a scrambled transponder ping and crosstalk in shorthand you don't have context for.

## Mechanics

- **Passive comms scan:** Local chatter includes a reference to "the new sleeve" — someone arrived two cycles ago, paid for a private berth in cash-equivalent, hasn't been seen since
- **Active scan risk:** Any digital query matching Valare's profile may trigger her savant detection programs
