## Recap & Transition

- The PCs have just navigated the chaos of the Icarus Hub, survived the uprising, and confronted the fallout of Nowak’s actions.
- Valare’s core has been recovered, and the emotional VR interaction revealed the need to find and convince her fork to merge.
- The team has new leads: Cere’s habitats, Talon contacts, the Shadow Market, and Cere’s mining colony.

## Key Objectives

### 1. Track Down Valare’s Fork

- Investigate Cere’s habitats and the Shadow Market for contacts (Seren, Kal Paddock).
- Explore Cere’s mining shafts and refugee quarters for clues.
- Prepare for tense negotiations and possible moral dilemmas.

### 2. Deal with Civil Unrest Fallout

- Lighthouse Security is cracking down; the PCs may be targeted as instigators or witnesses.
- The Crimson Sons and Kestrel remain active threats, seeking to eliminate loose ends.

### 3. Interrogate Nowak (if captured)

- Use information from Valare’s merged memories to press Nowak for details about the mysterious benefactor and Talon plans.

## Scene Guidance

### 1. Arrival at Cere

- Describe Cere’s harsh, lawless environment: failing infrastructure, suspicious locals, and dangerous mining tunnels.
- Emphasize the urgency—Valare’s fork is hiding, and time is running out before Talon agents or Coalition operatives intervene.

### 2. Investigation & Social Encounters

- Refugees and vendors may provide leads, but require bribes, favors, or acts of goodwill.
- Kal Paddock is a key NPC—his information may be valuable or misleading.
- The Deepshaft Tavern and Core Markets are prime locations for gathering intel.

### 3. Exploration & Hazards

- Mining shafts present environmental dangers: collapses, gas pockets, scavenger creatures.
- PCs must balance risk and reward, possibly splitting up or using specialized skills to navigate hazards.

### 4. Emotional & Ethical Challenges

- Convincing Valare’s fork to merge requires empathy, persuasion, and possibly mnemonics editing.
- Highlight the trauma and fear associated with merging—roleplay the fork’s reluctance and the PCs’ approach.

### 5. Consequences & Next Steps

- **Success:** Valare merges, providing critical intel and future support.
- **Failure:** The fork flees or is lost, limiting the PCs’ options and Talon reputation.
- The PCs’ actions shape future Coalition and Talon relations.

## GM Tips

- Track time and evolving threats—NPCs may escape, die, or turn on the PCs if delayed.
- Use Cere’s atmosphere to heighten tension and stakes.
- Let player choices drive the narrative, especially in social and ethical dilemmas.
