# Session 8: Recap

## Key Events

- The party restored [[Valare]]’s core, uncovering the truth behind the data scrub and [[Isabella Nowak|Nowak]]’s role.
- Learned Valare’s fork is hiding in [[Siri]]’s habitats, with leads to Talon contacts ([[Seren]]), the [[Shadow Market]], and [[Cere]]’s mining colony.
- Negotiated with Valare’s fork, who is traumatized and reluctant to merge, requiring empathy and persuasion.
- Secured Valare’s core in a vault, setting up the next phase of the mission.
- Dan acquired a battle cruiser, giving the party new travel and tactical options.

## Next Steps for Session 9

- Track down Valare’s fork in [[Siri]]’s habitats, [[Cere]]’s mining colony, and the [[Shadow Market]]. Use contacts like [[Seren]] and [[Kal Paddock]] for leads.
- Approach the merge with Valare’s fork: choose empathy, logic, manipulation, or leverage. Each method affects Talon relations and Valare’s trust.
- Prepare for confrontation with [[Isabella Nowak|Nowak]], [[Chimera]], and the [[Crimson Sons]]. Expect evolving threats and shifting alliances.
- Use the battle cruiser and new intel to plan travel, infiltration, and possible combat or negotiation. Time is critical—NPCs may escape or turn hostile if delayed.
- Navigate Cere’s lawless environment, facing hazards, suspicious locals, and moral dilemmas.

## Session Outcome

The party stands at a crossroads: their choices in handling Valare’s fork, the merge, and their approach to the final showdown will shape their reputation, alliances, and the fate of the illegal research. Session 9 promises high stakes, tense investigations, and the resolution of the Shadow War plotline. GM guidance: let player actions drive the narrative, highlight emotional and ethical challenges, and use Cere’s atmosphere to heighten tension and urgency.
