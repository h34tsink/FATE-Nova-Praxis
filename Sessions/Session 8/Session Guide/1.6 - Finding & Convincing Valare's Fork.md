- The players will need to **track down the fork’s location**, which requires some investigation and savvy social maneuvering. They might need to speak to Talon sympathizers, hack into station records, or negotiate with less-than-reputable contacts who may have helped [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]’s fork escape.

  - **Possible Leads**:
    - **Nearby Habitats**: [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] mentions a couple of small nearby habitats where her fork could be hiding. These habitats are known to be loosely aligned with the Talons and may provide refuge for someone on the run.
         - [[0.1 Fork on Cere]]
  
    - **Talon-Friendly Contact**: There is a Talon contact named **[[Seren – Talon-Friendly Contact|Seren]]**, who has helped members of the Talons in the past. Seren operates out of a hidden outpost in an asteroid cluster and may know where [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]'s fork is hiding.
         - [[Seren – Talon-Friendly Contact]]

    - **[[Shadow Market]]**: The players could also try their luck at the [[Shadow Market]], a notorious haven for those living off the grid. Here, they might find clues about where [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]'s fork went or someone who helped facilitate her escape.

#### **Handling the Fork**

Once the players locate [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]'s fork, they will find her **resleeved in a [[Cybersleeve]]**, clearly nervous and very guarded. She’s been living off the grid, and it shows in her worn-out demeanor. She’s frightened not only of being captured but also of the prospect of [[Merging]].

- The players need to convince her to **accept the merge**. This can be approached in several ways:

  - **Persuasion**: Using kindness and reassurance, players could promise to protect her from further harm, appealing to her desire for safety.

 	- **Logic and Reason**: They might try to convince her that [[Merging]] will make her more powerful—reuniting the knowledge from the two versions will allow her to be fully functional and contribute more effectively to the Talon cause.

  - **Manipulation**: Players could deceive her into thinking that the merge is the only way to avoid a worse fate—perhaps by implying that [[House Tsarya]] is on her tail, and she can’t afford to be weak or fragmented.

  - **Leverage**: The players could even use **leverage** against her—threatening to abandon her entirely unless she agrees to merge. This approach may have consequences on how [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] and the Talons perceive the players afterward.

- **The Emotional Conflict**:

  - Make this a **tense and emotional scene**. [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]'s fork will likely argue back, fearful and angry. She might cry out that the memories will break her or that she's finally found freedom and doesn't want to give that up.

  - The players’ words and actions here should have a tangible impact on the scene. If they use **empathy**, [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] might soften, trusting them despite her fears. If they use **coercion**, she might reluctantly agree, but with a deep sense of betrayal.

**PCs try to reassure her or convince her to merge:**

**Valare**: (Listening, her breathing still shaky) "You don’t understand what it’s like... The fear... the helplessness. To feel your body fail and know there's nothing left. It’s like falling into a bottomless pit, knowing you’ll never hit the ground because there’s nothing there to catch you."

- _She looks at the players with wide eyes, searching for any sign that they actually understand. She reaches up, touching her temple as if trying to hold herself together._

**Valare**: "If I merge, it has to be for something more. Something... worth living through that nightmare again. If you promise me that... if you make me believe it... maybe I can do it."

- _Valare wipes her eyes, trying to regain composure, but the fear is still there, evident in every trembling movement. Her voice grows softer, almost a plea._

**Valare**: "Just... promise me I won’t be left behind after. That I’ll matter. That all of this will matter."

---

**If the players succeed in convincing her:**

**Valare**: (Nods slowly, her expression weary) "Alright... I’ll do it. I’ll merge." (She closes her eyes, breathing deeply to steady herself) "But you stay here. You make sure it happens smoothly. If anything goes wrong... you keep me safe."

- _She looks at the players, her eyes a mix of fear and determination. Her body is still trembling, but she stands, as if preparing herself for what’s to come._

**Valare**: "I want to live... not just survive. If this is the way, then... let’s get it over with."
