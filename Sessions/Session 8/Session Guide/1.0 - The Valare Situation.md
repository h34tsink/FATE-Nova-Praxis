### Adding Valare’s Situation Before Part 3: Civil Unrest

To set up the events leading into Part 3, you need to address [[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]]'s current predicament. Here’s a detailed step-by-step guide to introduce [[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]]’s involvement, the players' actions, and potential complications before diving into Part 3.

#### **1. Introduction to Valare’s Situation**

- **Who is [[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]]?**
    - [[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]] was hired by [[Isabella Nowak]] to erase any trace of the PCs' Extranet activity related to their previous mission. She has since turned against [[House Tsarya]], wanting to help the oppressed apostates. However, her activities caught the attention of a Centurion, and she was killed when a grenade went off during a smuggling operation. Her sleeve and core are hidden in the ship known as the Chickenhawk.
- **[[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]]'s Condition**:
    - [[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]]'s sleeve was damaged, and her mnemonic core now remains intact in the Chickenhawk's engine room. The PCs must retrieve her core and attempt to get information from her to proceed further.

#### **2. Recovering Valare’s Core**

- **Getting to the Chickenhawk**:
    
    - The Chickenhawk is attached to Vantage station via a damaged docking umbilical, which makes internal access impossible. Players must find an external entry point.
    
    - The PCs have two options for getting into the ship:
        1. **Travel Through the Arm**: Players can attempt to access the ship by navigating down the heavily secured passageway inside the station arm. They need to bypass three sealed blast doors and a security checkpoint manned by Centurions and Lonestar drones.
        2. **Exit the Station**: Another approach is for the PCs to go outside the station and enter via the mag-locked connection on top of the Chickenhawk. Cybersleeved PCs won't need suits but will need mag boots or zero-g assist devices to move efficiently.
        
- **Security Measures**:
    
    - Depending on the PCs' actions and how cautious they are, they may find themselves under heavy security scrutiny. If Lester, who is helping them, gets captured, Talons will know the players’ plan and potentially intercept them. Players might also find Lester's body if the plan gets leaked.

#### **3. Interaction with Valare**

- **[[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]]'s Sleeve**:
    - Upon reaching the Chickenhawk, players find [[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]]’s sleeve, bruised and swollen due to the explosion and vacuum exposure. Her mnemonic core is intact, and she can be interacted with in Virtual Reality.

- **Virtual Reality Meeting**:
    - If brought up in VR, [[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]] is clearly traumatized by her death experience. Players need to calm her to extract valuable information. She suffers from an "Extreme Mental Consequence" due to the trauma. The players may need to use skills such as **Persuasion** or **Mnemonics Editing** to stabilize her emotional state.

- **[[Characters/NPCs/Valare|Valare]]’s Requirements**: [[1.2 - Valare Mnemonic Core]]
	- [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare]]’s mnemonic core reveals that she possesses a **remote backup module**. Before her death, she had set up a safety protocol that allowed for a backup of her consciousness to be made remotely. Unknown to the PCs, **this fork has already been resleeved** and exists independently somewhere off the Icarus Hub. This presents a significant problem: the fork of [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] does not want to merge with the version in the core.
#### **Outcome**
- **If the Players Convince [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] to Merge**:
  - The fork agrees to merge, but it’s not an easy process. [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] will need time to **integrate the memories**, during which she is vulnerable and prone to panic attacks or flashbacks. The PCs will need to **protect her** during this time, as the merge leaves her unstable and temporarily incapacitated.

  - Once merged, [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] will provide the information they need—including details about the **mysterious benefactor** aiding the Talons and the plans for the upcoming mutiny. The experience leaves her shaken but grateful, and she may even offer to help the players moving forward.

- **If the Players Fail to Convince [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]**:
  - If the players cannot convince her to merge, [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]’s fork refuses outright. She may even try to **escape**, causing the players to decide whether to let her go or **forcefully contain her**.

  - Without the merge, the players can still interrogate her for information, but the fork is fragmented, meaning she only has **partial intel**. She knows about Nowak hiring her, but the deeper secrets—the benefactor, the timing of the mutiny—are locked in the mnemonic core
  
  - This outcome may force the players to **find another way** to unlock the core’s memories, such as taking it to a skilled **Savant** or using an **advanced data extraction tool**. It adds additional steps and challenges to their mission.
#### **4. Information Provided by Valare**

- **Nowak’s Location**:
    - [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] provides information about Nowak, who is based on the Icarus Hub. She informs the PCs that Nowak was initially attempting to eliminate any evidence linking her to the events involving the PCs. Nowak wanted plausible deniability regarding illegal activities on the hub.

- **Talon Activities**:
    - [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] also reveals that she had been working with the [[Talons of Freedom]] on Icarus Hub and Vantage before her death. The Talons were preparing a rebellion against [[House Tsarya]], and [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] played a key role in covering their activities and smuggling supplies until her death.
