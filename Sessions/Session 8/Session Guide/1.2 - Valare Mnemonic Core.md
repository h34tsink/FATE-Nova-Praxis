[[Sessions/Session 7/Valare]]’s mnemonic core reveals that she possesses a **remote backup module**. Before her death, she had set up a safety protocol that allowed for a backup of her consciousness to be made remotely. Unknown to the PCs, **this fork has already been resleeved** and exists independently somewhere off the Icarus Hub. This presents a significant problem: the fork of [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] does not want to merge with the version in the core.

### **Setting the Scene**
- - **Fragmented Mindset**: [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]'s mindset is at a level 4 fragmentation. This level of damage makes it illegal under Coalition regulations to reinitialize her consciousness. If the players attempt to bring her into a VR environment, the Coalition AI will warn them about the damage and risks involved, emphasizing that such an action is prohibited in her current state.

	- **Coalition AI Warning**: If Players scan the mindset first.
		- "Warning: The subject's mnemonic core has been detected at fragmentation level 4. Initializing consciousness under such conditions presents severe risks, including irreversible cognitive breakdown and data corruption. This level of fragmentation violates Coalition safety protocols. Proceeding with any VR reinitialization is illegal and will be reported to Coalition authorities. Please abort any action to reinitialization immediately."

	- **Coalition AI Warning**:  If Players try reinitialization 
		- "Warning: Subject's mnemonic core is detected at Level 4 fragmentation. Initializing the mindset under these conditions is strictly prohibited by Coalition regulations. Severe risks include irreversible cognitive collapse, complete data corruption, and permanent psychological damage to the entity. Attempting to proceed with reinitialization will result in automatic penalties, including a Reputation Hit of 10, and further attempts will be escalated to Coalition authorities. This action is not authorized. Please terminate immediately or face consequences."

- **Consequences**: If players attempt to bypass this warning and proceed with reinitializing [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]'s mindset without addressing the fragmentation, they will receive a **Reputation Hit of 10**. Furthermore, reinitialization will be blocked unless they either **hack** the system to bypass safety checks or **repair** the fragmentation to a safer level.

---

- **The Backup Fork of [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]]** is now resleeved and has left the Icarus Hub. [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] has ideas about where her fork might be hiding, but it’s going to take some investigation to track her down. She believes the fork could have sought refuge on one of several nearby habitats or perhaps even with a Talon-friendly contact in a different sector.

- **The Problem**: The backup fork **knows** that [[Sessions/Session 7/Valare|Valare]] died from vacuum exposure and is terrified of the memory merge. She understands that [[Merging]] with the mnemonic core would mean taking in all the traumatic memories that the core contains—including the horror of death.
  The fork has decided that she would rather exist separately, even if it means withholding critical information that the PCs desperately need.
