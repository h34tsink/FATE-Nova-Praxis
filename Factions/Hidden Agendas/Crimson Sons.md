# Crimson Sons

A [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura\|House Kimura]] subsidiary that operates as a shell company for the [[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Red Sun\|Red Sun]] yakuza organization. On paper, Crimson Sons is a legitimate security and personnel management firm. In practice, it launders Red Sun operations through Kimura's corporate structure.

## Function

- **Official:** Security consulting, personnel placement, corporate investigations
- **Actual:** Red Sun recruitment pipeline, revenue laundering, intelligence gathering for Kimura
- **Key service:** Provides "contractors" (Red Sun operatives) to other Kimura subsidiaries needing deniable muscle

## Relationship to Red Sun

The Red Sun evolved from pre-Exodus yakuza traditions. Kimura tolerates (and profits from) Red Sun activities through Crimson Sons, which gives the criminal organization corporate legitimacy and access to House infrastructure. In return, Red Sun handles problems that Kimura can't address through official channels.

## Relationship to Kestrel

[[Kestrel]] operates within this ecosystem — officially a "talent acquisition specialist" for Crimson Sons, actually a Kimura operative managing deniable contracts. Her shop on [[Locations/Cere\|Cere]], "Serendipity: Consultations and Arbitrations," is a Crimson Sons front.
