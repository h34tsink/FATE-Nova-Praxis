# Remnant Forces

Fragmented survivors, holdouts, and ex-command elements from the pre-Coalition governments — the National Freedom Alliance and the United Federation of Earth. They lost the Consolidation Wars and the final engagement at Tellerus Fields on Mercury, but never fully surrendered.

## Origins

When the [[Glossary/Technophage\|technophage]] devastated Earth in 70 PS, the surviving Alliance and Federation militaries were scattered. The [[Glossary/Exodus\|Exodus]] forced everyone into space, and the rising megacorporations consolidated into the [[Factions/The Coalition/_Coalition Overview\|Coalition of Free States]]. The remnant forces — broken units, loyal officers, ideological holdouts — refused to submit.

The last major engagement was the **Battle of Tellerus Fields** on Mercury, where remnant forces seized antimatter production facilities and attempted to hold them against Coalition troops. They lost. Survivors scattered into Mercury's tunnel network, and the organized military resistance effectively ended.

## Current State

Remnant forces today are not a unified faction. They are:

- **Tunnel rats** hiding in Mercury's vast tunnel systems, surviving on scavenged supplies
- **Scattered cells** across outer colonies where Coalition enforcement is thin
- **Ideological sympathizers** who remember (or romanticize) the old governments
- **Recruitment pools** for more organized resistance groups like the [[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Talons of Freedom\|Talons of Freedom]]

Some remnant veterans joined the Talons under [[Legate Aaron Blackburn\|Legate Blackburn]] — himself a Coalition officer who secretly sabotaged an assault on remnant survivors, allowing them to regroup. The Talons are the remnants' most successful evolution.

## Campaign Relevance

Remnant forces are background pressure — they explain where the Talons' military expertise comes from and why outer colonies resist Coalition authority. Individual remnant NPCs might be encountered as:

- Bitter veterans working as mercenaries or smugglers
- Tunnel-dwelling survivalists with pre-Exodus tech knowledge
- Recruiters feeding people into the Talons pipeline
- Old soldiers with intelligence about Coalition military weaknesses
