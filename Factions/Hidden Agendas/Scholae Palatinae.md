## Overview

The Scholae Palatinae are elite Praetorian military units maintaining presence throughout Coalition space. Unlike Phalanx Formations (which deny existence), Scholae Palatinae maintain limited public presence while operating as fully-resourced mobile fortresses serving mysterious masters. Their relationship to core Coalition power structures remains ambiguous and dangerous.

## Military Structure

### Unit Composition
Each Scholae Palatinae unit organized around Praetorian Guard principles:
- **Elite membership** - 4-6 members per unit; highly trained specialists
- **Specialist team** - Each member brings distinct expertise/capability
- **Fully resourced** - Access to advanced weapons, vehicles, and technology
- **Mobile fortress** - Operate independently with extensive equipment and supplies
- **Rapid response** - Can deploy and execute complex operations rapidly

### Equipment & Weaponry
- **Military-grade systems** - Advanced weapons exceeding standard Coalition issue
- **Exotic technology** - Some equipment appears pre-Exodus or experimental
- **Vehicle capability** - Mobile vehicles adapted for combat and infiltration
- **Independent supply** - Self-sufficient; can operate extended periods without resupply
- **Destructive potential** - Small units with capability to neutralize much larger forces

## Operational Organization

### Geographic Distribution
- **Widespread presence** - Known to operate throughout Coalition space
- **Strategic positioning** - Located at key political, military, and corporate centers
- **Known bases** - Unlike Phalanx, some Scholae Palatinae bases are publicly acknowledged
- **Visible operations** - Some activities conducted in public; others clearly classified
- **Numbers ambiguous** - Confirmed existence of 3+ units; total strength unknown

### Command Authority
- **Tetragon service** - Answer to mysterious "Tetragon" authority
- **Independent operations** - Don't answer to Coalition military command
- **Unknown masters** - Tetragon identity and nature remains classified
- **Operational autonomy** - Choose missions and operational methods freely
- **Unclear allegiance** - Which faction(s) they actually serve unknown

## The Tetragon Mystery

### Identity Question
Scholae Palatinae loyalty to "Tetragon" raises fundamental questions:
- **Not Coalition government** - Don't answer to Coalition Oversight or Senate
- **Not single House** - Appear to serve multiple Houses simultaneously (sometimes conflictingly)
- **Hidden structure** - Could be secret council, ancient organization, alien force
- **Named deliberately** - "Tetragon" implies four-sided structure or four leaders
- **Purposeful obscurity** - Identity deliberately hidden from Coalition awareness

### Theories
- **Ancient order** - Possibly pre-Exodus organization maintaining itself through centuries
- **Alien masters** - May serve non-human intelligence
- **Collective AI** - Possible serving evolved Mimir or artificial intelligence
- **Corporate cabal** - Secret House alliance or combination of Houses
- **Individual master** - Single powerful individual controlling multiple factions

### Implications
- **Threat to Coalition** - Force controlling elite military units outside Coalition authority
- **Hidden power** - Real power structure may lie behind Tetragon
- **Competing agendas** - Multiple factions all answer to same ultimate authority
- **Coordination explanation** - Might explain otherwise mysterious coordination between factions
- **True enemy** - Tetragon could be actual Coalition adversary
