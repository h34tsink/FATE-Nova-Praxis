## Overview

The Phalanx Formations are secret Coalition military teams operating under Oversight command authority. They handle humanity's most dangerous weapons, existential threats, and classified operations requiring absolute secrecy and plausible deniability. Unlike public military forces, Phalanx teams maintain minimal official presence while wielding extraordinary destructive capability.

## Organization & Command Structure

### Oversight Authority
- **Direct command** - Answer only to Oversight, not standard Coalition military
- **Operational independence** - Free from normal military chain of command restrictions
- **Resource allocation** - Receive unlimited funding without accountability
- **Mission authorization** - Conduct operations approved by Oversight council
- **Personnel selection** - Recruit members from military and special forces

### Unit Organization
- **Small teams** - Operate in small, mobile units for operational security
- **Elite composition** - Highly trained specialists selected for specific competencies
- **Compartmentalization** - Teams operate independently; don't know other teams' missions
- **Rotation system** - Regularly rotate personnel to prevent knowledge accumulation
- **Expendable perception** - Can be disavowed if operations exposed or fail

### Geographic Distribution
- **Scattered locations** - Positioned throughout Coalition space
- **Strategic placement** - Located near potential crisis points
- **Autonomous operation** - Operate from hidden bases independent from Coalition infrastructure
- **Rapid deployment** - Can reach any location in Coalition space within hours
- **Unknown quantity** - Exact number of teams and personnel kept secret

## Operational Capabilities

### Weapons & Equipment
- **WMD management** - Maintain and deploy weapons of mass destruction
- **Experimental systems** - Use bleeding-edge military technology
- **Specialized gear** - Equipment tailored for specific mission requirements
- **Technical sophistication** - Access to technology beyond standard Coalition military
- **Classified weaponry** - Weapons so dangerous their existence remains hidden

### Tactical Specialization
- **Elimination operations** - Assassinate high-value targets
- **Containment** - Isolate and contain threats to civilization
- **Cover-up operations** - Clean up after disasters and hide evidence
- **Covert insertion** - Deploy to hostile locations without detection
- **Crisis response** - Handle existential threats requiring military response

## Mission Categories

### Existential Threat Response
- **Alien contact** - Handle potential alien arrival or first contact scenarios
- **AI activation** - Respond to rogue AI threats (like evolved Savants)
- **Biological threats** - Contain pandemic or bioengineered threats
- **Dimensional anomalies** - Unknown threats with existential implications
- **Planet-scale danger** - Any threat endangering entire worlds or populations

### Internal Security
- **Faction containment** - Suppress factions becoming too powerful/dangerous
- **Military coup prevention** - Eliminate military threats to Coalition government
- **Religious extremism** - Suppress religious movements threatening Coalition stability
- **Scientific disaster** - Contain experiments gone wrong or rogue research
- **Power consolidation** - Eliminate threats to Oversight control

### Weapons Management
- **WMD deployment** - Deliver weapons of mass destruction when authorized
- **Nuclear arsenal** - Maintain nuclear weapons and prevent unauthorized access
- **Bioweapons** - Manage biological and genetic weapons
- **Experimental weapons** - Test exotic weapons in isolated locations
- **Destruction operations** - Destroy weapons of mass destruction when necessary

### Cover-Up Operations
- **Witness elimination** - Remove evidence of classified operations
- **Evidence destruction** - Clean up after classified events
- **Information control** - Hunt down and retrieve classified information leaks
- **False flag operations** - Create incidents blamed on other factions
- **History revision** - Ensure Coalition records omit sensitive information

## Operational Methods

### Plausible Deniability
- **Shell operations** - Conduct missions through civilian or foreign covers
- **Disavowal capability** - Can deny involvement if operations discovered
- **Rogue elements theory** - Blame operations on dissident groups if exposed
- **Corporate fronts** - Use shell companies to maintain operational cover
- **Civilian casualties acceptable** - Limited concern for collateral damage

### Secrecy Protocol
- **Information compartmentalization** - Operatives know only their specific mission
- **Personnel termination** - Some operatives killed after mission completion to prevent information leaks
- **Memory modification** - Potential use of technologies to suppress memories
- **Self-destruct option** - Equipment designed to destroy evidence if captured
- **Burn protocol** - Elimination of all evidence of operations if compromised

### Rules of Engagement
- **Authorized to operate anywhere** - No geographic restrictions on operations
- **Authorized to use any force** - No restrictions on weapons or tactics employed
- **Authorized to eliminate threats** - Kill anyone perceived as threat to operation
- **Authorized to commit crimes** - Can commit acts normally forbidden by Coalition law
- **Zero accountability** - No legal consequences for actions taken under orders

- **Check and balance failure** - Completely unaccountable to any civilian authority
- **Potential autonomy** - Phalanx may now operate semi-independently from Oversight control
