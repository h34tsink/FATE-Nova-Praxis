## Overview

The Rings of Olympus are Mars' largest criminal organization, specializing in drugs, prostitution, and illegal trade. Despite criminal nature, they maintain reputation for taking care of their own and have become crucial support system for apostates surviving in Coalition space.

## Organization & Structure

### Rank System
Unique identifying method based on rings (source of organization name):
- **Entry members** - Single simple golden ring
- **Advancement** - Earn small diamonds to add to ring; three diamonds = advancement
- **Multiple rings** - After three diamonds, earn second ring; cycle continues
- **Highest rank** - Members with ring on every finger, each bearing three diamonds

### Geographic Base
**Olympus** - Mars city with exceptionally tight security and dense mesh infrastructure. Organization maintains primary operations here despite security environment.

### Operational Scale
- **Large organization** - Extensive reach throughout Coalition space
- **Distributed network** - Multiple cells and operations across multiple worlds
- **Survival expertise** - Deep knowledge of Coalition enforcement patterns and weaknesses

## Criminal Operations

### Primary Markets
- **Drug trade** - Manufacture, distribution, and sale of illegal substances
- **Prostitution** - Extensive sex work operations
- **Theft & smuggling** - Stealing and moving contraband
- **Kidnapping & extortion** - High-value criminal operations
- **Murder for hire** - Contract killing services
- **Black market goods** - General illegal merchandise trafficking

### Apostate Support
Unique role assisting apostates:
- **Employment provision** - Offer illegal work opportunities for apostates without other options
- **Protection services** - Shield apostate communities from Coalition enforcement
- **Supply networks** - Provide access to goods unavailable through normal channels
- **Integration challenge** - Apostates working for Rings often forced to compromise values and morality

## Market Position & Alliances

### Strategic Partnerships
- **House Turin** - Recent relationship facilitating weapons trade; Rings provide compiler access to support weapons smuggling
- **Talons of Freedom** - Recent deal to meet urgent weapons demands
- **Pirate organizations** - Cooperation for smuggling and distribution
- **Other criminal syndicates** - Occasional partnerships on specific operations

### Coalition Relationship
- **Enforcement** - Subject to Protectorate and security force operations
- **Mesh security evasion** - Possess extensive knowledge of security holes and blind spots
- **Corruption** - Have members infiltrated into Centurion and other security services
- **Adaptation** - Adjust operations to match Coalition enforcement patterns

## Internal Culture

### Loyalty System
Despite criminal activities, Rings maintain reputation for:
- **Internal care** - Members genuinely look out for each other
- **Reciprocal loyalty** - Those who remain loyal receive loyalty in return
- **Mutual protection** - Betrayal meets swift, violent response
- **Resource sharing** - Profits distributed to support membership

### Member Benefits
- **Income stability** - Reliable payment for criminal work
- **Community** - Sense of belonging and family
- **Protection** - Organization shields members from Coalition and rival threats
- **Status opportunity** - Path to advancement and increasing power

## Operational Security

### Mesh Knowledge
Olympus' tight security and dense mesh infrastructure provides advantage:
- **Security holes** - Know all weaknesses in city's security systems
- **Blind spots** - Exploit gaps in monitor coverage
- **Defensive capability** - Able to protect operations from Coalition interference
- **Criminal haven** - Operate openly within Olympus despite official security

### Coalition Infiltration
- **Centurion connections** - Have members within Coalition military forces
- **Security force access** - Connections to local law enforcement
- **Information advantage** - Early warning of raids and enforcement operations
- **Corruption network** - Exploit venality within security apparatus

## Role in Campaign

Rings of Olympus represent:
- **Criminal alternative** - Functioning social structure outside Coalition
- **Apostate lifeline** - Critical support for marginalized populations
- **Black market nexus** - Central hub for illegal economy
- **Moral ambiguity** - Provide essential services through criminal means
- **Political player** - Participate in Shadow War for various factions
