The **Talons of Freedom** are a remnant group from the old United States military, now operating as a significant insurgent force against the **Coalition**. Their primary objective is to destabilize Coalition control and, potentially, reclaim power.

### Summary

The **Talons of Freedom** represent a significant threat to the Coalition's stability. Their clandestine operations, strategic alliances, and ambitious plans pose a serious challenge to the established order. This insurgent group could lead to major conflicts and upheavals within the **Nova Praxis** setting.

---

### Operations and Tactics

- **Origins:**  
	Formed from the remnants of the **Alliance** and **Federation** after the Exodus, the Talons were responsible for devastating attacks against the nascent Coalition.
    
- **Modus Operandi:**  
    The Talons engage in various clandestine activities, including:
    
    - **Espionage and Infiltration:**  
        Planting agents within House operations on Luna.
    - **Raiding and Hijacking:**  
        Attacking House facilities and seizing valuable assets, such as the fabrication templates from **House Dalianis' lunar shipyard**.
    - **Black Market Operations:**  
        Running a vast network for trading illegal goods, specializing in weapons and armaments to bolster their forces.
- **Current Objective:**  
    Orchestrating a major strike against the Coalition, aiming to capture the **Icarus Hub** orbiting [[Proch]]. This ambitious operation includes plans to cripple Coalition defenses, seize resources, and potentially spark a wider rebellion.
    

---

### Key Figures

- **Legate Aaron Blackburn:**  
    The Talons' leader, secretly operating from within **House Silva** as a high-ranking legate. He masterminded the Talons' formation, orchestrating their operations while maintaining a cover within the Coalition.
    
- **Blackburn's Lieutenants:**  
    Three individuals reporting directly to Blackburn, responsible for executing his orders and managing specific aspects of the Talons' operations. Their names are not disclosed.
    
- **Rin Sakamoto:**  
    A rogue scientist with expertise in bioengineering, sought by **House Kimura**. The Talons recruited her and are leveraging her knowledge to potentially weaponize a powerful microbe.
    
- **Valare:**  
    A Savant smuggler who defected from **House Tsarya** and joined the Talons, assisting with their smuggling operations and preparations for the **Icarus Hub** attack.
    

---

### Relevant Locations

- **Vantage:**  
    A space station serving as a museum and Coalition base, the Talons maintain a strong presence here, utilizing it as a hub for their operations and recruitment.
    
- **Icarus Hub (Proch):**  
    The Talons' primary target, a crucial station orbiting **Proch**. The hub represents a strategic asset, a source of resources, and a potential flashpoint for rebellion.
    
- **Rho:**  
    A lunar city known for entertainment and illicit activities. The Talons operate within this bustling environment, leveraging its black market connections.
    

---

### Additional Notes

- Growing concerns within the Coalition highlight the increasing power and influence of the **Talons of Freedom**.
- The Talons' relationships with other factions, such as **House Turin** and the **Rings of Olympus**, underscore their strategic maneuvering within the complex underworld of the Coalition.
