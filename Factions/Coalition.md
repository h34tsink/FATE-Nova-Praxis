# Coalition

The **Coalition** is a governmental and corporate alliance in Nova Praxis, representing established power structures and bureaucratic oversight.

## Overview

The Coalition maintains order through regulated commerce, legal frameworks, and institutional authority. They are one of the primary factions competing for control in the Nova Praxis setting.
