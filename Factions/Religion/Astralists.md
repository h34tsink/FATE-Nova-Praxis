## Overview

Astralists represent one of four major religious movements in Coalition space. Their faith centers on belief that human souls or consciousness exists on a higher astral plane, transcending physical existence.

## Core Doctrines

### Soul & Astral Plane
- **Dual existence** - Consciousness exists simultaneously on physical and astral planes
- **Spiritual continuity** - Physical death does not mean soul death; consciousness continues on astral plane
- **Ascension path** - Life represents journey toward spiritual evolution and astral enlightenment
- **Connection possibilities** - Some practices enable temporary astral projection or plane-walking experiences

### Relationship to Apotheosis
Astralists view consciousness uploading and Apotheosis technology with nuanced perspective:
- **Technology as tool** - Mimir-based consciousness backup can be viewed as capturing astral essence
- **But not salvation** - Cannot achieve transcendence through technology alone
- **Spiritual requirement** - True enlightenment requires spiritual advancement, not just digital transfer
- **Compatibility questions** - Debate exists whether Apotheosized consciousness retains soul/astral connection

## Political Alignment

### House Alignment
- **House Kimura** - Significant Astralist population; many Kimuran Astralists serve as arbiters and peacemakers
- **Diverse representation** - Astralists exist across Coalition, though concentrated in certain Houses
- **Political moderation** - Astralist faith associated with non-violent conflict resolution

### Coalition Role
- **Peace arbitrators** - Many Astralists serve as neutral arbiters in contractual disputes and conflicts
- **Mediation services** - Astralist philosophers and priests valued for conflict de-escalation
- **Political integration** - Unlike some religions, Astralists actively participate in Coalition governance

## Notable Characteristics

### Practices & Spirituality
- **Meditation traditions** - Emphasize personal spiritual development
- **Ascetic disciplines** - Some adherents practice minimal material attachment
- **Communal gatherings** - Regular assemblies for spiritual communion and study
- **Prophecy and divination** - Some Astralist traditions include divination practices

### Compatibility with Coalition
Unlike Traditional Religions and some Neo-religions, Astralists:
- **Embrace certain technology** - See Mimir-tech as compatible with spiritual growth
- **Participate in politics** - Active political involvement, not separatist
- **Peaceful approach** - No tradition of violent opposition to Coalition
- **Cross-faction acceptance** - Respected even by Purist Houses

## Schisms & Variants

While unified under astral plane belief, Astralist faith includes various sects:
- **Orthodox Astralists** - Maintain traditional teachings about astral plane and soul
- **Mimir-compatible sects** - Embrace Apotheosis technology as spiritual tool
- **Ascetic orders** - Pursue enlightenment through material renunciation
- **Divination traditions** - Emphasize prophetic and mystical practices
