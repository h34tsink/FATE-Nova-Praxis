## Overview

**Primary Influence:** Chengdan, Olympus
**Primary Evolutionary Agenda:** Transhuman
**Market Discounts:** Agents, Computers & Software, Cybersleeves, Cybernetic Augmentations, Virtuality Software, Recon Warframes

House Kimura formed from merger of fierce pre-Coalition competitors: Japanese **Aria Softworks** and South Korean **Hiske Electronics**. While Aria's software superiority matched Hiske's hardware reliability, unification allowed both to redirect competitive resources toward dominating broader markets and expanding technological innovation.

## Current Operations

### Technology Dominance
- **Premier manufacturer** - Computers, computer software, agents, cybersleeves, cybernetic augmentations, civilian drones
- **Cost-efficient production** - Cheaper and efficient production without sacrificing quality
- **Market saturation** - Kimuran equipment built into large percentage of Coalition electronics
- **Customer support solution** - Standardized backdoors for technician remote access; extensive SIM employment for on-site assistance

### Warframe Market
- **Recon Warframes specialty** - Lighter, agile designs with superior sensor systems
- **Direct competition with Tsarya** - Tsarya excels at heavy assault warframes; neither maintains dominance
- **VirtuVac subsidiary** - Market leader in persistent and temporary virtual environments; aggressive response to Cipriani's recent market encroachment
- **Virtual sabotage** - Shadow War fights using Savants/CISTs destabilizing rival virtual environments (public denial despite ongoing operations)

## Intelligence Operations

### SIM Network
Kimura employs more SIMs than any other House—officially for software development/testing and customer support, unofficially maintaining massive spy network across Coalition meshes and Extranet, monitoring other Houses.

### Underworld Operations
- **Red Sun relationship** - Evolved from Yakuza; handles off-books work Kimura avoids openly doing
- **Crimson & Sons** - Shell subsidiary for Shadow War operations and seedy jobs
- **Preferred operatives** - Transhumans or Red Sun-affiliated crews (both ideal); treats Purists with suspicion as potential Cipriani spies

## Politics & Ideology

### Transhuman Support
Kimura is extremely liberal politically and does not support the Humanity Preservation Act. Despite preferring non-violent approaches (many embracing Astralist faith), the House has sufficient muscle for necessary conflicts.

## House Relationships

- **Alliance with Dalianis** - Computer system supply deal; Dalianis agrees civilian drone focus
- **Alliance with Silva** - Reciprocal arrangements: Kimura supplies electronics for Silva's shipbuilding; Silva provides discounted PAc weapons for Kimuran Recon Warframes (hidden from Tsarya)
- **Rivalry with Cipriani** - Virtual vacation market competition turning hostile; intense VirtuVac competition
