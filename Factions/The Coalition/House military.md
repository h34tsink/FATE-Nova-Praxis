# House Military Forces

Each Coalition House maintains its own military and security forces, operating independently but coordinating through the [[Factions/The Coalition/Protectorate\|Protectorate]] for joint operations. House militaries are the primary armed forces in Coalition space — the Protectorate provides coordination, not command.

## House Military Overview

| House | Primary Military Assets | Specialty |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani\|Cipriani]] | Energy infrastructure defense, facility security | Minimal military; contracts out to Tsarya for armor/weapons |
| [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis\|Dalianis]] | Starship fleets, combat drones, [[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Scholae Palatinae\|Scholae Palatinae]] | Naval supremacy, expansion enforcement |
| [[Factions/Houses/House Jinzhan\|Jinzhan]] | Ground forces, Earth quarantine fleet, kill-sats | Fortification, siege warfare, ordinance |
| [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura\|Kimura]] | Cyber warfare units, SIM operatives, drone networks | Information warfare, electronic dominance |
| [[Factions/Houses/House Silva\|Silva]] | Biotech combat units, medical corps, mercenary contracts | Biological augmentation, field medicine |
| [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya\|Tsarya]] | Heavy armor, warframes, weapons manufacturing | Armored assault, weapons supply to other Houses |

## Key Military Organizations

- **[[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Scholae Palatinae\|Scholae Palatinae]]:** Elite Dalianis units operating as Coalition shock troops. Used for rapid response, pirate interdiction, and frontier enforcement.
- **[[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Phalanx Formations\|Phalanx Formations]]:** Coalition-organized combined-House military units for large-scale operations.
- **Lighthouse:** Dalianis security subsidiary providing station/facility protection and tactical response.

## Rank Structure

House militaries use their own internal rank systems. The Coalition Protectorate uses standardized ranks for joint operations:

- **Legate:** Senior field commander (equivalent to Colonel/Brigadier)
- **Centurion:** Mid-level officer (equivalent to Captain/Major)
- **Auxiliary:** Contracted personnel (the PCs' typical designation)
