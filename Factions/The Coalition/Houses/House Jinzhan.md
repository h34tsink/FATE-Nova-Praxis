# Jinzhan (GIN-ZAH-ON)
**Traits:** Strongly Purist, Independent and Isolationist, Seeks the destruction of [[Earth]]

**Discounts:** Cost of purchasing any of the following types of goods is reduced by 1:
- Construction
- Security Technology & Services
- Food
- Explosives
- Ship & Vehicle Weaponry 

**Skill Bonus:** In addition, choose Investigation or Survival. You gain a +1 bonus on rolls with your chosen Skill.
