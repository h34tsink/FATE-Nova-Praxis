# The Protectorate

The Coalition's military coordination and enforcement structure. The Protectorate doesn't command House militaries directly — it coordinates joint operations, sets defense policy, and deploys combined-House units when threats exceed any single House's capacity.

## Structure

The Protectorate is governed by the **Tetragon**, a military council composed of senior officers from all six Houses. The Tetragon sets strategic priorities and authorizes major operations. Day-to-day military coordination falls to the Protectorate's standing staff.

### Key Components

- **Tetragon Command:** Strategic authority. [[Admiral Drivas]] sits on the command staff.
- **[[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Scholae Palatinae\|Scholae Palatinae]]:** Elite combined-House units for rapid deployment, piracy interdiction, frontier pacification.
- **[[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Phalanx Formations\|Phalanx Formations]]:** Larger combined-arms formations for sustained campaigns.
- **Earth Quarantine Fleet:** Jointly operated by [[Factions/Houses/House Jinzhan\|Jinzhan]] and [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis\|Dalianis]]; maintains the kill-sat network and blockade around Earth.

## Political Dynamics

The Protectorate is perpetually caught between House interests. Each House wants the Protectorate strong enough to defend its assets but not strong enough to threaten its autonomy. This tension means:

- **Underfunding** of frontier defense (each House expects others to pay)
- **Intelligence silos** (Houses withhold intel from each other)
- **Jurisdictional disputes** when operations cross House territory boundaries
- **Loyalty conflicts** for officers who serve both their House and the Protectorate

## Campaign Relevance

The Protectorate is the coalition military machine. PCs have encountered it through Scholae Palatinae (Session 5) and will face it again when the Icarus Hub uprising triggers a military response. [[Admiral Drivas]] represents the Tetragon's authority — and its limitations.
