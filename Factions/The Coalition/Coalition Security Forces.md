# Coalition Security Forces

The standing internal security and rapid-response units for Coalition territories. Distinct from House military forces — security forces handle law enforcement, station security, and civil order rather than military operations.

## Structure

- **Coalition Vigilance Service (CVS):** The primary civilian law enforcement body. Operates across all Coalition territories. Enforces CID regulations, HPA compliance, and general civil law.
- **Station Security:** Local security forces on individual stations and habitats. Often House-affiliated (e.g., Lighthouse provides security on Dalianis stations).
- **Protectorate Rapid Response:** Military-grade units deployed for situations beyond civilian law enforcement — terrorism, insurrection, piracy interdiction.

## Key Functions

- **CID verification** and identity enforcement
- **[[Factions/Ideologies/Humanity Preservation Act\|HPA]] compliance** inspections and violation prosecution
- **Apostate monitoring** at Coalition borders and registered stations
- **Smuggling interdiction** and black market disruption
- **Counter-terrorism** against [[Factions/Ideologies/Purifiers\|Purifier]] extremists and [[Factions/Hidden Agendas/Talons of Freedom\|Talon]] cells

## Campaign Relevance

Security forces are the PCs' most common antagonist in daily operations — checkpoint sweeps, CID verification, cargo inspections. The crew's "officially dead" status since Session 6 makes every security encounter a potential disaster.
