## Captain Sam "Starhawk"  Guinness
- **Division:** Centurions (Security Patrol)
- **Appearance:** 
    - Tall, rugged man with close-cropped salt-and-pepper hair and piercing blue eyes.
- **[[Characters/Players/Grace Pryzbylski/Background]]:** 
    - Captain Guinness has a storied history in the Centurions, having been a key figure in various high-profile operations across Coalition space. Known for his no-nonsense approach and unwavering dedication to duty, he’s respected by both his peers and subordinates.
- **Character Trait:** 
    - Commanding presence, tactical thinker.
- **Notable Equipment:** 
    - Lethal weapon coded to his CID, Communication drone for quick relays.
 - **Role:**
    - Captain of the frigate "Nightshade"; experienced in both aerial dogfights and strategic ship-to-ship warfare.

## Legate Jorin "Ecliptor" Xan
- **Division:** 1st Legion - SACom (SAY-COM)
- **Appearance:** 
    - Athletic build, bald with various cybernetic enhancements visible around his temples.
- **Background:** 
    - An expert in space and aerial combat, Jorin has overseen multiple fleet deployments and has a reputation for pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in aerial strategy.
- **Character Trait:** 
    - Visionary, calculated risk-taker.
- **Notable Equipment:** 
    - Personal Holo-projector for real-time battle simulations.
- **Role:**
    - One of the leading strategists of the 1st Legion; oversees large-scale operations and ensures SACom's dominance in the void of space.

## 1st Legion Marines: SACom (SAY-COM)

### Lieutenant Eleanor "Starfire" Marquez

- **Description**: Tall, athletic build, with a distinctive scar across her left cheek.
- **Status**: Biosleeved with enhanced vision augmentations.
- **Psychological Profile**: Determined and methodical; trusts her team implicitly.
- **Role**: Adept in coordinating aerial strikes and leading her team in boarding actions.

### Sergeant Malik "Nebula" Jensen

- **Description**: Medium height, muscular, bald with a series of constellation tattoos on his scalp.
- **Status**: Pure, believes in the power of the human body and mind.
- **Psychological Profile**: Calm under pressure, a rock for his team but has a fierce protective streak.
- **Role**: Specializes in heavy weapons and bombing runs.

### Corporal Aria "Comet" Nguyen

- **Description**: Petite, agile, with bright silver eyes.
- **Status**: Cybersleeved with enhanced reflex augmentations.
- **Psychological Profile**: Quick thinker, sometimes acts before assessing the entire situation.
- **Role**: Reconnaissance and sniper, often sent ahead for scouting.

### Specialist Theo "Meteor" Kowalski

- **Description**: Broad-shouldered, gruff appearance, beard streaked with gray.
- **Status**: Pure, often voices skepticism about heavy reliance on tech.
- **Psychological Profile**: Veteran of many campaigns, wary of authority but deeply loyal to comrades.
- **Role**: Demolitions and breach expert.

### Private First Class Lina "Pulsar" Espinoza

- **Description**: Lanky with an ever-present grin and a shock of curly red hair.
- **Status**: Biosleeved with minor enhancements for stamina.
- **Psychological Profile**: The joker of the group, maintains morale but can be impulsive.
- **Role**: Communications and tech support, responsible for maintaining the squad's gear during missions.
