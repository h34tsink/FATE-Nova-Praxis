## Ideological Factions

Major philosophical movements within Coalition space:

- [[Ideologies/Apostates|Apostates]] (~20% of humanity) - Live outside Coalition system without CIDs

- [[Ideologies/Transhumans|Transhumans]] - Advocate augmentation and enhancement, oppose HPA
