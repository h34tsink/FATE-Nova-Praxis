# Humanity Preservation Act

The most politically charged law in Coalition space. The HPA defines the legal boundaries of human augmentation — what you can do to your body, your mind, and your ego without becoming a criminal.

## Core Provisions

- **Augmentation limits:** Maximum number of augmentations per body/sleeve, enforced by built-in compliance systems in most cyberware.
- **Fork prohibition:** Creating unauthorized copies of an ego is illegal. Each person gets one CID, one ego, one existence. Violations are punishable by erasure of all unauthorized copies.
- **Synthesis restrictions:** Full bio-synthetic merger (Synthesis) is heavily regulated and requires special dispensation.
- **Sleeve registration:** All sleeves must be registered with a CID. Unregistered sleeves are contraband.
- **Mnemonic core controls:** Backup and restoration of egos must go through licensed facilities with full Coalition records.

## Enforcement

- **CVS inspections:** Random and targeted augmentation compliance checks at stations, checkpoints, and border crossings.
- **Penalties:** Minor violations result in fines and mandatory reversal. Major violations (unauthorized forks, unregistered Synthesis) result in erasure — permanent deletion of the offending ego.
- **[[Factions/Ideologies/Purifiers\|Purifiers]]:** Extremist vigilantes who enforce the HPA through violence when they believe official enforcement is too lenient.

## Campaign Relevance

The HPA is central to the [[The Chimera Conspiracy - Arc Tracker\|Chimera arc]]. Chimera's existence — a gestalt of five merged egos — is the most extreme HPA violation possible. Anyone who discovers him is legally obligated to report it. Capturing his mnemonic core is proof of [[House Kimura]]'s illegal research program, which is itself a massive HPA violation.

The PCs' own augmentations, resleeving, and fork encounters put them in constant contact with HPA enforcement.
