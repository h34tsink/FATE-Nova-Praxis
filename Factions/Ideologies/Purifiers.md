## Overview

Purifiers represent the violent extremist fringe of Purist ideology—organized activists and terrorists who use violence, sabotage, and murder to oppose augmentation, Apotheosis, and transhumanism.

## Ideology & Motivation

### Core Beliefs
- **Augmentation as corruption** - View technological enhancement as fundamental violation of human nature
- **Apotheosis as damnation** - Consciousness uploading represents loss of soul
- **Transhumanism as blasphemy** - Active opposition to human evolution through technology
- **Violent necessity** - Justified in using force to prevent "corruption" of humanity

### Distinction from Mainstream Purists
While mainstream Purists oppose augmentation ideologically and politically, Purifiers embrace violence:
- **Extremist position** - Represent fringe, not mainstream Purist thought
- **Mainstream rejection** - Most Purists explicitly disavow Purifier violence
- **Isolated operations** - Organized in cells rather than unified movement
- **Coalition response** - Pursued as terrorists/extremists rather than political movement

## Operational Profile

### Target Categories
- **Augmentation facilities** - Resleeve centers, enhancement clinics, cybernetic shops
- **Transhumanist personnel** - Particularly heavy-augmented or posthuman individuals
- **Apotheosis research** - Targeting consciousness uploading and Apotheosis programs
- **Infrastructure** - Compiler networks and technological systems supporting augmentation

### Tactics
- **Bombing campaigns** - Destroying augmentation facilities with explosives
- **Assassination** - Targeting prominent transhumanists and posthumans
- **Sabotage** - Disrupting augmentation and enhancement operations
- **Intimidation** - Using violence to discourage augmentation choices

### Known Incidents
- **Facility destruction** - Multiple documented resleeve and augmentation center attacks
- **Personnel attacks** - Assassination campaigns against prominent transhumanists
- **Infrastructure damage** - Sabotage of compiler networks and technological systems

## Coalition Response

### Protectorate Operations
- **Extremist classification** - Treated as terrorists rather than political movement
- **Active pursuit** - Protectorate and Centurions conduct elimination operations
- **Legal prosecution** - Captured Purifiers face maximum Coalition penalties
- **Intelligence gathering** - Extensive surveillance and infiltration efforts

### Challenges
- **Cell structure** - Distributed organization prevents centralized elimination
- **Public sympathy** - Some Purists sympathize with anti-augmentation goals despite rejecting violence
- **Motivation supply** - Ideological commitment means no shortage of recruits
- **Escalation pattern** - Violence continues despite lack of strategic success

## Notable Purifier Actions

While specific documented incidents in Session 9 context may be limited, Purifiers:
- **Consistently attack resleeve facilities** - Targeting consciousness backup and resurrection technology
- **Target enhanced military personnel** - Attacking Coalition soldiers with extensive augmentation
- **Campaign against Savants** - Opposition to artificial consciousness and digital beings
- **Disrupt transhuman communities** - Attacking apostate settlements with transhuman populations
