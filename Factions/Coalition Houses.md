# Coalition Houses

A comprehensive reference for all Coalition Houses in Nova Praxis.

---

## 🏛️ House Cipriani

> [!house]- Overview
> **🌍 Primary Influence:** Mercury  
> **🌐 Secondary Influence:** Cygnus Alpha  
> **🧬 Evolutionary Agenda:** Purist
> 
> House Cipriani was a **founding force** behind the Coalition, leveraging its immense assets to gain a **near-monopoly on energy production** from Mercury. Known for **ruthless business tactics** and strong **Purist ideology**.

> [!info]- Market Discounts
> - Atmospheric Flying Vehicles
> - Energy (Broadcast Power Generators, etc.)
> - CISTs
> - Transportation

> [!note]- Key Information
> **Alliances:** House Tsarya (tight), House Silva (partners in crime)
> 
> **Rivalries:** House Dalianis (long-standing feud), House Kimura (virtual vacation competition)
> 
> **Hiring Preferences:** Often recruits **Purist apostates** for off-the-books operations due to shared bio-conservative ideals.

## 🏛️ House Dalianis

> [!house]- Overview
> **🌍 Primary Influence:** Olympus  
> **🌐 Secondary Influence:** Luna  
> **🧬 Evolutionary Agenda:** Purist
> 
> **Foremost manufacturer of starships** operating the galaxy's largest shipyard, Ansari Station. Aggressively **expansionist**, focusing on jump drive technology. Pragmatic leadership prioritizes results over ideology.

> [!info]- Market Discounts
> - Starships
> - Rail/Coil Guns
> - Combat Drones

> [!note]- Key Information
> **Alliances:** House Kimura (computer systems trade), House Jinzhan (Earth quarantine cooperation)
> 
> **Rivalries:** House Cipriani (Cygnus Alpha resources), House Tsarya (Ceres ice fields)
> 
> **Hiring Preferences:** Results-oriented, open to working with posthumans if effective.

## 🏛️ House Jinzhan

> [!house]- Overview
> **🌍 Primary Influence:** Shouye  
> **🌐 Secondary Influence:** Earth Quarantine  
> **🧬 Evolutionary Agenda:** Purist
> 
> Construction and security specialists enforcing **Earth quarantine**. Deeply **Purist, isolationist, and technophobic** society. Researching **planet-destroying weapons** to eradicate technophage.

> [!info]- Market Discounts
> - Construction
> - Security Technology & Services
> - Food
> - Explosives
> - Ship & Vehicle Weaponry

> [!note]- Key Information
> **Alliances:** House Dalianis (Earth quarantine, Mars development)
> 
> **Rivalries:** Rarely deals with apostates
> 
> **Hiring Preferences:** **Experienced mercenaries**, often screening out crews with apostate members.

## 🏛️ House Kimura

> [!house]- Overview
> **🌍 Primary Influence:** Chengdan  
> **🌐 Secondary Influence:** Olympus  
> **🧬 Evolutionary Agenda:** Transhuman
> 
> **Leading tech manufacturer** dominating computer hardware/software, cybersleeves, and cyber-augmentations. Employs **massive spy network of SIMs**. Politically **liberal**, actively opposes Humanity Preservation Act.

> [!info]- Market Discounts
> - Agents
> - Computers & Computer Software
> - Cybersleeves
> - Cybernetic Augmentations
> - Virtuality Software
> - Recon Warframes

> [!note]- Key Information
> **Alliances:** House Dalianis (computer/drone trade), House Silva (electronics)
> 
> **Rivalries:** House Cipriani (virtual vacation market)
> 
> **Hiring Preferences:** Works through subsidiary Crimson & Sons (Red Sun ties), prefers **transhuman or Red Sun-affiliated operatives**.

## 🏛️ House Silva

> [!house]- Overview
> **🌍 Primary Influence:** Luna  
> **🌐 Secondary Influence:** Phobos  
> **🧬 Evolutionary Agenda:** Transhuman
> 
> Biotech experts openly disregarding **Humanity Preservation Act**. Conducts genetic experimentation on indentured servants. Operates strictly as corporation with "reset" clauses in contracts.

> [!info]- Market Discounts
> - Biosleeves
> - Biotech Augmentations
> - PAc Weaponry

> [!note]- Key Information
> **Alliances:** House Tsarya (smuggling contacts), House Cipriani (business ventures), House Kimura (electronics)
> 
> **Rivalries:** House Dalianis (PAc weapons rivalry)
> 
> **Hiring Preferences:** Mercenary crews with reputation for **quick, efficient, discreet missions**. Operates Thetis Revival Labs (best resleeving facility).

## 🏛️ House Tsarya

> [!house]- Overview
> **🌍 Primary Influence:** Proch  
> **🌐 Secondary Influence:** Luna  
> **🧬 Evolutionary Agenda:** Purist
> 
> **War profiteers** manufacturing armor and assault warframes. Actively **instigate conflicts** to boost demand. Pragmatically hiring **Apotheosized** despite Purist traditions, causing internal friction.

> [!info]- Market Discounts
> - Vehicular & Personal Armor
> - Ground Vehicles
> - Assault Warframes

> [!note]- Key Information
> **Alliances:** House Cipriani (trade agreements), House Silva (less legitimate relationship)
> 
> **Rivalries:** House Kimura (warframe market), House Dalianis (Ceres ice fields)
> 
> **Hiring Preferences:** Recently shifted to hiring **Apotheosized for dangerous, expendable roles**.

## 🕶️ Apostate

> [!house]- Overview
> **🌍 Primary Influence:** Independent/Non-Coalition  
> **🌐 Secondary Influence:** Underground Networks & Black Markets  
> **🧬 Evolutionary Agenda:** Live outside Coalition control, forge own path
> 
> You are a ghost. Little record of your life exists. Living as independent operator with access to underground networks and black markets.

> [!info]- Market Discounts
> - Black Market Goods
> - Underground Services
> - Untraceable Equipment
> - Apostate Networks
> - Independent Technology

> [!note]- Special Rules
> **⭐ Free Stunt:** Non-Citizen
> 
> **📊 No Rep Rating:** Apostates do not have a Rep Rating
> 
> **💪 Apostate Favor Bonus:** +2 Assets tests for Favor requests from fellow apostates
> 
> **Role:** Serve as intermediaries between hostile Houses, conduct sensitive operations, provide services where House presence would be problematic.
