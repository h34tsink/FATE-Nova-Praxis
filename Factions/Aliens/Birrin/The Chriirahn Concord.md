# The Chriirahn Concord

> *"We are not gatekeepers. We are those who survived the gateless flood. There is a difference."*  
> — Concord Diplomatic Brief, 2726 CE

## Faction Overview

**Full Name:** The Chriirahn Concord (commonly "The Concord")  
**Type:** Multi-house governmental coalition representing [[Factions/Aliens/Birrin/Birrin Species - Biology and Culture|Birrin]] species  
**Primary Function:** Offworld diplomatic and containment authority  
**Scale:** Regional power (quiet routes, relay platforms, deep-archive assets)  
**First Contact with Humanity:** 2725 CE  
**Current Relationship:** Cautious cooperation with active containment oversight

Use this when treating the Concord as a faction-level "character" in conflicts.

**High Concept:** *The Galaxy's Most Polite Containment Protocol*

**Trouble:** *Humanity Is an Uncontrolled Anomaly With Legs*

**Other Aspects:**
- *Resonance Doctrine: Measure Twice, Touch Never*
- *Silent Routes and Dark-Running Logistics*
- *Post-Fall Memory: We Don't Do Collapse Again*

### Skills (FATE Core Version)

**Great (+4):** Resources, Lore  
**Good (+3):** Notice, Will  
**Fair (+2):** Contacts, Rapport, Investigate  
**Average (+1):** Fight, Athletics, Deceive, Provoke

### Approaches (FATE Accelerated Version)

**Careful +3, Clever +3, Forceful +2, Sneaky +2, Quick +1, Flashy +1**

### Stress & Consequences (Faction-Level)

**Stress Tracks:**
- **Influence:** 3 boxes (1/2/3) — political capital, diplomatic standing
- **Operations:** 3 boxes (1/2/3) — material assets, personnel, infrastructure

**Consequences:**
- **Mild Political:** (2) *Diplomatic Incident* / *Strained Relations*
- **Moderate Material:** (4) *Fleet Element Damaged* / *Quiet Pier Compromised*
- **Severe Spiritual:** (6) *Containment Doctrine Questioned* / *Post-Fall Trauma Triggered*

(Yes, "spiritual" consequences are real for the Concord — their culture treats doctrinal/philosophical damage as seriously as physical harm.)

### Faction Stunts

**Harmonic Quarantine**  
Once per session, the Concord may declare a scene aspect **Quarantine Field** with two free invokes (represents deploying resonance-cage technology and containment teams).

**Silent Interdiction**  
+2 to overcome when blocking transmissions, masking routes, or forcing "quiet" logistics (probe networks and dark-running expertise).

**Containment Authority**  
+2 to Rapport or Provoke when issuing safety directives in a crisis, especially around relics or anomalies (backed by species-wide trauma and practical experience).

**Archive Recall**  
Once per session, ask the GM one question about an ancient site, phenomenon, or historical precedent; get a true, useful answer (it may be incomplete). Represents deep institutional memory.

## Organizational Structure

### Governance

**Coalition Model:**
- Multi-house parliamentary system
- No single house holds majority
- Consensus-based decision making
- Emergency powers vested in Quarantine Marshal council
- Rotating chairship (5-year terms)

**Houses:**
- Major houses: 12 (each represents historical power centers)
- Minor houses: 47 registered
- All houses have voting representation
- Power balanced to prevent pre-Fall concentration

**Checks & Balances:**
- Archivist Council (historical memory keepers)
- Warden Authority (operational independence)
- Navigator Guild (economic power base)
- Brood Council (cultural/social advocacy)

### Offworld Operations

**Diplomatic Corps:**
- First contact specialists
- Treaty negotiators
- Human liaison officers
- Cultural translators
- Embassy staff at major Coalition stations

**Containment Division:**
- Relic wardens
- Quarantine marshals
- Resonance technicians
- Crisis response teams
- Archive researchers

**Navigation Network:**
- Silent route mappers
- Relay station operators
- Dark-running logistics
- Independent contractor liaisons
- Probe recovery teams

## Assets & Resources

### Relay Stations ("Quiet Piers")

**Function:**
- Minimal-signature observation posts
- Secure communication relays
- Emergency supply caches
- Quarantine staging points
- Dark-running waypoints

**Locations:**
- Cold orbits in unexplored systems
- Lagrange points in monitored space
- Deep-space positioning between gates
- Hidden in asteroid fields
- Coordinates classified (need-to-know)

**Characteristics:**
- Passive sensors only (no active scanning)
- Resonance-based communication (harder for humans to detect)
- Self-sufficient for years
- Minimal crew (2-6 Birrin)
- Can deploy containment teams rapidly

**Plot Use:**
- First contact location
- Safe haven / neutral ground
- Source of silent route data
- Quarantine enforcement point
- Observation post revealing PC actions were watched

---

### Silent Route Network

**Definition:**
- Navigation paths with minimal sensor footprint
- Avoid major traffic lanes and monitoring stations
- Use natural sensor-shadow (asteroid fields, stellar phenomena)
- Mapped over centuries of probe exploration
- Valuable trade secret

**Access:**
- Concord closely guards complete maps
- Partial routes sold/traded selectively
- Probe-lineage families maintain private knowledge
- Some routes overlap with human-discovered paths
- New routes constantly mapped

**Value:**
- Smuggling / covert operations
- Avoid hostile monitoring
- Faster travel (avoiding checkpoints)
- Strategic military advantage
- Intelligence gathering

**Plot Use:**
- MacGuffin (route data PCs need)
- Smuggling mission requirement
- Escape route from pursuit
- Discovering Concord has been watching all along
- Competitive mapping with human explorers

---

### Resonance Technology

**Harmonic Instruments:**
- Structural analysis tools
- Communication devices (vibration-based)
- Anomaly detectors
- Containment field generators
- "Listening" technology (non-invasive sensing)

**Containment Cages:**
- Portable relic quarantine rigs
- Generate stabilizing harmonic fields
- Reduce Pneuma manifestation strength
- Prevent cascade effects
- Require trained operator

**Applications:**
- [[Mechanics/FATE/Pneuma Rules/_Pneuma Rules|Pneuma]] containment
- Archaeological safety
- Structural engineering
- Non-invasive medical diagnostics
- Communication across language barriers

**Human Perspective:**
- Looks like mix of science and ritual
- Effectiveness undeniable but mechanism unclear
- Some human scientists trying to reverse-engineer
- Concord reluctant to share underlying theory
- Trade in instruments possible but expensive

## Strategic Goals

### Primary Objectives

**1. Prevent Second Fall**
- Monitor human expansion for collapse indicators
- Intervene in resource-crisis scenarios
- Share post-Fall lessons (when listened to)
- Maintain species survival protocols
- Never again risk extinction-level events

**2. Contain Anomaly Threats**
- Quarantine relic sites
- Monitor [[Mechanics/FATE/Pneuma Rules/_Pneuma Rules|Pneuma]] manifestations
- Track "wildfire" incidents (humans accessing what they shouldn't)
- Maintain Echo site surveillance
- Prevent cascade effects

**3. Establish Safe Coexistence**
- Build working relationship with Coalition humanity
- Create containment cooperation frameworks
- Trade knowledge and technology carefully
- Develop mutual defense protocols
- Preserve Birrin cultural/political independence

**4. Map the Unknown**
- Continue probe exploration programs
- Expand silent route network
- Discover alien sites before humans do
- Understand Architect/Mimir connection
- Locate and secure potential hazards

### Current Priorities (2726+ CE)

**Immediate:**
- Establish formal treaty with Coalition (negotiation phase)
- Contain spreading Pneuma awareness among humans
- Investigate Mimir's intentional human enhancement
- Respond to PC crew's actions (variable)

**Short-term (5-10 years):**
- Build joint containment infrastructure
- Train human warden equivalents (if they'll listen)
- Expand trade beyond basic goods
- Map human gate network thoroughly
- Assess long-term human stability

**Long-term (50+ years):**
- Prevent human civilization collapse
- Achieve stable interspecies cooperation
- Solve Pneuma containment problem definitively
- Understand Architect disappearance
- Ensure both species survive whatever comes next

## Clocks & Tracking

Use these to track Concord faction state in your campaign.

### Wildfire Index (0–6)

**What It Tracks:** Human recklessness around anomalies/Pneuma  
**Ticks When:**
- PCs or other humans interact with relics unsafely
- Pneuma incidents without containment
- Gate network instabilities
- Corporate experimentation with dangerous tech
- Ignoring Concord warnings

**Effects:**
- 0-2: Observation only
- 3-4: Diplomatic pressure, warnings issued
- 5: Active interdiction (blocking access, silent sabotage)
- 6: Full containment enforcement (military action authorized)

### Concord Escalation (0–6)

**What It Tracks:** Concord willingness to enforce containment by force  
**Ticks When:**
- Wildfire Index reaches thresholds
- Humans refuse cooperation
- Containment breaches cause casualties
- Political pressure from Birrin houses
- Echo sites show increased activity

**Effects:**
- 0-2: Diplomatic engagement
- 3-4: Economic pressure, route access restricted
- 5: Limited military deployment
- 6: Full quarantine enforcement ("for your own safety")

### Human-Birrin Trust (0–8)

**What It Tracks:** Positive relationship development  
**Ticks When:**
- PCs follow containment protocols
- Successful joint operations
- Humans demonstrate long-term thinking
- Cultural exchange deepens
- Shared crisis overcome together

**Effects:**
- 0-2: Cold professional relationship
- 3-4: Limited cooperation, mutual respect growing
- 5-6: Genuine alliance forming
- 7-8: Deep trust, full information sharing, joint doctrine development

### Mimir Breadcrumb Trail (0–8)

**What It Tracks:** Discovery of Architect/Mimir connection  
**Ticks When:**
- Echo sites investigated
- Pneuma manifestations analyzed
- Ancient archives decoded
- Birrin share historical data
- PCs piece together pattern

**Effects:**
- 0-2: Fragments only, no pattern yet
- 3-4: Pattern emerging, questions multiplying
- 5-6: Clear Mimir manipulation visible
- 7-8: Full scope revealed (human-Pneuma connection intentional)

## Relations with Human Factions

### Coalition Houses

**House Silva (Military):**
- **Status:** Wary cooperation
- **Contact:** Joint containment operations
- **Tension:** Silva aggression vs. Concord caution
- **Potential:** Best human partners for security operations

**House Kimura (Science/Medicine):**
- **Status:** Active collaboration (with friction)
- **Contact:** Xenobiology research, medical technology trade
- **Tension:** Kimura curiosity vs. Concord secrets
- **Potential:** Could unlock hybrid technologies (or cause disaster)

**House Dalianis (Economics/Trade):**
- **Status:** Profitable but careful
- **Contact:** Route data exchange, goods trade
- **Tension:** Dalianis profit-focus vs. safety protocols
- **Potential:** Economic interdependence creates mutual survival incentive

**House Håvik (Media/Culture):**
- **Status:** Complicated
- **Contact:** Cultural exchange, entertainment industry
- **Tension:** Some Birrin see this as degrading; others profit
- **Potential:** Humanizes both species to each other (or creates stereotypes)

### Other Groups

**Savants / Apostates:**
- **Status:** Maximum threat level
- **Reason:** Represent exactly the reckless Pneuma experimentation Concord fears
- **Response:** Quarantine on sight if possible
- **Complication:** Some are sympathetic/understandable

**SIM Advocates:**
- **Status:** Philosophical opposition
- **Reason:** Birrin value physical reality; SIM escapism seen as pre-Fall warning sign
- **Response:** Disapproval but non-intervention
- **Common Ground:** Both want to prevent collapse

**Promethic Collective:**
- **Status:** Ideological ally (with reservations)
- **Reason:** Both oppose unchecked corporate exploitation
- **Tension:** Different methods and goals
- **Potential:** Natural alliance if Collective proves stable

## Plot Hooks & Story Seeds

### First Contact Scenarios

**The Quiet Pier** (Full mini-arc in original file)  
PCs discover derelict platform that responds with "structured resonance" — first contact with Concord observation post.

**The Wrong Question**  
PCs ask about navigation shortcut; Birrin navigator asks what they're carrying; mutual suspicion escalates.

**Resonance Hail Misfire**  
Concord tries to warn PCs about anomaly; human systems interpret as weapon lock; shootout narrowly avoided.

### Cooperation Scenarios

**Joint Containment**  
Relic site threatens both species; must combine human improvisation with Birrin protocols to survive.

**Cultural Exchange**  
Embassy posting: PC crew hosts Birrin envoy (or vice versa); comedy and drama from misunderstandings.

**Silent Route Trade**  
Navigator offers route data for favor; favor turns out more complicated than expected.

**Archive Expedition**  
Birrin archivist needs human help exploring site too dangerous for Concord alone; truth uncovered challenges both species.

### Conflict Scenarios

**Wildfire Interdiction**  
Concord decides PC crew is too dangerous; silent sabotage or open confrontation forces choice.

**Quarantine Enforcement**  
Warden must seal breach; PCs inside; both sides think they're right; tragic necessity vs. rescue imperative.

**The Spear Returns**  
Ancient probe's orbit brings it back; both species race to claim it; historical significance vs. technological salvage.

**House Revival**  
Pre-Fall house conflict resurfaces; PCs caught between Birrin factions; Concord unity threatened.

### Revelation Scenarios

**The Fall Was Not Natural**  
Evidence suggests pre-Fall Birrin encountered Pneuma; may have contributed to collapse; Concord has been hiding this.

**Human-Pneuma Connection Intentional**  
Mimir specifically enhanced human Pneuma access; Birrin discover this; implications terrifying.

**Third Species Found**  
Another alien civilization discovered; Concord methodology vs. human approach tested; competition or cooperation?

**Birrin Rogue Pneumatics**  
Some Birrin learning human-style Pneuma; Concord civil conflict erupts; PCs must choose sides.

## GM Guidance

### Portraying the Concord

**Tone:**
- Polite but firm
- Experienced but traumatized
- Competent but cautious
- Alien but comprehensible

**Avoid:**
- Obstructive for its own sake
- Incomprehensibly alien
- Purely antagonistic
- Comic relief bumbling

**Goals:**
- Make players understand Concord perspective
- Create genuine dilemmas (both sides have points)
- Reward cultural learning and protocol respect
- Punish recklessness (but fairly)

### Balancing Relationships

**Too Friendly:**
- Loses tension and distinct perspective
- Becomes human allies with extra limbs
- Pneuma reveal loses impact
- Safety protocols become meaningless

**Too Hostile:**
- Becomes generic antagonist faction
- Loses interesting cultural depth
- Players can't build trust
- Cooperation impossible (reduces options)

**Just Right:**
- Cooperation possible but requires work
- Trust earned slowly through actions
- Cultural differences create friction AND solutions
- Can be allies, antagonists, or both depending on context

### Revelation Pacing

**Early Campaign:**
- Concord is competent alien faction
- Resonance technology mysterious but functional
- Safety protocols seem excessive
- Post-Fall trauma hinted at

**Mid Campaign:**
- Post-Fall details revealed (scale of tragedy)
- Resonance = Pneuma containment methodology
- Concord fear of human recklessness explained
- Trust or distrust crystallizes based on PC actions

**Late Campaign:**
- Full Pneuma truth shared (or discovered)
- Mimir's intentionality revealed
- Joint approach to threats essential
- Both species face common challenges
