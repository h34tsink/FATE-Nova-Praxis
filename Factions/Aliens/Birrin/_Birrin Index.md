## What Are the Birrin?

**Species Type:** Hexapodal (six-limbed) sapient aliens  
**Origin:** Planet Chriirah, hothouse world with superheated equatorial band  
**First Contact:** 2725 CE (Nova Praxis timeline)  
**Relationship Status:** Cautious cooperation with containment oversight  
**Key Trait:** Post-apocalyptic culture obsessed with preventing another collapse

### Three-Sentence Summary

The **Birrin** are humanity's first confirmed alien contact: six-limbed egg-layers from a tropical world who survived near-extinction when their First Technological Age collapsed. Their culture is built around **containment doctrine** — safety protocols driven by species trauma — and they react with dread to humanity's reckless curiosity around ancient artifacts and [[Mechanics/FATE/Pneuma Rules/_Pneuma Rules|Pneuma]] phenomena. They offer cooperation, but their diplomatic envoys carry quarantine orders in their pockets, just in case.

## Biology & Culture Overview

### Physical Characteristics

- **Height:** ~2 meters (6 feet)
- **Mass:** 80-120 kg
- **Limbs:** Six (three pairs) + two dorsal radiators
- **Eyes:** Independently mobile stalks
- **Mouth:** Quad-opening beak
- **Lifespan:** 80-100 years natural; extended via medical tech
- **Reproduction:** Egg-laying; clutches of 3-4

### Homeworld: Chriirah

- **Type:** Hothouse terrestrial (Venus-sized)
- **Gravity:** 0.92 G
- **Oceans:** 78% coverage
- **Climate:** Hot; extensive tropical swamps
- **Notable:** "The Kiln" — superheated equatorial band acting as cultural/geographical divide

### The Fall (1200-1000 years ago)

**What Happened:**
- Pre-Fall civilization achieved spaceflight, fusion power, interstellar probe construction
- Exponential growth overwhelmed planetary resources
- Crop failures, flooding, regional wars
- Destroyed probe recovery mission sparked total war
- Billions died; civilization collapsed
- Dark age lasted over 1000 years

**Legacy:**
- Post-collapse trauma encoded in culture
- "We don't do collapse again" core philosophy
- Containment doctrine developed
- Multi-house cooperation enforced
- Cautious, measured approach to all expansion

### Current Technological Level

- **Spaceflight:** Second technological age (200+ years)
- **FTL:** No native FTL; uses Coalition gates with permission
- **Unique Tech:** Resonance/harmonic systems for communication and containment
- **Exploration:** "Silent routes" — minimal-signature navigation paths
- **Infrastructure:** Relay stations ("Quiet Piers") throughout explored space

## The Chriirahn Concord

### What Is It?

The **Concord** is the multi-house governmental coalition representing all Birrin in offworld operations. It's their diplomatic, containment, and exploration authority combined.

**Core Philosophy:** *Measure twice, touch never* — containment over curiosity, protocol over improvisation, long-term survival over short-term gain.

### Key Organizations

**Diplomatic Corps** — First contact, treaty negotiation, human liaison  
**Containment Division** — Relic wardens, quarantine marshals, crisis response  
**Navigation Network** — Silent route mappers, relay operators, dark-running logistics  
**Archivist Council** — Historical memory, knowledge preservation, pattern recognition

### Strategic Assets

**Quiet Piers** — Minimal-signature relay stations in hidden locations  
**Silent Routes** — Mapped navigation paths avoiding major traffic and sensors  
**Resonance Technology** — Harmonic instruments for communication, analysis, containment  
**Warden Teams** — Trained specialists for Pneuma spill response and relic quarantine

## Pneuma Connection

> **GM Spoilers Below — Reveal Gradually**

### What Birrin Know (Truth)

The Birrin have independently discovered **[[Mechanics/FATE/Pneuma Rules/_Pneuma Rules|Pneuma]]** (the "interaction layer" between consciousness and reality). They call it **The Resonance** or **The Deep Structure**.

**Key Facts:**
- Recognized ~400 years ago (Second Age research)
- Requires extensive training for safe interaction
- Accessed through harmonic/vibrational methodology
- Untrained contact causes mental, physical, spiritual damage
- May have contributed to The Fall (suppressed/debated theory)

**Birrin Approach:**
- Trained practitioners only (warden-level certification)
- Resonance-cage containment mandatory
- Physical vibration-based methods
- Safer but slower/more limited than human instinct
- Years of study required

**Human Approach:**
- Direct mental interface
- Spontaneous/intuitive access
- Minimal training possible
- Powerful but unstable
- High backlash risk

### What Terrifies Them

Humans accessing Pneuma is like children with nuclear weapons:
- No species-wide safety culture
- Corporate/house competition will drive dangerous experimentation
- Untrained access risks cascade effects
- Could destabilize entire sectors
- Reminiscent of pre-Fall recklessness that killed billions

### The Core Dilemma

- **Containment:** Quarantine all Pneuma access (Concord position)
- **Cooperation:** Teach humans safe methods (some Birrin advocate this)
- **Competition:** Learn human methods to stay relevant (rogue Birrin doing this)
- **Revelation:** Share full truth vs. maintain secrets (ongoing debate)

## Quick GM Questions

**"Should Birrin be antagonists?"**  
No. They're deuteragonists with different methodology. They can oppose PCs without being villains. Best used as complicated allies.

**"How alien should they feel?"**  
Understandable motivations, alien methods. Relatable emotions, unique expressions. Different enough to be interesting, similar enough to cooperate with effort.

**"When do I reveal the Pneuma connection?"**  
Early: mysterious resonance tech. Mid: strong reactions to Pneuma. Late: full explanation. Match to campaign pacing; don't rush.

**"What if PCs ignore containment protocols?"**  
Tick Wildfire Index. Issue warnings. Then escalate: diplomatic pressure → route access restricted → silent sabotage → open interdiction. Make consequences real but fair.

**"Can Birrin PCs use Pneuma?"**  
Yes, but with limitations. Warden-trained (safe, slow) or secret student (powerful, risky). See [[Factions/Aliens/Birrin/Birrin Species Rules (FATE)#Birrin & Pneuma|Birrin & Pneuma]] section.

**"How do I roleplay Birrin speech?"**  
Measured, careful word choice. Literal interpretation. Physical punctuation (radiator shifts, eyestalk movements, clicking). Polite but firm. "We respectfully insist on quarantine protocols."
