---

## How to Read This

- **MUST** = this beat needs to happen this session or the arc breaks
- **SEED** = plant a hint, reference, or breadcrumb — no resolution needed
- **DROP** = deliver a specific piece of information to the players
- **FLEX** = can happen here or be shifted forward/back as pacing demands
- **REACT** = triggered by player action, not GM scheduling

Each beat links to its source document for full details.

## Session Prep Checklist

Before each session, check:

1. **What MUST happen this session?** (Non-negotiable beats)
2. **What can I SEED?** (Passing references, NPC comments, background details)
3. **What DROPs are ready?** (Prepared handouts, interview fragments, data files)
4. **What's FLEX?** (Beats I can pull forward or push back based on pacing)
5. **Did the players do something last session that triggers a REACT?**
