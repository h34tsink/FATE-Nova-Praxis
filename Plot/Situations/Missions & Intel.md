## 🎭 **Holo-Masquerade Ball**
- **Mission:** [[House Cipriani]]'s holo-masquerade ball draws numerous houses. Players should exploit holographic mask anonymity, interacting with guests like Octavia Cipriani (Biosleeved) to uncover Elysian Enigma clues.
- **Intel from Octavia Cipriani:** 
  - She reveals the existence of a secured data center where sensitive information about the Elysian Enigma project is stored. She also hints at the location of the data center and suggests that an insider might be willing to help the players.
  - **Snippet:** "*The masks may hide our faces, but secrets, they always have a way of coming out.*"

## 💽 **Data Heist**
- **Mission:** Navigate the fortified data center governed by Garrick Knox (Pure). Bypass advanced security measures and retrieve Elysian Enigma data.
- **Intel from Garrick Knox:** 
  - Although Garrick doesn't directly provide information to the players, they obtain a partially corrupted data fragment about the Elysian Enigma project after overcoming the security measures he has implemented at the data center.
  - **Snippet:** "*Every byte of data has a story, but not all are meant to be read.*"

## 🕵️ **Ambush and Interrogation**
- **Mission:** Strategically ambush Victor Belmont (Cybersleeved) from [[House Cipriani]] during his rendezvous with black-market dealer Xander Kress ([[Apostate]]). Extract Elysian Enigma intel without alerting others.
- **Intel from Victor Belmont:** 
  - Upon interrogation, Victor discloses that a conflicted employee within [[House Cipriani]]'s research facility is willing to share more information about the Elysian Enigma. He also provides the date and location of the employee's next communication window.
  - **Snippet:** "*Why all this cloak and dagger? What's the Elysian Enigma to you?*"

## 🌀 **The Cybernetic Puzzle**
- **Mission:** Decipher the corrupted Elysian Enigma data fragment. Enter a virtual realm, confronting intricate puzzles crafted by Ariadne Vega (Pure).
- **Intel from Ariadne Vega:** 
  - After the players solve Ariadne's cybernetic puzzles, they recover the lost data from the corrupted fragment. This information includes the Elysian Enigma's development [[Timeline]], the names of key personnel involved, and hints about the AI's potential capabilities.
  - **Snippet:** "*Codes, ciphers, and puzzles - they're all just ways to speak without words.*"

## 🕶️ **Undercover Infiltration**
- **Mission:** Go undercover into [[House Cipriani]]'s research base as recruits under Dr. Lucia Ferro (Biosleeved). Unearth Elysian Enigma intel without blowing your cover.
- **Intel from Dr. Lucia Ferro:** 
  - While infiltrating the research facility, the players overhear Dr. Ferro discussing the Elysian Enigma project in a video conference. She confirms its advanced AI Invention Machine nature and reveals that a successful demonstration is planned soon, further emphasizing the urgency of their mission.
  - **Snippet:** "*You're new here, aren't you? Remember, in this place, trust is a rare commodity.*"

## 🤐 **The Reluctant Informant**
- **Mission:** Befriend Rafael Cortez (Cybersleeved), a torn [[House Cipriani]] worker. Elicit Elysian Enigma specifics without endangering him.
- **Intel from Rafael Cortez:** 
  - Rafael provides the players with insider knowledge about the Elysian Enigma project, such as its location within the research facility, security measures, and the identities of other individuals who might be willing to assist the players in their investigation. He also informs the players about Marissa "Speed Daemon" Delgado, who has crucial information but demands a high price.
  - **Snippet:** "*If you knew the true nature of Elysian, you'd understand my fear.*"

## 🚗 **Street Racing for Intel**
- **Mission:** Engage in a treacherous urban race, competing against Marissa "Speed Daemon" Delgado ([[Apostate]]) while dodging Officer Helena Chu (Biosleeved).
- **Intel from Marissa "Speed Daemon" Delgado:** 
  - After winning the street race, Marissa provides the players with detailed blueprints of the Elysian Enigma project, including its core architecture, power sources, and potential weak points that could be exploited to sabotage or take control of the AI Invention Machine.
  - **Snippet:** "*In this race, it's not just speed that matters, but the secrets you unlock.*"
