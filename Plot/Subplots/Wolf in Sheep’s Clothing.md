Ansari Station has been harried by a string of murders. 
	
The victims were killed to cover up the theft of an unknown number of override codes, codes that the House had previously denied existed. 
	
These codes can grant total control of a ship—a powerful tool in the right hands. Thus far, the House has managed to cover up the murders, fearing the consequences should the details leek.
