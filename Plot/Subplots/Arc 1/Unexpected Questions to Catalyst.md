#### Unexpected Player Questions and GM-Prepared Answers

1. **Question: "Is Catalyst your real name, or is it just a codename?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "Names serve as labels for convenience. Call me Catalyst, as that is the role I play in this operation."
2. **Question: "Have you worked with anyone we might know before?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "I've interacted with various individuals across the system, but confidentiality is key in my line of work. Specific names are not something I can disclose."
3. **Question: "What's with the fancy outfit? Is it functional or just for show?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "My attire is a blend of form and function, designed for various purposes. Rest assured, it's more than just a fashion statement."
4. **Question: "Can you recommend a good place to eat on the station?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "While culinary delights are not my expertise, the merchant area has a variety of options. Try the 'Stellar Delicacies' eatery for some local flavors."
5. **Question: "Do you believe in extraterrestrial life?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "The universe is vast and full of mysteries. It's naive to think we are alone, but our focus now is on the task at hand."
6. **Question: "What's your opinion on the latest holo-series everyone's talking about?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "Entertainment is a valuable escape for many, but I'm afraid I don't have the luxury of time to keep up with holo-series."
7. **Question: "Are there any secret areas or hidden treasures on this station we should know about?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "Every station has its secrets. While I can't confirm hidden treasures, I'd advise keeping your eyes open. You never know what you might find."
8. **Question: "What do you do for fun when you're not orchestrating covert operations?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "Leisure is a luxury I seldom afford myself. My work demands constant attention."
9. **Question: "Have you ever been in love?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "Personal emotions do not play a role in my duties. Let's keep our focus on the mission."
10. **Question: "If we succeed, can we get a tour of [[House Cipriani]]'s private collections or archives?"**
    
    - **Answer:** "[[House Cipriani]] values its privacy, and access to its collections is highly restricted. However, successful completion of this mission will certainly elevate your standing."
