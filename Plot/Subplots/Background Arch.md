[[Carmen Ashford]] is a brilliant engineer and Technophage researcher. During the experiment, she had an accident. She became unconscious and spent three days in a coma as a result. She had a vision during this time about how she would become the savior of humanity and save us from the universe's heat death. The development of von Neumann-like self-replicating probes was necessary for the first part of her quest. that would then construct solar collector rings around our sun to generate the required power for the probes.

She realized the planet [[Mercury]] could be used to mine the material, which could be done in half the world's mass.
The procedure will be repeated once the probes are fully operational. while also creating brand-new planets that humanity can live on and colonize.

Building the auto-replicating nano swarms necessary to construct the probes is Carmen's first obstacle. because the Technophage catastrophe on [[Earth]] has made the technology illegal. that nearly destroyed humanity. In order to carry out her plans and reverse engineer the Technophage, she sets out to acquire a sample.
