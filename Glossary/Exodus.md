The mass evacuation of humanity from Earth following the release of the [[Glossary/Technophage]] during the closing years of the **Consolidation Wars**. As nation-state governments collapsed under the gray-fog nanoswarm, the largest corporations directed their resources to lift survivors offworld. Millions escaped; many billions did not. The Exodus marked the end of Earth as humanity's home and the beginning of corporate-led stewardship that would crystallize into the [[Factions/The Coalition/_Coalition Overview|Coalition]] and its [[Glossary/Coalition Houses|Houses]].

Earth has been under quarantine ever since, patrolled by [[Factions/The Coalition/Houses/House Jinzhan|House Jinzhan]] kill-sats to prevent the technophage from spreading. The phrase *"after the Exodus"* is the de facto zero point for [[Campaign Overview/Timeline of Events|Post-Singularity dating]] and the cultural memory that justifies most of the Coalition's surveillance state.
