An Aspect is a word, phrase, quote, or sentence that describes some particular trait of a person, place, or thing. You can spend a **[[Fate Point]]** (FP) to draw attention to (**invoke**) a particular Aspect, or gain an FP when an Aspect harms (**compels**) you. Aspects are generally written in italics.
