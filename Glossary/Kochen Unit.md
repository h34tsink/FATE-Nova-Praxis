# Kochen Unit  

**Origin:** Simon Kochen (*Free Will Theorem*)  
**Meaning:** A 'unit' of Pneuma influence; used in research papers and parody.  

---

## In-World Usage
- Academics jokingly measure Pneuma events in 'Kochen Units'.
- Street slang: 'He hit you with at least three Kochens, easy.'
- Occasionally cited in faction propaganda to minimize Pneuma's seriousness.

## Cultural / Faction Associations
- Favored by **Mimirians** in academic debates.
- Dismissed by **Purists** as pseudoscience.
- Used by **students** as a slang grading scale ('That test was a full Kochen').

## GM Hooks
- A stolen research paper defines a new calibration of Kochen Units.
- NPCs argue whether the PCs' Pneuma traces reached 'ten Kochens'.
- Black market artifact is advertised as 'certified 8 Kochen output'.
