# Sleeves

A comprehensive reference for all sleeve types in Nova Praxis.

## Biosleeves

Biosleeves are genetically engineered biological bodies that have undergone gene-fixing to eliminate hereditary diseases and defects. They represent advanced biological technology while maintaining an organic form.

### Alpha Biosleeve
**Cost:** 6 | **Physical Skill Ranks:** 8 | **Augmentation Points:** 2

Basic gene-fixed biosleeve with essential enhancements. Entry-level option with reduced aesthetics.

**Aspects:**
- Gene-Fixed Biosleeve
- Unattractive

**Included Augmentations:**
- Apotheosis

**Restrictions:** Unattractive appearance

### Beta Biosleeve
**Cost:** 7 | **Physical Skill Ranks:** 9 | **Augmentation Points:** 3

**Aspects:**
- Gene-Fixed Biosleeve

**Included Augmentations:**
- Apotheosis

### Gamma Biosleeve

## Cybersleeves

Cybersleeves are fully synthetic bodies with non-magnetic metal skeletons and synthetic muscles. They represent the cutting edge of cybernetic technology.

### Alpha Cybersleeve
**Cost:** 5 | **Physical Skill Ranks:** 7 | **Augmentation Points:** 3 | **AR:** 1

Basic mass-produced humanoid robot with obvious synthetic appearance and construction flaws.

**Aspects:**
- Synthetic Biology (Persistent)
- Inhuman Appearance
- Dubious Construction

**Included Augmentations:**

- Fully Synthetic

**Restrictions:** Inhuman appearance, Purist prejudice

## Sleeve Selection

Your starting sleeve cost is determined by: **MAX(Assets Skill Rank, Rep-Rating) + 4**

Physical skill ranks provided by your sleeve are distributed among Athletics, Endurance, and Perception with a maximum of 5 per skill.

Included augmentations do not count against your augmentation point total.
