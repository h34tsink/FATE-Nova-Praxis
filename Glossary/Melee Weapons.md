# Melee Weapons

A comprehensive reference for all melee weapons in Nova Praxis.

## Bladed Weapons

### Knife/Dagger
**Cost:** 0 | **WR:** 1 | **Range:** 1 TH  
**Aspects:** Small  
**Restricted:** No

A basic cutting implement that can serve as both tool and weapon. Knives and daggers are compact, easily concealed, and effective in close combat situations.

### Machete
**Cost:** 1 | **WR:** 2 | **Range:** 0  
**Restricted:** No

A broad, heavy blade originally designed for cutting through vegetation. The machete's weight and sharp edge make it an effective weapon for close combat.

### Dagger, Mono-Edged
**Cost:** 2 | **WR:** 2 | **Range:** 1 TH  
**Aspects:** Small, Mono-Edged  
**Restricted:** No

A dagger enhanced with mono-molecular edge technology. These bladed weapons feature an almost infinitely sharp edge created with a single carbon nanotube running the length of the blade.

### Short Sword, Mono-Edged
**Cost:** 3 | **WR:** 3 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Mono-Edged  
**Restricted:** No

A balanced one-handed sword with mono-molecular edge technology. The mono-edge never needs sharpening and maintains its cutting ability indefinitely, though the leading edge may need replacement over time.

### Longsword, Mono-Edged
**Cost:** 4 | **WR:** 4 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Large, Mono-Edged  
**Restricted:** Yes

A two-handed sword with mono-molecular edge technology. This weapon combines the devastating cutting power of mono-edge construction with the reach and leverage of a longsword design.

### Spear, Mono-Edged
**Cost:** 5 | **WR:** 4 | **Range:** 1 TH  
**Aspects:** Large, Mono-Edged  
**Restricted:** Yes

A polearm weapon with a mono-molecular edge spearhead. The spear's length allows attacks at greater distance while the mono-edge ensures devastating penetration.

### Halberd, Mono-Edged
**Cost:** 6 | **WR:** 5 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Long, Huge(P), Mono-Edged  
**Restricted:** Yes

A massive polearm combining axe, spear, and hook into one devastating weapon, enhanced with mono-molecular edge technology. The halberd's exceptional reach and versatility make it formidable but unwieldy.

## Axes

### Hatchet
**Cost:** 0 | **WR:** 1 | **Range:** 1 TH  
**Aspects:** Small  
**Restricted:** No

A small, single-handed axe originally designed for cutting wood. Its compact size makes it easy to conceal and effective in close quarters combat.

### Battle Axe, Mono-Edged
**Cost:** 4 | **WR:** 4 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Large, Mono-Edged  
**Restricted:** Yes

A two-handed battle axe enhanced with mono-molecular edge technology. The combination of the axe's crushing weight and the mono-edge's cutting power creates a devastatingly effective weapon.

## Blunt Weapons

### Punch/Kick (Unarmed)
**Cost:** 0 | **WR:** 0 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Unarmed, Small  
**Restricted:** No

Basic hand-to-hand combat using fists, elbows, knees, and feet. While not as effective as weapons, unarmed combat is always available and difficult to restrict.

### Small Club/Baton
**Cost:** 0 | **WR:** 1 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Small  
**Restricted:** No

A short bludgeoning weapon, often used by security personnel. Its compact size allows for easy concealment while providing more striking power than bare hands.

### Tonfa (Police Nightstick)
**Cost:** 0 | **WR:** 1 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Quick  
**Restricted:** No

A side-handled baton favored by law enforcement. The tonfa's design allows for rapid strikes and defensive blocks, making it effective for crowd control and personal defense.

### Hammer
**Cost:** 0 | **WR:** 1 | **Range:** 0  
**Restricted:** No

A basic tool that doubles as an improvised weapon. The hammer's weighted head delivers focused impact, making it more effective than most improvised weapons.

### Club/Baseball Bat
**Cost:** 0 | **WR:** 2 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Large  
**Restricted:** Yes

A long wooden or metal club, often a repurposed baseball bat. Its length provides leverage for powerful strikes, though its size makes it difficult to conceal.

### Large Club
**Cost:** 0 | **WR:** 2 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Huge(P)  
**Restricted:** Yes

A massive bludgeoning weapon that requires two hands to wield effectively. While devastatingly powerful, its enormous size makes it impractical in confined spaces.

### Gravitic Baton
**Cost:** 3 | **WR:** 3 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Gravitic  
**Restricted:** No

A powered melee weapon that utilizes magnetic fields to amplify and redirect natural gravity. The weapon's impact is dramatically increased with additional kinetic force delivered to the target.

### Gravitic Hammer
**Cost:** 4 | **WR:** 4 | **Range:** 0  
**Aspects:** Huge(P), Gravitic  
**Restricted:** Yes

A massive two-handed weapon combining traditional hammer design with gravitic amplification technology. The devastating impact force makes it extremely effective but difficult to wield in tight spaces.
