# Explosives

A comprehensive reference for all explosives in Nova Praxis.

## Grenades

### Fragmentation Grenade
**Cost:** 4 | **EXR:** 6 | **Area:** 1  
**Restricted:** Yes

A standard anti-personnel explosive device that fragments upon detonation, sending deadly shrapnel in all directions to attack everyone in the target zone.

### Incendiary Grenade
**Cost:** 5 | **EXR:** 6 | **Area:** 1  
**Restricted:** Yes

**Special:** Targets hit + zone On Fire

Creates intense flames that engulf the target area, setting both targets and the environment ablaze.

### Stun Grenade
**Cost:** 2 | **EXR:** 1 | **Area:** 1  
**Restricted:** Yes

**Special:** Targets hit are Stunned

Non-lethal flashbang that disorients and stuns targets with intense light and sound.

### Gooper Grenade
**Cost:** 4 | **EXR:** 4 | **Area:** 1  
**Restricted:** Yes

**Special:** Less-than-lethal, Super-sticky strands, Entangles targets

A less-than-lethal explosive that blasts the area with super-sticky strands that entangle those caught in the spray. The gooper creates movement restrictions without causing direct physical harm.

### Hell Ball
**Cost:** 5 | **EXR:** 8 | **Area:** 1  
**Aspects:** Anti-Vehicular, Incendiary  
**Restricted:** Yes

**Special:** Thermobaric, Massive overpressure, Extreme heat

A thermobaric grenade that releases combustible gases before exploding for massive overpressure and extreme heat. The Hell Ball creates an inferno with tremendous heat and devastating shockwaves, making it highly effective against both personnel and structures.

### Smoke Grenade
**Cost:** 2 | **Area:** 1  
**Aspects:** Concealment  
**Restricted:** No

**Special:** Creates Smokey aspect for scene

A non-lethal tactical device that creates a dense cloud of smoke to obscure vision and provide concealment. Often used by security forces for crowd control or tactical advantage.

## Demolitions

### High Explosive Satchel Charge
**Cost:** 8 | **EXR:** 10 | **Area:** 3  
**Aspects:** Anti-Vehicular, Demolitions  
**Restricted:** Yes

**Special:** Uses Demolitions skill, Attack test uses Demolitions

A powerful demolition charge designed for breaching fortifications and destroying structures. The satchel charge requires skill to place effectively but can cause massive devastation over a large area.
