# Gold

The **physical currency of the apostate world** — and the only universally-accepted medium of exchange outside [[Factions/The Coalition/_Coalition Overview|Coalition]] space. Where the Coalition runs on [[Glossary/Rep-Rating|Rep-Rating]] and [[Glossary/Compilers|compilers]], the rest of human civilization runs on gold.

## Forms

- **Small gold coins** — the standard unit for routine transactions
- **Small gold bars** — for larger sums
- **Gold flakes** sealed inside small clear plastic chips that display the flake's weight — the smallest denomination, used for street-level exchange

## Why Gold

Without [[Glossary/Compilers|compilers]], apostate enclaves still operate under a **scarcity economy**. Gold is the same currency it has been for most of human history: durable, portable, fungible, and impossible to counterfeit at the molecular level without nanotech the apostates don't have.

## Coalition Position

The use, possession, and refinement of gold currency is **illegal in Coalition space**. The [[Factions/The Coalition/Protectorate|Protectorate]] runs an ongoing campaign — informally called the **Gold War** — to find and destroy gold molds, refineries, and stockpiles. Coalition citizens caught with gold coins or flakes face criminal charges.

This is less about the metal itself and more about what gold *enables*: an economy the Coalition cannot trace, tax, or freeze. Gold is the financial backbone of the Shadow War, the Talon recruitment pipeline, the [[Glossary/Standard Deviation Project|Standard Deviation Project]], and every other operation the Coalition cannot put on a [[Glossary/CID|CID]] ledger.

## Where Gold Travels

- [[Locations/New Montreal]] — runs a fully gold-denominated commercial layer
- [[Locations/Santa Maria]] — apostate flotilla scavenging and repairs paid in gold
- **Asola** — Tsaryan workers paid in undervalued credits instead of Rep or gold, which is why they riot
- **Rho** — black market exchange where stolen goods, sensies, and souls are sold for gold
