# Armor

A comprehensive reference for all armor types in Nova Praxis.

## Unpowered Armor

### Flight Suit
**Cost:** 2 | **AR:** 0  
**Built-in Features:** Thermal Regulation  
**Max Features:** 0  
**Restricted:** No

Basic flight suit with thermal regulation capabilities.

### Reinforced Clothing/Protective Vest
**Cost:** 3 | **AR:** 1  
**Max Features:** 0  
**Restricted:** No

Civilian-grade protective clothing with basic armor plating.

### Vacuum Suit
**Cost:** 4 | **AR:** 1  
**Built-in Features:** Vacuum Sealed, Air Pack, Thermal Regulation  
**Aspects:** Bulky  
**Max Features:** 0  
**Restricted:** No

Essential for space operations, provides basic protection in vacuum environments.

### Reinforced Vacuum Suit
**Cost:** 5 | **AR:** 2

### Heavy Battle Armor

## Armor Features

### Air Pack
**Power Armor Only:** No

8 hours breathable air in sealed helmet. Provides 8 hours of breathable air in sealed helmet.

### Computer Gauntlet
**Power Armor Only:** No

PPC (Alpha Agent) with voice/holographic interface. Includes PPC with Alpha Agent, voice commands and holographic interface.

### Enhanced Mobility Actuators
**Power Armor Only:** Yes

+2 Athletics for movement tests. +2 bonus on all Athletics tests related to movement, climbing, etc.

### High-Density Plating
**Power Armor Only:** No

+1 to Armor Rating. Increases the armor's AR by +1.

### Hydrostatic Gel Layer
**Power Armor Only:** Yes

+3 AR vs explosions, AR absorbs falling damage. +3 bonus to AR when defending against explosions, AR absorbs stress from falling.

### Integrated Weaponry
**Power Armor Only:** Yes

Built-in weapon (pistol, PDW, dagger, or short sword), -1 Cost. Armor includes built-in weapon, reduces weapon cost by -1.

### Interface Surface
**Power Armor Only:** No

Touch interface for digital data transmission. Hand surface can transmit digital data via touch interface.

### Jump Jets
**Power Armor Only:** No

Jump 50 feet, +1 zone Hustling, +2 zones Running, +3 chase bonus. Jump up to 50 feet, extra movement zones, +3 bonus during chases.

### Light Screen Camouflage
**Power Armor Only:** Yes

Chameleon aspect when activated. Grants Chameleon Aspect when activated for near-invisibility.

### Mag-Traction
**Power Armor Only:** No

Electromagnet boots for zero-g traversal. Electromagnet boots allow safe traversal of zero-g environments.

### Motion Tracker
**Power Armor Only:** No

Detect motion up to 20 meters, Motion Tracker aspect. Sensors detect motion up to 20 meters, grants Motion Tracker Aspect.

### Reinforced Cyber-Musculature
**Power Armor Only:** Yes

+2 Athletics for physical tasks, +2 melee WR. +2 Athletics for lifting/grappling, +2 WR for melee/thrown attacks.

### Thermal Regulation System
**Power Armor Only:** No

Comfortable in -60°C to 140°C. Keeps wearer comfortable in extreme temperatures (-60°C to 140°C).

### Vacuum Sealed and Shielded
**Power Armor Only:** No

Protection from vacuum and radiation. Provides protection from vacuum and radiation hazards.

### Zero-G Assistance Jets
**Power Armor Only:** No

Zero-G Maneuverability aspect. Tiny thrusters aid zero-g maneuvering, grants Zero-G Maneuverability Aspect.
