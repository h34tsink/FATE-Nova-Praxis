# Planck Twitch  

**Origin:** Max Planck  
**Meaning:** The smallest detectable Pneuma nudge.  

---

## In-World Usage
- Used as slang for a minor Pneuma misfire.
- Street quip: 'Barely a Planck Twitch.'
- Corporations dismiss small anomalies as 'just a Twitch'.

## Cultural / Faction Associations
- Black ops record 'Planck Twitch' readings during surveillance.
- Mimirians argue Twitches are evidence of deeper Pneuma ubiquity.
- Common among technicians repairing systems after small anomalies.

## GM Hooks
- Logs show repeated 'Planck Twitch' anomalies hinting at hidden Pneumatics.
- An NPC's minor use of Pneuma is only detectable as a Twitch.
- Artifact sold as 'safe' because it only ever causes Twitches.
