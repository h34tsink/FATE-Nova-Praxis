A two-handed military assault rifle that uses electromagnetic rails to accelerate a dense projectile to hypersonic velocity. The Heavy Assault Rail-Rifle is the heaviest of the standard rail-pattern long arms, prized for chewing through armored opponents at standard combat ranges.

## Mechanics

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Weapon Rating (WR) / Exceptional Range | **+6** |
| Range | 4 |
| Cost | 7 |
| Aspects | **Armor Penetrating**, **Huge(P)**, *Reduce WR by -2 for Silent firing* |

Like all firearms, it is **Restricted** in Coalition space — possession requires a license tied to the user's [[Glossary/CID]], or apostate workarounds. Compare with the lighter **Rail-Carbine** (+5 WR) for tighter quarters and the **Sniper Rail-Rifle** variants for precision engagement at extreme range.
