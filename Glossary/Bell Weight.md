# Bell Weight  

**Origin:** John Bell  
**Meaning:** How much Pneuma disturbs local causality.  

---

## In-World Usage
- Researchers publish 'Bell Weight' classifications for Pneuma events.
- Street slang: 'That trick had some serious Bell on it.'
- HPA reports use Bell Weight as forensic evidence.

## Cultural / Faction Associations
- Adopted formally by **HPA** as a classification metric.
- Black markets brag about 'High Bell Weight' artifacts.
- Mimirians use Bell Weight readings to argue Pneuma's reality.

## GM Hooks
- A heist revolves around stealing Bell Weight calibration logs.
- NPCs accuse PCs of leaving behind a 'High Bell Weight' signature.
- Faction war over artifact rumored to produce 'Zero Bell Weight traces'.
