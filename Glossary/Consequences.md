Instead of suffering stress on your **[[Stress Track]]**, you may elect to take a special kind of **[[Aspect]]** called a Consequence. There are four types of Consequence: **Mild**, **Moderate**, **Severe(P)**, and **Extreme(P)**. These represent lasting effects from conflicts.
