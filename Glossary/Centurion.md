Heavily armed law enforcement officers of the [[Factions/The Coalition/Protectorate|Protectorate]], called in when standard security drones and patrol officers are deemed inadequate to handle a situation. Centurions are *part police, part soldier* — a highly trained, well-equipped division authorized to use **any means, including lethal force**, to apprehend or subdue a suspect.

## Behavior & Authority

- Deployed by the local AI [[Glossary/Monitors|Monitor]] when a situation escalates beyond drone response
- Authorized lethal force, but generally restrained — except when the suspect is known to carry a [[Glossary/Mnemonic Core|mnemonic core]], in which case Centurions are notably less cautious. *("It's a lot easier to bring a still-bloody core back to the station than a writhing and screaming suspect.")*
- Enforce both Coalition law and **House law**, but only a Centurion who is *also a member of the relevant House* can enforce that House's internal laws (see [[Factions/The Coalition/Houses Reference]])
- Sit between baseline patrol officers and the **Legion** (military) and **Praetorians** (special agents) in the Protectorate's escalation ladder

To a player character on the run, the appearance of Centurions usually means the GM has decided the situation has stopped being a chase and started being a fight.
