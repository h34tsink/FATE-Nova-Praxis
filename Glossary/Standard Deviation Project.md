A pre-[[Glossary/Exodus|Exodus]] longitudinal experiment in **ego resilience** designed by cognitive scientist **Dr. Maren Holt**. The project's full title — *"Standard Deviation: Controlled Ego Fusion Through Curated Psychological Complementarity"* — describes its true purpose: to identify and freeze a specific set of psychological archetypes that, when later revived and merged, would produce a stable composite consciousness rather than fragment under [[Glossary/Apotheosis|Apotheosis]] and resleeving.

Approximately 200 cryo-sleepers were stored in a sealed research archive. Each was selected for a specific role in the planned gestalt — *Face*, *Ghost*, *Lens*, *Bank*, *Shield* — and subjected to targeted [[Glossary/Mnemonic Editing|mnemonic edits]] before freezing to reinforce the trait Holt needed from them. Consent was never obtained, and the research was never published.

The current player characters are five of those subjects, revived a century later. Their "rescue" was not random and their defining flaws were not theirs to begin with. The intended outcome of the experiment is filed in the appendix of Holt's final paper under a single word: **[[Glossary/Chimera|CHIMERA]]**.
