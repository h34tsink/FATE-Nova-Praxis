# Compilers

A **molecular assembler** that builds finished goods from raw materials, assembling them at the atomic level. Compilers are the foundational technology of [[Factions/The Coalition/_Coalition Overview|Coalition]] post-scarcity society — and the single invention that did more than any other to reshape the world after the [[Glossary/Exodus|Exodus]].

## How They Work

A compiler is a swarm of nanomachines plus a feedstock hopper. Given the right **template software** and the right raw materials, it can fabricate almost anything: food, clothing, tools, electronics, vehicle parts. Templates are licensed and version-controlled.

A **decompiler** uses the same nanotechnology in reverse — breaking objects down into powdered or liquid feedstock for re-use.

## Coalition Control

Compilers in Coalition space are tied to a citizen's [[Glossary/CID|CID]] — every template request is logged, every fabricated item is traceable, and **restricted templates** (weapons, military hardware, controlled substances) require a license. This is the lever that makes Coalition society work: nobody starves, but nobody builds in private either.

The widespread deployment of compilers devastated the pre-Coalition world economy and necessitated the [[Glossary/Rep-Rating|Rep-Rating]] system as a replacement for currency.

## Outside the Coalition

[[Factions/Ideologies/Apostates|Apostates]] do not have access to compilers. Their settlements run on barter, scavenging, and [[Glossary/Gold|gold]]. Compilers do not function without [[Glossary/CID|CID]] authentication, which is why apostate cities like [[Locations/New Montreal]] cannot simply steal one and run it — the device requires a citizen's identity to authorize each compile.

Two notable exceptions exist in canon:

- **Gondolin** — operates a single working compiler whose security is cracked manually each use by a [[Glossary/Savant|Savant]] named Gulo. The existence of this compiler is one of the apostate world's best-kept secrets.
- **Sentinel Station** — military stockpile housing several hundred compilers cleared for restricted production (weapons, explosives, weaponized bacteria). One of the Coalition's principal apotheosis sites.

A Federation remnant group called **Jacob's Hand** is also known to have stolen a number of military-grade compilers without location-based restrictions.

## Continuity

- The [[Glossary/Standard Deviation Project|Standard Deviation Project]] and similar deep-conspiracy operations rely on access to **off-grid compilers** — the kind that don't log to the Coalition trace network.
