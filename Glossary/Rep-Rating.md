A numerical measure of a citizen's standing within the [[Factions/The Coalition/_Coalition Overview|Coalition]], administered by the **Inter-Rep** agency and continuously adjusted by AI [[Glossary/Monitors]] watching social interactions through the mesh. Rep is granted when others rate a citizen positively (a *bump*) and lost when they rate negatively (a *hit*). It functions simultaneously as social currency, political franchise, and access control.

## What Rep-Rating Determines

- **Standard of living above default** — higher Rep unlocks better housing, food, and templates
- **Voting weight** — a high-Rep citizen's vote literally counts for more
- **Access to restricted goods and venues** — exclusive clubs, restricted compiler templates, and certain colleges (e.g., the schools on [[Locations/Sol System/Helios|Helios]] require Rep 7+)
- **Buying power** — Rep can be exchanged like currency for favors and high-value goods
- **Resilience to losses** — high-Rep citizens absorb more hits before suffering consequences

Each [[Glossary/Coalition Houses|House]] applies its own modifiers, so an item that costs more Rep in [[Factions/The Coalition/Houses/House Cipriani|House Cipriani]] may be cheap inside [[Factions/The Coalition/Houses/House Silva|House Silva]]. **Apostates** — those living outside Coalition jurisdiction — have no Rep-Rating at all and must barter or use illegal gold currency. Failing to recycle, breaking minor laws, or simply being unpopular all chip away at Rep, making it the Coalition's primary instrument of soft control.
