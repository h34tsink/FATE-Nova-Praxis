**Antiparticle Exchange (APEX)** reactors are matter/antimatter power plants developed by the **Alliance** in the early Post-Singularity era. They harness controlled annihilation reactions to deliver effectively unlimited energy, and they are the technological backbone of every system in Nova Praxis that requires absurd amounts of power.

## What APEX Powers

- **Starship drives and onboard gravitics** — every interstellar vessel of meaningful tonnage runs on APEX, including the first Delta Type Dreadnought, *The Ravager*
- **[[Glossary/Jump Gate|Jump gates]]** — the original Phobos-to-Italy wormhole was opened using APEX output, and every gate since has required an APEX feed to maintain stable wormhole geometry
- **Habitat and station infrastructure** — large orbital and planetary settlements depend on APEX for life support, manufacturing, and the [[Glossary/Virtuality]] backbone

## Continuity

The Alliance built the first stable APEX reactors decades before they had enough antimatter production to feed them; demand only caught up once jump gate technology made the reactors strategically essential. Antimatter production is now dominated by [[Factions/The Coalition/Houses/House Cipriani|House Cipriani]]'s Mercury facilities, giving Cipriani enormous leverage over any House that wants to move starships or open new gates.
