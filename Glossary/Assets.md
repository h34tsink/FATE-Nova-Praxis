In **Strands of Fate**, *Assets* are the persistent off-sheet resources a character can call on — contacts, gear caches, safehouses, vehicles, debts owed, or institutional pull. Mechanically, each Asset is represented by an **[[Glossary/Aspect|Aspect]]** the player can [[Glossary/Invoke|invoke]] when the resource is brought into play, and can be [[Glossary/Compel|compelled]] when relying on it backfires (the contact is missing, the safehouse is burned, the favor comes due).

Assets are also the building blocks of **Organizations** in Strands of Fate: a faction, crew, or covert cell tracks its capabilities as a roster of Asset Aspects, and an attack on the organization is resolved by stressing or sacrificing those Assets in the same way an individual character sacrifices [[Glossary/Consequences|Consequences]].

For Nova Praxis play, Assets are how a PC's [[Factions/The Coalition/Houses Reference|House]] connections, apostate networks, smuggled gear, and remote allies become mechanically actionable rather than just background flavor.
