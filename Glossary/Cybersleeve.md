A **cybernetically-enhanced body** that an ego can be resleeved into. Unlike [[Biosleeve|biosleeves]], cybersleeves consist of synthetic materials and often include enhanced strength or durability. However, they can lead to lower **Cohesion** due to the differences between synthetic and biological systems.
