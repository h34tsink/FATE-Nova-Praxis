The Chickenhawk has suffered a damage, and is unable to safely dock due to its damaged umbilical system. Until this is repaired, the ship cannot establish a pressurized connection to another ship or station. Instead, it must attach itself using a mag-lock system; anyone entering or exiting the ship must expose themselves to space.

---
