# Vehicles

## Sophia (Warframe)

**Owner:** Kallius (deliciouslyuninvited@gmail.com)
**Type:** Warframe
**Cost:** 14
**Size:** 2
**Fate Point Refresh:** 2
**Armor Rating:** 2

### Vehicular Aspects

- Anti-Infantry Frame
- Aggressive Diplomacy
- Presence Is a Weapon

### Specialty Aspects

- Well-Maintained
- Cutting-Edge Electronics
- Custom Modifications

### Capabilities

- Weapons: 2
- Systems: 2
- Speed: 1
- Sensors: 1
- Structure: 1
- Maneuverability: 1

### Features

- **Jump Drive** (Mobility) — Faster-than-light fold-space propulsion. Base range 2 LY (+1 LY per Shift). 4 hr spool time. Fails near gravity wells (< 10x diameter distance)
- **Walker, Bipedal** (Mobility) — Humanoid or bird-leg warframe. ~50 km/h; gains Bipedal Walker Aspect
- **Reinforced Structure** (Defensive) — Strengthened internal framework and redundancies. +2 Structural Stress Boxes; may be taken up to Structure rating times
- **Point Defense System** (Defensive) — Automated guns intercept missiles and fighters. AR bonus = Weapons Capability vs. missiles. May spend 1 FP to attack all enemies in zone (WR 0)
- **Electronic Counter-Measures (ECM)** (Defensive) — Firewalls and anti-intrusion counteragents. +2 defense vs. system attacks; may be taken up to Systems rating times
- **Anti-Personnel Weapons** (Offensive) — Weapons calibrated for infantry combat. Attacks individual targets; WR = Size + 4. Treat as man-scale weapon vs. vehicles
- **Auto-Repair Swarm** (Functional) — Nanite or esper swarm conducts automatic repairs. +2 to repair rolls; can self-repair using Systems (no bonus)
- **Neural Interface** (Functional) — Direct neural control link via hostware or data-jack. +1 on all vehicle-related tests while connected

### Stress Tracks

- Structural: 5 boxes
- System: 4 boxes
