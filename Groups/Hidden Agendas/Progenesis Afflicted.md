## Overview

The Progenesis Afflicted are a population of mutated humans created through Dr. Schulz's biotech viral engineering. Infected with a regenerative virus designed to enhance healing capability, they instead manifest extreme mutations, severe physical deformity, and severe psychological deterioration across successive generations. They represent one of the Coalition's most disturbing and least-understood biological threats.

## Origins: Dr. Schulz's Experiment

### The Researcher
**Dr. Schulz** - Brilliant biotech engineer pursuing regenerative enhancement:
- **Original goal** - Develop virus enabling rapid cellular regeneration
- **Application** - Medical breakthrough offering healing for previously fatal conditions
- **Funding source** - Unknown; possibly Coalition, possibly private interests
- **Methodology** - Aggressive viral mutation and forced infection testing

### The Virus
**Progenesis Virus** - Engineered biotech pathogen with regenerative properties:
- **Cellular regeneration** - Can regrow damaged tissue and organs
- **Rapid healing** - Accelerates recovery from injuries
- **Mutation catalyst** - Induces rapid, uncontrolled genetic mutations
- **Transmission** - Passes between individuals; possibly airborne
- **Persistence** - Once infected, cannot be cured; permanent condition

### The Accident
Catastrophic failure during research:
- **Containment breach** - Laboratory security failed; virus escaped
- **Population infection** - Virus spread to surrounding population
- **Uncontrolled mutation** - Virus mutated unpredictably; regeneration became horrific deformity
- **Ongoing spread** - Virus continues propagating; infection rate unknown

## The Afflicted: Three Generations

### First Generation
**Dr. Schulz's "Successful" Subjects:**
- **Enhanced healing** - Virus functioned somewhat as intended; remarkable regenerative ability
- **Severe deformity** - Regeneration caused grotesque mutations and body horror
- **Psychological damage** - Reality of their transformation caused severe mental trauma
- **Behavioral issues** - Stress and mutation induced antisocial and violent tendencies
- **Fate** - Many deceased; some surviving members still in Coalition space
- **Stability** - Relatively stable compared to subsequent generations; longest-lived

### Second Generation
**Offspring of First Generation:**
- **Inherited mutations** - Virus passes to offspring; second generation inherits damaged genetics
- **Amplified deformity** - Mutations more severe; regeneration more chaotic
- **Accelerated deterioration** - Mental and physical degradation more rapid
- **Reduced lifespan** - Die younger than first generation despite regenerative ability
- **Behavioral instability** - Violent, unpredictable, difficult to control
- **Survival mechanism** - Increasingly monstrous regeneration may represent adaptation

### Third Generation
**Current Generation/Evolutionary Dead End:**
- **Extreme mutation** - Barely recognizable as human; appear as biological nightmares
- **Severe psychosis** - Mental degradation extreme; virtually non-functional
- **Physical horror** - Regeneration creates grotesque, nightmarish body configurations
- **Brief lifespan** - Most die in early adulthood; childhood even shorter
- **Barely sentient** - Reduced to animal-level consciousness in many cases
- **Dead end** - Evolutionary trajectory suggests further generations may not be viable

## Physical Manifestations

### Common Afflictions (vary by individual and generation)
- **Multiple limbs** - Extra arms, legs, or appendages regenerating incorrectly
- **Distorted features** - Faces stretched, compressed, or duplicated
- **Organ externalization** - Hearts, lungs, other organs visible outside body
- **Skeletal deformation** - Bones growing at wrong angles or multiple places
- **Tissue corruption** - Flesh appears infected, gangrenous, or necrotic despite regeneration
- **Sensory distortion** - Eyes in wrong places; hearing/smell altered
- **Invulnerability paradox** - Cannot be killed despite extreme fragility; keeps regenerating

## Psychological Degradation

### Mental Effects
- **Severe psychosis** - Detachment from reality; unable to distinguish illusion from fact
- **Personality disintegration** - Sense of identity completely fragmented
- **Pain sensitivity** - Constant awareness of body's wrongness causes chronic suffering
- **Aggression spikes** - Prone to sudden violent outbursts
- **Suicidal ideation** - Most Afflicted desperate to end their existence
- **Empathy loss** - Reduced ability to recognize others' suffering; callousness toward violence

## Population & Distribution

### Current Status
- **Population size** - Unknown; estimates range from dozens to thousands
- **Location concentration** - Highest density near original outbreak area
- **Migration patterns** - Slowly spreading through Coalition space despite efforts to contain
- **Hidden communities** - Some Afflicted form isolated settlements away from Coalition
- **Integrated infiltration** - Some hide among apostate or transhumans communities

### Integration Challenges
- **Visual identification** - Deformity makes hiding difficult; requires isolation or concealment
- **Discrimination** - Society rejects Afflicted; face extreme prejudice and violence
- **Medical treatment impossible** - Coalition doctors cannot treat condition; virus incurable
- **Social services failure** - Coalition unwilling to provide support; view as biological threat

## Coalition Response

### Official Response
- **Containment failure** - Unable to contain or eliminate virus spread
- **Theory vs. reality** - Coalition official denial versus persistent population
- **Quarantine attempts** - Ineffective; virus spreads faster than containment measures
- **Elimination policy** - Covert operations target Afflicted for destruction
- **Public ignorance** - General population largely unaware of threat

### Ethical Crisis
Afflicted represent unprecedented moral dilemma:
- **Sentient persons** - Despite mutations, retain some level of consciousness and rights
- **Biologically dangerous** - Carry contagious virus threatening general population
- **Suffering crisis** - Exist in extreme pain and psychological agony
- **Mercy killing question** - Is elimination compassionate euthanasia or genocide?
- **Responsibility issue** - Coalition/Dr. Schulz responsibility for created suffering
