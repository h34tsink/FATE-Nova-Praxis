## Overview

**Primary Influence:** Olympus, Luna
**Primary Evolutionary Agenda:** Purist
**Market Discounts:** Starships, Rail/Coil Guns, Combat Drones

House Dalianis traces its roots to three European military defense contractors: German-owned **Keppler Shipyards**, British-owned **Macrotech Robotics**, and French-owned **Habarcq Armaments**, coordinated by Greece's **Promethic Technologies**. While once the dominant economic power during the Consolidation Wars, new technologies (APEX reactors, particle acceleration weapons, advanced drone manufacturing) eroded their monopoly.

## Current Operations

### Military-Industrial Focus
- **Starship manufacturing** - The best in the Coalition; operates Ansari Station, the galaxy's largest shipyard
- **Combat drones** - Almost sole manufacturer for Coalition Fleet (military and maintenance drones)
- **Primary contracts** - Main manufacturers of the Coalition Fleet itself and operators of Tartarus (maximum-security space prison)
- **Jump gate research** - Control of Odysseus Station (Luna-orbiting jump gate facility); continuous optimization of jump technology

### Strategic Positioning
Dalianis is the most expansionist House, claiming expansion as both military necessity (countering Earth's technophage threat) and corporate interest (more colonies = more ships/jump rings needed). Recent expansion efforts target apostate/remnant territory under guise of "exploratory R&D," laying groundwork for colonization. **Lighthouse** security subsidiary enforces expansion agenda when resistance meets these efforts.

## Political Structure & Relationships

### Shadow War Tactics
Unlike most Houses focused on market competition, Dalianis invests heavily in gaining legitimate political power—specifically control of Olympus' seat of power. They use "straightforward" methods to lubricate bureaucratic approval for colonization initiatives.

### House Alliances
- **Alliance with Kimura** - Kimura buys majority of shipboard computer systems; Kimura agrees to focus on civilian drones, leaving military drone market to Dalianis
- **Alliance with Jinzhan** - Mutual support for Earth quarantine; Dalianis provides ships/equipment, Jinzhan provides quarantine operations and Primatech Labs R&D
- **Long feud with Cipriani** - Cipriani's energy monopoly forces Dalianis to purchase expensive APEX reactors; ongoing Cygnus Alpha iron supply competition from Consolidation Wars era
- **Arms race with Silva** - Silva's PAc weapons outclass Dalianis' railgun/coil gun tech in-atmosphere; Dalianis partners with Tsarya to develop PAc-resistant armor; Silva expands shipbuilding to counter-compete
- **Territorial dispute with Tsarya** - Coveting volatile materials in Tsarya-controlled Ceres ice fields; Tsarya refuses relinquishment

### Apostate Recruitment
Dalianis is "willing to compromise" - leadership values results over political concerns. The feudal House structure increasingly respects ability to produce outcomes. Crews of posthumans and heavily augmented operatives can obtain backing and membership if clearly right for the job.
