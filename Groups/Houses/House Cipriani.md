## Overview

**Primary Influence:** Mercury, Cygnus Alpha
**Primary Evolutionary Agenda:** Purist
**Market Discounts:** Energy, Compilers, CIST Technology, Vehicles, Clothing, Transportation

House Cipriani was founded by three major corporations: Italian vehicle manufacturer **Giordino Automotive, Inc.**, Egyptian clothing company **Abbasi Textiles**, and Saudi Arabian energy giant **Tahan Power**. Their combined networking capabilities and vast reputations drove support for the Consociationalist government that eventually became the Coalition.

## Current Operations

### Energy Monopoly
Cipriani operates the vast majority of Mercury's antimatter production through subsidiaries **Specular Spread** and **Infinity Squared**, providing them with near-complete dominance over Coalition energy markets. This monopoly gives them leverage in virtually all tech production deals requiring power cells—meaning every House pays Cipriani for their products.

### Product Lines
- **Compilers** and civilian technology
- **CIST technology** and specialized equipment
- **Vehicles** (commercial and luxury)
- **Public transportation systems**
- **Clothing and textiles**
- **Specialized high-end products** via niche luxury market

**Icarus** subsidiary handles all interplanetary import/export logistics.

## Political Structure & Relationships

### Shadow War Activities


Cipriani is renowned for ruthlessness in protecting energy interests and CIST R&D investments. They almost never enter contracts that don't favor them and always maintain escape clauses. Off-the-books deals to maintain market advantage are standard practice.

### House Alliances


- **Alliance with House Tsarya** - Strong, lasting trade agreement for armor and transplasidium in high-end vehicles
- **Alliance with House Silva** - Termed "partners in crime"; shared business ventures in Rho (Luna), IT Alpha (Europa), and abandoned New Turin mining colony; cooperate against Dalianis
- **Rivalry with House Kimura** - Intense competition in virtual vacation industry (Cipriani's new market entry vs. Kimura's VirtuVac dominance); Shadow War fights between Savants and CIST-wielders in virtual environments
- **Long feud with House Dalianis** - Competition over Cygnus Alpha's iron supplies and mutual dependency on APEX reactors and starship transport

## Recruiting Apostates



Despite Purist ideals, Cipriani accepts apostate applications. The process requires:

1. Gaining attention of influential member
2. Paying for patronage through favors
3. Relatively easy membership once foot in door is gained

Cipriani prefers hiring Purist apostates for off-book operations, valuing shared ideals and lack of ties to other Houses. Heavy augmentation and posthumans are avoided when possible.

## Purist Ideology



Cipriani's Purist composition strains relationships with bio-liberal Houses like Kimura. The House's form-over-function approach extends from their lavish product lines to their corporate culture—aesthetics and prestige drive many decisions.
