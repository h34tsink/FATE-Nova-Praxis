#apostate
Apostates are individuals who lack a Coalition ID, estimated to represent around 20% of humanity. Their origins are diverse; some are born outside Coalition space, while others choose to remove their ID voluntarily. The reasons for apostasy vary, with some individuals disagreeing with the Coalition's practices or choosing to leave the Coalition out of personal preference or as a result of exile.

Life for an apostate is fraught with challenges, typically harder than that of Coalition citizens. They face the constant risk of arrest in certain regions, underscoring their precarious existence on the fringes of society. However, there is an opportunity for apostates to gain citizenship through [[The Houses]]. Each House provides its own unique criteria for granting citizenship, offering a pathway for apostates to reintegrate into society. Despite this opportunity, the process of earning citizenship is known to be difficult, with a low rate of success, making it a formidable challenge for those seeking to change their status.
