Building on the success of the **Phalanx Formations** and **Praetorians**, the Coalition has developed the Scholae Palatinae to address larger threats.

## Overview
Unlike the solitary Praetorians or self-sufficient Phalanx Formations, the Scholae Palatinae operate collectively and are fully supported by the Coalition. Typically comprising four to six Praetorians, each Scholae unit is equipped with a ship, crew, scientists, medics, security, vehicles, and advanced weaponry and armor. Their ships serve as mobile fortresses and command centers, equipped with top-tier facilities, sensors, weapons, and defense systems, capable of deploying various tactical assets.

## Operational Independence and Oversight
While the Scholae enjoy significant autonomy and resources, they are not beyond oversight. Designated specialists and officers on board have the authority to override Scholae decisions for the crew's and Coalition's benefit.

## Role and Secrecy
The Scholae Palatinae are a key strategy against insurrection and [[apostate]] threats. In peacetime, they survey new star systems and research deep-space phenomena. Although their existence is officially secret, controlled leaks and rumors enhance their intimidating reputation, fostering an aura of mystery and power.

The public suspects the existence of at least three active Scholae units, but their exact number remains unknown.
