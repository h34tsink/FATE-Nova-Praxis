## Minister Aria Daxton
- **Division:** Ministry of Parks and Wildlife
- **Appearance:** 
    - Middle-aged with wavy golden hair, always seen in elegant, yet environmentally-themed attire.
- **[[Background]]:** 
    - A fervent environmentalist, Aria has dedicated her life to preserving the diverse ecosystems of Coalition planets. She often clashes with other Ministries over development projects.
- **Character Trait:** 
    - Passionate, sometimes headstrong.
- **Notable Equipment:** 
    - Data pad with information on various flora and fauna.

## Marine Sergeant Elara Nyx
- **Division:** 2nd Legion - The Coalition Marine Corps
- **Appearance:** 
    - Scarred face, tall, muscular, with a stern countenance.
- **Background:** 
    - A veteran Marine, Elara has seen battles across numerous worlds. Known for her prowess with both ranged and melee weapons, she embodies the spirit of the Marines.
- **Character Trait:** 
    - Disciplined, fiercely loyal to her squad.
- **Notable Equipment:** 
    - High-end PAc rifle, mono-edge blade.

## Agent Rael Voss
- **Division:** 3rd Legion - ICON
- **Appearance:** 
    - Slim, average height, always in dark, inconspicuous clothing. Eyes constantly scanning surroundings.
- **Background:** 
    - A top spy for ICON, Rael has infiltrated numerous organizations, providing critical intelligence to the Tetragon.
- **Character Trait:**

- **Notable Equipment:**

## Praetorian Lysa Terro
- **Division:** 4th Legion - Praetorians
- **Appearance:** 
    - Graceful yet menacing, with sharp angular features and a distinct tattoo on her left cheek.
- **Background:** 
    - One of the thirty-six Praetorians, Lysa is a legend. She's hunted down some of the most dangerous fugitives in Coalition space and has a near-perfect success rate.
- **Character Trait:** 
    - Determined, ruthless in pursuit.
- **Notable Equipment:** 
    - Advanced tracking device, override codes for compilers.
