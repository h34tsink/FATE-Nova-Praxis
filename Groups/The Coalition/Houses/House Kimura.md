# Kimura (KEE-MOO-RAH)
**Traits:** Transhumanist, Highly Techno-Progressive, Traditionally Neutral, Masters of Software

**Discounts:** Cost of purchasing any of the following types of goods is reduced by 1:
- Agents(A.I.)
- Computers & Computer Software
- Cybersleeves
- Cybernetic Augmentations
- Simulated Augmentations
- Lite Combat Frames (Walkers Size 6-8) 

**Skill Bonus:** In addition, choose Notice or Knowledge (Software). You gain a +1 bonus on rolls with your chosen Skill.
