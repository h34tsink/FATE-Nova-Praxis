# Dalianis (DAH-LEE-ON-IS)
**Traits:** Purist, Competitive and Expansionist, Small Arms, Starships and Combat Drones

**Discounts:** Cost of purchasing any of the following types of goods is reduced by 1:
- Starships
- Rail/Coil Guns
- Combat Drones

**Skill Bonus:** In addition, choose Piloting or Knowledge (Hardware). You gain a +1 bonus on rolls with your chosen Skill.
