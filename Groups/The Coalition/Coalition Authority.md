## Coalition Authority

The Coalition is the ultimate governing force in certain domains as established by the **Consolidation Concord**. 

### Responsibilities:

- **Protectorate Stewardship**: Wields the Protectorate as an expression of the populace's desires.
  
- **Infrastructure Oversight**: Takes care of structures such as the jump ring network.
  
- **Citizen Welfare**: Guarantees all citizens a baseline quality of life.
  
- **Healthcare Management**: 
    - Ensures the provision and safety of medical services.
    - Upholds the Humanity Preservation Act.
  
- **Equal Representation**: Certifies that no group, regardless of race, sex, religion, or creed, is silenced within the Coalition.
  
- **Economic Oversight**: Currently overseen by InterRep, they supervise the economy.
  
- **Inter-House Diplomacy**: Resolves quarrels between Houses and can dissolve them if required.
  
- **Ministry Management**: They run and uphold the Ministries, ensuring daily governance of the Coalition proceeds without hitches.
