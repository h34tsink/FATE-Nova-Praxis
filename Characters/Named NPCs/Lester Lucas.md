# Lester Lucas

**Summary:** Smuggler and [[Factions/Houses/House Silva]] member, captain of [[Characters/NPCs/Vehicles/The Chickenhawk]]. Wrong-place-wrong-time victim.

## Lore

Lester Lucas is a smuggler operating under [[Factions/Houses/House Silva]], captain of the freighter known as [[Characters/NPCs/Vehicles/The Chickenhawk]]. He was running weapons from the Naesock Processing Hub to the Talons of Freedom on Vantage when things went sideways. An explosion ripped through the deal, and Lucas caught shrapnel — surviving, but barely.

The Talons seized his ship in the aftermath, leaving Lucas stranded and terrified on Vantage. He can be found drowning his fears at the Waypoint Bar, looking over his shoulder at every loud noise. His ship, [[Characters/NPCs/Vehicles/The Chickenhawk]], is registered under a false CID, which makes reclaiming it through legal channels impossible without exposing his smuggling operation.

Lucas is a man running out of time. If the PCs don't intervene to protect him, the Talons of Freedom will track him down, interrogate him for what he knows about Silva's supply chain, and kill him. He's desperate enough to cooperate with anyone who offers safety, but too paranoid to trust easily.
