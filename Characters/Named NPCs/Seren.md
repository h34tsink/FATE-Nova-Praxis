# Seren

**Summary:** [[Talons of Freedom]] network contact — a cautious, transactional intermediary who trades movement channels and resupply paths for proof of trust and hard currency.

## Lore

Seren works the network side of the [[Talons of Freedom]] — not a fighter, not a leader, but the kind of contact every insurgent organisation needs: someone who knows which routes are open this week, which couriers are still loyal, and which safehouses haven't been burned. Her loyalty to the Talon network is non-negotiable, but her loyalty to any specific operator is gated behind proof. She operates on the principle that everyone pays — currency is optional, but everyone pays *something*.

She suspects more than she says. She knows that multiple factions are currently converging on the [[Valare]] hunt and that the political weather around Talon operations is shifting fast. She does not know — and does not want to know — the command-level political agreements above her cell. Her safehouse coordinates and defense capacity are the things she will lie about, deflect about, and disappear about before discussing.

To the PCs she is a leverage point: they can buy a lead from her, but they cannot buy unconditional access. The first time they take a route she gives them, she expects them to burn it after.
