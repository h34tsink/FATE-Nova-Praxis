# Tarj McClaren

**Summary:** Centurion of [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis]] putting pressure on apostates in Asola.

## Lore

Tarj McClaren is a Centurion operating under [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis]], assigned to Asola where he puts pressure on the local apostate community — specifically Kestrel's people. He represents the Coalition's law enforcement presence in a region where the line between Protectorate authority and apostate self-governance is constantly contested.

McClaren's current focus is a Pure apostate named Jebediah, whom he believes is selling the drug Symmetry. Whether this belief is well-founded or a pretext for broader crackdowns on apostate activity is left to the GM's interpretation, but McClaren pursues the lead with the dogged persistence of a man who believes he is doing the right thing.

The tension McClaren creates between Protectorate authority and apostate communities is a microcosm of the larger political dynamics in the Nova Praxis setting. He is not necessarily a villain — he may genuinely be trying to stop a dangerous drug trade — but his methods and mandate put him in direct conflict with the PCs' apostate contacts and allies.
