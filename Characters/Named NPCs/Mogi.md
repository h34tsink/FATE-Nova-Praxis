# Mogi

**Summary:** [[Factions/Houses/House Jinzhan]] representative. Pure. Murdered by [[Traeger|The Viking]].

## Lore

Mogi — a nickname meaning "Mosquito" in Korean — is a [[Factions/Houses/House Jinzhan]] representative who becomes entangled in a deadly chain of events. He initially met with [[Traeger|The Viking]] to arrange passage through the Earth quarantine, a transaction that placed him in direct contact with one of the system's most dangerous operatives.

Later, Mogi has a change of heart — or perhaps a crisis of conscience — and comes forward to the Protectorate about the dealings. He arranges to meet the PCs in Stygia, a virtual space, to share what he knows. This decision costs him his life.

The Viking tracks Mogi down and kills him while he is logged into Stygia via ego channel. The method is particularly horrific: because Mogi was connected through his ego channel, the murder happens to his digital self — a violation that carries unique existential weight in a transhuman society. As a Pure individual, Mogi's death is final in a way that Sleeved or SIM deaths are not, making his murder both a narrative tragedy and a statement about the Viking's brutality.
