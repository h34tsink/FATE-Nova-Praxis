# Kestrel

**Summary:** [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura]] Red Sun operative and former apostate sympathizer — the talent acquisition specialist who put the original crew on the contract that created [[Chimera]], now hunting [[Isabella Nowak]] to bury the evidence.

## Lore

Kestrel works the seam between [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura]]'s official channels and the [[Apostates|apostate]] underground. The Machinations source describes him as a "Kimuran Astralist and apostate sympathizer" based out of [[Asola]], operating as a talent acquisition specialist for several clients. His job is to find Auxiliaries for jobs that the Houses cannot be seen authorizing directly — and to handle them quietly when those jobs go wrong.

He is the man who handed the crew the salvage contract from [[Expanse Worldsystems]]. Per the contract terms, he was forbidden from disclosing that the real client was [[Isabella Nowak]] of [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya]]. He arranged the standard pre-mission backup, briefed the PCs on the [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura]] outpost they were sweeping, and then waited for them to come back. When four days passed without contact, he reported the silence to Nowak, who declared the crew dead. He believed her.

He was wrong. The crew was not dead — they had been ego-merged into [[Chimera]] by [[Dr. Haruto Nakamura]]. Now Kestrel is on the cleanup detail himself: Kimura wants every loose thread connected to [[Opportunity Four]] severed, and Nowak is at the top of the list. He is moving on [[Sessions/Session 9/Part 3 - Civil Unrest/The Nowak Situation|Icarus Hub]] with Red Suns in tow, on a fixed timetable that ticks regardless of what the PCs do.

The complication: Kestrel still has divided loyalties. His apostate-sympathizer history is real, and he is uncomfortable with how much collateral the cleanup is generating. Pushed correctly, he might offer an off-ramp — but never one that publicly betrays Kimura command.
