# Declan "Patch" Yuen

**Summary:** Docking bay mechanic on [[Locations/Asteroid Belts/Asteroids/Cere|Cere]]. Sees every ship that arrives. Remembers hulls, not politics.

## Lore

Declan Yuen — universally called "Patch" — is the only full-time mechanic in Cere's main docking bay. He patches hull damage, refuels ships, and runs post-dock diagnostics for anyone willing to pay or barter. He's been doing this for nine years. Before Cere, he worked a Kimura shipyard; before that, a Silva freighter. He doesn't talk about why he left either job.

Patch is aggressively apolitical. He doesn't care about factions, conspiracies, or who's hiding from whom. He cares about ships. He can describe every vessel that's docked in the last month — hull configuration, engine wear, what repairs they needed, how long they stayed. He remembers mechanical details the way other people remember faces.

He's useful to the PCs not because he knows anything secret, but because he's an honest, reliable witness to traffic patterns. If they need to know what ships have come and gone, or whether a particular vessel is still on Cere, Patch is the fastest answer.
