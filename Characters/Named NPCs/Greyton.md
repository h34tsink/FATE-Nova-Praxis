# Greyton

**Summary:** Legendary ancient assassin whose memories may pre-date the Exodus. Part of a mythical assassin clan.

## Lore

Greyton is a name whispered in the darkest corners of Coalition space — a rumor to most, a death sentence to those who know better. Clad in a gray skull cybersleeve with featureless black eyes, Greyton is the most feared assassin in known space. The name may refer to one impossibly ancient individual, a series of forks sharing an identity, or an entire clan of killers operating under a single legend. Nobody knows the truth, and Greyton prefers it that way.

What is known — or rather, what is suspected — is that Greyton's memories pre-date the Exodus itself, making the entity one of the oldest continuous consciousnesses in existence. A head full of lifetimes has honed skills to a razor edge. Greyton operates with a simple creed: "For the Clan!" and an absolute conviction that the clan has never been, and never shall be, defeated.

Greyton is an [[Factions/Ideologies/Apostates|Apostate]], operating outside the House system entirely. The cybersleeve is a forgotten design — a Gamma-class synthetic body that predates modern manufacturing standards, further deepening the mystery of Greyton's true origins. One shot is always enough.
