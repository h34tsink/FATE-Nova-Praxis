# Briar "Silvermist" Blackwood

Seasoned acquisitions specialist and grey-market fixer. Sharp wit, ruthless negotiation tactics, and a reputation as one of the best in the business. She operates out of the [[Locations/Mercurian Tunnel System|Mercurian Tunnel System]] and has connections across Coalition and Apostate networks.

5'9", short jet-black hair, piercing green eyes, lean muscular build. Usual attire: sleek black jumpsuit with various pockets and compartments. Moves like someone who expects to need an exit in a hurry.

Primary contact for [[Characters/Players/Dustin (Grift) Halloway/Dustin (Grift) Halloway - FATE Character|Grift]]. Their relationship is transactional — she brings jobs, he delivers, and her favors always come with strings attached.

## Lore

Silvermist grew up in the slums of a large space station and learned to fend for herself from a young age. She discovered a talent for acquiring goods and information, which led her to a career as an acquisitions specialist. She's worked for wealthy corporations, shady criminal organizations, and everything in between — building a reputation as one of the best in the business.

She operates on a simple principle: everyone needs something, and she can get it. The price is never just credits. It's favors, information, access — currency that appreciates over time. Her network spans Coalition and Apostate space, and she trades on relationships the way [[Characters/Players/Kallius/Kallius - FATE Character|Kallius]] trades on memories.

### Relationship with Grift

Briar pulled Grift out of a bad situation early in his post-cryo life and has been collecting on that debt ever since. She brings him jobs, introduces him to contacts, and occasionally shields him from consequences — but nothing is free. Every favor has interest. Grift knows this, tolerates it, and sometimes wonders if she's the closest thing he has to a friend. She'd say the same, and mean it, and still leverage it if she had to.

### GM Notes

- **Compel hook:** *Never Lets Go of Leverage* — she knows things about the PCs that she hasn't used yet. When she does, it won't be cruel. It'll be business.
- **Plot hook:** Briar sent the crew to the Kimura facility in [[Sessions/Session 0/Session 0 - Teaching Scenario (FATE Migration)|Session 0]]. Who is *her* buyer? Does she know what's in the sublevel, or was she played too?
- **Voice:** Casual warmth over cold calculation. She calls people "sweetheart" right before she names her price. Never raises her voice. Never bluffs — every threat is a promise she intends to keep.
