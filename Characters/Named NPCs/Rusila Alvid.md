# Rusila Alvid

**Summary:** Pirate leader operating out of New Turin. Key target in Campaign Arc 3.

## Lore

Rusila Alvid leads a pirate faction from an abandoned materials-processing plant in New Turin. She is a significant target during Campaign Arc 3, and her capture has cascading consequences for the rest of the campaign.

Alvid spends much of her time jacked into virtualities rather than dealing with the gritty realities of her operation in person. She is a regular at the Rusty Jug Pub in Chicago — a virtual space, not the ruined city — where she conducts business and socializes with contacts across the system. This preference for virtual presence makes her both elusive and vulnerable, depending on how the PCs approach her.

When captured, Alvid proves to be the key that unlocks the next phase of the campaign. She gives up contacts within [[Factions/Hidden Agendas/House Turin]], revealing that remnant forces are hiding in the Oort Cloud. This intelligence is the critical transition point between Campaign Arcs 3 and 4, making Alvid's capture — alive and cooperative — essential to the larger narrative.
