# Udo Grein

**Summary:** Deep shaft scavenger on [[Locations/Asteroid Belts/Asteroids/Cere|Cere]]. Knows the tunnels better than anyone alive. Has found signs of someone hiding down there.

## Lore

Udo Grein has been scavenging Cere's abandoned mining shafts for over a decade. He strips old equipment, recovers usable materials, maps unstable sections, and sells route knowledge to anyone who needs to go deeper than the public areas. He lives alone in a pressurized alcove at the junction of shafts four and seven, and he prefers it that way.

Udo is not hostile — just deeply territorial and conditioned by years of solitary work in dangerous terrain. He knows which tunnels are stable, where the toxic gas pockets accumulate, where the old mining equipment can be salvaged, and where the rock is too fractured to support foot traffic. He navigates by memory, sound, and vibration. He doesn't trust sensors — they lie underground.

Recently, Udo has noticed signs that someone else is living in the deep galleries. Power taps on old mining conduits. Air vents scrubbed cleaner than the ambient baseline. Debris cleared from passages that haven't been used in years. He hasn't found the person — whoever it is knows how to stay hidden — but the signs are unmistakable to someone who reads the shafts the way Udo does.

He hasn't reported this to anyone. It's not his business. But if someone offered the right price — or the right reason — he could narrow the search area considerably.
