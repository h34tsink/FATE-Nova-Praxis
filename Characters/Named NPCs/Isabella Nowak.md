# Isabella Nowak

**Summary:** High-ranking [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya]] executive and head of [[Expanse Worldsystems]] — the woman who commissioned the salvage contract that led to the creation of [[Chimera]].

## Lore

Isabella Nowak runs [[Expanse Worldsystems]], a [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya]] subsidiary specialising in deep-space salvage and reclamation contracts. She is procedural, transactional, and politically careful — the kind of executive who survives by ensuring every catastrophic decision is filed under "the least bad option" with paperwork to match.

Her name lives on the contract that started everything. Nowak hired [[Kestrel]] as a cut-out to recruit an Auxiliary crew for what she described as a routine sweep of an apparently abandoned [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura]] outpost. The catch — required by the contract — was that her identity be hidden from the operators. The outpost was not abandoned. It was Mindscapes' illegal mnemonics lab, run by [[Dr. Haruto Nakamura]]. The crew that took her contract were ego-merged into [[Chimera]], and when Kestrel went four days without contact, Nowak declared them dead and the mission a wash.

What Nowak knew at the time of the contract — versus what she suspected, or scrubbed afterward — is the central question the PCs are racing to ask her face-to-face during the [[Sessions/Session 9/Part 3 - Civil Unrest/The Nowak Situation|Icarus Hub mutiny]]. Her own people are turning on her, [[Kestrel]] is moving to erase her as a liability, and forged digital traces are framing the PCs for the uprising. She is willing to bargain — coordinates for [[Opportunity Four]], full chain of custody, even a name-clearing — in exchange for one thing: extraction.

Nowak is not a combatant. Her weapons are documentation, deniability, and the institutional reflex of [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya]] to protect its executives until the moment it doesn't.
