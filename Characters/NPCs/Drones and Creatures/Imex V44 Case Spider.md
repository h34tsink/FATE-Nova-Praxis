# Imex V44 Case Spider

**Summary:** Beta infiltration drone disguised as a briefcase. Transforms into a spider-like surveillance and combat platform.

## Lore

The Imex V44 Case Spider is a marvel of covert engineering. In its dormant state it appears to be an ordinary briefcase, but when activated it unfolds into a multi-legged spider drone capable of wall-crawling, surveillance, and limited combat engagement. Favored by intelligence operatives and corporate spies for its concealability and versatility.

## Mechanics

**Type:** Beta Drone (Restricted)
**Cost:** 5
**Size:** -2

### Aspects

- Synthetic Biology(P)
- Inhuman Appearance
- Cyberspider Drone
- Quality Visual Sensors

### Skills

**Sleeve Skills:** Athletics 3, Endurance 1, Perception 3
**Other Skills:** Firearms 1, Melee 1, Resolve 2, Stealth 3, Strategy 2

### Augmentations

- Fully Synthetic
- Custom Built (Suitcase Form, Stealth Enhancements)
- Alternative Mobility System (Wall-Crawling Spider Legs)
- Hidden Weapon System (Piercing Spike)
- Very Tiny Size (Size -2)

### Combat Stats

**Physical Defense:** AR: 2 *(+2 on Athletics-based defense tests due to size)*

**Physical Attacks:**

- **Heavy Rail-Pistol** WR: 4 *(Armor Penetrating, Small; reduce WR by -2 for Silent mode)*
- **Piercing Spike** WR: 2 *(Small)*

## GM Notes

Folds into an inconspicuous briefcase for transport. Stealth 3 makes it excellent for infiltration. Size -2 gives +2 to Athletics defense. The back-mounted railgun packs surprising punch for its size. Perfect for spy and recon scenarios. Can be deployed as an advance scout or left behind as a surveillance asset.
