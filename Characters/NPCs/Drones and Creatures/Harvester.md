# Harvester

**Summary:** Delta drone designed to sever heads and collect mnemonic cores. Flying beetle-like hunter.

## Lore

Less than half a meter long, the Harvester resembles a large flying beetle with three gravitic flight pods, scythe-like mandibles, and a plastirope tether with a serrated barb. Designed for a singular horrific purpose: severing heads and collecting the mnemonic cores within. These drones operate autonomously, hunting targets with sonar and closing in with terrifying speed.

## Mechanics

**Type:** Delta Drone (Restricted)
**Cost:** 9
**Size:** -1

### Aspects

- Synthetic Biology(P)
- Inhuman Appearance
- Flying Head Hunter
- Quick and Agile
- Gravitic Flight Pods
- Sonar Reception Array

### Skills

**Sleeve Skills:** Athletics 3, Endurance 3, Perception 2
**Other Skills:** Melee 5, Resolve 2, Strategy 2

### Augmentations

- **Head Hunter** — Any target Taken Out is automatically decapitated; Fate Points cannot prevent this
- Fully Synthetic
- Optimized Power Systems
- Armed (Mandibles and Tether Spear)
- Alternative Mobility System (Gravitic Flight Pods)
- Sonar Reception Array

### Combat Stats

**Physical Defense:** AR: 4

**Physical Attacks:**

- **Mono-Edge Mandibles** WR: 5 *(Armor Penetrating)*
- **Tether Spear** WR: 3 *(Inflicts "Tethered" Aspect; Athletics Diff:3 to escape)*

## GM Notes

The Head Hunter augmentation is brutal — any target Taken Out is automatically decapitated. Fate Points cannot prevent this. Use sparingly for horror and threat. The Tether Spear pins targets in place (Athletics Diff:3 to break free) while the mandibles finish the job. Pairs well with multiple Harvesters swarming a single target.
