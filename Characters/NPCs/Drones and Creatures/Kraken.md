# Kraken

**Summary:** Epsilon combat drone — a horrific mass of tentacles designed for close-quarters killing.

## Lore

Developed by the Federation pre-Exodus for clearing buildings and bunkers, the Kraken is a purpose-built killing machine designed to hunt and destroy in confined quarters. A nightmare of tentacles bristling with bladed talons and particle accelerator weapons, it represents the apex of pre-Collapse drone warfare. Extremely rare in the post-Exodus era — a relic of a more desperate time.

## Mechanics

**Type:** Epsilon Drone (Restricted)
**Cost:** 12
**Size:** 1

### Aspects

- Synthetic Biology(P)
- Inhuman Appearance
- Horrid Mass of Tentacles and Death
- Dedicated Murder Drone
- Massive
- Eight Deadly Tentacles
- Beholder Sensory Suite(P)
- Purpose-Built Killing Machine

### Skills

**Sleeve Skills:** Athletics 4, Endurance 4, Perception 3
**Other Skills:** Firearms 3, Melee 3, Resolve 2, Strategy 1

### Augmentations

- **Multi-Attacker** — One additional free attack action per turn
- Fully Synthetic
- Optimized Power Systems x2
- Armed (Mono-Edge Talons and PAc Tentacles)
- Alternative Mobility System (Tentacles)
- Extra Limb
- Beholder Sensory Suite
- Custom Built

### Combat Stats

**Physical Defense:** AR: 4

**Physical Attacks:**

- **Mono-Edge Talons x3** WR: 5 *(Armor Penetrating)*
- **PAc Tentacles x3** WR: 5 *(Beam Weapon, Particle Accelerator)*

## GM Notes

Multi-Attacker gives it TWO attack actions per turn. With six weapon systems (3 talon + 3 PAc), it can engage multiple targets simultaneously. WR:5 with Armor Penetrating on the talons makes it lethal in melee. Federation-era relic — extremely rare. Use as a boss-level threat. Consider giving it a dramatic entrance and allowing PCs time to plan before engagement.
