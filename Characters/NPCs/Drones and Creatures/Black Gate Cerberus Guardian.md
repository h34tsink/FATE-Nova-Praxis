# Black Gate Cerberus Guardian

**Summary:** Gamma security drone shaped like a large skinless dog. Autonomous patrol and guard platform.

## Lore

Standing about one meter at the shoulder, the Black Gate Cerberus Guardian is a canine-inspired security drone with a deliberately intimidating skinless appearance — shiny black synthetic musculature exposed beneath a transparent outer shell. The Series III Cerberus can run faster than most land craft. Widely deployed for facility security, perimeter patrol, and high-value asset protection.

## GM Notes

About 1 meter at the shoulder. Skinless appearance with shiny black synthetic musculature — deliberately intimidating. AR 6 makes it very tough. Low WR:2 bite but Melee 4 means it hits reliably and can generate spin/advantages easily. Neura-Surge lets it take an extra action at cost. Excellent for facility security encounters. One of the sample characters, Christov Draiku, owns one of these.
