# Ardent Lonestar Armed Responder

**Summary:** Gamma humanoid security drone. Two meters tall, heavily armored, equipped with military-grade weapons.

## Lore

The Ardent Lonestar Armed Responder is the corporate security workhorse — a two-meter-tall humanoid combat drone bristling with armor plating and carrying a heavy coil assault rifle. Its humanoid form allows it to use cover, operate in human-scale environments, and wield standard weapons. Bearing its employer's corporate sigil prominently, the Lonestar serves as both security asset and visible deterrent. Commonly deployed in corporate lobbies, spaceports, and high-security facilities.

## GM Notes

The corporate security workhorse. AR 7 + WR:7 makes this a serious threat. Voice-Stress Analyzer lets it detect lies during interrogation — useful for checkpoint and access-control scenes. Humanoid form allows it to use cover and standard weapons. Often seen in corporate lobbies, spaceports, and high-security facilities. Deploy in pairs or squads for credible corporate security encounters.
