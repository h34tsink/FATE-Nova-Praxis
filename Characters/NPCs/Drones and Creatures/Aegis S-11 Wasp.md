# Aegis S-11 Wasp

**Summary:** Alpha personal defense drone shaped like a synthetic wasp. Hovers near its owner as a bodyguard.

## Lore

About one-third of a meter long, the Aegis S-11 Wasp is a compact personal security drone that hovers near its owner, providing autonomous threat detection and response. Shaped like a synthetic wasp with translucent wings, it is small enough to be unobtrusive but packs a surprisingly lethal particle accelerator pistol. A common sight accompanying corporate executives and high-value individuals.

## Mechanics

**Type:** Alpha Drone (Restricted)
**Cost:** 4
**Size:** -2

### Aspects

- Synthetic Biology(P)
- Inhuman Appearance
- Cyberwasp Drone
- Basic Physical Security Programming

### Skills

**Sleeve Skills:** Athletics 1, Endurance 1, Perception 3
**Other Skills:** Firearms 2, Resolve 1, Stealth 3, Strategy 3

### Augmentations

- Fully Synthetic
- Alternative Mobility System (Flight Capable, Wasp Wings)
- Very Tiny Size (Size -2)

### Combat Stats

**Physical Defense:** AR: 2 *(+2 on Athletics-based defense tests due to size)*

**Physical Attacks:**

- **Light PAc Pistol** WR: 4 *(Particle Accelerator, Small)*

## GM Notes

About 1/3 meter long. Hovers near its owner as a personal security drone. Strategy 3 means it is reasonably smart about threat assessment. WR:4 PAc pistol is surprisingly lethal for such a small platform. Makes a good "bonus action" companion for important NPCs — it shoots back when its owner is attacked.
