# Ardent Lonestar Armed Responder — Rail Variant (LS-7R)

A field-modified variant of the standard [[Ardent Lonestar Armed Responder]] (LS-7), swapping the Heavy Coil Assault Rifle for a [[Glossary/Heavy Assault Rail-Rifle]]. Deployed against hardened targets where armor penetration consistency matters more than raw weapon rating.

## Lore

The LS-7R designation is unofficial — a loadout swap, not a separate model. [[Ardent]] field teams swap the coil platform for the rail rifle when facing targets with powered armor or vehicle-class threats. Common in anti-[[Centurion]] contingency operations and station defense against armored boarders.
