# Honey Badger

**Summary:** Size 3 light assault warframe. [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya]]-designed bipedal combat walker.

## Lore

The Honey Badger is a light assault warframe designed by [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya]] as a direct assault platform. A bipedal combat walker bristling with weapons, it is not subtle, not pretty, and loud as hell. Heavily armored for frontal assault and mounting three separate weapon systems, the Honey Badger is designed to walk into a fight and end it decisively. What it gains in raw firepower and durability, it sacrifices entirely in speed and maneuverability.

## GM Notes

Slow (Speed 0, Maneuverability 0) but devastating. Three weapon systems all at WR:7-8 mean it can obliterate most targets. AR 5 + Reinforced Structure makes it a tank. "Don't ask her to pivot" can be compelled for flanking maneuvers — smart PCs will exploit its inability to turn quickly. A terrifying presence on the battlefield. Consider giving PCs advance warning so they can plan around its weaknesses rather than face it head-on.
