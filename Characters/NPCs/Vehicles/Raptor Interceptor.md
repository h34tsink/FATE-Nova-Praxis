# Raptor Interceptor

**Summary:** Size 2 short-range fighter drone built by [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis]] to protect otherwise defenseless ships.

## Lore
The Raptor is a Dalianis-designed interceptor that can be crewed or operated autonomously via its onboard drone pilot AI. It trades durability for offensive punch — a glass cannon built to hit hard and fast. Commonly deployed in pairs from carrier ships or orbital stations.

## GM Notes
Can be crewed or drone-piloted. The AI pilot has effective Pilot 2 and Firearms 2 when unmanned. "Long-Range Sensors Leave Much to be Desired" can be compelled to let PCs sneak past or ambush them. Low AR:2 means they go down fast if hit — true glass cannons. Deploy in pairs for a challenging encounter; three or more for a deadly one.
