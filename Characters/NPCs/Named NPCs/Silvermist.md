# Briar Blackwood (Silvermist)

## Background
Briar Blackwood, known in the underworld as **Silvermist**, is a notorious fixer who orchestrates high-stakes covert operations. With her distinctive silver hair that shimmers like moonlight and piercing gray eyes, she cuts an enigmatic figure in the shadows of corporate and criminal worlds alike.

Her reputation is built on meticulous planning, strategic brilliance, and an extensive network that spans both underworld contacts and corporate insiders. Silvermist is known for always being two steps ahead, speaking with calm confidence, and having zero tolerance for failure. Those who work with her either prove themselves useful and reliable, or quickly find themselves on the wrong end of her considerable influence.

Her high-tech elegant attire, often featuring smart fabrics that adapt to surroundings, reflects both her sophistication and practical approach to her dangerous profession.

## Campaign Role
Silvermist serves as a high-level contact, mission provider, or information broker. She can provide access to restricted areas, arrange meetings with dangerous individuals, or supply specialized equipment—for the right price. Her history with the characters makes her a complex ally who remembers past favors and slights.

## GM Notes
**Personality:** Calm, commanding, and always in control. Speaks precisely and economically. Respects competence and abhors incompetence. Never reveals more than necessary but always knows more than she says.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Access to corporate boardrooms and criminal hideouts
- Real-time intelligence on major operations
- Can arrange complex heists, extractions, or infiltrations
- Extensive blackmail material on important figures

**Tactical Notes:** Avoids direct confrontation, preferring to work through intermediaries. Her power lies in information, connections, and careful preparation rather than personal combat ability.

**Plot Hooks:**
- Offers lucrative contracts requiring specialized skills
- Provides critical information in exchange for future favors
- Tests character competence with increasingly complex jobs
- Her network becomes compromised, requiring character assistance
- Rival fixers target her operations
