# The Fallen - Death

**State:** [[SIM]]  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** The Fallen *(Rogue Savant Collective)*  
**Rep:** N/A *(Operates beyond normal society)*

## Background
Death is one of **The Fallen**, a rogue **[[Savant]]** representing humanity's fascination with ending and erasure. Unlike War's overt violence or Conquest's systematic control, Death specializes in silent elimination and the complete obliteration of memory and identity. Their **[[SIM]]** state allows them to exist as living shadow, infiltrating systems and manifesting as **Formless Darkness** that devours light and hope.

Suffering from severe **Fragmentation Psychosis** and a compulsive desire to kill, Death has become obsessed with the concept of final endings. Their most terrifying ability is the **Touch of the Grave**—physical contact that wipes **mnemonic cores**, effectively erasing a person's memories and personality. Victims survive physically but lose themselves completely, requiring days of restoration from backups or extensive memory editing.

Death manifests wielding an **Infused Simulated Mono-Edged Scythe**, a weapon that exists partially in digital space and can cut through both matter and data. They move as living darkness, immune to conventional harm and driven by an inexorable need to bring final silence to all consciousness.

## Campaign Role
Death serves as a stealth assassin among The Fallen, eliminating key figures and spreading terror through memory erasure. They're the threat that strikes from shadows, leaving victims alive but hollow.

## GM Notes
**Personality:** Coldly obsessed with endings and erasure. Speaks little, preferring to let actions convey their message. Views memory and identity as illusions to be stripped away.

**Tactical Approach:**
- Infiltrates through digital networks as living shadow
- Eliminates targets through stealth and memory erasure
- Uses system blackouts and disruption to create chaos
- Manifests as formless darkness to resist conventional attacks
- Targets key memories and identities rather than just physical destruction

**Key Abilities:**
- **Touch of the Grave:** Wipes memories on contact
- **Formless Darkness:** Enhanced armor and status immunity
- **Shadow Infiltration:** Moves unseen through networks
- **System Disruption:** Blackouts and power failures

**Plot Hooks:**
- Targets important figures for memory erasure
- Infiltrates secure systems as living shadow
- Leaves victims alive but identity-wiped
- Creates fear through stealth assassinations
- Seeks to erase specific knowledge or memories
