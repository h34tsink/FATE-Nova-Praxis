# The Keeper

**State:** Unknown *(Likely enhanced [[Biosleeve]] or [[SIM]])*

**Allegiance:** Independent *(Guardian of Ancient Knowledge)*  
**Rep:** N/A *(Operates outside normal society)*

## Background
The Keeper is a guardian of ancient knowledge and secrets in Nova Praxis's high-tech world. This rare individual values and protects information lost or hidden from the rest of civilization. Cloaked in garments blending old-world mysticism with modern technology, they carry an air of deep mystery.

Reserved and cryptic, The Keeper speaks in riddles and parables, believing knowledge must be earned rather than freely given. Their exceptional intelligence, combined with skills in cryptography and ancient languages, makes them a formidable protector of secrets. They use advanced technology to conceal and safeguard information from those who would misuse it.

## Campaign Role
The Keeper serves as a source of crucial ancient knowledge and lost secrets. Characters must prove their worthiness through tests and riddles to gain access to information that could change the course of their mission.

## GM Notes
**Personality:** Cryptic, patient, and deeply wise. Never gives direct answers - everything is a test or lesson.
