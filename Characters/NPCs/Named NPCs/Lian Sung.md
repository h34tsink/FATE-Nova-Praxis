# Lian Sung

**Summary:** [[Factions/Houses/House Jinzhan]] representative on Titan. Handles PC logistics for the Enceladus mission.

## Lore

Lian Sung is a [[Factions/Houses/House Jinzhan]] representative stationed on Titan, serving as the PCs' logistics coordinator for the Enceladus mission. She is in her mid-sixties but appears to be in her early-to-mid-thirties — Pure by choice, but augmented with discreet biotech enhancements that blur the line between natural aging and technological intervention.

Sung handles the practical details of the mission: setting the PCs up with apartments on Titan, arranging for their gear, and securing a ship for the journey to Enceladus. She presents Titan as safe and secure, far removed from the [[Talons of Freedom]] ambushes that plague other regions of the system. Whether this confidence is fully justified remains to be seen.

When the PCs' equipment is ready, Sung instructs them to meet her at an unused docking bay — a quiet, out-of-the-way location that suggests Jinzhan prefers to keep this operation off the public radar. She is professional, efficient, and focused on getting the team deployed without unnecessary complications.
