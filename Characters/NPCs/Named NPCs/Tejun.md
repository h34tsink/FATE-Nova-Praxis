# Tejun

**Summary:** [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura]] SIM advisor to the Scholae Palatinae.

## Lore

Tejun is a SIM — a purely digital consciousness — serving as advisor to the Scholae Palatinae on matters of public relations, law, and politics. Assigned by [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura]], Tejun functions as the voice of The Coalition within the Scholae team, ensuring that operations remain within acceptable political and legal boundaries.

More pointedly, Tejun serves as [[Admiral Drivas]]' eyes and ears among the Scholae operatives. Loyalty flows upward to the Tetragon first and to the PCs second — a priority that Tejun does not attempt to hide. If the PCs step outside their mandate or make politically inconvenient choices, Tejun will note it and report it.

Tejun openly dislikes and distrusts [[Concetta Allegro]], viewing her [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani]] connections and off-the-books methods as liabilities. This interpersonal friction creates tension within the Scholae team and forces the PCs to navigate competing loyalties among their own support staff.
