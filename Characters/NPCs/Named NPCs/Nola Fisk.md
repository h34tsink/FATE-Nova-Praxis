# Nola Fisk

**Summary:** Shift supervisor on [[Locations/Asteroid Belts/Asteroids/Cere|Cere's]] Anapos Station. Corporate employee with a quiet conscience.

## Lore

Nola Fisk has been a shift supervisor on Anapos Station for four years. She manages crew rotations, shipping manifests, and station maintenance schedules. On paper, she's a model corporate employee — clean reports, no incidents, no complaints. In practice, she's the reason the Refugee Quarters have been getting extra oxygen rations for the past eighteen months.

Nola grew up on a belt station that failed. She remembers what it felt like when the air got thin and corporate didn't send help fast enough. She took the Anapos posting because it was stable, but Cere changed her. She sees how the corporate layer operates — skimming resources, underreporting population, treating the unofficial residents as a problem to be managed rather than people to be served — and she's quietly decided to push back in the only way she can: small acts of resource redistribution buried in maintenance reports.

She's not a rebel. She doesn't want to burn her career or make a political statement. She wants the reports to be accurate, the station to run safely, and the people in the lower levels to breathe. If the PCs need access to Anapos, shipping records, or crew information, Nola can provide it — but only if she believes they won't create the kind of incident that ends up in an audit.
