# Aaron Blackburn

**Summary:** True leader of the [[Talons of Freedom]], operating under deep cover as a Legate of [[Factions/Houses/House Silva]].

## Lore

Aaron Blackburn is one of the most dangerous people in the Coalition, and almost no one knows it. On the surface, he is a Legate of [[Factions/Houses/House Silva]] — a high-ranking official within the House structure, trusted and respected. In reality, he is the founder and true leader of the [[Talons of Freedom]], the most significant anti-Coalition insurgent organization in existence.

Blackburn's origin story is a masterclass in deception. The Tetragon tasked him with eliminating remnant United States military forces that had survived the [[Glossary/Exodus|Exodus]] — holdouts who refused to submit to the new House-based order. Blackburn was chosen because of his credentials and loyalty. What the Tetragon did not know was that Blackburn was himself a loyalist to the old order.

Rather than destroying the remnants, Blackburn staged their elimination. He fabricated evidence of their destruction while secretly recruiting them into a new organization: the Talons of Freedom. From his position within House Silva, he leads the Talons from afar through a cell structure managed by three lieutenants, none of whom know the full scope of his identity or plans.

Blackburn's ultimate goal is a strike at Luna — the seat of Coalition power. His true identity is one of the most closely guarded secrets in the setting, and its exposure would send shockwaves through every House.
