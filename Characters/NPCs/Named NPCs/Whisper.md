# Whisper

**State:** Unknown *(Likely enhanced [[Biosleeve]] for covert operations)*  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** Independent Black Market Dealer  
**Rep:** Underground *(Known only to criminal networks)*

## Background
Operating in the shadows of **[[Locations/Vantage Station]]**, Whisper is a black market dealer who specializes in restricted items and illegal services. Known for conducting business exclusively in gold and favors, Whisper maintains a careful network of contacts and informants that keeps them one step ahead of Coalition security forces.

Whisper's reputation is built on discretion, quality forgeries, and access to items that are impossible to obtain through legal channels. Whether you need false CIDs for Apostates, restricted augmentations, or information on upcoming security sweeps, Whisper can provide—for the right price. Their network includes unlicensed technicians, corrupt officials, and desperate individuals willing to trade information for survival.

The dealer's ability to access restricted areas of the station and provide high-quality contraband has made them an essential, if dangerous, contact for anyone operating outside the law on Vantage.

## Campaign Role
Whisper serves as a black market contact for acquiring restricted items, false documentation, and sensitive information. They're essential for characters operating outside the law but dealing with them carries significant risks of Coalition attention.

## GM Notes
