# Castor

**Summary:** Crew member aboard the Meatlocker, partner and handler of [[Traeger|The Viking]].

## Lore

Castor is the brains behind [[Traeger|The Viking]]'s operation, serving as partner, handler, and crew member aboard the Meatlocker. Where the Viking is raw violence and intimidation, Castor is calculation and contingency planning. He rarely leaves the Viking's side, functioning as both tactical advisor and the only person capable of talking Traeger down from a fight.

Castor carries a Fog of War CIST — a device that suppresses mesh connectivity in the immediate area, keeping fights off the public record and out of Protectorate surveillance. This makes any confrontation with the Viking-Castor pair significantly more dangerous, as backup and witnesses are effectively cut off.

His operational discipline extends to data extraction: Castor downloads harvester files and transmits them directly to his [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis]] superiors. He also carries a high-end remote backup module, meaning that even if killed, his resurrection comes with an up-to-the-minute sensie of everything that happened — making him nearly impossible to permanently silence. If the PCs kill Castor, he comes back knowing exactly who did it and how.
