## Background
Carmen Ashford is a brilliant engineer and **[[Technophage]]** researcher whose life changed forever during a catastrophic experiment. The accident left her unconscious for three days, during which she experienced a profound vision that convinced her she is destined to become humanity's savior—preventing the universe's heat death through radical technological intervention.

Her grand plan involves creating von Neumann-like self-replicating probes that will construct massive solar collector rings around Earth's sun. To gather the necessary materials, she intends to mine half of **[[Locations/Mercury]]**'s mass, eventually creating new planets for humanity to colonize. The first phase requires building auto-replicating nano swarms, but the **[[Technophage]]** catastrophe on **[[Locations/Earth]]** has made such technology strictly illegal.

Undeterred by legal restrictions, Carmen actively seeks **[[Technophage]]** samples to reverse engineer, believing the ends justify the means. Her unwavering conviction that she alone can save humanity makes her extremely dangerous—a person of genuine good intentions whose methods could destroy everything she claims to protect.
