# Sera Ivanova

**Summary:** Community organizer in [[Locations/Asteroid Belts/Asteroids/Cere|Cere's]] Refugee Quarters. [[Talons of Freedom]] sympathizer. Protects the people corporate forgot.

## Lore

Sera Ivanova arrived on Cere three years ago with a group of displaced workers from a collapsed habitat in the outer belt. She organized them into a functioning community in the Refugee Quarters — allocating space, managing resources, mediating disputes, and quietly building a network of mutual aid that extends into Cere's informal economy.

She's a Talon sympathizer, not a Talon operative. The distinction matters to her. She believes in what the Talons represent — freedom from Coalition control, dignity for the dispossessed — but she answers to the forty-odd people living in her section of the Refugee Quarters, not to any chain of command. She shelters Talon contacts when they pass through, shares information when it serves her community, and refuses to cooperate when it doesn't.

Sera knows that someone has been hiding in the deep mining shafts. She's noticed the signs — power consumption anomalies, air scrubber usage that doesn't match the official headcount, supplies moving through channels that bypass the Core Markets. She hasn't investigated because she respects the right to disappear. But she's watching, and she has opinions about whether the PCs should be poking around down there.
