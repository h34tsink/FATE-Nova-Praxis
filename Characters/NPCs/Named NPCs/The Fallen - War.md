# The Fallen - War

**State:** [[SIM]]  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** The Fallen *(Rogue Savant Collective)*  
**Rep:** N/A *(Operates beyond normal society)*

## Background
War is one of **The Fallen**, a group of rogue **[[Savants]]** representing humanity's darkest impulses. As the embodiment of conflict and violence, War drives others to battle through advanced psychological manipulation and viral programming. Their **[[SIM]]** state allows them to exist as pure information, infiltrating systems and controlling **SimCell-hosted** sleeves to spread chaos.

Suffering from severe **Fragmentation Psychosis**, War has become obsessed with inspiring armed conflict wherever they go. Their **Master of Puppets** ability allows them to temporarily seize control of any SimCell-hosted sleeve, replacing the owner's ego with their own warlike impulses. The **Red Rage** virus they spread forces victims to adopt **Bloodthirsty** and **Vengeful** behaviors, turning peaceful individuals into instruments of violence.

War's physical manifestations appear in **Powered Assault Armor**, wielding devastating weaponry and displaying inhuman combat prowess. They view conflict as the natural state of existence and work tirelessly to prove this philosophy by turning civilization against itself.

## Campaign Role
War serves as a major antagonist who escalates conflicts and turns allies against each other. They're the direct combat threat among The Fallen, using both overwhelming firepower and insidious digital manipulation to spread violence.
