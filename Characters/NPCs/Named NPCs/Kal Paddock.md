# Kal Paddock

A miner and information broker in Cere's Deepshaft Tavern. Kal is known for his connections, willingness to trade information for favors, and his ambiguous loyalties.

## Notable Traits
- Knows the ins and outs of Cere
- Willing to help for a price
- Distrustful of outsiders

## GM Notes
- Use Kal for scenes involving information exchange, negotiation, or as a wildcard NPC.
- Possible Aspects: *Information Has a Price*, *Distrustful Local*, *Wildcard Broker*.
