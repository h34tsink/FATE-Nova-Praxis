# Dr. Haruto Nakamura

**Summary:** Head of illegal mnemonics research at a Mindscapes outpost. Creator of [[Chimera]].

## Lore

Dr. Haruto Nakamura heads a remote outpost operated by Mindscapes, a subsidiary of [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura]], where he conducts experiments that would horrify even the most permissive ethics board in the Coalition. His specialty is ego-merging — the forced combination of multiple backed-up consciousnesses into a single entity. His masterwork is [[Chimera]], created by merging the PCs' own backed-up egos into a composite being without their knowledge or consent.

Nakamura is not a combatant. If violence breaks out at his facility, he takes cover or flees. His weapons are patience, curiosity, and the security systems of his outpost — the main hatch responds only to his CID, giving him leverage even when cornered. What he wants is simple: more time, more egos, more data. The ethics of his work do not concern him. The suffering of his subjects does not register. He is driven purely by intellectual curiosity pushed to its most amoral extreme.

Cold, methodical, and utterly without remorse, Nakamura represents the dark side of [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura]]'s obsession with the mind. Whether the House sanctions his work or merely looks the other way is a question worth asking — and one Nakamura will not answer.
