# The Summoner

**State:** [[SIM]] or Enhanced [[Cybersleeve]]  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** Independent *(Master of Digital Realms)*  
**Rep:** N/A *(Virtual world domination focus)*

## Background
The Summoner is a master of digital conjuring who manipulates Nova Praxis's virtual world at will, summoning constructs, data, and resources as needed. Often seen with various high-tech devices, their attire features digital interfaces and holographic displays that respond to their commands.

Charismatic and ambitious, The Summoner constantly seeks new challenges within the digital realm. Their proficiency in virtual reality manipulation, hacking, and digital creation allows them to summon digital constructs or reshape virtual environments to their advantage. They view the digital world as their domain to master and control.

- **Living Digital Interface** - *Surrounded by holographic displays and tech*
