# Kestrel

**State:** [[Biosleeve]] (Custom)  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura|House Kimura]] *(Red Sun operative)*  
**Rep:** 5

## Background
Kestrel has taken it upon himself to help the poor apostates of **[[Locations/Asola|Asola]]**. Using his contacts among the **Coalition** and apostates, Kestrel arranges deals between the two factions. He is often called upon by discreet employers to perform dirty jobs with crews recruited from both sides.

Though Kestrel does not fully understand the depth of the **Shadow War**, he is beginning to suspect that "fixers" like himself are more common than most believe. As a Red Sun operative working for House Kimura, he walks a careful line between his official duties and his genuine concern for apostate welfare.

## Motivation
Kestrel's primary concern is for himself, though he truly cares for the apostates he represents. He works with them to afford better lives, but if forced to choose, Kestrel will prioritize loyalty to House Kimura over the apostates or any other allies.

## Campaign Role
Kestrel serves as the perfect fixer and information broker - someone who can connect PCs with jobs from both official and unofficial sources. His dual loyalties create constant tension and opportunities for betrayal or redemption. He's valuable as both patron and potential enemy.

## GM Notes
- **Use for job offers** that require crossing factional lines
- **Internal conflict** between House loyalty and apostate sympathy creates dramatic tension
- **Information source** about both apostate underground and House operations
- **Potential betrayer** if loyalties are tested too hard
- **Excellent for moral dilemmas** - not clearly good or evil
