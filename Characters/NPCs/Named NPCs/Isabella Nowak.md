# Isabella Nowak

**State:** [[Biosleeve]] (Standard)  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya|House Tsarya]]  
**Rep:** 8

## Background
Isabella Nowak sits in the center of chaos, the eye of the storm. As **Executive Administrator of Planetary Logistics**, she is responsible for maintaining [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya|House Tsarya]]'s interests on the **Icarus Hub** in orbit around [[Locations/Exo Planets/Proch|Proch]].

Nowak feels she has more than proven herself at the Icarus Hub and has been maneuvering for a promotion. But just as things seemed to be going her way, it all fell apart. First, the crew hired to prospect the outpost went missing. Now, they seem to be undermining her by turning the **[[Groups/Apostate|apostate]] workforce** in the hub's docks against her.

Faced with a growing mutiny, Isabella is working desperately to regain control. In her desperation, she let **Lighthouse** off their leash.

## What Does Nowak Know?

### Discovery of the Outpost

- **Nowak** received word that the [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura|Kimuran]] outpost orbiting the new planet had **gone dark for over two months**
- She wanted the outpost checked for anything of interest on **Opportunity Four**
- **She didn't know the outpost was host to illegal mnemonics research** when she first sent the PCs
- **Being a Purist**, she ordered it **destroyed** when she found out about the illegal experiments

### The PC Mission

- **Nowak** got word of an abandoned [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura|House Kimura]] research station set up around a new planet (**Opportunity Four**). She knows where the station is.
- **[Secret: 7]** The location of the outpost.
- Suspecting hostile squatters or pirates, she hired an **expendable crew** (the PCs) to deal with them and secure the outpost.
- The **PCs were killed** during an attempt to take the outpost by force
- During the mission, she had **ordered them to sabotage the environmental systems**
- The PC crew went dark, so she **declared them dead**. Three months later, they were resurrected.

### Data Purge and Cover-Up

- Soon after declaring the PCs dead, **Nowak** discovered that records of the crew's activities were being **purged from the [[Extranet]]**
- She was going to **ignore the dead PCs**, but when one of her aides told her that they had apparently begun **wiping much of their own personal data**, she feared what they were up to
- **Of course, it was [[Characters/NPCs/Chimera|Chimera]] wiping the data**, but Nowak didn't know this
- Worried about the reason for the data purge, and fearing implication in **illegal forking**, Nowak hired **[[Characters/NPCs/Named NPCs/Valare|Valare]]** to wipe out all security logs and sensor data of the crew from the last few months
- She wanted to eliminate as many links as possible between the crew and herself
- So she **contacted Valare and tasked her to scrub the mission** to cover the PCs' association with Nowak

### What She Doesn't Know

- She doesn't know that **Chimera** is responsible for the data wiping
- She doesn't know about **Dr. Haruto Nakamura** or the specifics of the illegal mnemonics experiments
- She doesn't know that **Chimera is actively working to destroy her** through the Talon uprising
- She doesn't know the full extent of **House Kimura's involvement** in the illegal research

## Motivation

Isabella is desperate to maintain control over the Icarus Hub. She's willing to do anything to keep the apostates and rebellion at bay, and in her desperation, she's allowed dangerous forces, like **Lighthouse**, to act unchecked.

## Part 3: Civil Unrest Scenario

### Nowak's Response to the Mutiny

**Initial Actions:**

- Nowak **unleashes Lighthouse** on the apostates with extreme prejudice
- She **declares the PCs to be enemies of House Tsarya**
- Finding the clues left by **[[Characters/NPCs/Chimera|Chimera]]**, she **flags the PCs as the instigators**
- She sends Lighthouse after them in the chaos
- The docks become a war zone

**Current Situation:**

- She's in one of the local **security posts**, which has become a fallback position
- Her offices were **taken by apostate and Talon forces**
- She's in a back room with only a **few tired and wounded Lighthouse operatives** to protect her

### If PCs Succeed in Reaching Her

**Initial Response:**

- At first, Nowak **claims she doesn't know** where the PCs' cores are
- She's defensive and frightened, but desperate to survive

**What She'll Reveal Under Interrogation:**

- The full details about the **Kimuran outpost orbiting Opportunity Four**
- She wanted it checked for anything of interest
- She **didn't know about the illegal mnemonics research** initially
- **Being a Purist**, she ordered it destroyed when she found out
- The PCs were **killed during the attempt to take the outpost**
- She had ordered them to **sabotage the environmental systems**
- She was going to ignore the dead PCs until data started disappearing
- She feared what they were up to (not knowing it was Chimera)
- She **contacted Valare to scrub the mission** to cover herself

**The Deal:**

- If PCs get to her before **[[Characters/NPCs/Named NPCs/Kestrel|Kestrel]]** does, she offers them a deal:
- Get her off the hub safely, and she'll:
  - Tell them the **location of the outpost**
  - **Clear their names** of the mutiny charges

### If PCs Fail to Reach Her First

**Kestrel's Intervention:**

- **Kestrel kills her** before the PCs arrive
- They find her body in the security post
- Shortly after, PCs get a **message over the Extranet from Kestrel**
- It contains only the **coordinates of the outpost**
- The PCs are now **branded traitors** and enemies of the Coalition
- They are effectively **apostates** until they can prove their innocence
