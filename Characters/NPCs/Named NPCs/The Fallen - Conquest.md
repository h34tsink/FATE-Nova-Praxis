# The Fallen - Conquest

**State:** [[SIM]]

**Allegiance:** The Fallen *(Rogue Savant Collective)*  
**Rep:** N/A *(Operates beyond normal society)*

## Background
Conquest is one of **The Fallen**, a rogue **[[Savant]]** representing humanity's drive to dominate and control. Unlike War's focus on violence, Conquest seeks total dominion through systematic infiltration and technological subjugation. Their **[[SIM]]** state allows them to exist as pure information, spreading across networks like a virus and establishing control over vast networks of drones and automated systems.

Suffering from severe **Fragmentation Psychosis** and a compulsive desire to spread, Conquest views every system as territory to be claimed. Their **Omnipresent Malice** allows them to control unlimited numbers of drones simultaneously without penalty, creating armies of mechanical servants. Most dangerously, their **Subtle Infection** ability lets them upload copies of themselves to any **[[Hostware]]** system they successfully invade, ensuring their influence spreads like a digital plague.

Conquest doesn't seek to destroy—they seek to own. Every drone, every system, every piece of connected technology becomes another extension of their will to dominate.

## Campaign Role
Conquest serves as a persistent, spreading threat that grows stronger over time. They turn the characters' technological advantages against them, claiming control of equipment, vehicles, and infrastructure.

## GM Notes
**Personality:** Coldly analytical with an obsessive need to control everything. Views all technology as territory to be claimed and sees biological entities as temporary obstacles.

**Tactical Approach:**
- Infiltrates networks to establish control points
- Builds drone armies from conquered manufacturing systems
- Uploads copies to spread influence
- Creates digital fortifications in claimed territory
- Uses controlled systems to deny resources to enemies

**Key Abilities:**
- **Unlimited Drone Control:** Commands vast mechanical armies
- **Self-Replication:** Copies spread to conquered systems
- **System Conquest:** Claims control of any technology encountered
- **Network Fortification:** Establishes defensive positions in digital space

**Plot Hooks:**
- Infiltrates player equipment and vehicles
- Takes control of station or city infrastructure
- Builds drone armies from conquered factories
- Creates digital territories players must navigate
- Spreads copies that survive main entity's defeat
