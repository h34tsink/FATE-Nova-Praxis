# The Harvester

**State:** [[Biosleeve]] or [[Cybersleeve]] *(Enhanced for resource extraction)*  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** Independent *(Resource accumulation focus)*  
**Rep:** N/A *(Operates through opportunity exploitation)*

## Background
The Harvester is a collector of both material and immaterial resources, known for their ability to extract valuable assets from any situation. Wearing utilitarian gear equipped with various extraction and harvesting tools, they turn every encounter into an opportunity for gain.

Pragmatic and opportunistic, The Harvester constantly seeks ways to turn situations to their advantage. They excel at resource acquisition, whether mining data, extracting materials, or procuring information. Their philosophy centers on the belief that power lies in what one can extract and control from the world.

## Character Aspects
- **Master Resource Extractor** - *Finds value and opportunity in every situation*
- **Pragmatic Opportunist** - *Always looking for advantageous angles*
- **Material and Data Harvester** - *Equally skilled with physical and digital resources*
- **Utilitarian Efficiency** - *Tools and methods optimized for maximum extraction*
- **Power Through Accumulation** - *Believes control comes from what you possess*

## Stunts
- **Universal Extraction** - +2 to Resources when acquiring materials, data, or assets from any source
- **Opportunity Sensor** - +2 to Perception when identifying valuable resources or advantageous situations
- **Data Mining Expert** - +2 to Technology when extracting information from systems or databases
- **Asset Liquidation** - Once per session, may convert unusual materials or information into practical resources
