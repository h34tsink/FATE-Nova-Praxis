# Joss Taban

**Summary:** Shadow Market courier on [[Locations/Asteroid Belts/Asteroids/Cere|Cere]]. Young, fast, scared. Knows the routes, not the reasons.

## Lore

Joss Taban is seventeen, wiry, and has been running packages through Cere's unofficial passages since he was fourteen. He works for whoever pays — Shadow Market dealers, smugglers, occasionally the refugee community when they need something moved discreetly. He doesn't open packages. He doesn't ask questions. He runs.

Joss knows Cere's unofficial infrastructure better than most: the maintenance crawlways, the dead-zone corridors where surveillance doesn't reach, the timing of enforcer patrols, the gaps in Trident's security coverage. He's useful not for what he knows about people, but for what he knows about *movement* — how to get from A to B without being seen.

He's been busier than usual lately. More packages, more unfamiliar faces, more tension in the Shadow Market. He doesn't understand why, but he can feel the change in rhythm. It makes him nervous.
