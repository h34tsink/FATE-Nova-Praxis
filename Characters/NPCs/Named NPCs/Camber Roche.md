# Camber Roche

**Summary:** Mayor of Gondolin apostate enclave. SIM. Former Frenchman willing to cooperate with the PCs.

## Lore

Camber Roche is the mayor of Gondolin, an apostate enclave struggling to survive outside the protection and resources of the Houses. A former Frenchman now existing as a SIM, Roche carries the weight of his community's survival on digital shoulders.

Roche has reached a turning point. He no longer believes that banditry is necessary for Gondolin's survival — a pragmatic shift that puts him at odds with the more militant elements of his enclave. He is willing to help the PCs take down local bandits, seeing cooperation with the Protectorate as a path toward legitimacy rather than a betrayal of apostate independence.

To back up his intentions, Roche has an ally: a Savant named Gulo who possesses the ability to extract sensies from mnemonic cores. This capability can prove the identities of killers and raiders, giving the PCs hard evidence rather than hearsay. Roche offers this resource freely, understanding that building trust requires tangible gestures.
