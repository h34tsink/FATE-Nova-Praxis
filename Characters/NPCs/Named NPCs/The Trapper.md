# The Trapper

**State:** [[Biosleeve]] *(Enhanced for stealth and tactics)*  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** Independent *(Tactical warfare specialist)*  
**Rep:** N/A *(Operates through ambush and misdirection)*

## Background
The Trapper is an expert in setting traps and ambushes, a tactical genius who can turn any environment into a lethal maze for enemies. Equipped with various gadgets and camouflage gear, they blend into surroundings with supernatural ease.

Calculating and patient, The Trapper believes in preparation and surprise. Their skills in guerrilla warfare, trap-setting, and environmental manipulation are legendary. They excel at predicting enemy movements and using terrain to devastating advantage.

## Character Aspects
- **Master of Tactical Ambushes** - *Turns any environment into a deadly maze*
- **Patient Strategic Genius** - *Believes in preparation over direct confrontation*
- **Environmental Camouflage Expert** - *Blends seamlessly into any terrain*
- **Predictive Movement Reader** - *Anticipates enemy actions and routes*
- **Preparation Over Power** - *Values cunning and foresight above brute force*

The Trapper creates deadly tactical scenarios and teaches the value of preparation. They can fortify areas, create ambush zones, or serve as a cautionary example of patience over aggression.
