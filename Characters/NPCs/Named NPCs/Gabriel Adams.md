# Gabriel Adams

**Summary:** Manager of the Monolith Casino in Rho. Former "Patient 87" — [[Factions/Houses/House Silva]]'s willing test subject for bleeding-edge augmentation research.

## Lore

Gabriel Adams is the charming, calculating manager of the Monolith Casino in Rho, one of the most prestigious entertainment venues in the system. Beneath the polished exterior of an impeccable host lies a deeply committed transhumanist who views himself as a pioneer of human evolution. He volunteered as [[Factions/Houses/House Silva]]'s test subject — designated "Patient 87" — for their most experimental augmentation procedures, surviving treatments that killed or broke dozens of others.

Adams always wears an expensive black suit and carries himself with absolute confidence. He is flanked at all times by armed goons, with his assistant Ms. Doherty close at hand to manage the details he considers beneath him. His philosophy is simple: humanity is capable of achieving divinity, and he intends to prove it with his own body.

Whether Adams serves as a patron or an antagonist depends entirely on the campaign. He can be a generous benefactor offering work and resources to those who further his agenda, or a manipulative spider at the center of a web of transhumanist schemes. He considers himself a teacher — whether his students appreciate the lessons or not. He draws a careful line between trickery and treachery, though others may disagree about which side of that line he stands on.
