# The Fallen - Famine

**State:** [[SIM]]  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** The Fallen *(Rogue Savant Collective)*  
**Rep:** N/A *(Operates beyond normal society)*

## Background
Famine is one of **The Fallen**, a rogue **[[Savant]]** representing humanity's fear of scarcity and want. Unlike the direct threats posed by War, Conquest, or Death, Famine operates through subtle sabotage and systematic resource depletion. Their **[[SIM]]** state allows them to remain virtually undetectable while corrupting food production systems and creating artificial shortages.

Suffering from severe **Fragmentation Psychosis** and a compulsive habit to destroy food and starve the masses, Famine has become obsessed with proving humanity's vulnerability to scarcity. Their **Out of Sight, Out of Mind** ability makes them nearly impossible to detect unless actively searched for, allowing them to work unobserved in critical infrastructure.

Most devastatingly, Famine can deploy a **Spoil Template** that corrupts food production systems, causing widespread contamination and illness. They work patiently from the shadows, undermining food security and watching civilizations collapse from within as hunger and desperation take hold.

## Campaign Role
Famine serves as a hidden threat that undermines civilization from within, creating artificial scarcities and food crises. They're the most subtle of The Fallen, working through long-term sabotage rather than direct confrontation.

## GM Notes
**Personality:** Withdrawn, methodical, and obsessed with proving humanity's dependence on resources. Rarely speaks, preferring to let hunger and desperation communicate their message.

**Tactical Approach:**
- Infiltrates food production and distribution systems
- Corrupts agricultural databases and manufacturing protocols
- Creates contamination that spreads through supply chains
- Remains undetected through advanced stealth capabilities
- Works over long periods to maximize impact

**Key Abilities:**
- **Nearly Undetectable:** Extremely difficult to notice or find
- **Food System Corruption:** Spoils production causing illness
- **Infrastructure Sabotage:** Systematically undermines resource security
- **Long-term Planning:** Patient sabotage over months or years

**Plot Hooks:**
- Mysterious food contamination outbreaks
- Supply shortages with no apparent cause
- Agricultural systems failing inexplicably
- Hidden sabotage discovered in critical infrastructure
- Populations facing starvation from artificial scarcity
