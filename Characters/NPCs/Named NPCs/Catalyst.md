# Catalyst

**Summary:** Enigmatic representative of Chariot, a [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani]] subsidiary. Hires Auxiliaries for sensitive work.

## Lore

Catalyst is the sole point of contact between the PCs and Chariot, a subsidiary of [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani]]. Tall and slender, Catalyst occupies a biosleeve with a feminine build, smooth hairless skin that carries an almost lustrous quality. The sleeve is striking and deliberately ambiguous — androgynous in a way that seems intentional rather than incidental.

Catalyst's attire is consistent and distinctive: a black skin-tight bodysuit beneath a flared skirt or coat, and an ebon circlet or crown set with a single huge diamond positioned precisely over the "third eye." The voice matches the appearance — neither masculine nor feminine, carrying a barely audible echo that makes every word feel weighted and deliberate.

In demeanor, Catalyst is calm, serene, and graceful. Every movement is measured, every word chosen with care. Catalyst offers the PCs Auxiliary contracts to investigate smuggling operations and other sensitive matters that Chariot cannot address through official channels. The relationship is professional but opaque — Catalyst reveals only what is necessary and deflects personal questions with practiced ease. What Chariot's true agenda is, and how Catalyst fits into it, remains unclear.
