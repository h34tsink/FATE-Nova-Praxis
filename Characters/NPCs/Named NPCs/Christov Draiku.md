# Christov Draiku

**State:** [[SIM]]  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya|House Tsarya]]

## Background
Christov Draiku is a [[SIM]] with an obsession with programming and cooking. His unique perspective as a digital consciousness allows him to interface directly with ship systems, making him both the ship's technical specialist and culinary expert. His reluctance to leave his "home environment" reflects the common SIM attachment to their digital domains.
