# Mira Okafor

**Summary:** Independent tech vendor in [[Locations/Asteroid Belts/Asteroids/Cere|Cere's]] Core Markets. Supply chain broker, information trader, and the person who knows what everyone on Cere has been buying.

## Lore

Mira Okafor runs a stall in the Core Markets selling refurbished mining equipment, salvaged electronics, and "specialty items" she doesn't advertise openly. She's been on Cere for six years — long enough to outlast three corporate overseers and two market purges. Her survival strategy is simple: be too useful to remove.

She knows Cere's supply ecosystem better than anyone. Every piece of equipment that moves through the Core Markets passes through her awareness, even if it doesn't pass through her hands. She tracks purchases the way an intelligence analyst tracks troop movements — who's buying survival gear in bulk, who suddenly needs medical supplies, who's stockpiling oxygen filters for a location that officially doesn't exist.

Weeks before the PCs arrive, someone bought a specific list of supplies from Mira's network: long-duration rations, a portable air scrubber, signal-dampening mesh, and a compact fabrication terminal. Mira doesn't know who the buyer was — the transaction went through a dead-drop — but she remembers the list. It's the kind of shopping list someone makes when they plan to disappear underground for a long time.
