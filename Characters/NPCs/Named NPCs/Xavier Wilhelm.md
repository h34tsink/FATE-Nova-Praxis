# Xavier Wilhelm

**State:** Pure  
**Size:** 0  
**Allegiance:** [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani|House Cipriani]]  
**Rep:** 4

## Background
Xavier Wilhelm is an idealistic apostate hoping to unite mankind regardless of race, creed, or state. Born and raised among the apostates of the Uranian colony, he maintains a strong belief in human unity while serving House Cipriani. His motto "Divided we fall" reflects his core philosophy of cooperation across factional lines.
