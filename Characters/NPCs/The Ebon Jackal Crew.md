# The Ebon Jackal Crew

A sample crew that demonstrates diverse character concepts, allegiances, and conflicts within the Nova Praxis setting. This group can serve as NPCs, rivals, allies, or even as inspiration for player characters.

## Crew Overview

**Ship:** The Ebon Jackal (Salvage Vessel)  
**Captain:** Jace Welder  
**Mission:** Salvaging parts to build next-generation racing shuttles  
**Tension:** Each crew member has hidden agendas beyond the stated mission

## Crew Members

### Jace Welder - Captain & Pilot
- **State:** Pure
- **Allegiance:** [[Factions/Ideologies/Apostates|Apostate]]
- **Role:** Idealistic leader, skilled pilot
- **Motivation:** Breaking records and achieving legacy
- **Conflict:** Trusting nature vs crew's hidden agendas

### Durian Konrad - Political Operative
- **State:** [[Glossary/Biosleeve]] (Delta)
- **Allegiance:** [[Factions/Houses/House Silva|House Silva]]
- **CID:** `DK55SV20` (Legal Coalition CID)
- **Role:** Diplomat and manipulator
- **Motivation:** Political advancement and balance of power
- **Conflict:** Distrusted by superiors, ties to apostate arms dealers

### Xavier Wilhelm - Idealist Warrior
- **State:** Pure
- **Allegiance:** [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani|House Cipriani]]
- **CID:** `XW40CP77` (Legal Coalition CID)
- **Role:** Combat specialist and moral compass
- **Motivation:** Human unity across factional lines
- **Conflict:** Apostate upbringing vs House loyalty

### Tiver Andlat - Criminal Savant
- **State:** [[Glossary/Cybersleeve]] (Jinsoku)
- **Allegiance:** [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura|House Kimura]]
- **CID:** `TA18KM52` (Legal Coalition CID)
- **Role:** Technical infiltration and criminal connections
- **Motivation:** Personal advancement and control
- **Conflict:** Criminal background vs legitimate operations

### Christov Draiku - SIM Chef/Programmer
- **State:** [[Glossary/SIM]]
- **Allegiance:** [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya|House Tsarya]]
- **CID:** `CD72TS14` (Legal Coalition CID)
- **Role:** Ship systems and culinary specialist
- **Motivation:** Culinary fame and digital comfort
- **Conflict:** Digital limitations vs physical world needs

## Campaign Usage

### As NPCs
- **Rivals:** Competing salvage crew with complex motivations
- **Allies:** Potential partners for dangerous missions
- **Patrons:** Each member could hire PCs for different reasons

### As Story Elements
- **Factional Tension:** Each member represents different Houses/groups
- **Moral Complexity:** No clear heroes or villains
- **Resource Access:** Diverse skills and connections
- **Character Development:** Models for PC growth and conflict

### Plot Hooks
- **The Hidden Agenda:** What are each member's true goals?
- **Loyalty Crisis:** Conflicting House allegiances create tension
- **The Perfect Score:** Joint mission requiring all their skills
- **Betrayal and Redemption:** Trust broken and potentially rebuilt

## Mechanical Notes
- **Balanced Skills:** Crew covers piloting, combat, social, and technical needs
- **State Diversity:** Examples of Pure, [[Glossary/Biosleeve]], [[Glossary/Cybersleeve]], and [[Glossary/SIM]]
- **House Representation:** Four different major factions represented
- **Conflict Potential:** Built-in tensions for compelling roleplay
