## Background

Kallius is a black market memory merchant — a mnemonic savant who treats the past as currency and himself as the bank. Operating in [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura|House Kimura's]] shadow network, he buys, sells, and manipulates memories using illegal mnemonic editing techniques that would land him in a Coalition prison if discovered.

He carries a dangerous secret — something the Coalition would kill to bury — and the weight of it drives both his paranoia and his obsessive pursuit of neural perfection. Despite inhabiting a heavily augmented Delta Cybersleeve, part of him longs for his Pure self, the unaugmented body he left behind. That Alpha Biosleeve sits in storage like a conscience he can't quite discard.

His downtime is spent researching ways to halt ego fragmentation — a threat that's becoming personal after being possessed by the rogue S.I.M. "Death" in Session 5.
