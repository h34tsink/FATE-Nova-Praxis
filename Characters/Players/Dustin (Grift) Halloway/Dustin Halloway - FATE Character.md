## Background

Dustin "Grift" Halloway is a digital ghost who has never felt at home in his own skin — or anyone else's. A natural social engineer and systems infiltrator, Grift drifted through random jobs and petty corporate theft before finding his niche in the grey space between House Kimura's legitimate operations and its shadow network.

He has a complicated relationship with [[Characters/Named NPCs/Briar Blackwood|Briar Blackwood]], whose favors always come with strings attached. A food delivery gig that went sideways during a social engineering op left a mark — he still flinches when deliveries come up. Underneath the cynicism and digital locks, Grift pines for a normal life he's not sure ever existed.

## GM Notes

- **Compel Targets:** Curiosity Kills (walks into obvious traps), Briar's Favors (called in at worst moments), Corporate Slavery (refuses legitimate authority), Craves Sensations (lured by physical temptations)
- **Invoke Targets:** Digital Locks (hacking), Truth Hunter (investigation), Ghost in the System (infiltration/stealth)
- **Approach:** Grift prefers social engineering and system intrusion over combat. He'll talk his way in, hack what he can't talk through, and fight only as a last resort.
- **Growth Arc:** Finding something worth being loyal to; resolving identity crisis between digital existence and physical longing
