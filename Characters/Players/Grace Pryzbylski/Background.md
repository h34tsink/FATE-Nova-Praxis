Grace Przybylski, a 30-year-old transgender shut-in from Niagara Falls, NY, lived her life through online deliveries, gaming, and remote work as a programmer. She was accepted into the Standard Deviation cryo project and, upon being revived and digitized, struggled to acclimate to her new female sleeve and unwanted public attention. Tired of enduring bigotry, Grace took her own life, only to be revived again. Her friend [[Kallius]] provided her psychiatric care and offered her a place in his SIM cell. Grace found solace and empowerment as a SIM, interacting with the world through drones and online games. She is on a personal journey towards recovery and has found opportunities with [[House Kimura]].
