## Background

Grace Pryzbylski was a 30-year-old transgender shut-in from Niagara Falls who lived her pre-cryo life through online deliveries, gaming, and remote programming. She was accepted into the [[Glossary/Standard Deviation|Standard Deviation]] cryo project and slept through a century of human history.

Upon revival and digitization, Grace struggled to acclimate to her new female sleeve and the unwanted public attention that came with being a cryo survivor. Enduring transphobia and a world she didn't recognize, she took her own life — only to be resurrected again. Her friend [[Characters/Players/Kallius/Kallius - FATE Character|Kallius]] arranged psychiatric care and offered her a place in his [[Rules and Mechanics/Augmentations#SIM Cell Module|SIM cell]].

Grace found empowerment as a [[Glossary/SIM|SIM]], discarding the body that caused her pain and interacting with the physical world exclusively through drones and mesh sensors. She's on a personal recovery journey, channeling her engineering talent into drone operation for [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura|House Kimura]].

**NPC Ally:** [[Characters/Players/Grace Pryzbylski/NPC Ally|Aiden Ishida]] — transgender drone enthusiast and fellow Kimura member. Helps Grace source rare drone blueprints and software for jobs.
