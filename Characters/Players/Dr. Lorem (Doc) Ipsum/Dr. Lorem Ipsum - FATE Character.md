## Background

Dr. Lorem "Doc" Ipsum is an astronaut-origin operative who has pushed the boundary between flesh and machine further than most Silva operatives would dare. His body — a Gamma Biosleeve threaded with Synthesis nanocybernetics — is a testament to House Silva's biotech supremacy: immune to poison and disease, barely reliant on food or sleep, and housing an esper swarm that obeys his thoughts like an extension of his nervous system.

Doc carries the rigid bearing of a man trained for deep-space operations: methodical, precise, and uncompromising in his standards. He keeps his word with almost pathological consistency — a gentleman's code that predates his augmentation — but pairs it with a quiet certainty that he is, objectively, better than most people he meets. Whether this superiority complex stems from his Savant mindset hack or was always there is a question even Doc avoids answering.

His deepest vulnerability is fire. Something happened during hibernation — the details are locked behind trauma — but open flame, plasma discharge, and even aggressive thermal signatures trigger a visceral fear response that no amount of Synthesis perfection can override.

Between missions, Doc pursues what he calls "ascension" — a theoretical state where biological and synthetic architectures merge so completely that the distinction becomes meaningless. His interlude writings reference a consciousness named Sid Acharya who experienced the heat death of the universe through digital time dilation — whether this is allegory, recovered memory, or something stranger remains unclear.

## GM Notes

- **Compel Targets:** Fire Still Burns (panic near plasma/fire, exploitable in ship combat or station fires), Evolved Beyond the Baseline (condescension alienates allies, underestimates "lesser" opponents), A Gentleman's Word (bound by promises to enemies, cannot lie even when it would save the mission), Scion of Silva (House calls in favors mid-mission)
- **Invoke Targets:** Lord of the Swarm (esper combat versatility), Ascension (biotech knowledge), One with the Swarm (nanomachine offense/defense), A Gentleman's Word (social trust from keeping promises)
- **Party Role:** Heavy combat (esper swarm + firearms + power armor), field engineer, pilot. Strongest raw damage output through Esper Lord (SINC +3, WR +4 to +6) and Heavy PAc PDW (Firearms +4, WR 5). Also the party's primary pilot.
- **Esper Lord Tactics:** Slam (Diff 1, WR+4) for quick attacks, Stab/Slash (Diff 2, WR+6) for heavy hits, Devour (Diff 3, ignores armor) against armored targets, Block for zone defense. Swarm has Physical Skills = SINC +2 = 5 and takes Deteriorations at 2/4/6.
- **Vulnerability:** Fire phobia is the crack in the armor — literally. An opponent who uses plasma or incendiary weapons can trigger the phobia compel even while Doc is in power armor. The Supremacist trait (Evolved Beyond the Baseline) means he may refuse to retreat from a "lesser" opponent.
- **Growth Arc:** Resolving the tension between his code of honor and his superiority complex. Pursuing "ascension" — will it lead to transcendence or isolation? Confronting the fire trauma and what it reveals about his pre-augmentation self.
