## Background

Aeddarius Crucial is a newly minted lord of [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis|House Dalianis]] — the Coalition's military-industrial powerhouse. He embodies the Dalianis paradox: a Purist who commands loyalty through composure and strategic brilliance, yet deploys a two-meter combat drone to do his killing and hides a chemical dependency behind aristocratic poise.

Elevated to lordship recently enough that the title still feels like a weapon pointed at him, Aeddarius was assigned to infiltrate Europa Station in Session 1 — a mission that revealed both his tactical skill and his willingness to use people as assets. He recruited [[Characters/Players/Azaria Dawson/Azaria Dawson - FATE Character|Azaria Dawson]] under false pretenses as cover, discovered Apostate operatives embedded in the station's maintenance team, and has since positioned himself as the party's strategic commander.

His addiction — substance unspecified, masked by wealth and composure — is the crack in the marble. When supply is available, he's the sharpest mind in the room. When it isn't, the aristocrat starts to show his seams.

## GM Notes

- **Compel Targets:** The Habit Behind the Composure (withdrawal at worst moment, supply leveraged by NPCs), Always Challenges the Strongest Foe (picks fights he shouldn't), Newly Minted Lord (House assigns mission mid-arc), Ruthless Pragmatist (alienates allies with cold calculus)
- **Invoke Targets:** Aesthetic Aristocrat (social dominance), Purity Is Strength (ideological conviction), Ludwig (drone combat/interrogation), Battlefield Command (tactical repositioning)
- **Ideological Tension:** Purist who travels with a SIM, three Sleeved companions, and a war drone. Dalianis pragmatism explains it — results matter more than doctrine — but it's a compel waiting to happen when a true-believer Purist challenges him.
- **Party Role:** Strategic commander, face in political/noble contexts, drone combatant. Weakest in direct physical combat (Physical Stress 4, no real melee/firearms focus beyond +2).
- **Growth Arc:** Earning the lordship vs. being consumed by it. Confronting the contradiction between Pure ideals and pragmatic ruthlessness. Managing the addiction before it manages him.
