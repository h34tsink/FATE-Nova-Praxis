## Background

Azaria Dawson is a celebrity chasing her next big break — and she doesn't much care what it costs. Aligned with [[Factions/Houses/House Silva|House Silva]], she navigates the intersection of fame, biotech, and the Coalition's media apparatus with equal parts charm and ruthlessness.

Her recent excitement centers on a Sensi recording project — a deal that could catapult her from minor celebrity to household name. Whether the content she's recording is entirely above board remains to be seen.

Azaria is the party's face: the one who talks her way past security, reads a room in seconds, and makes the right impression at the right time. She's also the most physically capable party member in raw biosleeve terms, with a Beta Biosleeve tuned for performance and enhanced with pheromone control.

> **Note:** Several aspects remain undefined. Christa should finalize High Concept and fill in Belief, Connection, Disadvantage, and Expertise aspects.
