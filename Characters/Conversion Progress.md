## Conversion Process

### Standard Workflow

1. **Review SWADE stats** and background
2. **Apply FATE template** structure
3. **Convert skills** using JSON reference data
4. **Map stunts** to FATE equivalents
5. **Create appropriate aspects** from SWADE traits
6. **Maintain narrative elements** and relationships
7. **Add conversion notes** for transparency

### Key Conversion Principles

- **Preserve character essence** while adapting mechanics
- **Use FATE aspects** to capture SWADE mechanical benefits
- **Maintain campaign connections** and relationships
- **Reference JSON data** for consistency
- **Include GM notes** for campaign usage
