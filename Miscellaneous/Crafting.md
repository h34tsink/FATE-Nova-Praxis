## Blending Arcane Devices with Nova Praxis Technology

The sources provide a rich foundation for incorporating **Arcane Devices** () into the sci-fi setting of Nova Praxis. However, they don't offer a one-to-one translation of these rules. The challenge, then, is to blend the concept of imbuing objects with special powers from Arcane Devices with the existing technological framework of Nova Praxis.

### Bridging the Gap: SINC and Savant Programs

Savant Programs and SINC () are the cornerstones of extraordinary technological manipulation in Nova Praxis. These programs grant Savants a unique level of control over technology, exceeding the capabilities of standard users. This makes them ideal candidates for adapting the "Arcane Device" concept:

- **SINC as the Enchanting Force:** Instead of mystical energies, Savants use their SINC skills to imbue objects with special functionalities. Think of it as "programming" rather than "enchanting."
- **Savant Programs as Templates:** Specific Savant Programs can serve as the blueprints for the unique effects embedded within an Arcane Device.

**1. Cloak of Invisibility**

- **Base Item:** Standard clothing, potentially enhanced with light-bending materials (drawing on Force Field technology mentioned in).
- **Savant Program:** **Invisibility** () program.
- **Activation:** The wearer concentrates to activate the program embedded within the clothing, triggering the invisibility effect.
- **Power Points and Limitations:** Follow the guidelines for Arcane Devices (), potentially incorporating a Touch Limitation for reduced cost.

**2. Deflector Bracers**

- **Base Item:** Wrist-mounted bracers, possibly incorporating existing shield technology ().
- **Savant Program:** **Deflection** () or **Barrier** () program.
- **Activation:** The wearer triggers the program, generating a localized shield that deflects incoming projectiles or energy blasts.
- **Power Points and Limitations:** Consider a Range Limitation (perhaps limiting the shield to close proximity to the wearer) to adjust the Power Point cost.

**3. Mind-Reading Headset**

- **Base Item:** A headset designed to interface with neural implants.
- **Savant Program:** **Mind Reading** () program.
- **Activation:** The wearer focuses through the headset, attempting to penetrate the target's thoughts.
- **Power Points and Limitations:** The Power Point cost could be adjusted based on the range of the mind-reading ability and potential defenses against such intrusions (for example, firewalls mentioned in).

### Challenges and Considerations

Adapting Arcane Devices to Nova Praxis presents some unique challenges:

- **Balancing with Existing Technology:** Ensure that Arcane Devices don't become overwhelmingly powerful compared to standard technologies. Consider limitations, costs, and potential risks to maintain balance.
- **SINC Skill as a Bottleneck:** Crafting effective Arcane Devices hinges on the Savant's SINC skill. This could create a disparity in crafting capabilities among players.
- **Glitches and Unforeseen Consequences:** Integrate the possibility of **Glitches** () into the creation and use of Arcane Devices. Critical failures could trigger malfunctions, unexpected side effects, or even dangerous backlashes.

### Expanding on the Possibilities

Remember, the examples above are just a starting point. The potential for creating unique Arcane Devices in Nova Praxis is vast. By combining Savant Programs with existing technologies and applying the core principles of Arcane Devices, you can craft a wide range of sci-fi gadgets and tools that enhance the immersive experience of the setting.
