Seven Actions

ActionWhat It Does

ContestOpposed roll; highest Effort wins. Tie = both succeed

Defense is reactive — you roll when attacked, it does not cost your action.

Aspects

Types

  • Character: permanent (HC + ABCDE + state)
  • Situation: last for a scene or until removed
  • Temporary: from Maneuvers; gone once invoked/compelled
  • Effect: from Spin; gone once used
  • Consequences: last until recovered
  • Persistent (P): invoke/compel for +2 or reroll free

Scope Rule

One Aspect per scope per test. Scopes: Character, Scene, Zone, Equipment, Consequence, Stunt, Augmentation, Another Character's. Persistent Aspects are exempt.

"Therefore" Test

"I have [Aspect], therefore it helps me [do this]." Two logical leaps = stretch.

Invoking Aspects

Spend 1 FP or use a free invoke. Choose one:

  • +2 to your Skill test
  • Reroll all your dice
  • Create a Block (usually Potency 2)
  • Make a Declaration

Free invokes: first use of Aspects from Maneuvers, Spin, or inflicted Consequences.

Persistent Aspects: +2 bonus and reroll are always free. Blocks/Declarations still cost 1 FP.

Compelling Aspects

Accept a complication = earn 1 FP. Effects:

  • −2 penalty on your test
  • Forced reroll
  • Suffer a Block or Declaration
  • Narrative complication

Refuse a compel: pay 1 FP.

Persistent Aspect compels: declining always costs 1 FP. You do NOT gain FP from P-Aspect compels.

Damage & Stress

Stress = Shifts + WR − AR

ExR (Explosive Rating) adds to the attack roll, not stress after a hit. Edge (mental) adds to attack roll. Determination adds to defense roll.

Consequences

TypeAbsorbsPhysicalMental

  • Type must match the attack (physical consequence for physical damage, etc.)

Concessions

  • Offered any time you take a Consequence
  • Absorbs all stress from the triggering attack
  • You do NOT auto-gain FP for conceding
  • Gain 1 FP only if opponent refuses a reasonable concession (they lose 1 FP)
  • You narrate the outcome — not the attacker

Conflict

Per Turn

  • 1 Simple Action (Attack, Maneuver, Block, etc.)
  • 1 Free Action (move to adjacent zone, draw weapon, short speech)
  • Instant Actions (defense doesn't count; limit 1 other per turn)

Supplemental Action

Do a second thing alongside your Simple Action: −1 penalty to primary test. Additive with other penalties (e.g., Hustle = −2).

Combined Skills

Roll primary, modified by secondary: higher = +1, lower = −1, same = +0.

Turn Order

Strategy Skill determines initiative. GM may shift order based on fiction.

Common Mental Attacks

  • Lies: Guile vs. Insight
  • Manipulation: Diplomacy vs. Cohesion/Resolve
  • Threats: Intimidation vs. Resolve
  • Seduction: Diplomacy/Guile vs. Resolve/Insight

Edge 0 = half-hearted. Edge 5 = playing on target's fears/weaknesses. Can exceed 5 for extreme scenarios.

Extended Actions

Mark = Difficulty × 3

Make repeated Skill tests. Accumulate Shifts until total ≥ Mark.

  • Spin: reduces remaining Mark by 1
  • Stall: catastrophic failure, cannot continue

Stunts

  • 3 Stunts at character creation (+ free State stunts)
  • Stunts do NOT cost Refresh
  • Gain more at Major Milestones
  • Savant Programs count as stunt slots
  • Savant stunt = 1 slot + 1 free program; extra programs = extra slots
