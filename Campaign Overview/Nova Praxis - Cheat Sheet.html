Scope Rule

One Aspect per scope per test: Character, Scene, Zone, Equipment, Consequence, Stunt, Augmentation. Persistent exempt.

"I have [Aspect], therefore it helps me [do this]." Two leaps = stretch.

☰

Invoking Aspects

Spend 1 FP or free invoke. Choose one:

  • +2 to your Skill test
  • Reroll all your dice
  • Create a Block (usually Potency 2)
  • Make a Declaration

Free invokes: first use of Aspects from Maneuvers, Spin, or Consequences. Persistent: +2/reroll always free; Blocks/Declarations still cost 1 FP.

☰

Compelling Aspects

Accept complication = earn 1 FP. Refuse = pay 1 FP.

  • −2 penalty, forced reroll, Block/Declaration against you, or narrative complication
Persistent compels: declining always costs 1 FP. No FP earned from P-Aspect compels.
☰

Fate Points & Refresh

  • Refresh: 5 (Pure = 7). Refreshed stunt: +1 (up to 3x)
  • Reset to Refresh each session. FP do NOT carry over
  • Spend: invoke Aspect, power Stunt, refuse compel
  • Earn: accept compels, Aspects invoked against you, concede

Per skill: rank 1-2 = +1 box, rank 3-4 = +2, rank 5+ = +3. ExR adds to attack roll, not stress. Edge (mental) adds to attack roll. Determination adds to defense.

Consequences

TypeAbsorbsPhysMental
Mild21 hour1 day
Moderate41 day1 week
Severe (P)61 week1 month
Extreme (P)8SpecialPermanent
  • One Mild/Mod/Sev/Ext per stress track (up to 12 for Savants, 8 otherwise)
  • Type must match attack. First invoke/compel is free
  • Downgrade on recovery: Sev → Mod → Mild → gone
  • Emergency aid: Diff 2, 10 min = remove Mild in field
Distress Concession: Take Extreme on a different track (e.g., Extreme Mental from physical = shock).
☰

Concessions

  • Offered when you take a Consequence. Absorbs all stress from that attack
  • You do NOT auto-gain FP. Gain 1 FP only if opponent refuses (they lose 1 FP)
  • You narrate the outcome — not the attacker

Conflict

Per Turn

  • 1 Simple Action (Attack, Maneuver, Block, etc.)
  • 1 Free Action (move adjacent, draw weapon, short speech)
  • Instant Actions (defense free; 1 other per turn)

Supplemental Action

Second thing alongside Simple: −1 to primary. Additive with other penalties.

Combined Skills

Primary + secondary: higher = +1, lower = −1, same = +0.

Turn Order

Strategy Skill. GM may adjust for fiction.

Extended Actions

Mark = Difficulty × 3

Repeated tests. Accumulate Shifts ≥ Mark. Spin: Mark −1. Stall: catastrophic, cannot continue.
