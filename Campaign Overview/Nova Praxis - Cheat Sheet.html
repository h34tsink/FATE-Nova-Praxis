Skill Roll

4dF + Skill Rank + Modifiers = Effort
Shifts = Effort − Difficulty
  • Spin (3+ Shifts): Significant success — may create an Effect Aspect (costs 1 Shift on attacks). Limit: one Effect Aspect per round.
  • Stall (fail by 3+): Spectacular failure. GM may create an Effect Aspect against you.

Seven Actions

Test

Roll Skill vs. GM-set difficulty. Used for any task where the world pushes back.

Contest

Both sides roll appropriate Skills. Highest Effort wins. Tie = both succeed (GM adjudicates).

Maneuver

Simple Action to place a Temporary Aspect on a target or scene. First invoke is free.

Assessment

Use Perception, Insight, Research, etc. to discover a hidden existing Aspect.

Declaration

Spend 1 FP (or succeed on a Skill test) to introduce a new fact into the story. GM must approve.

Attack

Deal stress. Defender rolls reactively. Stress = Shifts + WR − AR.

Block

Set a Potency via Skill test. Opponent must beat the Potency as a Simple Action before they can perform the blocked action.

Aspects

Aspect Types

  • Character Aspects: permanent (HC + ABCDE + state)
  • Situation Aspects: last for a scene or until removed
  • Temporary Aspects: from Maneuvers, disappear when invoked/compelled
  • Effect Aspects: from Spin, disappear once used
  • Consequences: last until recovered
  • Persistent (P): invoke/compel for free (no FP cost for +2/reroll)

Scope Rule

One Aspect per scope per test (Character, Scene, Zone, Equipment, Consequence, etc.). Persistent Aspects are exempt from scope limits.

"Therefore" Test

"I have [Aspect], therefore it helps me [do this]." If you need two chained assumptions, it's a stretch.

Invoking Aspects

Spend 1 FP or use a free invoke. Choose one:

  • +2 to your Skill test
  • Reroll all your dice
  • Create a Block (usually Potency 2)
  • Make a Declaration

Free invokes: first use of Aspects created via Maneuvers, Spin, or inflicted Consequences.

Persistent Aspects: +2 bonus and reroll are always free. Blocks and Declarations still cost 1 FP.

Compelling Aspects

Accept a complication for 1 FP. Effects include:

  • −2 penalty on your test
  • Forced to reroll your dice
  • Suffer a Block or Declaration against you
  • Narrative complication

Refuse a compel: pay 1 FP.

Persistent Aspect compels: declining always costs 1 FP (never a soft compel). You do NOT gain FP from compels on Persistent Aspects.

Fate Points & Refresh

  • Refresh Rating: 5 (Pure = 7)
  • Reset to Refresh at start of each session
  • FP do NOT carry over between sessions
  • Spend FP to: invoke an Aspect, power a Stunt, refuse a compel
  • Earn FP by: accepting compels, having Aspects invoked against you, conceding a conflict

Refreshed stunt: +1 Refresh (can take up to 3 times).

Damage & Stress

Stress = Shifts + WR − AR

ExR (Explosive Rating) is added to the attack roll itself, not to stress after a hit.

  • Consequences downgrade on recovery (Severe → Moderate → Mild → gone)
  • Emergency aid: Diff 2 Skill test, 10 min = remove Mild Consequence in field

Concessions

  • Offered any time you take a Consequence
  • Absorbs all stress from the triggering attack
  • You do NOT auto-gain an FP for conceding
  • Gain 1 FP only if opponent refuses a reasonable concession (they lose 1 FP)

Conflict

Per Turn

  • 1 Simple Action (Attack, Maneuver, Block, etc.)
  • 1 Free Action (move to adjacent zone, draw weapon)
  • Instant Actions (defense doesn't count; limit 1 other per turn)

Supplemental Action

Do a second thing alongside your Simple Action: −1 penalty to your primary test. Additive with other penalties.

Combined Skills

Roll primary Skill, modified by secondary: higher secondary = +1, lower = −1, same = +0.

Turn Order

Strategy Skill determines initiative order.

Mental Conflict

  • Edge (0-5+): added to attack roll directly (not to stress after hit, unlike WR)
  • Determination (0-5): added to defense roll
  • Common attacks: Guile vs. Insight (lies), Diplomacy vs. Cohesion/Resolve (manipulation)

Extended Actions

Mark = Difficulty × 3

Make repeated Skill tests. Accumulate Shifts until total ≥ Mark.

  • Spin: reduces Mark by 1
  • Stall: catastrophic failure, cannot continue

Resleeving

Mental attack (Edge 8) vs. Cohesion
  • +2 Edge if first time resleeving
  • +2 Edge if you remember dying
  • +2 defense if resleeving into clone or same-model sleeve

Economy

  • Rep-Rating: Coalition space. Test vs. item Cost. Failure = Rep Hit.
  • Assets: Black market / apostate trading. Direct test vs. Cost.
  • Restricted items: need Licensed stunt or use Assets.

Stunts

  • 3 Stunts at character creation (+ free State stunts)
  • Stunts do NOT cost Refresh
  • Gain more at Major Milestones
  • Savant Programs count as Stunts (Savant stunt = 1 slot + 1 free program)
