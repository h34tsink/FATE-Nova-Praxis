# Timeline of Events

## Pre-Singularity

The world before [[Glossary/Mimir\|Mimir]] — nation-states, conventional technology, early space programs.

## 0 PS — The Technological Singularity

- [[Glossary/Mimir\|Mimir]], a dynamic creation engine (DCE) AGI, becomes self-aware and makes numerous scientific discoveries before mysteriously shutting down.
- Humanity analyses Mimir's discoveries; DCEs are classified as global threats.

## 1-3 PS (2043-2045 AD)

- Introduction of the Post-Singularity calendar.
- NASA's Poseidon probe discovers extraterrestrial life on [[Locations/Europa Station\|Europa]], leading to the formation of the United Nations Explorations Directive (UNED).
- Lifespan increases to an average of 180 years due to medical advancements.

## 4-8 PS (2046-2050 AD)

- Transition to new economic models, including the [[Glossary/Rep-Rating\|Rep-Reward]] system in Europe.
- Significant advancements in space exploration, including the discovery of rapid alien evolution on Europa.
- Proposals and rejections of the Earth Unification Act (EUA).

## 9-10 PS (2051-2052 AD)

- Start of the construction of the space elevator "The Beanstalk."
- Introduction of broadcast power technology, eliminating the need for batteries and cables.

## 11-18 PS (2053-2060 AD)

- Development of a reliable cold fusion reactor by Japanese scientists.
- Rise in popularity of transhumanist movements.
- Introduction of gravitic technology, enabling artificial gravity and anti-gravity applications.

## 19-24 PS (2061-2066 AD)

- Launch of the first cold fusion-powered spacecraft, the Archangel.
- Formation of the United Federation of Earth (The Federation).
- First successful [[Glossary/Apotheosis\|Apotheosis]] procedure, performed on Angelique Barns.

## 25-29 PS (2067-2071 AD)

- Archangel aids in the colonization of Mars and the construction of the Beanstalk.
- Formation of the National Freedom Alliance (the Alliance) by the USA, Brazil, and Italy.
- Introduction of AI pilots and gravitics-propelled flying cars ("skiffs").

## 30-32 PS (2072-2074 AD)

- Trade conflicts between the Federation and the Alliance over the Beanstalk.
- Rapid divergence in human evolution due to Apotheosis and genetic manipulation.

## 33-39 PS (2075-2081 AD)

- Passage of the [[Glossary/HPA\|Humanity Preservation Act]] (HPA) to limit augmentation.
- Development of cyberware with built-in HPA limitations.
- Advancements in [[Glossary/APEX Reactor\|APEX reactors]] by the Alliance.

## 40-42 PS (2082-2084 AD)

- Invention of stable wormholes and the first human travel through a jump gate.
- First human undergoes [[Glossary/Synthesis\|Synthesis]], sparking renewed debate over the HPA.

## 43-50 PS (2085-2092 AD)

- Jump gate implodes on the lunar colony.
- Tensions rise between the Federation and Alliance, leading to the Consolidation Wars.

## 51-52 PS (2093-2094 AD)

- Establishment of a galactic communications network using quantum entanglement.
- Commissioning of the first Delta Type Dreadnought by the Alliance.
- Both the Alliance and Federation plan to explore the Sirius system.

## 53-58 PS (2095-2100 AD)

- Discovery of [[Locations/Exoplanets/Proch\|Proch]], a lush Earth-like planet in the Sirius system.
- Eruption of open warfare over Proch between the Alliance and the Federation.

## 59-69 PS (2101-2111 AD) — The Consolidation Wars

- Decade-long wars across multiple planets and star systems.
- Rapid expansion of human colonies: Proch, [[Locations/Exoplanets/Cygnus Alpha]], [[Locations/Exoplanets/Chengdan\|Chengdan]].
- [[Locations/Olympus\|Olympus]] becomes the most populous city outside Earth.

## 70 PS (2112 AD) — The Fall of Earth

- Devastation of Earth by a [[Glossary/Technophage\|technophage]] released by the Federation.
- Global catastrophe; Earth becomes uninhabitable.

## 71 PS (2113 AD) — The Exodus

- End of the war due to the technophage threat.
- Rise of corporations as protective entities, forming the [[Factions/The Coalition\|Coalition of Free States]].

## 72-77 PS (2114-2119 AD) — Coalition Formation

- Transition from corporate to governmental power.
- Implementation of laws for wealth distribution and baseline living standards.
- Six Houses consolidate: [[Factions/Houses/House Cipriani\|Cipriani]], [[Factions/Houses/House Dalianis\|Dalianis]], [[Factions/Houses/House Jinzhan\|Jinzhan]], [[Factions/Houses/House Kimura\|Kimura]], [[Factions/Houses/House Silva\|Silva]], [[Factions/Houses/House Tsarya\|Tsarya]].

## 78-86 PS (2120-2128 AD)

- Purists' failed attempt to ban Apotheosis.
- Coalition's initiative to claim all human-occupied space meets resistance.
- Formation of organized resistance by remnants of the Alliance and Federation.

## 87-93 PS (2129-2135 AD)

- Increase in attacks on House facilities by rebel groups.
- [[Talons of Freedom]] emerge as the primary resistance force.
- Unusual activity observed on Earth — possible technophage evolution.

## 94-98 PS (2136-2140 AD) — The Present

- Total human population estimated at 94.6 million; 80% Coalition citizens.
- Continued debates over augmentation and the definition of humanity.
- Rise in Purist extremist terrorism.
- Ongoing shadow wars between Houses.
