NOVA PRAXIS — Character Creation Guide

Follow steps 0-8 in order

0 Concept

Who is this person? What do they want? Why are they running deniable ops? Don't overthink it — a sentence or two is enough to start.

1 Aspects (ABCDE)

Write 6-7 Aspects. Each should be double-edged — useful when invoked, interesting when compelled.

4 Select Stunts

3 Stunts at creation (+ free State stunts). Stunts do NOT reduce Refresh. Gain more at Major Milestones.

Key stunts to consider:

  • Savant: unlocks SINC + 1 free program (requires Apotheosis)
  • Licensed: buy restricted gear with Rep-Rating
  • Skilled: +1 to one non-physical skill (max 5)
  • Drone-Jock: cheaper, better drones
  • Specialty Aspect: free invoke once/scene on a specific skill
  • Refreshed: +1 Refresh (up to 3 times)
  • Battlefield Command: move allies as Instant Action
  • Reputable: +2 Rep-Rating
  • Transhumanist: +2 Aug Points, +1 max augmentations

6 Starting Rep-Rating

Rep-Rating = House Networking + 2

Immediately gain a bump with Value = your Diplomacy.

Apostates: no Rep-Rating. Use Assets for all purchases.

7 Starting Equipment

Free items by descending Cost:

  • 1st item: MAX(Assets, Rep-Rating) + 2
  • 2nd item: MAX(Assets, Rep-Rating) + 1
  • 3rd item: +0, then −1, etc. until Cost = 1
  • Unlimited items at Cost 0-1

Restricted items limit: Assets + 1 (unless Licensed stunt).

Starting sleeve (Sleeved characters): MAX(Assets, Rep-Rating) + 4.
