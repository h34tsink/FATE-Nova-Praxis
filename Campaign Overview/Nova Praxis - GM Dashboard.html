Invoking Aspects

Spend 1 FP or free invoke:

  • +2 to Skill test
  • Reroll all dice
  • Create Block (Potency 2)
  • Make Declaration

Persistent: +2/reroll free. Blocks/Declarations cost FP.

Compelling Aspects

Accept = +1 FP. Refuse = pay 1 FP.

  • −2 penalty, forced reroll, Block/Declaration, or narrative complication

Persistent: decline always costs FP. No FP earned from P compels.
