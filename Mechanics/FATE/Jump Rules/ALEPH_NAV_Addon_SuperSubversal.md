# ALEPH NAV — Superversal & Subversal Jumps (Addon v1.1)
*A plug-in for **ALEPH NAV: A Multiverse Navigation Framework for TTRPGs***  
This module adds two advanced traversal modes that sit alongside your normal “interversal” jumps:
- **Subversal** — dives into **embedded layers**: pocket continua, timeline shards, myth-spaces, simulation layers, demi-planes.
- **Superversal** — leaps **above** a universe’s law manifold to sibling **meta-regimes**: other law-stacks, meta-engines, creator layers.

It slots cleanly into Play Mode and extends the Tech Appendix with precise packet/layout fields. Mix with the **Dark Matter Alephs** add-on for delicious synergy.

---

## PART I — PLAY MODE (DROP-IN)

### 1) Three Jump Modes (choose per scene)
| Mode | Where It Goes | Typical Use | Requirements | Base TN | Risk on Fail |
|---|---|---|---|---:|---|
| **Intraversal** | Same universe, different region/system/timeline branch (your default) | Travel & plot | Beacon Lock ≥ 1 or Gate | 10 | Mishap |
| **Subversal** | **Inside**-the-universe layers: pocket realities, demi-planes, sim shards | Heists, vaults, myth-logic | **Substrate Key** *or* Local Halo Q≥2 *or* Known Micro-Gate | 11 | Catastrophe (soft) |
| **Superversal** | **Above**-law jumps to another **law stack** (meta-neighbor universe) | New physics, deity courts, editor layer | **Meta Handle** + Gate (or LGR≥2) + Beacon Lock ≥ 2 | 12 | Catastrophe (hard) |

> **Designer intent:** Subversal is *deep*, Superversal is *high*. Deep runs are weird and sticky; high runs are majestic and cruel.

**Modifiers (stack with core):**
- **Good Beacon Lock:** −1 TN (intraversal), 0 (subversal), +0 (superversal)
- **Known Gate:** −1 TN (any)
- **Law Gradient (LGR steps):** +1 TN per step (intraversal/superversal only)
- **Halo Profile:** if using DM Add-on: **caustic** grants +1 die to Subversal; **void** adds +1 TN to Superversal.

---

### 2) New Resources & Keys
- **Substrate Key (consumable or attunement):** A token/ritual/phrase that matches a pocket layer’s **myth-logic**. Spend to count as **Known Micro-Gate**.
- **Meta Handle (ship module or covenant):** Grants permission to touch the law-stack above. Counts as “gateway proof” for Superversal. Risk: once/session, on Mixed/Fail add **Strain +1**.
- **Breadcrumbs (stack, 0–3):** Every scene you stay **inside** a Subversal layer without Survey, add 1 Breadcrumb. When you exit, roll a slip check: TN 8 + Breadcrumbs (on fail: you pop out **adjacent** but not exact).

---

### 3) Moves (system-agnostic wording)

**Dive the Substrate (Subversal Jump)**  
When you descend into a pocket/embedded layer using a Substrate Key, Halo caustic, or a Micro-Gate:
- Roll the **Nav Test** at Subversal TN (11 plus mods).  
- **On Success:** Arrive; pick 1: **Quiet Entry** (no notice) or **Anchor** (set Exit Anchor; +1 die to leave).  
- **On Mixed:** Arrive with **Myth-Logic Tax** (GM picks 1 condition) and mark **Breadcrumbs +1**.  
- **On Fail:** **Catastrophe (soft)**—choose or roll (see Tables).

**Ascend the Stack (Superversal Jump)**  
When you leverage a Meta Handle and a Gate (or a brutal law gradient) to leap above:
- Roll the **Nav Test** at Superversal TN (12 plus mods).  
- **On Success:** Arrive; reduce **Drift** to 0 (the meta layer re-normalizes you) and gain **1 Intel** about law topology.  
- **On Mixed:** Arrive, mark **Strain +1** and **Drift 1**; something from the meta notices you.  
- **On Fail:** **Catastrophe (hard)**—choose or roll (see Tables).

**Thread the Interstice (Leave Subversal)**  
When you exit a Subversal layer:  
- If you set an **Anchor**, leave clean.  
- Else roll vs **TN 8 + Breadcrumbs**. Success: you emerge where intended; Mixed: **Temporal Slip (minutes-hours)**; Fail: **Adjacent Egress** (neighbor zone/gate). Clear Breadcrumbs to 0.

---

### 4) Conditions & Tags (new)
- **Myth-Logic Tax:** pick one until appeased—*Speak in Titles*, *Pay with Names*, *No Iron Tools*, *Honor the Warden*. Clear by obeying or offering a cost (loot/favor).
- **Anchor (scene tag):** A marked, resonant point to exit from a Subversal layer. Provides +1 die to **Thread the Interstice**; expires when used or if the scene turns hostile.
- **Meta Glare:** While in Superversal space, disadvantage on stealth; the place sees *paths*, not people.

---

### 5) Gates (types & prices)
- **Micro-Gate (Subversal):** closet-sized seams in myth-logic; price is a *gesture* (a rhyme, a ring of salt, a prime sum, a personal memory).
- **Macro-Gate (Superversal):** cyclopean spans or conceptual elevators; price is *context* (a vow, a relinquished label, a system exploit).

**Gate Price Oracles (d6 each):**  
- **Subversal Price:** 1 rhyme, 2 name, 3 memory, 4 blood, 5 salt/iron, 6 secret  
- **Superversal Price:** 1 vow, 2 title, 3 law swap, 4 paradox token, 5 reputation, 6 sacrifice

### 6) Tables

**Subversal Mishaps (soft catastrophes, d6):**  
1. **Topology Creep:** Rooms reorder behind you; +1 Breadcrumbs.  
2. **Warden Toll:** Choose: lose **Intel** or mark **Glitch**.  
3. **Sim Overscan:** NPCs pause; you leave **ghost artifacts** (GM will use later).  
4. **Totem Clash:** Your gear offends local rules; disable 1 tag until appeased.  
5. **Causal Damp:** All reactions feel syrupy; −1 die to quick actions for this scene.  
6. **Ejection Cough:** Layer spits you to **Adjacent Egress** and marks you—next guards recognize your scent.

**Superversal Catastrophes (hard, d6):**  
1. **Schema Tear:** Lose 1 playbook ability/feat until you rest outside the meta.  
2. **Audit Ping:** A meta-entity opens a ticket on your existence. Clock: 4-segments “Closure.”  
3. **Law Swap:** For one scene, invert a core expectation (magic↔tech, heat flows cold→hot, etc.).  
4. **Anchor Loss:** Your home Gate forgets you; +2 TN to return until you reintroduce yourself (ritual or cost).  
5. **Name Spill:** Your true name leaks into local logs; −1 to social defenses vs meta denizens this arc.  
6. **Drive Scald:** Mark **Strain +2** and **Drift 2**; your dampers hiss blue.

### 8) Gear & Upgrades
- **Substrate Loom (ship tag):** Once/mission, auto-Anchor on Subversal arrival.  
- **Myth Scrip (kit, 3 uses):** Bypass **Myth-Logic Tax** once/use.  
- **Meta Handle (ship core):** Required for Superversal; while installed, gain **+1 Intel** when you succeed on **Ascend the Stack**.  
- **Title Veil (oddity):** In superversal space, treat your name as noise; ignore **Name Spill** once/session.

---

### 9) Economy Hooks
- **Paradox Token (rare):** Spend to **rewrite one check outcome** by 1 band (Fail→Mixed, Mixed→Success) at a narrative cost; on spend, mark **Drift +1**.  
- **Keys & Permits:** Factions sell access: Substrate Keys (Subversal passes), Meta Permits (Superversal corridors).

### 10) Worked Example (2 minutes at table)
Crew wants to rob a myth-vault (Subversal), then escape upward (Superversal).  
- **Prep:** Substrate Key from the Keyforge; Meta Handle online.  
- **Jump 1 (Subversal):** TN 11 → Mixed: **Myth-Logic Tax (Speak in Titles)** + Breadcrumbs 1.  
- **Heist:** They Anchor near a statue.  
- **Leave:** Thread the Interstice at TN 9 (8 + Breadcrumbs 1) → Success.  
- **Jump 2 (Superversal):** TN 12 with Known Gate (−1) and Lock 2 (0) → Success: Arrive, **Drift 0**, gain **1 Intel** about the law-court. A Clerk eyes them for later…

## PART II — GM PLAYBOOK

### 11) Factions & NPCs
- **Wardens of the Thin Places** (Subversal): sentient rules that wear masks; they trade in compliance and nostalgia.  
- **The Meta Clerks** (Superversal): bureaucrats of change; speak in diffs; they can stamp your route—*for a fee*.  
- **Backsolvers** (Both): criminals who specialize in forging Keys, forging **Titles**, and erasing your gate debts.

### 12) Clocks & Arcs
- **Closure (meta audit)** 4-segments → Entity terminates your “erroneous path.”  
- **Crumb Storm** (subversal drift) 6-segments → You’re swallowed by the pocket’s story and must bargain to be released.  
- **Gate Rivalry** (any) 4-segments → Another crew contests your route; their beacons spoof yours.

### 13) Mixing with Dark Matter Alephs
- **Caustic halos** give **+1 die** to **Dive the Substrate** (they act like waveguides).  
- **Void halos** impose **+1 TN** to **Ascend the Stack** (thin signal).  
- **DMSH** (dark-matter spectral hash) helps authenticate **Micro-Gates**; reduce spoof chance by one step if you have a fresh DMSH.

## APPENDIX — TECH & PACKET EXTENSIONS (diegetic)

### A) Taxonomy
- **Intraversal:** Within LSP cell; ICF varies minimally; TOP fixed.  
- **Subversal:** **TOP′** (sub-topology) inside a host’s manifold; **CGA′** is a *subgraph slice* that reattaches to the host graph.  
- **Superversal:** Step into **LSP̄** (neighbor law-stack); ICF resets to a new program root; **TOP** may be undefined until you select a child universe.

### B) Address Suffixes
Extend the pilot alias and packet:

**Pilot Alias:**  
`… / MODE:INTRA|SUB|SUPER / ANCH:0|1 / CRUMBS:n`

**Packet Fields (delta):**
```text
MODE  : [1 byte] 00 intra, 01 sub, 10 super
ANCH  : [1 bit]  Anchor present?
CRUMB : [2 bits] Breadcrumbs 0-3
PERMIT: [8 bytes] Meta Permit or Substrate Key ID
```

### C) Routing Notes
- **Superversal:** treat ΔL measured against a *meta* norm; routes go via **Phase-Boundary Gateways** whose SLAs decay quickly (publish TTL).  
- **Subversal:** routing is **narrative**, not metric; cost is mutual information lost between host and pocket (cap by session length).

### D) Safety
- **ECC:** Increase redundancy one step for MODE=SUPER packets; flip to **Black-Sky Mode** on beacon loss.  
- **Anti-Spoofing:** For Subversal micro-gates, include **myth-proofs** (zero-knowledge rhyme commitments). For Superversal, require **title-signed** tickets from a Clerk.
