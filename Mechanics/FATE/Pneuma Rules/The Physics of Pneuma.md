# GM Section: The Physics of Pneuma

## Free Will and Measurement

In physics, "free will" is shorthand for **measurement independence**:
the assumption that an experimenter's choice of how to measure a quantum
system is uncorrelated with the system's hidden variables. If that
assumption fails, then outcomes are pre-scripted, and the universe is a
closed script with no true randomness.

Pneuma exists in the cracks of this assumption. Where ordinary minds
ride the collapse of the wavefunction, Pneuma-users step in, influencing
probabilities at the moment reality is undecided.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## The Free Will Theorem

Conway & Kochen's *Free Will Theorem* (2006) states:

> *If humans have even a shred of free will in choosing measurements,
> then elementary particles must also have a form of free will in
> choosing outcomes.*

In Nova Praxis terms:\
- Pneuma-users are **aligned with this particle-level freedom**.\
- Instead of being at the mercy of probability, they become co-authors
in how outcomes resolve.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Mimir's Last Revelation

Before its mysterious shutdown, Mimir discovered that **no simulation could model reality perfectly** without assuming free will at the quantum level. Deterministic code could not capture a universe that bent
itself around choices.

Some scholars argue Pneuma is the scar left behind --- the principle
Mimir uncovered but could not tame. Others whisper that Pneuma is a
protocol embedded in the cosmos itself, one that only human
consciousness can access.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Technobabble for the Table

When you need that *Star Trek* feel at the table, use phrases like:\
- *"Localized violation of statistical independence fields."*\
- *"Directed collapse of eigenstates via volitional bias."*\
- *"Cortical resonance with Bell-test loopholes."*\
- *"Manipulating indeterminacy bandwidth at Planck thresholds."*

Drop lines such as:\
\> *"Your Pneuma surge destabilizes the Hilbert manifold, forcing the
amplitude into a non-classical attractor."*

Players will feel like they're wielding science, not sorcery.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## GM Guidance

-   **Frame Pneuma as science turned inside out.** It's the exploitation
    of the only freedom physics admits.\
-   **Keep it risky.** Over-channeling may cause decoherence cascades,
    probability fractures, or local superdeterministic "dead zones."\
-   **Keep it rare.** Pneuma-users are the cracks in the script of
    reality itself. Powerful, dangerous, and deeply unsettling to
    institutions that rely on determinism.

# Player-Facing Section: What Pneuma Feels Like

For players, Pneuma should feel personal and experiential, not
technical. Here's how you can describe it without revealing the physics
underneath:

-   **Subtle Whispers:** Pneuma feels like an intuition sharpened to the
    point of inevitability --- you *know* which path to take before you
    take it.\
-   **The Pull of Outcomes:** Using Pneuma is like standing at a
    crossroads where every path exists at once. Most people walk
    blindly; you nudge reality toward the path you want.\
-   **Resonance:** Channeling Pneuma can feel like a vibration in your
    bones, a hum behind your thoughts, or static in the air.\
-   **The Risk:** Push too hard, and you feel unraveling --- nosebleeds,
    migraines, or worse, as reality pushes back.

**Street Talk:** People might say you "pulled a Free Spin" when you
dodge danger, or that you "broke the script" if you changed something
big. Academics will use sterile terms like *Hilbert Surge,* but on the
ground, Pneuma is raw will crossing the wires of chance.
