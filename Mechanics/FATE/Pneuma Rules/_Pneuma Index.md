## Quick Reference

### For Players
- Pneuma feels like **intuition sharpened to prescience**
- Using it is like **standing at a crossroads and pulling the better path toward you**
- Effects are **local, brief, and leave forensic traces**
- Rare, risky, and often illegal under HPA law

### For GMs
- Frame Pneuma as **science turned inside out**
- Keep it **rare** — cracks in the script of reality
- **Every use leaves a Signature** that can be traced
- Houses hoard Void-Cores; black markets trade Synthetics
- Purists and HPA treat overt Pneuma as existential threat
- Mimirians call it "the intended next step"

### Campaign Integration
- Introduce gradually through artifacts first
- Birrin NPCs can provide containment context
- Use physics slang terms for corporate/academic scenes
- Faction attitudes vary wildly (conflict driver)
