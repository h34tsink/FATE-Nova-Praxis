# Melee Weapons

A comprehensive reference for all melee weapons in Nova Praxis.

---

## ⚔️ Bladed Weapons

> [!weapon]- Knife/Dagger
> **💰 Cost:** 0 | **⚔️ WR:** 1 | **📏 Range:** 1 TH  
> **🎯 Aspects:** Small  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A basic cutting implement that can serve as both tool and weapon. Knives and daggers are compact, easily concealed, and effective in close combat situations.

> [!weapon]- Machete
> **💰 Cost:** 1 | **⚔️ WR:** 2 | **📏 Range:** 0  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A broad, heavy blade originally designed for cutting through vegetation. The machete's weight and sharp edge make it an effective weapon for close combat.

> [!weapon]- Dagger, Mono-Edged
> **💰 Cost:** 2 | **⚔️ WR:** 2 | **📏 Range:** 1 TH  
> **🎯 Aspects:** Small, Mono-Edged  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A dagger enhanced with mono-molecular edge technology. These bladed weapons feature an almost infinitely sharp edge created with a single carbon nanotube running the length of the blade.

> [!weapon]- Short Sword, Mono-Edged
> **💰 Cost:** 3 | **⚔️ WR:** 3 | **📏 Range:** 0  
> **🎯 Aspects:** Mono-Edged  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** No
> 
> A balanced one-handed sword with mono-molecular edge technology. The mono-edge never needs sharpening and maintains its cutting ability indefinitely.

> [!weapon]- Longsword, Mono-Edged
> **💰 Cost:** 4 | **⚔️ WR:** 4 | **📏 Range:** 0  
> **🎯 Aspects:** Large, Mono-Edged  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
> 
> A two-handed sword with mono-molecular edge technology combining devastating cutting power with reach and leverage.

> [!weapon]- Spear, Mono-Edged
> **💰 Cost:** 5 | **⚔️ WR:** 4 | **📏 Range:** 1 TH  
> **🎯 Aspects:** Large, Mono-Edged  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
> 
> A polearm weapon with a mono-molecular edge spearhead. The spear's length allows attacks at greater distance while the mono-edge ensures devastating penetration.

> [!weapon]- Halberd, Mono-Edged
> **💰 Cost:** 6 | **⚔️ WR:** 5 | **📏 Range:** 0  
> **🎯 Aspects:** Long, Huge(P), Mono-Edged  
> **⚠️ Restricted:** Yes
> 
> A massive polearm combining axe, spear, and hook into one devastating weapon, enhanced with mono-molecular edge technology.
