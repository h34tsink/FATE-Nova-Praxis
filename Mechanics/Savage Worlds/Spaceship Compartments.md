## Bridge
- **Function**: Command center for navigation, communication, and ship systems.
- **Importance**: Vital for strategic decision-making and control of the ship.
## Engineering
- **Function**: Houses main engine, power generators, and mechanical components.
- **Role**: Key for maintenance, repairs, and ship functionality.
## Cargo Hold
- **Function**: Storage area for salvaged materials and goods.
- **Customization**: Size and security features can be tailored to needs.
## Crew Quarters
- **Function**: Living spaces for the crew including sleeping areas and personal storage.
- **Impact**: Crew comfort and morale are influenced by the quality of these quarters.
## Medical Bay
- **Function**: Facility for treating injuries and medical research.
- **Equipment**: Can be equipped with advanced medical technology.
## Armory
- **Function**: Storage for the ship's weapons and defense systems.
- **Upgrades**: Possible improvements for combat capabilities.
## Science Lab
- **Function**: Used for analysis and research, especially in salvage operations.
- **Equipment**: Can have specialized equipment for various scientific investigations.
## Communications Array
- **Function**: Manages all ship communications.
- **Upgrades**: Enhancements can improve range and security.
## Airlock and Docking Bays
- **Function**: Used for entering/exiting the ship and docking with other vessels.
- **Customization**: Security and docking procedure improvements.
## Life Support Systems
- **Function**: Regulates atmosphere, temperature, and gravity.
- **Upgrades**: Enhanced for efficiency or redundancy.
## Recreational Area
- **Function**: Space for relaxation and entertainment.
- **Features**: Includes entertainment options and social areas.
## Escape Pods
- **Function**: Emergency evacuation for the crew.
- **Consideration**: Number and quality are crucial for survival scenarios.
