### BUY AND SELL RARE OR ILLEGAL GOODS
- **Skill**: Persuasion
- **Successes Needed**: 2-5
- **Costs**: Purchase price + markup for rarity/legality
- **Setbacks**: Legal trouble, counterfeit goods, seller betrayal, or item unavailability

Covers procuring hard-to-find or illegal items through negotiation and payment, with the complexity based on the item's rarity and legality.

### FENCE ITEMS
- **Skill**: Persuasion
- **Successes Needed**: 1-2
- **Setback**: Theft of goods, legal ambush, claim by original owner

For selling stolen, marked, or specialized goods discreetly, with success based on the item's value and legality.

### EARN MONEY THROUGH BUSINESS
- **Use**: Work Project for active income or Learn an Edge Project for passive income via the Rich or Filthy Rich Edge.

Represents earning income through business ventures, either by direct involvement or by establishing a self-sustaining business.

### HEAL AND RECOVER
- **Skills**: Vigor or Spirit (or caretaker's Healing)
- **Successes Needed**: 2 for minor issues, 4 for major
- **Costs**: $250 for professional care
- **Setback**: Treatment failure or additional costs

Used for recovery from physical or mental trauma, with professional care improving chances of success.

### CAROUSING
- **Skills**: Common Knowledge, Gambling, Persuasion, Vigor
- **Setback**: Bad outcomes based on card draw
- **Outcome**: Varied based on card draw, involves gaining or losing money and narrative developments.

A downtime activity for relaxation and potential plot developments, resolved through card draws.

### ESTABLISH CONTACTS
- **Skill**: Persuasion
- **Successes Needed**: 3+ for new contacts, upgrades require additional successes
- **Setback**: Loss of progress or downgrade in contact relationship

Allows establishing or improving relationships with groups or individuals for future aid, using a Favor Die system to represent the relationship's strength.

### FIND SOMETHING OUT
- **Skills**: Various, based on investigation method
- **Successes Needed**: 2-5, or multiple smaller projects for complex mysteries
- **Setback**: Misinformation, negative social consequences

For uncovering information or solving mysteries, with gameplay integrating major discoveries.

### GATHER RUMORS
- **Skill**: Persuasion
- **Successes Needed**: 1 per rumor
- **Costs**: Bribes for bonuses
- **Setback**: Negative reputation or misinformation

Collects local rumors and plot hooks, with outcomes influenced by spending and social interactions.

### PLAN A JOB
- **Skill**: Varies by planning aspect
- **Successes Needed**: Each success grants a Benny for the job
- **Costs**: Bribes or gear for bonuses
- **Setback**: Increased job difficulty, compromised planning

Prepares for heists or missions, granting Heist Bennies for overcoming challenges during the operation.

### BUILD A BASE
- **Skill**: Varies (e.g., Repair, Trade, Electronics)
- **Successes**: 3 for +1 Support bonus; +3 more for +2
- **Costs**: $500 for +1; +$1,000 for +2
- **Setbacks**: Damage to base, unexpected intrusions

Enables upgrading a base with facilities that provide bonuses to specific Projects, enhancing the group's capabilities.

### CRAFT GEAR
- **Skill**: Repair or Trade (Electronics for modern/sci-fi gear)
- **Successes**: Each success = $200 worth of gear
- **Costs**: 25% of total cost for raw materials
- **Setbacks**: Damage to tools or materials, loss of progress

Allows creation of gear from raw materials, especially beneficial when using special or rare materials for enhanced effects.

### MAKE ARCANE CONTRAPTION
- **Skill**: Arcane skill
- **Successes**: Each success = 3 Power Points of contraptions
- **Costs**: $20 per Power Point
- **Setbacks**: Dangerous malfunctions, temporary injury

Facilitates the creation of one-shot arcane gadgets, with special bonuses for characters with the Artificer Edge.

### MAKE ARTIFACTS
- **Skill**: Arcane skill
- **Successes/Costs**: Depends on the artifact (see chart)
- **Tools**: $500 for necessary lab and library
- **Setbacks**: Loss of components, introduction of quirks

Involves creating permanent magic items that recharge or don't use Power Points, requiring special materials and the Artificer Edge.

### INCREASE A TRAIT
- **No roll needed; downtime based on ½ next Die Type**
- A method for a one-time Trait increase per Rank, emphasizing the effort required to improve core capabilities.

### LEARN AN EDGE
- **Skill varies; avoid making it the same as Edge benefits**
- **Successes**: 4 for Novice Edge, +1 per Rank beyond
- **Setbacks**: Issues with training resources or mentors

Allows learning new Edges through study and practice, with restrictions to maintain balance with normal advancement.

### RECOVER EDGE BENEFITS
- **No roll required; uses downtime**
- For regaining benefits of Edges like Trademark Weapon or Beast Master that have recovery stipulations.

### RESEARCH A POWER
- **Skill**: Research
- **Successes**: 2 for re-trapped Power, 3+ for new/custom Power
- **Costs**: $100 per Rank for new Powers
- **Setbacks**: Rival interference, lab accidents

Enables learning or creating new Powers, with a focus on incorporating unique trappings or creating entirely new effects.
