# Nova Praxis Character Augmentations Guide

In the world of Nova Praxis, advanced technology allows individuals to enhance their bodies and direct their own evolution through elective **augmentations**. These can be used to treat injuries or to push human capabilities beyond their natural limits.

There are two primary types of augmentations:

* **Cybernetic Augmentations (Cyberware)**: These introduce synthetic materials into a living body or enhance an existing cybersleeve. This often involves nanomachines replacing biological tissue or bridging the gap between synthetic and biological systems where limbs are replaced.
* **Biotech Augmentations (Bioware)**: These are created through **genetic manipulation**, typically by introducing a retrovirus designed to modify the human genome, or by implementing modifications during a sleeve's initial design. Bioware consists purely of biological material, making it immune to Savant Programs and unsuitable for cybersleeves or synthetic limbs.

## Purchasing Aftermarket Augmentations

If you wish to add an augmentation to a pure human body or an existing sleeve after character creation, it is considered an "aftermarket" purchase.

* The **Cost** to add an aftermarket augmentation is calculated as: **Total Number of Augs you already have + (Augmentation Value (AV) of the new aug x 2)**.
* Augmentations like Apotheosis do not count towards your total augmentation limit or the cost calculation.
* Installation generally takes a few hours and requires an Education test (Difficulty = 1 + Aug's AV).
* A sleeve can support a total number of augmentations equal to its **Endurance Skill x 2**.
* Purchasing **restricted augmentations** requires a special license (such as the Licensed Stunt).

Upgrading an existing aftermarket augmentation follows a similar cost calculation: **Total Number of Augs you already have + (Total AV of Aug + all Upgrades x 2)**.

## List of Character Augmentations

Here is a comprehensive list of augmentations available to characters, with their details as described in the sources:

* **Adrenal Boost Module**  [Human or Biosleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Allows you to trigger an adrenaline surge as a Free Action, granting a **+1 bonus to Athletics and Melee Skills** and increasing stress absorbed by Physical Consequences by +1 for the duration. These bonuses last for a number of rounds equal to your Endurance, after which you gain the **Got the Shakes Aspect** until the end of the scene.

* **Alternative Mobility System**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Your legs are replaced or augmented with biological or cybernetic non-humanoid limbs, giving you a **Persistent Aspect** related to your movement method (e.g., *Wheeled(P)*, *Cyberspider Legs(P)*, *Snake-Like Lower Body(P)*). Applying this as bioware to a human body or biosleeve is a violation of the HPA.
  * **Upgrade: Flight Capable** (+2 AV): You gain the ability to **fly** at twice the average human running speed and receive a **+3 bonus to Hustle, Run, or related tests**. You also gain a Persistent Aspect describing this augmentation.

* **Apotheosis**  [Requires 2, 5]
  * **Base AV**: 3 (or free with sleeve purchase)
  * **Note**: Does not count against your total augmentation limit.
  * **Effect**: Your sleeve has undergone **Apotheosis**, featuring a **mindset** and **mnemonic core**. You gain the *Apotheosized Aspect*, reflecting that your mind is now software. This grants virtual immortality, allowing your ego to be downloaded into a new body even after death. The mindset acts as an implanted PPC, providing an augmented reality interface, storing data, and running programs, managed by an onboard Agent.
  * **Upgrade: Cognet Capable** (+1 AV) [Restricted]: Your mindset can run a **Cognet**, a secure multi-channel communication package for encrypted communication.
  * **Upgrade: Remote Backup** (+1 AV): Your mnemonic core can perform **wireless uploads to a secure server** while you sleep, allowing restoration from a remote backup if your core is lost after death.
  * **Upgrade: Kill Switch** (+1 AV): A device that, with the proper password, can shut down your mindset and wipe your mnemonic core via the Extranet.

* **Auditory Amp and Filter Module**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Replaces parts of your inner ear or synthetic equivalent, allowing you to **hear sounds a normal person couldn't** and filter out background noise. You gain the *Auditory Amplifier and Filtering Module(P) Aspect*.

* **Arterial Toxin Filter**  [Human or Biosleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: A nanocybernetic filtering system implanted in your heart cleanses blood of toxins and unwanted bacteria, helping resist foreign substances and aiding natural healing. You gain the *Arterial Toxin Filter(P) Aspect*.

* **Beholder Sensory Suite**
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Dozens of tiny sensors throughout your sleeve provide **full 360-degree vision**. You gain the *Beholder Sensory Suite(P) Persistent Aspect*.
  * **Upgrade: Hyper-Aware** (+1 AV): Enhanced interface grants a **+3 bonus on initiative tests** in physical conflicts as long as you can see the threat.

* **Bio-Skin Sheath**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: A natural, skin-like substance that covers cybernetic limbs or entire cybersleeves, providing **tactile sensation** and making the sleeve feel more alive. It appears visibly non-biological due to HPA requirements. If covering a cybersleeve, the *Synthetic Biology Aspect* from Fully Synthetic is no longer Persistent. This augmentation does not count against your aug limit.
  * **Upgrade: Seemingly Real** (+1 AV) [Restricted]: Your bio-skin sheath appears as real human skin, removing the *Inhuman Appearance Aspect* and making it indistinguishable from a living human body to the naked eye or SMR transceiver (but not X-rays).
  * **Upgrade: Charlatan** (+2 AV) [Restricted]: (See Charlatan under Smuggler's Compartment for details on its function).

* **Cogware Partition**  [Restricted]
  * **Base AV**: 3
  * **Effect**: Your mindset has a partition to store **cogware**, which streams self-contained data directly into your conscious mind, allowing you to perform tasks you wouldn't normally know. Activating it loads a specific cogware piece, granting an **Aspect** (e.g., *Kung-fu Master*, *Shuttle Pilot*). Running cogware stresses your mind, requiring a Cohesion test to activate (Difficulty 4, Simple Action, Duration: Scene).
  * **Upgrade: Multi-Partition Cog-Drive** (+2 AV): Your mindset can load two pieces of cogware simultaneously (Execution Difficulty 6).

* **Cosmetic Customization**  [Free with Sleeve Purchase 1]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Allows for exaggerated crystalline canines, glowing reptilian eyes, chrome skin, etc., primarily for aesthetic purposes and to make a statement. You gain an Aspect describing your modification(s). Does not count against your aug limit. Purchasing more than once for a human or biosleeve violates the HPA.

* **Crypto-Laser Link**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: An IR laser transceiver for **encrypted, invisible, and silent wireless communication** with other crypto-laser transceivers. Can be controlled mentally with a mindset or via a PPC.

* **Custom Built**  [Sleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Your sleeve was custom-built for a specific task (e.g., high-gravity worlds, harsh climates, stealth, intimidation). The sleeve gains an Aspect describing its special purpose.

* **Data Jack and Storage**  [Free with Apotheosis 1, 5]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: A port on your body or sleeve for **wired connection to an outside system**, connecting to a hidden data storage device. If you have a mindset, it can allow read/write access to partitions.
  * **Upgrade: Secured** (+0 AV): A partitioned, highly secure, encrypted file system accessible only with specific passkeys.

* **ELS System (Emergency Life Support System)**  [Human or Biosleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 2 (reduced to 1 if you have Synthesis)
  * **Effect**: Installed in the torso, it automatically activates when you are in life-threatening danger (poison, toxins, starvation, dehydration), stopping the heart and pumping heavily oxygenated blood and nutrients to critical areas, along with painkillers, to help you survive until safety.

* **Esper Vent**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Parts of your sleeve are replaced with hollow synthetic sections to store an **esper swarm**, with vents allowing them to emerge from your body. Commonly placed in arms.
  * **Upgrade: Charlatan** (+1 AV) [Restricted]: (See Charlatan under Smuggler's Compartment for details on its function).

* **Exceptional Genegineering**  [Biosleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Bioware Augs are seamlessly implemented. Once per scene, you may **invoke an Aspect from a bioware Augmentation for free**. Increases your sleeve's total augmentation limit by +1.

* **Extra Limb**  [Restricted on Biosleeves 6]
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: You have **one or more additional prehensile limbs** (genetic tampering or grafted cybernetics). You gain a **Persistent Aspect** describing the limb (e.g., *Prehensile Tail(P)*, *Cybernetic Tentacle(P)*), which can be invoked for bonuses on grappling, climbing, running, or complex work.

* **Fibro-Muscle Weave**  [Human or Biosleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Your muscles are woven with synthetic fibers, granting **super-human strength**. You gain the *Fibro-Muscle Weave Aspect*, which can be invoked for Athletics tests or melee/thrown weapon attack tests (after hitting).
  * **Upgrade: Titan Weave** (+2 AV): Your muscles are more synthetic than biological, making the *Fibro-Muscle Weave Aspect* **Persistent**.

* **Fully Synthetic**  [Cybersleeve Only 4, 5]
  * **Base AV**: N/A
  * **Effect**: Your sleeve is **completely synthetic**, with no biological components, providing fine motor control, strength, and durability. You gain the **Persistent Aspect Synthetic Biology(P)**, representing the sleeve's composition and the ego's tendency to fragment. You are immune to drugs, poisons, vacuum, and prolonged microgravity effects. You do not need to eat, drink, or breathe. Requires a 2-hour daily "sleep" for your Agent to repair psychological damage. This augmentation does not count against your aug limit.
  * **Upgrade: Reinforced Substrate** (+1 to Sleeve Cost): Your skeleton is reinforced, increasing your **Endurance by +2** and adding the **Persistent Aspect Slow and Heavy(P)**.
  * **Upgrade: High Tensile Muscular Fibers** (+2 to Sleeve Cost): Your synthetic muscles are much stronger, granting the same benefits as the Fibro-Muscle Weave Augmentation.

* **Gills Gene-Graft**  [Human or Biosleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: You have fish-like gills on your neck, allowing you to **draw oxygen from water**. You can still breathe air. This technically violates the HPA but was granted an exception.

* **Gyroscopic Stabilizers**
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Gyroscopes in your wrists and elbows stabilize your hands, reducing recoil from firearms and aiding delicate tasks. You gain the *Gyro-Stabilized Aspect*, which can be invoked for delicate tasks, full-auto attack tests, or when taking aim.

* **Hidden Weapon System**  [Restricted]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Your sleeve contains a **hidden weapon** (e.g., retractable blade, shoulder-mounted gun). You must acquire the weapon separately and then purchase this augmentation for installation.
  * **Upgrade: Charlatan** (+1 AV) [Restricted]: (See Charlatan under Smuggler's Compartment for details on its function).

* **Light Screen Camouflage**  [Restricted]
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Skin woven with magnetic field generators bends light around you, making you **nearly invisible** to visible and IR light. You gain a **+3 bonus on Stealth tests** when not moving, or **+2 when moving**.

* **Massive Size**  [Sleeve Only, Restricted on Biosleeves 6]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Your sleeve is **huge**, towering 3 to 4 meters. Biosleeves of this size are illegal. Sleeves with this gain **Size 1**, a **+1 bonus to WR of all melee weapon attacks**, and the *Massive Aspect*.

* **Medichine Factory**  [Human or Biosleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: A nanomachine factory implanted in your body produces "medichines" that aid natural healing, stop bleeding, close wounds, and ward off infection. **Reduces Physical Consequence recovery time by 1 time increment**.

* **Nano-Cyber Limb**  [Human or Biosleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: A nanocybernetic prosthetic replaces one of your limbs. These are stronger and more versatile than natural limbs. You gain an Aspect (e.g., *Cyber-Arm*, *Shiny New Leg*) representing the limb, which can be invoked for benefit in relevant actions. Nano-cyber limbs can support other augs.
  * **Upgrade: Bioskinned** (+1 AV): Your limb is covered in bioskin, providing a more natural feel and greater sensitivity. It can appear natural or synthetic.
  * **Upgrade: Multiple Limbs** (+1 AV): Two limbs of the same type are replaced, granting a **Persistent Aspect** (e.g., *Nanocybernetic Arms(P)*).

* **Neura-Surge**  [Restricted]
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Threaded augmented nervous system allows you to enter "overdrive" as an Instant Action, allowing you to move and process information so quickly that the world appears to slow down. You can perform an immediate Simple Action. Stresses the body, requiring an Endurance test to activate. Activation difficulty increases by +1 for each subsequent use in the same scene (Difficulty 4, Duration: Turn).

* **Optimized Power System**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: A top-of-the-line power management system for your cyberware. Once per scene, you may **invoke an Aspect from a cyberware Augmentation for free**. Increases your augmentation limit by +1.

* **Particle Detection and Analysis Suite**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Bolsters olfactory glands with a highly sensitive chemical analysis device, granting a **heightened sense of smell** (can track by scent) and allowing output of chemical composition data to a PPC or mindset. You gain the *Particle Detection and Analysis Module Aspect*.
  * **Upgrade: Integrated Filter** (+1 AV): Module filters out harmful gasses and sterilizes airborne diseases, making you **immune to such harm**.

* **Performance Enhanced**  [Sleeve Only]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Your sleeve is of higher quality, granting **1 additional Skill Rank** to a Physical Skill of your choosing, allowing it to surpass the normal maximum of 5. Can be purchased up to three times for different skills.
  * **Upgrade: Top of the Line** (+2 AV): Grants an additional +1 bonus rank (to max 7) in a Skill already enhanced by Performance Enhanced.

* **Pheromone Control & Sensitivity**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: You can **emit desired pheromones** to subtly influence emotions of biological beings and actively sense others' pheromones. Once per scene, gain a **+2 bonus on tests to convince biological beings or determine their emotional state**.
  * **Upgrade: Aura** (+1 AV): You emit high levels of pheromones, granting a **+1 bonus on all Diplomacy or Guile tests/contests** when socializing with biological beings for the scene. Can be activated for free with Exceptional Engineering.

* **Physiological Behavior Mods**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Genetic code modifications trigger specific emotional responses (e.g., desire to bond, urge to hunt, overactive libido). You gain an Aspect reflecting this behavior (e.g., *Pack Mentality*, *Insatiable Curiosity*, *Solitary Predator*).

* **Poison Gland**  [Restricted]
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Your body produces a toxin, activatable at will, releasing poison into your saliva (administered via kiss or bite). Basic version produces **two uses of Potency 4 poison per day**.
  * **Upgrade: Lethal Dose** (+1 AV): Poison has a **Potency of 6**.
  * **Upgrade: Increased Production** (+1 AV): Produce **four uses of poison each day**.

* **Reinforced Skeleton**
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Bones or frame fortified with ceramic and non-magnetic metals, making them nearly indestructible against blunt force. The amount of stress absorbed when taking a **Severe or Extreme Physical Consequence is increased by +3**.

* **Retinal Implant**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: One eye replaced by a nanocybernetic prosthetic, functioning like a normal eye but with **variable zoom** and enhanced detail in the dark. Can pair with computers for entoptic display and AR interface. You gain the *Retinal Implant Aspect*.
  * **Upgrade: IR Receiver** (+1 AV): See infrared light, gaining a **+2 bonus to notice objects with different temperatures**.
  * **Upgrade: Optic Drone** (+1 AV): Prosthetic eye discharges a tiny winged spider reconnaissance drone, allowing you to see what it sees. While active, you gain the **Persistent Aspect Distracted(P)**.
  * **Upgrade: X-Ray Sight** (+2 AV): Eye implant emits X-Rays, allowing you to **see through most objects** (e.g., walls, clothing, doors) up to 1 ft (variable density). Activate as Simple Action, Duration: Scene. You gain the **Persistent Aspect X-Ray Vision (P)**.

* **Retractable Claws**  [Restricted]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Hands outfitted with razor-sharp catlike claws emerging from beneath fingernails. They have a **Weapon Rating of 2**. Can administer poison if you have the Poison Gland Augmentation.

* **SIM-Cell Module**  [Requires a mindset]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Your mindset has a special partition and second mnemonic core to **host another ego (SIM)**. The visiting SIM can view the real world through your senses and use your mindset as its own if allowed. Communication occurs without wireless signals leaving your mindset.

* **Skinweave Armor**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Skin woven with spider silk and graphene mesh, making it strong and resistant to damage without affecting outward appearance. Provides an **Armor Rating of +1**, stacking with other armor.

* **SMR Transceiver and Translation Suite**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Sensors emit/receive sub-millimeter radiation, allowing you to **see through non-metal/water objects** (clothing, paper, wood, masonry, plastic, ceramics), aiding in detecting hidden weapons. Poorer resolution than normal sight.

* **Smuggler's Compartment**  [Restricted]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: A disguised, insulated compartment in your body (e.g., thigh, torso) for storing an item the size of your fist or a small pistol. Will not set off metal detectors but contents are visible with X-Ray scanners unless upgraded.
  * **Upgrade: Charlatan** (+1 AV): Uses light screen technology to **fool X-ray, SMR, or other radiation-based sensor systems** by showing a hologram of what should be there instead of what is.

* **Sonar Reception Array**
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Sensors on face/neck analyze sound and send data to the brain's visual cortex, granting **real-time sonar** ("seeing" in the dark) and aiding in perceiving objects covered by a light screen. You gain the **Persistent Aspect Sonar Reception Array(P)**.

* **Stillness Body Lock System**
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Nanocybernetically modified joints can be selectively locked, and a small amount of oxygen can be stored with each breath, allowing you to hold your breath for extended periods. Popular for precision shooters to eliminate extraneous motion or for "poser" scenes. You gain the *Stillness Body Lock System Aspect*.

* **Striking Looks**  [Human or Biosleeve Only, Requires 1]
  * **Base AV**: 1
  * **Effect**: Your sleeve is exceptionally attractive. You gain the *Striking Looks Aspect*. This augmentation does not count against your aug limit. Cybersleeves require Bio-Skin Sheath with Seemingly Real upgrade to take this.
  * **Upgrade: Absolutely Stunning** (+1 AV): You are almost unnaturally attractive, gaining the **Persistent Aspect Absolutely Stunning(P)**.

* **Subdermal Plating**
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: A layer of thin, flexible armor beneath your skin, deflecting bullets and blades. Increases stress absorbed by **Mild or Moderate Physical Consequences by +2** (e.g., Moderate absorbs 6 instead of 4).
  * **Upgrade: Composite Plating** (+1 AV): Reinforced with lightweight composite materials, increasing stress absorbed by **Mild or Moderate Consequences by +3**.

* **Synthesis**  [Biosleeve Only, Requires a mindset 2, 3]
  * **Base AV**: 3
  * **Effect**: Weaves nanocybernetic fibers through living tissues, creating a seamless blend of man and machine, making the body tougher, stronger, faster, more durable, and enhancing senses. You gain the *Synthesized Biological Perfection Aspect*. You are **immune to poisons and disease**, unaffected by prolonged microgravity, and only need to eat, drink, and sleep half as much in a broadcast power field. Viewed by transhumanists as the pinnacle of human evolution, but by hardcore Purists with disgust.

* **Targeting Support System**  [Restricted]
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Sensors in your hand, arm, and mindset sync with a targeting support module on a firearm, snapping the weapon on target. You gain a **+1 bonus on all ranged attacks with firearms** equipped with a synced module.

* **Tiny Size**  [Restricted in Biosleeves 6]
  * **Base AV**: 2
  * **Effect**: Your sleeve is very small (less than a meter high). Biosleeves of this size are illegal per HPA. Sleeves with this gain **Size -1**, suffer a **-1 penalty to WR of unarmed/melee weapon attacks**, but gain a **+1 bonus on Stealth tests and Athletics-based defense tests**. You gain the *Tiny Aspect*.
